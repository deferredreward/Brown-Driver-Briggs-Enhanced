נָגַף verb strike, smite (Aramaic נְגַף); -

Qal Perfect ׳ נ 2Chr 13:15; Isa 19:22 ( וְ consecutive); suffix נְגָפוֺ 2Chr 21:18, etc.; Imperfect יִגֹּף Exod 21:35 + 2 t.; וַיִּגֹּף Exod 32:35 + 4 t.; אֶגּוֺף Ps 89:24 +, etc.; Infinitive absolute נָנֹף Isa 19:22 construct לִנְגֹּף Exod 12:23 (twice in verse); נָגְפוֺ vExod 12:27 — strike, smite, of serious (even fatal) injury, compare accusative of person Exod 21:22; Exod 21:35 ( E ); one's foot against (בְּ) stone Ps 91:12 absolute (stumble) Prov 3:23 especially of judgment, subject ׳ י, object individual (fatal stroke) 1Sam 25:38; 1Sam 26:10; 2Sam 12:15; 2Chr 13:20; 2Chr 21:18 (לָחֳלִי); object people (absolute) Exod 12:23 (twice in verse); Exod 12:27 ( J E ), Exod 32:35; Josh 24:5 ( E ), Isa 19:22, compare vIsa 19:22 see also Ps 89:24 compare with accusative of cognate meaning מַגֵּפָה Zech 14:12; Zech 14:18; 2Chr 21:14 (followed by also בְּעַמְּךָ); with בְּ instrumental Exod 7:27 of causing defeat in battle, compare accusative + לִפְנֵי Judg 20:35; 1Sam 4:3; 2Chr 13:15; 2Chr 14:11.
Niph`al Perfect נִגַּף 2Sam 10:15 נִגְּפוּ v2Sam 10:19 + 2 t., etc.; Imperfect יִנָּגֵף 2Chr 6:24 וַיִּנָּגֵף 1Sam 4:2 + 4 t.; Infinitive absolute נִגּוֺף Judg 20:39 construct הִנָּגֵף 1Kgs 8:33 Participle נִגָּף Deut 28:25 ; Judg 20:39, etc.; — be smitten, before enemy, in battle, followed by לִפְנֵי Judg 20:32 ; Judg 20:39 (twice in verse); 1Sam 4:2; 1Sam 7:10; 2Sam 10:15; 2Sam 10:19; 2Sam 18:7; 1Kgs 8:33; 2Kgs 14:12 ; 1Chr 19:16; 1Chr 19:19; 2Chr 6:24; 2Chr 25:22; Lev 26:17; Num 14:42; Deut 1:42 absolute Judg 20:36 ; 1Sam 4:10 ; 2Sam 2:17; 2Chr 20:22 Participle after נָתַן, followed by לִפְנֵי Deut 28:7; Deut 28:25 . see נָגַע Niph`al
Hithpa`el Imperfect יִתְנַגְּפוּ Jer 13:16 = stumble, subject רַגְלֵיכֶם, followed by על (location); compare Qal (Ps 91:12) and נֶגֶף 2.
