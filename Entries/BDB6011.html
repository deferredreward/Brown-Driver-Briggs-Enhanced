I. עֵילָם proper name [of a people and territory] Elam, Αιλαμ, Ἐλαμεῖραι, well-known country and people northeast of Lower Tigris (Assyrian Elamtu DlPa 320 ff. COT Gen 10:22 ); — as early invaders of Palestine Gen 14:1; Gen 14:9, allies of Assyr. Isa 22:6 foe of Babylon Isa 21:2 abode of dispersed Israelites Isa 11:11 prophesied against Jer 49:34; Jer 49:35; Jer 49:36 (twice in verse) ( Qr , Kt עולם), Jer 49:37 Jer 49:38 Jer 49:39; Ezek 32:24, compare ׳ כָּלמַֿלְכֵי ע Jer 25:25 called 'son' of Shem Gen 10:22 ( P ) = 1Chr 1:17 purely local designation הַמְּדִינָה ׳ ע Dan 8:2.
