BDB758 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB760

אֲרוּכָה noun feminine healing of a wound, restoration (properly the new flesh that grows at the wounded spot, Arabic ; FiJPh xiii. 114-6, so Fl De on Isa 58:8 see also Di) — ׳ א 2Chr 24:13; Neh 4:1 אֲרֻכָה Jer 30:17; Jer 33:6 construct אֲרֻכַת Jer 8:22 אֲרֻכָֽתְךָ Isa 58:8 — always figurative

a. healing, restoration of Israel Isa 58:8, here with verb צָמַח, elsewhere with עָלָה; Jer 8:22 (|| רָפָא), with עָלָה Hiph`il, subject ׳ י, Jer 30:17; Jer 33:6 (|| id.)
b. restoration of walls of temple 2Chr 24:13, of walls of Jerusalem (compare Fi above) Neh 4:1 (both with עלה).
