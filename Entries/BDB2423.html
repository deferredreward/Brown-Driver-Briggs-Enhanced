BDB2422 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2424

זְעָקָה noun feminine cry, outcry, absolute ׳ ז Jer 18:22 + 5 t.; construct זַעֲקַת Gen 18:20 + 3 t.; suffix זַעֲקָתִי Job 16:18 זַעֲקָתָם Isa 15:5 + 3 t.; —

1. outcry, against, זַעֲקַת ׳ סְדֹם וגו Gen 18:20 ( J ).
2. cry of distress, concerning something זַעֲקַתשֶֿׁבֶר Isa 15:5 (object of יְעֹעֵ֑רוּ; compare בִּבְכִי in prev. clause); absolute vIsa 15:8 (subject of הִקִּיפָה, || יְלָלָה); Jer 18:22 (׳ תִּשָּׁמַע ז), Jer 20:16; Jer 48:4; Jer 50:46; Neh 5:6; Neh 9:9 (all with שׁמע), Jer 48:34 (jointed with נתן קול), Job 16:18; Esth 9:31 (lamentation), compare Esth 4:1 (גְּדוֺלָה וּמָרָה ׳ ז, accusative of cognate meaning with verb with זעק); specifically זעקתדָּֿ֑ל Prov 21:13 cry of poor; ׳ קול ז Isa 65:19 (|| קול בכי), Jer 51:54; Ezek 27:28.
3. outcry, clamour זעקת מושׁל בַּכְּסִילִים Eccl 9:17 (opposed to דברי חכמים בְּנַחַת נשׁמעים).
