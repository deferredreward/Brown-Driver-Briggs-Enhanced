אָבִיב noun masculine Lev 2:14 collective (LagBN 207 Infinitive)

1. fresh, young ears of barley Exod 9:31 indefinite Lev 2:14 קָלוּי בָּאֵשׁ ׳ מִנְחַת בִּכּוּרִים ליהוה א.
2. חֹדֶשׁ הָֽאָבִיב month of ear-forming, or of growing green, Abib, month of Exodus & passover, Exod 13:4; Exod 23:15; Exod 34:18 (twice in verse) ( J E ), Deut 16:1 (twice in verse) (1st month = circa April = הָרִאשֹׁן, ׳ הַחֹדֶשׁ הָר (q. v.) in P ; see Di Exod 12:2 = post-exilic נִיסָן q. v.)
