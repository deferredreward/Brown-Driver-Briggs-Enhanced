Biblical Hebrew

[טָרַד] verb pursue, chase, be continuous (Arabic pursue; II. prolong one's voice; VIII. continue uninterruptedly: Assyrian ‰arâdu , drive away COTGloss, so Aramaic , טְרַד) — only

Qal Participle active וְדֶלֶף טֹרֵד Prov 19:13 a continuous dripping, dropping, i.e. one in which one drop pursues another; so טוֺרֵד ׳ ד Prov 27:15 in both simile of a contentious woman.
