עָיֵף adjective faint, weary; — ׳ ע absolute Gen 25:29 +; feminine עֲיֵפָה Jer 31:25 +; plural עֲיֵפִים Judg 8:4 +; — from exertion and hunger Gen 25:20; Gen 25:30 ( J ), Judg 8:4; Judg 8:5; Deut 25:18 (+ יָגֵעַ), compare Isa 5:27, ׳ הָנִיחוּ לֶע Isa 28:12 (figurative), 2Sam 16:14 (si vera lectio, proper name, of a location needed, compare We Dr HPS); + רָעֵב, צָמֵא 2Sam 17:29 specifically from thirst Isa 29:8 (simile), Job 22:7, ׳ נֶפֶשׁ ע Prov 25:25 and (figurative) Jer 31:25 (|| דָּֽאֲבָה); hence אֶרֶץ צִיָּה וְעָיֵף Ps 63:2 (figurative, on masculine see KöSynt. § 334 f.), compare Ps 143:6 (simile), Isa 32:2 (opposed to צֵל, in simile); ׳ ע that is beast Isa 46:1 — See also foregoing.
