[שָׁחַד] verb give a present, bribe (Eccl 35:14 Aramaic שְׁחַד, bribe; perhaps also Assyrian šidê, gifts, DlWB 643); —

Qal Imperfect 2 feminine singular, accusative of person וַתִּשְׁחֳדִי אוֺתָם Ezek 16:33 thou didst bribe them to come (לָבוֺא; || נָתַן נְדָנִים); read probably also Infinitive construct suffix שַׁחֲדָהּ Isa 47:11 to buy it off (for ᵑ0 שַׁחְרָהּ, so Kr Gr Buhl CheHpt Heb 142, compare Marti; see Prov 6:35); Imperative masculine plural שִׁחֲדוּ בַעֲדִי (Ges«GKC:64a»§ 64a) Job 6:22 give a bribe for me.
