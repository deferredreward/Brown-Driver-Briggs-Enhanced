תִּהוֺם noun feminine Gen 7:11 + 7 t. and masculine Job 28:14 + 5 t. (AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 62 Köii. 2. 167 Ency. Bib.DEEP) deep, sea, abyss (almost always in poetry); — absolute ׳ ת Gen 1:2 +; plural absolute תְּהֹמוֺת Ps 77:17 +, etc., ׳ בַּתּ Isa 63:13 + Isa 106:9 (only here with article); construct תְּהוֺמוֺת Isa 71:20 (but see 5 infra); —

1. deep, of subterranean waters, Gen 49:25 (poem in J ; opposed to שָׁמַיִם), Deut 33:13 (opposed to id.); ׳ רַבָּה מַעְיְנוֺת ת Gen 7:11; Gen 8:2 ( P ; || אֲרֻבֹּת הַשָּׁמַיִם), ׳ עִינוֺת ת Prov 8:28 (|| שְׁחָקִים), Job 28:14; Job 38:16 (both || יָם); תְּהוֺם רַבָּה Amos 7:4 (probably), רַבָּה ׳ מִשְׁפָּטֶיךָ ת Ps 36:7 (opposed to אֵל צִדְקָֽתְךָ כְּהַרְרֵי), Isa 51:10 (perhaps); so plural תְּהֹמוֺת, Prov 8:24 (|| מַעְיָנוֺת), Prov 3:20 (opposed to שְׁחָקִים), and probably Ps 33:7 (|| מֵי הַיָּם), Ps 135:6 ( + יַמִּים).
2. (deep) sea, overwhelming Tyre Ezek 26:19 (|| הַמַּיִם הָרַבִּים ), roaring at theoph. Hab 3:10 in general, || יָם, Job 38:30 (׳ פְּנֵי ת); || מַיִם Jonah 2:6 alonE Job 41:24 figurative, קוֺרַא ׳ אֶלתֿ ׳ ת Job 42:8 (|| מִשְׁבָּרִים, גַּלִּים; but possibly here of Jordan, compare 4); in plural = abysses of sea, Exod 15:5; Exod 15:8 (of Red Sea, so) Isa 63:13 || Ps 106:9; Ps 77:17 also Ps 78:15 (in simile), Ps 107:26 (poetic of hollows of great waves, opposed to שָׁמַיִם); vaguely, כָּלתְּֿהֹמוֺת Ps 135:6; Ps 148:7.
3. primaeval ocean, deep, in Hebrew cosmogony, ׳ פְּנֵי ת Gen 1:2 ( P ; || פְּנֵי הַמַּיִם), Prov 8:27 (|| שָׁמַיִם), Ps 104:6. — (compare, further, GunkSchöpfung u. Chaos 21 ff. OCWhitehouseHast. DB COSMOGONY ZimKAT3. 492 f., 509 f., 585).
4. deep, depth, of river Ezek 31:4 (Nile; || מַיִם, + נַהֲרוֺתֶיהָ), v Ezek 31:15 (|| id.); plural of bursts of water fertilizing Canaan, יוצאים בבקעה ובהר Deut 8:7 ( + עֲיָנֹת, נַחֲלֵי מַיִם). — On Ps 42:8 see 2.
5. abyss (si vera lectio): תְּהוֺמוֺת הָאָרֶץ = Shejôl , Ps 71:20, but Ol We תַּחְתִיּוֺת.
