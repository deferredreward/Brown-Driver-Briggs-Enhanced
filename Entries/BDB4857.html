BDB4856 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4858

[מִשְׁאֶ֑רָת] noun feminine a household vessel, AV(Exodus), RV kneading-trough; vessel in which was dough before it was leavened (Exod 12:34) (hence usually derived from שְׂאֹר leaven (with שׂ — by error? — for שׂ), but this not certain; compare מַשְׂרֵת above); — only suffix מִשְׁאַרְתֶּ֑ךָ Deut 28:5; Deut 28:17 (both + טַנְאֲךָ); plural suffix מִשְׁאֲרוֺתֶיךָ, Exod 7:28 (J; + תַּנּוּרֶיךָ), מִשְׁאֲרֹתָם Exod 12:34 (E).

מְשֻׁבָה see מְשׁוּבָה. מִשְׁבְּצוֺת see שׁבץ

מַשְׂבֵּר, [מִשְׁבָּר] see שׁבר.

[מִשְׁבָּת] see שׁבת. מִשְׁגֶּה see שׁגה.
