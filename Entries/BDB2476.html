Qal Perfect ׳ ז Exod 24:6; Lev 17:6, suffix זְרָקוֺ Exod 9:8, זָָֽרְקָה Hos 7:9 (12 t. Perfect); Imperfect יִזְרֹק Lev 7:2 + 16 t.; Infinitive לִזְרֹק Ezek 43:18 Imperative זְרֹק Ezek 10:2 Participle זֹרֵק Lev 7:14, plural זֹרְקִים 2Chr 30:16
1. toss (in handfuls, see Exod 9:8 or from a bowl, מִזְרָק; opposed to הִזָּה to sprinkle with the finger):
a. cummin Isa 28:25 , dust, followed by עַל 2Chr 34:4 ; Job 2:12, ashes השׁמימה, heavenward, Exod 9:8; Exod 9:10 ( P ), coals of fire על העיר Ezek 10:2 .

c. water for purification, followed by על, Ezek 36:25.
2. intransitive (si vera lectio) Hos 7:9 גַּם שֵׂיבָה זָָֽרְקָה בּוֺ grey hairs are profuse upon him (? read זָָֽרְחָה, as in 2Chr 26:19).
