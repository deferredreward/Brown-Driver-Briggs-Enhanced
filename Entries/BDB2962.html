Biblical Hebrew

[חָנַק] verb strangle (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic חֲנַק, ; Arabic ); —

Niph`al Imperfect וַיֵּחָנַ֑ק 2Sam 17:23 strangled himself (+וַיָּ֫מָת).
Pi`el Participle מְחַנֵּק Nah 2:13 strangle, of lion strangling prey, figurative of Ninevitish king (object not expressed).
