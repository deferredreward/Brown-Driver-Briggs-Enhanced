2. water-plant, reed, 1Kgs 14:15; Isa 19:6 ( + סוּף), Isa 35:7 (+ גֹּמֶא); collective, ׳ חַיַת ק Ps 68:31 beasts of (the) reeds (dwelling among them), compare וּבִצָּה ׳ בְּסֵתֶר ק Job 40:21 הָרָצוּץ ׳ מִשְׁעֶנֶת הַקּ 2Kgs 18:21= Isa 36:6, ׳ ק ׳ מִשׁ Ezek 29:6 , all figurative of weak support; compare רָצוּץ ׳ ק Isa 42:3 (spared by ׳ עבד י).

a. measuring-rod, קְנֵח הַמִּדָּה Ezek 40:3 ; Ezek 40:5 (6 cubits long, see אַמָּה ) Ezek 42:16 (twice in verse); Ezek 42:17; Ezek 42:18; Ezek 42:19.
