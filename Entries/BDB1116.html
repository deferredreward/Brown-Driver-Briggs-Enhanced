[בָּלַג] verb gleam, smile (Arabic ) — only

Hiph`il Imperfect 1 singular with וְ subordinate וְאַבְ֫לִיגָה Job 9:27 + 2 t.; Participle מַבְלִיג Amos 5:9 —
1. shew a smile, look cheerful Ps 39:14 ; Job 9:27 ; Job 10:20.
