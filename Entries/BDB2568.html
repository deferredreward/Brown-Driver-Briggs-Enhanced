BDB2568 [H2310]

BDB2567 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2569

חָדֵל adjective forbearing, lacking, הֶחָדֵל as substantive Ezek 3:27 he that forbeareth (opposed to שׁמע, compare חָדֵל

Qal near the end); construct חֲדַל אִישִׁים Isa 53:3 lacking men (i.e. forsaken by them); — ceasing, transient מֶהחָֿדֵל אָ֑נִי Ps 39:5, but read rather חֶלֶד see Checritical note.
