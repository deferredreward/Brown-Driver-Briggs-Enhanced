אֶשְׁכֹּל proper name

1. masculine an Amorite, brother of Mamre, dwelling in neighbourhood of Hebron Gen 14:13; Gen 14:24.
2. in combination נַחַל אֶשְׁכֹּל Num 13:23; Deut 1:24 אֶשְׁכּוֺל ׳ נ Num 13:24; Num 32:9 — valley of Eshcol, = valley of a cluster, region of Hebron (compare Num 13:22); in Num 13:24 derived from ׳ א cluster, q. v.; see however Di on the passage
