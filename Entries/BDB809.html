BDB808 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB810

יאֹשִׁיָּ֫הוּ, יאֹשִׁיָּה proper name, masculine (׳ י supporteth)

1. יאֹשִׁיָּהוּ king of Judah, son of Amon 1Kgs 13:2; 2Kgs 21:24; 2Kgs 21:26 + 11 t. Kings, + 19 t. Chronicles, + 17 t. Jeremiah + Zeph 1:1 also יאֹו֯שִׁיָּהוּ Jer 27:1.
2. יאֹשִׁיָּה a returned exile Zech 6:10.

אִשָּׁה see below III. אנשׁ.
