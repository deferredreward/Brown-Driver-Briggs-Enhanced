BDB1719 [H1607]

Biblical Hebrew

[גָּעַשׁ] verb shake, quake (Late Hebrew id.) —

Qal Imperfect וַתִּגְעַשׁ Ps 18:8 a = 2Sam 22:8 a Kt ( Qr Hithpa`el); — quake of earth at theoph., Ps 18:8 = 2Sam 22:8 (read Kt ; || רעשׁ).
Pu`al Imperfect be shaken up, convulsed יְגֹעֲשׁוּעַֿם Job 34:20 a people are convulsed.
Hithpa`el shake back and forth, toss or reel to and fro וַיִּתְגָּעַשׁ Qr 2Sam 22:8 a (< Kt Qal); יִתְגָָּֽעֲשׁוּ Jer 46:7 , וַיִּתְגָָּֽעֲשׁוּ Ps 18:8 b + 2 t.; — of mountains Ps 18:8b = 2Sam 22:8 b; of waves of sea, Jer 5:22 of waters of Nile Jer 46:7 (compare Jer 46:8).
Hithpo`el Perfect וְהִתְגֹּעֲשׁוּ consecutive Jer 25:16 Imperfect יִתְגֹּעֲשׁוּ Jer 46:8 id. of waters of Nile Jer 46:8 of drunken men, reel to and fro, Jer 25:16 (figurative of nations confounded by ׳ י).
