אֶלְקָנָה proper name, masculine (God has created, or taken possession )

a. father of Samuel 1 Sam 1-2 (8 t.) 1Chr 6:12; 1Chr 6:19.
b. son of Korah Exod 6:24.
c. a ruler in Jerusalem in the time of Ahaz 2Chr 28:7.
d. one of David's warriors 1Chr 12:6.
e. several Levites (α) 1Chr 6:8; 1Chr 6:10; 1Chr 6:21. (β) v.1Chr 6:11 v.1Chr 6:20. (γ) 1Chr 9:16 (δ ) 1Chr 15:23 .
