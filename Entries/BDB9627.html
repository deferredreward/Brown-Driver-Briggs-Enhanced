כַּשְׂדָּ֑י proper name [of a people] Chaldean (see Biblical Hebrew כַשְׂדִּים ); — absolute ׳ כ Dan 2:10 emphatic כשדיא Dan 5:30 Kt ( Qr כַּשְׂדָּאָה), כסדיא Ezra 5:12 Kt ( Qr כַּסְדָּאָה); plural absolute כַּשְׂדָּאִין Ezra 3:8; Ezra 5:11, emphatic כשדיא Kt , כַּשְׂדָּאֵי Qr , Dan 2:5; Dan 2:10; Dan 4:4; Dan 5:7 —

1. Chaldean by race Dan 3:8; Dan 5:30; Ezra 5:12.
2. as learned, of the class of Magi (Biblical Hebrew ׳ כ 1.c.), Dan 2:5; Dan 2:10 (twice in verse); Dan 4:4; Dan 5:7; Dan 5:11.
