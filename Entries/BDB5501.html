BDB5501 [H4525]

Biblical Hebrew

מַסְגֵּר noun [masculine]

1. locksmith, smith.
2. dungeon:
— always absolute:
1. collective smiths, וְכָלהֶֿחָרָשׁ ׳ וְהַמּ 2Kgs 24:14 compare 2Kgs 24:16, Jer 24:1; Jer 29:2 .
2. dungeon Isa 24:22 (compare סגר Pu`al); figurative of exile Isa 42:7 (׳ הוֺצִיא מִמּ); נַפְשִׁי ׳ הוֺצִיאָה מִמּ Ps 142:8 .
