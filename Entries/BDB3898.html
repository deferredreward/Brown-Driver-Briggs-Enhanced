[כָּבַס]51 verb (tread), wash (Late Hebrew id.; Phoenician כבס as participle; Assyrian kabâsu , tread (down), subjugate, kibšu , step, path, DlHWB) —

Qal Participle only מְסִלַּת שְׂדֵה כוֺבֵס highway of fuller's (= treader's, washer's) field Isa 7:3; Isa 36:2 = 2Kgs 18:17.
Pi`el Perfect וְכִּבֶּס consecutive Lev 13:6 + 11 t.; כִּבֵּס Gen 49:11; 2Sam 19:25 , etc.; Imperfect יְכַבֵּס Lev 11:25 + 16 t.; 2 masculine singular suffix תְּכַבְּסֵנִי Ps 51:9, etc.; Imperative suffix כַּבְּסֵנִי Ps 51:4 feminine singular כַּבְּסִי Jer 4:14 Participle plural מְכַבְּסִים Mal 3:2 —
1. wash garments (i.e. by treading) Exod 19:10; Exod 19:14 ( E ), 2Sam 19:25 figurative of Judah Gen 49:11 elsewhere chiefly P : Lev 6:20; Lev 11:25; Lev 11:28; Lev 13:6; Lev 13:34; Lev 15:11; +; || רָחץ (which = wash person) Lev 14:8; Lev 14:9; Lev 15:5; Lev 15:6; Lev 15:7; Lev 15:8; Lev 15:10 ; Lev 15:11 + (׳ כ in Lev 27 t., || ׳ ר 15 t.; in Num 8 t., || ׳ ר 3 t.); Participle = fuller (compare Qal), only ׳ בֹּרִית מְכ Mal 3:2.
2. wash person, only in poetry and figurative אִם תְּכַבְּסִי בַּנֶּתֶר Jer 2:22 כַּבְּסִי מֵרָעָה לִבֵךְ Jer 4:14 with suffix Ps 51:4 (|| טהר; + מֵעָוֺן); Ps 51:9 (|| חִטֵּא).
Pu`al Perfect וְכֻבַּס consecutive Lev 13:58; Lev 15:17 be washed, of garment.
Hothpa`al (Ges «GKC:54»§ 54, 3) Infinitive אַחֲרֵי הֻכַּבֵּס אֶתהַֿנֶּגַע Lev 13:55 after the plague is washed out, so Lev 13:56.
