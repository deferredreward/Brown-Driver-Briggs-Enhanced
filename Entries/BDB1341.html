בְּרִיעָה

proper name, masculine 1. a son of Asher Gen 46:17 (twice in verse); Num 26:44; Num 26:45; 1Chr 7:30; 1Chr 7:31.
2. son of Ephraim 1Chr 7:23 (where explanation as if from בְּרָעָה).
3. a Benjamite בְּרִעָה 1Chr 8:13, בְּרִיעָה 1Chr 8:16.
4. a Levite 1Chr 23:10; 1Chr 23:11.
