[מָשָׁה] verb draw (Arabic cleanse uterus of camel, Aramaic clean, stroke, the face, etc.; מְשָׁא (Talmud) wash the hands; Zinjirli משׂי[י] perhaps wash oneself, DHMSendsch. 60); —

Qal Perfect suffix מִןהַֿמַּיִם מְשִׁיתִהוּ Exod 2:10 out of the water I drew him (used to explain משֶׁה, but see this below) Hiph`il Imperfect suffix = Qal, figurative יַמְשֵׁנִי מִמַיִם רַבִּים 2Sam 22:17 = Ps 18:7 (subject ׳י).
