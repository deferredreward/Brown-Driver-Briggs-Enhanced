מִשְׁלוֺחַ noun [masculine]

1. outstretching;
2. sending;
— ׳ מ only construct:
1. יָדָם ׳ מ Isa 11:14 Edom and Moab are the outstretching of their hand (that of which they take possession, compare √ Qal 3).
2. מִשְׁלֹחַ מָנוֺת אִישׁ לְרֵעֵהוּ Esth 9:19; Esth 9:22 the sending of portions to each other.
