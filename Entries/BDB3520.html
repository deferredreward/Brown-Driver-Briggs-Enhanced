BDB3519 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3521

[יָחַם] verb be hot, Pi`el conceive (Arabic see inclauerunt pecora, Frey; Aramaic יְחַם be hot, usually of sexual impulse of animals) —

Qal see חמם. Pi`el Perfect 3 feminine singular suffix יֶחֱמַתְנִי Ps 51:7 in sin my mother conceived me (|| חוֺלָ֑לְתִּי); Infinitive construct of heat of cattle in breeding, conception; בְּכָליַֿחֵם Gen 30:41 at every breeding-heat of the flock (J); בְּעֵת יַחֵם Gen 31:10 at the time of the flock's being hot in breeding (E); suffix 3 feminine plural לְיַחֲמֶנָּה Gen 30:41 in order that they might have breeding-heat (J).
