BDB1401 [H1348]

BDB1400 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1402

גֵּאוּת noun feminine majesty, Ps 93:1 + 7 t.; —

1. lifting up גֵּאוּת עָשָׁן column of smoke Isa 9:17 גֵּאוּת הַיָּם swelling of the sea Ps 89:10.
2. majesty of God Ps 93:1; Isa 26:10 גֵּאוּת עָשָׂה he hath done majestically Isa 12:5 עֲטֶרֶת גֵּאוּת crown of majesty Isa 28:1; Isa 28:3 (Samaria, on a round hill majestically commanding the country).
3. pride דִּבְּרוּ בְגֵאוּת they speak proudly Ps 17:10 so for נְאוֺת Ps 74:20 Bi Che.
