BDB2683 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2685

[חֶזְקָה] noun feminine strength, force (strictly Infinitive form from חזק see Ges«GKC:45»§ 45. 1 b) —

1. of urgency of ׳ י's hand in prophetic inspiration בְּחֶזְקַת הַיָד֑ Isa 8:11 (compare חָזַק Ezek 3:14).
2. of royal power כְּחֶזְקָתוֺ 2Chr 12:1; 2Chr 26:16 (|| חָזַק 2Chr 26:15), Dan 11:2.
