BDB7915 [H7387]

Biblical Hebrew

רֵיקָם adverb emptily, vainly; —

1. in empty condition, empty = with empty hands; with verb of sending Gen 31:42 ( E ), Deut 15:13; 1Sam 6:3 ; Job 22:9 of going forth Exod 3:21, returning Ruth 1:21; Ruth 3:17 = without an offering, ׳ לֹא יֵרָאוּ פָנַי ד Exod 23:15 ( E ) = Exod 34:20 ( J ), more fully Deut 16:16 (compare [פָנֶה] II. 2. ראה Niph`al 1.b.).
2. in vain, without effect: with שׁוּב 2Sam 1:22 (poem), Isa 55:11; Jer 14:3 (but see רֵיק 1), Jer 50:9 probably also ׳ צוֺרֲרִי ר Ps 7:5 those who were my adversaries in vain (without success); ׳ הַבּוֺגְדִים ר Ps 25:3 that deal treacherously in vain <in both most without cause ).
