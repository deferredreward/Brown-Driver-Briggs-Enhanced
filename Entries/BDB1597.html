[גֶּ֫זַע] noun masculine Job 14:8 stock, stem (Late Hebrew id., compare Syriac stem, trunk) — construct גֶּזַע Isa 11:1 suffix גִּזְעוֺ Job 14:8, גִּזְעָם Isa 40:24 — stock, stem of a tree Job 14:8 (|| שֹׁרֶשׁ); figurative ישׁי ׳ ג 'stem of Jesse' Isa 11:1 (|| שֹׁרֶשׁ); ׳ שֹׁרֵשׁ בָּאָרֶץ ג Isa 40:24 (|| נטע, זרע).
