I. דְּרוֺר noun [masculine] a flowing; free run, liberty; —

1. מָרדְּֿרוֺר Exod 30:23 ( P ) i.e. myrrh of flowing, fine-flowing myrrh compare מֹר & Di.
2. with קרא proclaim liberty, וּקְרָאתֶם דְּרוֺר בָּאָרֶץ לְכָליֿשְׁבֶ֑יהָ Lev 25:10 ( H ) in Jubilee year, compare Isa 61:1 (|| פְּקַחקֿוֺחַ); Jer 34:8; Jer 34:15; Jer 34:17 all reference to liberty of Sabbatical year; Jer 34:17 (iron.) lo ! I proclaim to you liberty unto the sword, unto pestilence & unto famine , i.e. liberty to be destroyed by sword, pestilence and famine, (a judgment of Yahweh); ׳ שְׁנַתהַֿדּ Ezek 46:17 i.e. year of Jubilee (cf Lev 25:10).
