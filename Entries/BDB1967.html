BDB1967 [H1827]

BDB1966 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1968

דְּמָמָה noun feminine (silence) whisper (on formation compare BaNB 87) — וְאַחַר הָאֵשׁ קוֺל דְּמָמָה דַקָּ֑ה 1Kgs 19:12 דְּמָמָה זָקוֺל אֶשְׁמָ֑ע Job 4:16 a whisper and a voice, i.e. an articulate whisper (compare Di); יָקֵם סְעָרָה לִדְמָמָ֑ה Ps 107:29 he settleth storm into whisper.
