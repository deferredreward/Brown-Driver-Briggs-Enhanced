BDB8822 [H8088]

BDB8821 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8823

שֵׁ֫מַע noun [masculine] hearing, report (on from compare LagBN 143); — ׳ שׁ absolute Isa 23:5 construct vIsa 23:5 +; suffix שִׁמְעִי Isa 66:19 etc.; — report, usually with genitive object: accusative with שְׁמַע Gen 29:13 ( J ) the tidings about Jacob, Isa 23:5 + (with suffix) Num 14:15 ( J E ), Deut 2:25 ; 1Kgs 10:1 = 2Chr 9:1; Nah 3:19; Hab 3:2; Jer 37:5; Jer 50:43; Isa 66:9 with ל genitive Isa 23:5 לַעֲדָתָם Hos 7:12 usually according to the report (heard by) their congregation (!), but inexplicable and probably corrupt, We Now; Marti (compare Oettli) עַלרָֿעָתָם (כְּשֵׁמַע as gloss) according to their wickedness ; שָׁוְא ׳ נָשָׂא שׁ Exod 23:1 ( E ) take up (utter) a false report; אֹוָן ׳ לְשׁ Ps 18:45 i.e. as soon as heard of, = mere report Job 42:5 (opposed to personal knowledge), compare שִׁמְעָהּ Job 28:22.
