Biblical Hebrew

[חָשַׁל] verb shatter (Biblical Aramaic חֲשַׁל shatter; Late Hebrew Pi`el, || משׁבר; Syriac forge a metal, Arabic drive cattle violently; Assyrian —ašâlu , shatter, destroy ZimBP 12 DlPr 42) —

Niph`al Participle figurative כָּלֿ הַנֶּחֱשָׁלִים Deut 25:18 all the shattered ones, i.e. those broken down, worn out, followed by וְאַתָּה עָיֵף וְיָגֵעַ; others think = חלשׁ.
