[מִדְרָשׁ] noun [masculine] study, exposition, midrash, only construct מִדְרַשׁ (late; common in Late Hebrew, in sense of imaginative exposition or didactic story) — מִדְרַשׁ סֵפֶר הַמְּלָכִים midrash of the book of Kings 2Chr 24:27 מִדְרַשׁ הַנָּבִיא עִדּוֺ midrash of the prophet Iddo 2Chr 13:22. These were probably of a didactic character, compare DrIntr 497.
