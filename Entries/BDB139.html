BDB138 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB140

אַדֶּ֫רֶת noun feminine glory, cloak — ׳ א absolute Josh 7:24 אַדָּ֑רֶת Ezek 17:8 construct Gen 25:25 + 4 t.; אַדַּרְתּוֺ 1Kgs 19:13 + 3 t.; אַדַּרתָּם Zech 11:3 —

1. glory, magnificence, of vine Ezek 17:8 (so Thes MV, but < adjective f. from אַדִּיר q. v.), of shepherds Zech 11:3 (or sub 2).
2. mantle, cloak (wide garment) of hair שֵׂעָר Gen 25:25; Zech 13:4 (as prophetic mantle, so perhaps Zech 11:3 of shepherds = false prophets) compare of Elijah 1Kgs 19:13 ; 1Kgs 19:19 ; 2Kgs 2:8; 2Kgs 2:13; 2Kgs 2:14 but אַדֶּרֶת שִׁנְעָר טוֺבָה (fine mantle of Shinar = Babylonian mantle — doubtless costly) Josh 7:21; Josh 7:24 ( J ) & (late) ׳ א alone Jonah 3:6.
