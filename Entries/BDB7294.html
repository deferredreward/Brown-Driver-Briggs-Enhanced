1. grave> Gen 35:20 (twice in verse); Gen 47:30; Deut 34:6; 1Sam 10:2; 2Kgs 9:28; 2Kgs 21:26; 2Kgs 23:30; Ezek 32:23; Ezek 32:24 ׳שְׂדֵה הַקּ 2Chr 26:23,
2. burial, Isa 14:20; Eccl 6:3 קְבוּרַת חֲמוֺר Jer 22:19.
