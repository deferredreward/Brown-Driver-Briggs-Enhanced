Biblical Hebrew

מַחְתֶּ֫רֶת noun masculine breaking in, burglary — יִמָּצֵא הַגַּנָּב ׳ אִםבַּֿמּ Exod 22:1 i.e. caught in the act ( J E ); so מְצָאתִים ׳ לֹא בַּמּ Jer 2:34, not in the act of breaking in didst thou find them (2 feminine singular suffix Ges«GKC:44»§ 44, 2, R 4), i.e. those whom thou hast slain were not detected in crime.
