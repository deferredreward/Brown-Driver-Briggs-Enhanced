אוֺר121 noun masculine Gen 1:3 (feminine Job 36:32; Jer 13:16 ) light (Assyrian urru = ûru Dlw). — absolute Gen 1:3 +; construct Judg 16:2 +; suffix אוֺרִי Ps 27:1, etc.; plural אוֺרִים Ps 136:7 —

1. light as diffused in nature, light of day Gen 1:3; Gen 1:4; Gen 1:5 ( P ) Job 3:9 ; Job 38:19 +.
2. morning light, dawn, אוֺר הַבֹּקֶר light of the morning Judg 16:2; 1Sam 14:36; 1Sam 25:34; 1Sam 25:36; 2Sam 17:22 ; 2Kgs 7:9; Mic 2:1 בֹּקֶר ׳ א 2Sam 23:4 (poem of David); ׳ עַדהָֿא Judg 19:26 (compare עַדהַֿבֹּקֶר Judg 19:25); לָאוֺר Job 24:14 מִןהָֿאוֺר עַד מַחֲצִית הַיּוֺם from dawn till mid-day, Neh 8:3 compare Prov 4:18.
3. light of the heavenly luminaries; הַלְּבָנָה ׳ א, || הַחַמָּה ׳ א moonlight & sunlight Isa 30:26 כּוֺכְבֵי אוֺר stars of light Ps 148:3 מְאוֺרֵי אוֺר luminaries of light Ezek 32:8 אוֺרִים = מְאוֺרִים Ps 136:7 so עֲלֵיאֿוֺר in sunshine Isa 18:4 the sun itself Job 31:26.
4. daylight אוֺר רְשָׁעִים light of the wicked Job 38:15 (their work-day being the night); יוֺם אוֺר a day of light Amos 8:9 (= a clear, sunshiny day).
5. lightning Job 36:32; Job 37:3; Job 37:11; Job 37:15 compare Hab 3:11 .
6. light of lamp Prov 13:9; Jer 25:10 of crocodile's hot breath Job 41:10.
7. light of life חַיִּים ׳ א Job 33:30; Ps 56:14 compare אוֺר Job 3:16; Job 3:20.
8. light of prosperity Job 22:28; Job 30:26; Ps 97:11; Lam 3:2.
9. light of instruction נֵר מִצְוָה וְתוֺרָה אוֺר Prov 6:23 the commandment is a lamp and instruction a light; so the Messian. servant is אוֺר גּוֺיִם Isa 42:6; Isa 49:6 compare עַמִּים ׳ א Isa 51:4 the advent of Messiah is shining of great light Isa 9:1 (twice in verse).
10. light of face אוֺר פָּנִים = bright, cheerful face (of men) Job 29:24 betokening king's favour Prov 16:15 (compare עֵינַיִם ׳ א Ps 38:11); of God = his shining, enlightening, favouring face Ps 4:7; Ps 44:4; Ps 89:16.
11. Yahweh is יִשְׂרָאֵל ׳ א Isa 10:17, as source enlightenment & prosperity; light & salvation Ps 27:1 light to guide Mic 7:8 compare Ps 43:3 everlasting light of Zion, instead of sun & moon Isa 60:19 ; Isa 60:20 house of Jacob is to walk in his light Isa 2:5.
