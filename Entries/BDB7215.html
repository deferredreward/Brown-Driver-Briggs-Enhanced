Biblical Hebrew

צְפִירָה noun feminine plait, chaplet; — absolute ׳ צ Ezek 7:7, צְפִרָה Ezek 7:10 construct צְפִירַת Isa 28:5 — chaplet, coronet, diadem, תִּפְצָרָה ׳ צ Isa 28:5 (figurative of ׳ י; || עֲטֶרֶת צְבִי) — ׳ צ Ezek 7:7; Ezek 7:10 is dubious; AV morning (as Ki, from Aramaic) is unsuitable; most (after AW ) doom, fate (as coming round upon one, compare RV Da Toy), but see DrHast. ii. 176 n; diadem is possible Ezek 7:10 (|| הַמַּטֶה; of royal house of Judah Co Berthol; or of Jerusalem Krae ), but not Ezek 7:7 conjectures in Co Berthol Krae.
