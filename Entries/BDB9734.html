BDB9734 [H5481]

Biblical Aramaic

סוּמְפֹּנְיָה noun feminine, bag-pipe, or double pipe, or Pan's pipe (see GFMJBL, 1905, 166 ff.) (Late Hebrew LevyNHWB iii. 492; Late Hebrew Aramaic סִימְלאספּוֺן is tube, especially vein, artery Idib. 513; loan-word from (late) Greek συμφωνία, Kraussii. 376, 390 Bev41 Dr Dan 3:5 NesMM 37 (hence also later Latin symphonia , Ital. zampogna), PrinceKB 9230); — absolute ׳ ס Dan 3:5; Dan 3:15, = סיפניה Kt , סוּפֹּנְיָה Qr Dan 3:10 (Syriac id.; compare Palmyrene ספון = σύμφωνος, in agreement, Lzb330 GACookeInscr. 338).
