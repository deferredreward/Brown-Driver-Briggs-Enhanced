BDB4733 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4735

[מָסָה] verb melt, dissolve, be liquefied (Ethiopic liquefy; Aramaic מְסָא melt away, dissolve, decay, coagulate; Assyrian masû is wash; see also מסס); — only Hiph`il Perfect 3 plural הִמְסִיו Josh 14:8 (on form see Ges«GKC:75»§ 75 R.17). Imperfect sf . יַּעמסֵם Ps 147:18 2 masculine singular וַתֶּ֫מֶס Ps 39:12 אַמְסֶה Ps 6:7 — cause to dissolve, melt, ice Ps 147:18 hyperb. בְּדִמְעָתִי עַרְשִׁי אַמְּסֶה Ps 6:7 with my tears I cause my bed to dissolve; וַתֶּמֶס כָּעָשׁ חֲמוּדוֺ Ps 39:12 = consume, cause to vanish; figurative followed by לֵב = intimidate Josh 14:8.

I. [מַסָּה] see מסס. II, III. מַסָּה see נסה.

[מִסָּה] see מִסַּת. מַסְוֶה see סוה

מְסוּכָה = מְשׂוּכָה see II. סוך (שׂוך).
