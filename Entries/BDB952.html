II. בּוּז noun masculine Prov 18:3 contempt — ׳ בּ absolute Gen 38:23 + 9 t.; construct Job 31:34 — (Wisdom Literature & poetry, except Gen 38:23 J ).

1. contempt springing from pride and wickedness Job 31:34; Ps 123:3; Ps 123:4 (|| לַעַג ); joined in one adverb. phrase with גאוה Ps 31:19 ׳ וָב ׳ בְּג; springing from prosperity and ease Job 12:5 = object of contempt Gen 38:23; Prov 12:8 || חֶרְפָּה Ps 119:22 ; Prov 18:3 .
2. judicial, poured out (שׁפך) by God, in judgment Job 12:21 = Ps 107:40.
