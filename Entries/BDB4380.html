BDB4380 [H3930]

BDB4379 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4381

[לֹעַ] noun [masculine] probably throat (external) (compare Late Hebrew לוֺעַ jaw, cheek, Aramaic לוֺעָא, id.); — וְשַׂמְתָּ֫ שַׂכִּין בְּלֹעֶ֑ךָ Prov 23:2 and thou shalt put a knife to thy throat (figurative for restraining oneself from indulgence in food).
