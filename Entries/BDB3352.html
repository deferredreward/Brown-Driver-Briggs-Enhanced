BDB3352 [H4301]

Biblical Hebrew

מַטְמוֺן noun masculine hidden treasure, treasure; — absolute מַטְמוֺן Gen 43:23 plural מַטְמֹנִים Jer 41:8; Prov 2:4 מַטְמוֺנִים Job 3:21 construct מַטְמֻנֵי Isa 45:3 (hidden) treasure Gen 43:23 (of money in sacks); מַטְמֻנֵי מִסְתָּרים Isa 45:3 (i.e. treasures now hidden in secret places, shall become spoil of Cyrus; || אוצרות חשׁך), Jer 41:8, appositive חִפִּים שְׁעֹרִים שֶׁמֶן דבשׁ; in comparative Job 3:21 (longing for death more than for treasure), Prov 2:4 (wisdom sought for like treasure).
