I. [יָעַל] verb only Hiph`il profit, avail, benefit; —

Hiph`il Perfect הוֺעִיל Hab 2:18 Imperfect יוֺעִיל Jer 2:11 + 2 t.; אֹעִיל Job 35:3 יוֺעִילוּ 1Sam 12:21 + 6 t.; יוֺעִ֫לוּ Jer 2:8; Jer 12:13 יוֺעִילוּךְ Isa 57:12 נוֺעִיל Job 21:15 Infinitive absolute הוֺעֵיל Jer 23:22 construct הוֺעִיל Jer 7:8 + 4 t.; Participle מוֺעִיל Jer 16:19 — profit, avail, benefit, always (except Job 30:13 where in bad sense, Isa 47:12) with negative, or in question implying negative; especially of idols or false gods (as unprofitable ), so Hab 2:18; Isa 44:9; Isa 44:10; Isa 57:12; Jer 2:8 אחרי לא יועילו הלבו, Jer 2:11 המיר כבידו בלוא יועיל, Jer 16:19 ואין בם מועיל, 1Sam 12:21, of vain confidences Jer 7:8, or promises Jer 23:32 (twice in verse) (followed by לְ); of Egypt as ally Isa 30:5 (followed by לְ), Isa 30:5 Isa 30:6 of wickedness Prov 10:2 wealth Prov 11:4 worthless men Job 30:13 (לְהַוָּתִי יועִילוּ i.e. promote it); of words Job 15:3 (|| דָּבָר לֹא יִסְכּוֺן); in general gain profit Isa 47:12; Isa 48:17; Jer 12:13; Job 21:15; Job 35:3.
