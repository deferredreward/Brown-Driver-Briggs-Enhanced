II. [חשׁק] verb denominative only Pi`el furnish with fillets or rings, and Pu`al passive;

Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine singular חִשַּׁק Exod 38:28 ( P ), subject Bezaleel.
Pu`al Participle מְחֻשָּׁקִים כֶּסֶף Exod 27:17; Exod 38:17 finished with silver fillets, in agreement with הָעַמּוּדִים (both P ).
