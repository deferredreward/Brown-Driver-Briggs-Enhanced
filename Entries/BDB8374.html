BDB8373 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8375

שְׁאַלְתִּיאֵל proper name, masculine (I have asked (him) of God; on accusative compare (late) Ps 137:3); — first son of Jehoiachin and uncle of Zerubbabel according to 1Chr 3:17 (compare v1Chr 3:18 v1Chr 3:19); but Zerubbabel's father Hag 1:1; Hag 2:23; Ezek 3:2; Ezek 3:8; Neh 12:1 (attempts at explaining in Be Kit Benz). — Σαλαθιηλ.
