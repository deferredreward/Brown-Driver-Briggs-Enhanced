I. בָּטַח verb trust (compare Arabic to throw one down upon his face Thes, lie extended on the ground, 'se reposer sur quelqu' un' Fl MV) —

Qal Perfect בָּטַח Ps 28:7 + 35 t.; Imperfect יִבְּטַח Job 40:23 + 27 t.; Imperative בְּטַח Ps 37:3 + 8 t.; Infinitive absolute בָּטוֺחַ Isa 59:4, construct בְּטֹחַ Ps 118:8 + 2 t.; Participle בֹּטֵחַ Ps 21:8 + 35 t.; passive בָּטוּחַ Ps 112:7; Isa 26:3.
