Biblical Hebrew

גָּלְיַת proper name, masculine (conspicuous ? On ending ת compare Dr 1Sam 17:4 & Nö in EutNab 73) ( גָּלְיָת 1Chr 20:5) Philistine giant slain by David 1Sam 17:4; 1Sam 17:23; 1Sam 21:10; 1Sam 22:10, but according to 2Sam 21:19 slain by Elhanan of Bethlehem (בֵּית הַלַּחְמִי; acc to 1Chr 20:5 Elhanan slew לַחְמִי brother of Goliath).
