BDB3058 [H2681]

BDB3057 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3059

I. חָצִיר noun [masculine] settled abode or haunt (|| form of חָצֵר above, or text error for it); — נְוֵה תַנִּים חָצִיר לִבְנוֺת יַעֲנָה Isa 34:13 a dwelling of jackals (and) a hunt for ostriches; also בִּנְוֵת תַנִּים רִבְצָהּ חָצִיר לְקָנֶה וָגֹ֑מֶא Isa 35:7 an abode, home, of reeds and rushes (so Che Di Du and others; others put this under II. חָצִיר); Che Du think the see mutilated, and would emend this line according to Isa 34:13.
