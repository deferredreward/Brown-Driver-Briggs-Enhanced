BDB6912 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6914

I. צְבִי noun masculine 2Sam 1:19 beauty, honour; — absolute ׳ צ 2Sam 1:19 +, צֶבִ֑י Dan 8:9 construct צְבִי Isa 13:19 +; plural construct צִבְאוֺת Jer 3:19 (so ᵑ7 Ki Thes Hi Gf Ol § 145 b Köii.l, 584 and others; > from צבא ᵑ6 Gie and others); —

1.
a. beauty, decoration, עֶדְיוֺ ׳ צ, of silver and gold Ezek 7:20, of products of soil Isa 4:2 (predict.)
b. elsewhere in figure: of drunkard's chaplet, תִפְאַרְתּוֺ ׳ צ Isa 28:1; Isa 28:4 (figurative of Samaria); ׳ עֲטֶרֶת צ Isa 28:5 (of ׳ י); ׳ הַצּ 2Sam 1:19 (Saul and Jonathan); ׳ גְּאוֺן כָּלצֿ, of Tyre Isa 23:9 מַמְלָכוֺת ׳ צ Isa 13:19 (of Babylonian ); of land of Israel (Judah), צְבִי הִיא לְכָלהָֿאֲרָצוֺת Ezek 20:6; Ezek 20:15 , גּוֺיִם נַחֲלַת צְבִי צִבְאוֺת Jer 3:19 (see above) heritage of the beauty of beauties of the nations, i.e. most beauteous heritage; ׳ הַצּ especially of Jerusalem and temple Dan 8:9 (Bev), compare ׳ אֶרֶץ הַצּ Dan 11:16; Dan 11:41 (see Dr), קֹֿרֶשׁ ׳ הַר צ Dan 11:45(i.e. temple-hill); of cities of Moab, אֶרֶץ ׳ צ Ezek 25:9.
2. = honour, דִּיק ׳ לַצּ ׳ צ Isa 24:16. — Ezek 26:20 read בִי ׳ וְתִתְיַצְּ for וְנָתַתִּי צְבִי, so Lxx Co Berthol Toy Krae and others
