שָׂחַק verb laugh (|| צהק, q. v.; Late Hebrew שָׂחַק, סָחַק; Ethiopic ); —

QalPerfect 3 masculine singular ׳וְשׂ consecutive Prov 29:9 3 plural שָֽׂחֲקוּ Lam 1:7; Job 30:1 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִשְׂחַק Job 39:7 +, etc.; Infinitive construct שְׂחוֺק Judg 16:24; Eccl 3:4 —
l. a. laugh at, usually in contempt, derision, with עַל person Job 30:1; Ps 52:8 עַל of thing Lam 1:7 with ל person God subject Ps 37:13; Ps 59:9 (|| לָעַג); with ל of thing or person= have no fear of, Hab 1:10; Job 5:22; Prov 31:25 also, figurative, of noble animals Job 39:7; Job 39:18; Job 39:22; Job 41:21 absolute, Prov 29:9 (opposed to רָגַז), of ׳י Ps 2:4 (|| לָעֵג), of wisdom Prov 1:26 (ב temporal; || id.).


\ \ b. with אֶל person, apparently of friendly laugh Job 29:24 in general Eccl 3:4 (opposed to בָּכָה).

2. sport, play, Judg 16:27. Pi`el Perfect 1 singular וְשִׂחַקְתִּ֫י 1Sam 6:21 Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיְשַׂחֶקֿ Judg 16:25, etc.; Infinitive construct לְשֵׂחֶקֿ Ps 104:26 Participle מְשַׂחֵק Prov 26:19; 1Chr 15:29, feminine מְשֵׂחֶ֫קֶת Prov 8:30; Prov 8:31 plural מְשַׂחֲקִים 2Sam 6:5 +, מְשַׂחֲקוֺת 1Sam 18:7 —
1. make sport, Judg 16:25 (ל person) 2Sam 2:14 (לִפְנֵי person; in tournament), Prov 8:30 (לִפְנֵי), vProv 8:31 ב loc).
2. jest, Prov 26:19.
3. play: including instrumental music, singing and dancing 1Sam 18:7, c ׳לִפְנֵי י. 2Sam 6:5 (ב instrumental), = 1Chr 13:8; 2Sam 6:21; 1Chr 15:29 ( + מְרַקֵּד); of merry-making Jer 15:17 (|| עָלַז), Jer 30:19; Jer 31:4 of children's sport Zech 8:5 of beasts Job 40:20; Ps 104:25 of man, play with (ב) crocod, Job 40:29. Hiph`il (declar.) Participle. plural מַשְׂחִיקִים 2Chr 30:10 they uttered mockery עֲלֵיהֶם (|| מַלְעִנִים).
