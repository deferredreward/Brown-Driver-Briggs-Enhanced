Biblical Hebrew

[נָאַם] verb denominative utter a prophecy, speak as prophet; —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine plural וַיִּנְאֲמוּ נְאֻם Jer 23:31 and they uttered (in the prophetic manner), it as an utterance (of ׳ י); they used the prophetic formula (compare ׳ אֹמְרִים נְאֻם י Ezek 13:6; Ezek 13:7).
