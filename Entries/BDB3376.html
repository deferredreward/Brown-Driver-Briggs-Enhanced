BDB3376 [H2961]

Biblical Hebrew

[טָרִי] adjective fresh, only feminine singular טְרִיָּה fresh, לְחִיחֲֿמוֺר טְרִיָּה Judg 15:15 the fresh jawbone of an ass, i.e. not yet dry and brittle; ׳ מַכָּה ט Isa 1:6 fresh, raw wound, not yield healed or even treated (so Ges Ew Brd Di Du; festering De Che).
