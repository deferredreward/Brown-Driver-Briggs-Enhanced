BDB6713 [H6468]

Biblical Hebrew

[פְּעֻלָּה] noun feminine work, recompence (in poetry and late); — construct פְּעֻלַּת Lev 19:13 +, suffix פְּעֻלָּתִי Isa 49:4, etc.; plural construct פְּעֻלּוֺת Ps 17:4, תֹ - Ps 28:5 —

1. work:
a. plural deeds of ׳ י Ps 28:5.
b. of men, Ps 17:4 (plural); toil, suffering Jer 31:16 good action 2Chr 15:7, wicked Isa 65:7, שָׁ֑קֶר ׳ פ Prov 11:18 .
2. wages (as earned by work) Lev 19:13; Prov 10:16 (|| תְּבוּאָה), Ezek 29:20 reward (from ׳ י) Isa 49:4; Isa 61:8 probably figurative of people won back by ׳ י in warfare Isa 40:10; Isa 62:11 (|| שָׂכָר); of punishment, Ps 109:20 .
