מִשְׁפָּחָה: 303 noun feminine clan (RSSemitic 1. 258, 2nd ed. 276; K 149, 2nd ed. 175 BaNB § 161 a); — absolute ׳ מ Amos 3:1 +; construct מִשְׁפַּ֫חַת Judg 9:1 +; suffix מִשְׁפַּחְתִּי Gen 24:38 +, etc.; plural מִשְׁפָּחוֺת Nah 3:4 +; construct מִשְׁפְּחוֺת Amos 3:2 +; suffix מִשְׁפְּחֹתֵיהֶם Gen 8:19 + 2 t., usually תָם- Num 1:18 + 84 t., etc.; —

1. clan:
a. family connection of individual Gen 24:38 (+ בֵּיתאָֿבִי), Gen 24:40 ( + id.), Gen 24:41 (all J ; = מוֺלַדְתִּי Gen 24:4), 2Sam 14:7, united in sacrifice 1Sam 20:6; 1Sam 20:29, in passover Exod 12:21 ( J E ; compare BenzEncy.Bib. PASSOVER, §§ 9, 12); compare Lev 20:5 ( H ), Lev 25:10; Lev 25:41 ( P ), Lev 25:49 ( P ; defined as מִשְּׁאֵר בְּשָׂרוֺ), non-Hebrew Lev 25:47 ( H ), Lev 25:45( P ), Judg 1:25 מִשְׁפְּחוֺתֶיהָ Josh 6:23 ( E or R ) is unintelligible, probably strike out (compare Steuern Holz; בֵּית אָבִיהָ in Josh 6:25 [ J ]), compare Num 27:4; Num 27:11 ( P ); בֵּית אֲבִי אִמּוֺ ׳ מ Judg 9:1 clan of his mother's father-house; including individuals, and included by שֵׁבֶט 1Sam 9:21 (twice in verse); 1Sam 10:21 with specific name, in series גֶבֶר, בַּיִת , ׳ מ, שֵׁבֶט Josh 7:14 (twice in verse); Josh 7:17 b, c (all J ; Josh 7:17 a read plural Di Benn, or שֵׁבֶט GFM Judg 13:2 ; Steuern allows either); compare Deut 29:17; Judg 21:24, ׳ אָבִי בְּיִשׂ ׳ מ 1Sam 1:18 (+ חַיַּי, see II [חַי]; בֵּיתשָֿׁאוּל ׳ אִישׁמִ֗֗֗מּ 2Sam 16:5 with name also Ruth 2:1 ; Ruth 2:3 and (non-Israel) Job 32:2.
b. in loose, popular sense= tribe, Judg 13:2; Judg 17:7; Judg 18:19, compare Judg 18:2 (|| שֵׁבֶט Judg 18:1), v Judg 18:11 (yet in these two read possibly plural, see GFM).
c. techn. divisions of people of Israel Num 11:10 ( J ) Jer 2:4 ; Jer 31:1 compare Jer 3:14 ; Num 1:2; Num 1:18; Num 2:34; Num 33:54 (all P ), so (post-exilic) Neh 4:7: Esth 9:28 (מְדִינָה, ׳ מ, דּוֺר).
d. usually ( P ) technical divisions of tribes of Israel, Exod 6:14; Exod 6:15; Exod 6:25; Num 3:15 + 112 t. Numbers (93 t. Num 26, including Num 3:43 where הַשּׁוּחָמִי apparently = Dan), 34 t. Josh 13-21; also 1Chr 6:4; 1Chr 6:45; 1Chr 7:5; division of Gershom, etc. (in Levi) Num 3:18; Num 3:19; Num 3:20 + 41 t. Num 3 + Num 4:18 compare (in other tribes) 1Chr 2:53; 1Chr 4:2; 1Chr 4:8; 1Chr 4:21; 1Chr 4:27; 1Chr 4:38; 1Chr 5:7 also Zech 12:12 b. c 13 b 14 a, and (distributive) Zech 12:12 a 14 b, pleonastic מַטֵּה אֲבִיהֶם ׳ מ Num 36:6 compare Num 36:8, and ( ׳ א ׳ מַטֵּה מ) Num 36:12.
e. late, divinity of other peoples 1Chr 16:28 = Ps 96:7; Ps 22:28 in Edom Gen 36:40 ( P ).
f. in wider sense=people, nation; כֹּל מִשְׁפְּחֹת תָאֲדָמָה Amos 3:2; Gen 12:3; Gen 28:14 (both J ), compare Jer 10:25; Ezek 20:32; Nah 3:4 (all || גּוֺיִם), Zech 14:17; Jer 1:15; Jer 25:9 as one's posterity Gen 10:30; Gen 10:31; Gen 10:32 (all P ), so probably Gen 10:5 ( P ; Di Dr); = all Israel Amos 3:1, Judah Mic 2:3; Jer 8:3 (הָרָעָה הַזּאֹת ׳ הַמּ), Israel + Judah, ׳ שְׁתֵּי הַמּ Jer 33:24 מִשְׁפַּחַת מִצְרַיִם Zech 14:18 compare Ps 107:41.
2. = guild 1Chr 2:55 (of scribes; originally hereditary in families).
3. = species, kind:
a. of judgments Jer 15:3.
b. animals Gen 8:19 ( P ).
4. plural = aristocrats Job 31:34 (compare English 'the classes').
