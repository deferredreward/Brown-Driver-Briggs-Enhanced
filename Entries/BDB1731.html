גָּרֵב

1. proper name, masculine (compare Palmyrene proper name, masculine גריבא Vog141) one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:38 = 1Chr 11:40.
2. proper name [of a location] (compare Sabean proper name, of a location גרבם HalNo. 379, 382) only in גִּבְעַת גָּרֵב Jer 31:39 hill of Gareb, apparently close to Jerusalem on the southwest compare Gf; see also Hi Che.

גַּרְגַּר, גַּרְגְּרוֺת see below גרר.
