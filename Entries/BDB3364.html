BDB3363 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3365

טֶ֫פַח noun [masculine]

1. a span, hand-breadth;
2. coping (?);
—
1. a span, hand-breadth, טֶ֫פַח 1Kgs 7:26 = 2Chr 4:5 of thickness of the molten sea; plural טְפָחוֺת Ps 39:6 (a few) hand-breadths are my days.
2. architectural term, perhaps coping, וּמִמַּסַּד עַדהַֿטְּפָחוֺת 1Kgs 7:9.
