אֲבִיגַ֫יִל proper name, feminine (my father is joy (?) originally אבגל? ( אֲבִגֵל? compare ᵑ0 below & NöZMG 1883, 537 Anm2).

1. wife of Nabal, then of David 1Sam 25:14; 1Sam 25:23; 1Sam 25:39; 1Sam 25:40; 1Sam 25:42; 1Sam 27:3; 1Sam 30:5; 2Sam 2:2; 1Chr 3:1 = אֲבִגַיִל 1Sam 25:3; 1Sam 25:36 אֲבִו֯גַיִל 1Sam 25:18, אֲבִיגָל֑ 1Sam 25:32, אֲבִיגַ֯לִ 2Sam 3:3.
2. sister of David 1Chr 2:16; 1Chr 2:17 = אֲבִיגַל 2Sam 17:25.
