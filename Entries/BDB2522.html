חַבּוּרָה, [חַבֻּרָה, חֲבֻרָה] noun feminine stripe, blow; — absolute ׳ הַבּ Isa 1:6 + 2 t.; suffix חַבֻּרָתִי Gen 4:23 חֲבֻרָתוֺ Isa 53:5 plural construct חַבֻּרוֺת Prov 20:30 suffix חַבּוּרֹתָ֑י Ps 38:6: — stripe, blow, stroke, Gen 4:23 my blow, i.e. for striking me ( J ), compare Exod 21:25 (twice in verse) ( J E ), also of injury to land of Judah (under figure of human body) Isa 1:6 (all || פֶּצַע, see below); of blows (singular collective) inflicted on suffering servant of ׳ י Isa 53:5 (plural) Ps 38:6, חַבֻּרוֺת פֶּצַע Prov 20:30, i.e. blows that cut in (|| מַכּוֺת).
