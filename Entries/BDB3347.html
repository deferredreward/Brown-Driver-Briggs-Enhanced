II. טָמֵא adjective unclean; — ׳ט Lev 5:2 + 58 t.; construct טְמֵא Lev 22:4 + 3 t.; feminine טְמֵאָה Lev 5:2 + 11 t.; construct טְמֵאַת Ezek 22:5; Ezek 22:10 plural טְמֵאִים Lev 11:8 + 10 t.; — unclean,

1. ethically and religiously שׂפתים ׳ט Isa 6:5 (twice in verse) unclean of lips; השׁם ׳ט Ezek 22:5 defiled of name, infamous; ׳טהור מִטּ Job 14:4.
2. ritually:
\ \ a. of persons, || טהור Deut 12:15; Deut 12:22; Deut 15:22; Eccl 9:2 נפשׁ ׳ט Lev 22:4 (H); (אדם) לנפשׁ ׳ט unclean for a (dead) person Num 5:2; Num 9:6; Num 9:7; Num 9:10 (P) = נפשׁ ׳ט Hag 2:13 elsewhere for various reasons Deut 26:14; Lev 5:2; Lev 13:11; Lev 13:16; Lev 13:44; Lev 13:45 (twice in verse); Lev 13:46; Lev 15:2; Lev 15:25; Lev 15:33; Num 19:13; Num 19:17; Num 19:19; Num 19:20; Num 19:22 (all P), 2Chr 23:19; Isa 64:5; Ezek 4:13; Ezek 22:10; Lam 4:15.
\ \ b. of animals Lev 5:2 (4 t. in verse); Lev 7:21 (twice in verse); Lev 11:4; Lev 11:5; Lev 11:6; Lev 11:7; Lev 11:8; Lev 11:26; Lev 11:27; Lev 11:28; Lev 11:29; Lev 11:31; Lev 27:11; Lev 27:27; Num 18:15 (all P), Lev 20:25 (twice in verse) (H), Deut 14:7; Deut 14:8; Deut 14:10; Deut 14:19.
\ \ c. of things in gen. Lev 11:35 (twice in verse); Lev 11:38; Lev 15:26; Num 19:15 (all P), Isa 52:11 food Judg 13:4; Hos 9:3 houses Jer 19:13 leprosy Lev 13:15; Lev 13:51; Lev 13:55; Lev 14:44; Lev 14:57 (P); offering Hag 2:14.
\ \ d. persons and things in general כל טמא Lev 7:19; Lev 7:21 (P); ובין הטהור ׳הבדיל בין הט Lev 10:10; Lev 11:47 לטהור ׳הודיע בין (ה)ט Ezek 22:26; Ezek 44:23 of aliens Isa 52:1 (|| עָרֵל), perhaps also Isa 35:8.
3. specifically of places: ׳מקום ט unclean place (place of refuse away from holy place and human habitation) Lev 14:40; Lev 14:41; Lev 14:45 (P); טמאה ארץ land on the east of the Jordan separated from the land of the tabernacle of ׳י Josh 22:19 (P); so ׳אדמה ט a foreign land Amos 7:17.
