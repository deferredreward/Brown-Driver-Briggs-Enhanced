BDB2038 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2040

הֶאָח interjection (onomatopoetic) expressing joy, Aha! always introduced by אָמַר , Isa 44:16 he says, Aha, I am warm, I have seen the fire. Of satisfaction over the misfortune of an enemy or rival, Ps 35:21; Ps 35:25 let them not say in their heart הֶאָח נַפְשֵׁנוּ Aha, so would we have it ! (lit our desire !) Ps 40:16 (= Ps 70:4), Ezek 25:3; Ezek 26:2; Ezek 36:2. Metaph., of the neighing of a war horse in the battle, Job 39:25.
