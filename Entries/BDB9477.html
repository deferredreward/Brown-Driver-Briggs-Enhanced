הֵן conjunction if, whether (Biblical Hebrew הֵן , q. v.: Zenjirli, Egyptian Aramaic (S-CPap. A 7 +), Palmyrene, Nabataean הן, Mandean הין if [ ᵑ7 Onk Jon use אם, ᵑ7 Jer אין, Syriac ]); —

1. if, Dan 2:6; Dan 3:15; Dan 3:17; Dan 4:24; Dan 5:16; Dan 5:17, הֵן לָא ֗֗֗ Dan 2:5; Dan 2:9; Dan 3:15 Dan 3:18 הֵן לָ֔א if not.
2. repeated, הֵן ֗֗֗ הֵן sive … sive, whether … or (compare Biblical Hebrew אִם, 1 b 1), Ezra 7:26 (so Palmyrene Tariff ii.c.19 ).
3. in indirect question, whether (compare אִם, 2.b.), Ezra 5:17b יִתְבַּקַּר ֗֗֗ הֵן אִיתַי ֗֗֗ . — See also לָהֵן. p. 50, 243, 1099
