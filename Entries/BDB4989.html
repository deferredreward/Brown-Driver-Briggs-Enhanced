[נְדִיבָה] noun feminine nobility, nobleness; — suffix נְדִבָתִי Job 30:15 plural נְדִיבוֺת Isa 32:8 (twice in verse); —

1. nobility of rank, honour Job 30:15.
2. plural = noble things as counselled by the נדיב in character Isa 32:8 (twice in verse).
