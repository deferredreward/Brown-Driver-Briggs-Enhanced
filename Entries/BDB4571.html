מֵ֫זַח noun masculine girdle (according to Bondi11 WMMAs.u.Eur.104 loan-word from Egypt mdµ, compare LagGGN 1889, 310); — absolute ׳מ: — in simile of curse enfolding the wicked Ps 109:19 (|| בֶּגֶד); figurative = restraint Isa 23:10 (but text dubious).
