II. [חָדָה] verb rejoice (Assyrian —adû DlPr 153; Aramaic , חֲדָא) —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּ֫חַדְּ Exod 18:9 (E), and Jethro rejoiced over (עַל of thing); jussive אַליִֿ֫חַדְּ Job 3:6 let it not rejoice among (בְּ) the days of the year, i.e. not take its place joyfully among them (figurative of day of Job's birth). — Klo proposes וְלֹא חָדוּבְּ֗֗֗ 1Sam 6:19 for וַיַּךְבְּ֗֗֗ compare also Dr Pi`el Imperfect suffix (causative) תְּחַדֵּהוּ בְשִׂמְחְה Ps 21:7 thou makest him joyful (Che cheerest him) with gladness (|| תְּשִׁיתֵהוּ בְרָכוֺת).
