תְּבוּנָה noun feminine understanding Deut 32:28 + 27 t.; suffix תְּבוּנָתִי Prov 5:1 + 7 t.; תובנתו Job 26:12 Kt (Qr תְּבוּנָתוֺ); תְּבוּנָם Hos 13:2 for תְּבוּנָתָם see De Ps 27:5 תְּבוּנָוֺת Ps 49:4 + 4t.; תְּבוּנוֺתֵיכֶם Job 32:11 —

1. the act Job 26:12; Ps 78:72; Ps 136:5; Prov 3:19; Prov 21:30; Prov 24:3; Jer 10:12 (= Jer 51:15) Ezek 28:4; Hos 13:2; Obad 1:7.
2. the faculty Exod 31:3; Exod 35:31; Exod 36:1 (P) Deut 32:28 (poetry) Job 12:12; Job 12:13; Prov 2:6; Prov 2:11; Prov 28:16; Isa 44:19 אִישׁ תְּבוּנָה Prov 10:23; Prov 15:21; Prov 17:27; Prov 20:5 אִישׁ תְּבוּנוֺת Prov 11:12 (= Obad 1:8 where אישׁ omitted by scribal error); דֶּרֶךְ תְּבוּנָה Isa 40:14.
3. the object of knowledge Prov 2:3; Prov 3:13; Prov 5:1; Prov 14:29; Prov 18:2; Prov 19:8; Ps 49:4; Ps 147:5; Isa 40:28; 1Kgs 5:9; 1Kgs 7:14 תטה לבך לתבונה incline thy mind to understanding Prov 2:2 reasons Job 32:11.
4. personified Prov 8:1 as teacher.
