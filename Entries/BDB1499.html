גְּדוּלָּה noun feminine greatness — 2Sam 7:21 + 3 t.; גְּדֻלָּה 1Chr 29:11 construct גְּדֻלַּת Esth 10:2 suffix גְּדוּלָּתוֺ Esth 1:4 גְּדֻלָּתוֺ Ps 145:3 גְּדְלָּתִי Ps 71:21 גְדֻלָּו֯תְךָ Ps 145:6 plural intensive גְּדֻלּוֺת 1Chr 17:19; 1Chr 17:21 — chiefly late Hebrew

a. of Psalmist Ps 71:21 Mordecai Esth 6:3; Esth 10:2, king Esth 1:4
b. of God's greatness, as an attribute 1Chr 29:11; Ps 145:3; Ps 145:6, or of his acts 2Sam 7:21 (compare Dr) v2Sam 7:23 = 1Chr 17:19 (twice in verse); 1Chr 17:21.
