[יָצוּעַ] noun [masculine] couch, bed (poetry); — suffix יְצוּעִי Gen 49:4 plural construct יְצוּעֵי 1Chr 5:1 suffix יְצוּעָ֑י Job 17:13 + 2 t. (+ 1Kgs 6:5; 1Kgs 6:6; 1Kgs 6:10 Kt , see יָצִיעַ ); couch, bed, of wedlock or concubinage, Gen 49:4 ( J ), hence 1Chr 5:1 in general Ps 63:7 , עֶרֶשׂ יְצוּעָ֑י Ps 132:3 בַּחשֶׁךְ רִפַּדְתִּי יְצוּעָ֑י Job 17:13 (|| אִםאֲֿקַיֶּה שְׁאוֺל בֵּיתִי; compare Ps 139:8).
