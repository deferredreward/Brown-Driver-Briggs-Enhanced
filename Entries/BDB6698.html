BDB6698 [H6455]

Biblical Hebrew

פִּסֵּחַ adjective lame; — absolute ׳ פ 2Sam 9:13 +; plural פִּסְחִים 2Sam 5:6 +; — as attributive Lev 21:18 (+עִוֵּר); predicate 2Sam 19:27, שְׁתֵּי רַגְלָיו ׳ פ 2Sam 9:13 lame as to both his feet; compare also (of animal) Deut 15:21 (+עִוֵּר); as substantive 2Sam 5:6; 2Sam 5:8 (twice in verse); Jer 31:8; Mal 1:8; Mal 1:13; Job 29:15 (all + עִוֵּר), also Isa 33:23; Isa 35:6; Prov 26:7.
