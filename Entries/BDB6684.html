BDB6683 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6685

פְּנוּאֵל proper name (face of God, compare GFM JU 8, 8; late Assyrian Panîli HilprUniv. Pa. Exp. ix. 68); — Φανουηλ, but in Gen εἶδος (τοῦ) θεοῦ:

1. location east of Jordan, near Jabbok Gen 32:32; Judg 8:8 (twice in verse); Judg 8:9; Judg 8:17; 1Kgs 12:25 = פְּנִיאֵל Gen 32:31: site unknown; see especially PaineBib.Sac.1878,481 ff.
2. masculine
a. in Judah 1Chr 4:4.
b. in Benjamin 1Chr 8:25 Qr ( Kt פניאל).
