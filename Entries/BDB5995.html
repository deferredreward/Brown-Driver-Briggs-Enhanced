BDB5994 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5996

[עָטַר] verb surround (Late Hebrew id., and derivatives; Phoenician עטרת wreath, עטר Pi`el denominative crown Lzb339; Aramaic עֲטַר; Assyrian etêru, spare, rescue); —

Qal Imperfect 2 masculine singular suffix כַּצִּנָּה רָצוֺן תַּעְטְרֶנּוּ Ps 5:13 as with a shield, with favour will thou surround him; Participle plural עֹטְרִים אֶלדָּֿוִד 1Sam 23:26 Saul and his men were surrounding (closing in) upon David.
