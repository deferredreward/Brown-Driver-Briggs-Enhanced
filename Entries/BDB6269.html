[עָצֵב, > עַצָּב] noun [masculine] toiler (properly sufferer; less well עֶצֶב toil Thes Köii. 1, 29 and others); — plural suffix עַצְּבֵיכֶם תִּנְנּ֑שֹׁוּ Isa 58:3 (dagesh forte dirimens Ges«GKC:20h»§ 20h) your toilers ye drive on; Klo Che עֲבֹט בְּיֶדְכֶם (compare Vrss in part) money lent on pledge ye exact.
