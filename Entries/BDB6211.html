BDB6210 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6212

עֲנָוָה noun feminine humility: — Prov 15:33 + 3 t.; עַנְוָהֿ (contracted because of Maqqeph, see BrMp) Ps 45:5 עֶנְוָָֽתְךָ Ps 18:36 (= עֲנֹתְךָ 2Sam 22:36 see I. ענה ; read עֶזְרָֽתְךְ Ol We); —

1. humility, meekness Ps 45:5 (dubious), Prov 15:33; Prov 18:12; Prov 22:4; Zeph 2:3 (probably gloss, see We Now).
2. condescension Ps 18:36 (dubious, see above).
