BDB5257 [H5278]

Biblical Hebrew

נֹ֫עַם noun masculine Ps 90:17 delightfulness, pleasantness (Ecclus נועם תירוש Eccl 32:6); — ׳ נ absolute Zech 11:7 + 4 t.; construct Ps 27:4; Ps 90:17 —

1. delightfulness of ׳ ׃י contemplated in temple Ps 27:4, shewn in his favour, אדניעָ֗֗֗לֵינוּ ׳ וִיהִי נ Ps 90:17.
2. name of one of two symbolic staves Zech 11:7; Zech 11:10.
3. pleasantness, as defining genitive: ׳ דַּרְכֵינֿ Prov 3:17 ׳ אִמְרֵינֿ Prov 15:26 ; Prov 16:24.
