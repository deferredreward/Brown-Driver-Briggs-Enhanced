BDB5576 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5578

[מְסֻכָֿה] noun feminine that with which one is covered, covering; — suffix מְסֻכָֿתָ֑ךָ Ezek 28:13 (so Baer Ginsb; van d. H. ׳ מְסֻכּ) all precious stones were thy covering (= thou wast covered with them); — the tradition is strong in favour of Raph¢ , but sense favours derivation from סכך.
