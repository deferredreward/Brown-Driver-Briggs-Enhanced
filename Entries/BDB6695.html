BDB6694 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6696

תִּפְסַח proper name [of a location] on Euphrates (probablyford (though LagBN 131 doubts); LewyFremdw. 146 compare Αάμψακος (on Bosphorus), explanation as לַפֶּסַח); — 1Kgs 5:4, northeast limit of Solomon's dominion; = Gk Θάψακος XenAnab. i. 4 etc.; identification with Kal' at Dibse , at the great easterly bend of the river, approximately Latitude 35° 55' north, Longitude 38° 20' east, JPPetersNation, May 23, 1889: Nippur i.96 ff.
