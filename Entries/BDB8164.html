BDB8163 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8165

I. מִשְׂגַּב noun [masculine AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 60] secure height, retreat; — ׳מ Psalm +; construct מִשְׂגַּב Isa 25:12 suffix מִשְׂגַּבִּי Ps 18:3 +, etc.; —

1. a. = strong-hold, חֹמֹתֶיךָ ׳מִבְצַר מ Isa 25:12.
\ \ b. figurative of security Isa 33:16.
2. figurative of God as refuge Ps 9:10 (twice in verse), Ps 18:3 = 2Sam 22:3; Ps 46:8; Ps 46:12; Ps 48:4; Ps 59:10; Ps 59:17; Ps 59:18; Ps 62:3; Ps 62:7; Ps 94:22; Ps 144:2.
