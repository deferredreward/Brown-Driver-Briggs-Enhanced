רַ֫הַב noun [masculine] literally storm, arrogance, but only as names, v, infr; — absolute ׳ר Isa 30:7, רָ֑הַב Job 9:13 +; —

1. mythical sea monster (compare BartonJAOS xiv.1 (1891), 22 f.): ׳עֹזְרֵי ר Job 9:13 || יָם Job 26:12; Ps 89:11 || תַּנִּין Isa 51:9.
2. emblematic name of Egypt, רַהַב וּבָבֶל Ps 87:4 || מצרים Isa 30:7.
