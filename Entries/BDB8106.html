BDB8105 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8107

[רָקַם] verb variegate (Late Hebrew id.; so Arabic , Ethiopic , compare ᵑ7 רְקָם, רִקְמָתָא variegated cloth or skin, Syriac freckles); —

Qal Participle active רֹקֵם, as substantive variegator, worker (weaver) in colours, with ב mater. Exod 38:23, ׳ מְלֶאכֶת חָרָשׁ וְחשֵׁב וְר Exod 35:35 especially ׳ מַעֲשֵׂה ר variegator's work Exod 26:69; Exod 27:16; Exod 28:39 = Exod 36:37; Exod 38:18; Exod 39:29 (all P ; all with mater. except Exod 28:39).
Pu`al Perfect 1 singular רֻקַּמְתִּי Ps 139:15 I was skilfully wrought (woven, || סכד Ps 139:13).
