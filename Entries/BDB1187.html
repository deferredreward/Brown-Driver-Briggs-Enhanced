BDB1187 [H1138]

Biblical Hebrew

בֻּנִּי proper name, masculine

1. Levites,
a. Neh 9:4, but read perhaps בִּנּוּי compare BeRy & see Neh 10:10; Neh 12:8; Ezra 8:33
b. בּוּנִּי (בֶּן) Neh 11:15, but read perhaps מִן בְּנֵי etc. compare BeRy.
2. a chief of people Neh 10:16, perhaps repetition of בָּנִי V:15 compare BeRy.
