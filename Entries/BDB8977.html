Biblical Hebrew

II. שָׁפָן proper name, masculine Saf(f)an:

1. secretary (הַסִּפֵר) under Josiah 2Kgs 22:3; 2Kgs 22:8 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 22:9; 2Kgs 22:10 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 22:12; 2Kgs 22:14; 2Chr 34:8; 2Chr 34:15 (twice in verse); 2Chr 34:16; 2Chr 34:18 (twice in verse); 2Chr 34:20.
2. father of אֲחִיקָם, contemporary of 1 (improbable = 1): 2Kgs 22:12; 2Kgs 25:22; Jer 26:24; Jer 39:14; Jer 40:5; Jer 40:9; Jer 40:11; Jer 41:2; Jer 43:6; 2Chr 34:20.
3. father of אֶלְעָשָׂה Jer 29:3, perhaps = 2.
4. father of גְּמַרְיָ֫הוּ Jer 36:10 (called הַסִּפֵר), Jer 36:11 Jer 36:12, perhaps = 1.
5. father of יַאֲזַנְיָ֫הוּ Ezek 8:11, possibly = 1.
