BDB5477 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5479

מֵסַב noun [masculine] that which surrounds, or is round; —

1.
a. plural surrounding places, מְסִבֵּי ׳ יְרוּשׁ 2Kgs 23:5 places round about Jerusalem.
b. as adverb מֵסַב round about 1Kgs 6:29 feminine plural מְסִבּוֺת on all sides, in all directions, Job 37:12 (of cloud Di < of lightning Bu Du).
2. suffix מְסִבּוֺ Song 1:12 dubious: round table Ew De; cushion, divan Bae Kau; compare on all these Bu. — מְסִבָּ֑י Ps 140:10 see סבב Hiph`il
