BDB2589 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2591

חִידָה noun feminine riddle, enigmatic, perplexing saying or question; — absolute ׳ח Judg 14:12 + 6 t.; suffix חִידָתִי Judg 14:18; Ps 49:5 חִידָֽתְךָ Judg 14:13 plural absolute חִידוֺת 1Kgs 10:1 + 4 t.; חִידֹת Num 12:8 suffix חִידֹתָם Prov 1:6 —

1. riddle = dark, obscure utterance Num 12:8 (J E; opposed to פֶּהאֶֿלפֶּֿה and מַרְאֶה); of something put indirectly and needing interpretation; — an allegory ׳חוד ח Ezek 17:2 (|| מְשֹׁל מָשָׁל); allegorical and figurative sayings Prov 1:6 (|| מְלִיצָה מָשָׁל and בְרֵי חֲכָמִים Di); enigmatic sentences and comparisons, declaring fate of Chaldeans Hab 2:6 (+ מְלִיצָה; || מָשָׁל), perplexing moral problem Ps 49:5 (with פָּתַח open, propound; || מָשָׁל), a lesson taught indirectly Ps 78:2 (מִנִּי קֶדֶם ׳ח, with הִבִּיעַ; || מָשָׁל).
2. riddle, enigma, to be guessed, in Judg 14:
\ \ a. with vb. חוּד Judg 14:12; Judg 14:13; Judg 14:16 propound a riddle.
\ \ b. with vb. הִגִּיד tell (give answer to) a riddle Judg 14:14; Judg 14:15; Judg 14:17; Judg 14:19.
\ \ c. with vb. מצא find out a riddle Judg 14:18.
3. perplexing questions by which queen of Sheba put Solomon to the test (נִסָּה) 1Kgs 10:1 = 2Chr 9:1.
4. with הֵבִין skilled in double-dealing Dan 8:23 (Bev).
