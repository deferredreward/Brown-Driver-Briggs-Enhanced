BDB5983 [H5835]

Biblical Hebrew

עֲזָרָה noun feminine apparently enclosure; — absolute ׳ ע:

1. ledge surrounding Ezekiel's altar; ׳ הָע הַתַּחְתּוֺנָה = הַקְטַנָּה ׳ הָע Ezek 43:14 , below הַגְּדוֺלָה ׳ הָע Ezek 43:14 ׳ הָע alone Ezek 43:17 Ezek 43:20; Ezek 45:19 (see Commentaries).
2. outer court of temple, הַגְּדֹלָה ׳ הָע 2Chr 4:9 with doors v 2Chr 4:9; 2Chr 6:13 (see 1 חָצֵר 3.b., and on ׳ ע in Herod's temple NowArchaeology ii. 78 f f. ).

עַזָּתִי see below עַזָּה.
