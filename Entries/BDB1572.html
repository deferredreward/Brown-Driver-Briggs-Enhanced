BDB1571 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1573

[גּוֺר] noun [masculine] whelp (as quarrelsome? or onomatopoeia? perhaps = Assyrian giru ZehnpfBAS i. 504) of lion; — גּוֺרֵי אֲרָיוֺת Jer 51:38 (|| כְּפִרִים) simile of Babylonians; compare גֹּרֹתָיו Nah 2:13 (|| id. v.Nah 2:14) figurative of Assyrians.
