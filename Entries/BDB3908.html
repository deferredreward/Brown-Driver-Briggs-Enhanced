BDB3908 [H4346]

Biblical Hebrew

מִכְבָּר noun masculine grating or lattice-work, construct מִכְבַּר; — Exod 27:4 and thou shalt make for it (the altar of burnt-offering in the tabernacle) מִכְבָּר מַעֲשֵׂה רֶשֶׁת נְהשֶׁת a grating, (even) a network of bronze, which surrounded the lower half of the altar ( Exod 27:5 b): so Exod 38:4 מִכְבַּר הַנְּחשֶׁת Exod 35:16; Exod 38:5; Exod 38:30; Exod 39:39.
