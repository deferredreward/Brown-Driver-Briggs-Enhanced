BDB5352 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5354

נְקֵבָה noun feminine female (perforata (BaNB 166 ); so Thes and most; > another view in SchwZAW xi (1891), 181f.; Idioticon 57; Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; Aramaic נוּקְבָּא, נוּקְבְּתָא, , ); — always absolute ׳ נ Jer 31:22 + 21 t. (all Pentateuch, and all P except Deut 4:16):

1. woman (or female child) Jer 31:22 (opposed to גֶּבֶר), Gen 1:27; Gen 5:2; Lev 12:5; Lev 12:7; Lev 15:33; Lev 27:4; Lev 27:5; Lev 27:6; Lev 27:7) Num 5:3 (all opposed to זָבָר), Num 31:15.
2. female animal Gen 6:19; Gen 7:3; Gen 7:9; Gen 7:16; Lev 3:1; Lev 3:6 (all opposed to זָכָר), Lev 4:28; Lev 4:32; Lev 5:6. — Deut 4:16 (opposed to זָכָר) may include both women and animals.
