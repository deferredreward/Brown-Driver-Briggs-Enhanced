BDB5655 [H5584]

Biblical Hebrew

[סָעָה] verb (dubious) rush, of storm-wind (compare Arabic (and ), go quickly, run, be energetic; Syriac make an attack upon); —

Qal Participle feminine singular מֵרוּחַ סֹעָה Ps 55:9 from rushing wind (and) from tempest (סָ֑עַר); Hup (not Now) Gr Dy סוּפָה or סְעָרָה.
