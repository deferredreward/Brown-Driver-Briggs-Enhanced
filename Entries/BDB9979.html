BDB9979 [H8213 H8214]

Biblical Aramaic

[שְׁפֵל] verb be low (see Biblical Hebrew); —

Haph`el bring low, humble: Perfect 2 masculine singular הַשְׁפֵּלְתְּ לִבְבָח Dan 5:22 thou hast not humbled thine heart; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְהַשְׁפִּ֑ל Dan 7:24, Infinitive לְהַשְׁפָּלָה Dan 4:34, Participle מַשְׁפִּ֑ל Dan 5:19, all with accusative of person bring low, put down.
