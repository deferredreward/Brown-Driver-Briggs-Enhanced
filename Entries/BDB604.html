Biblical Hebrew

[אָנַק] verb cry, groan (Aramaic אֲנַק —

Qal Imperfect יֶאֱנֹק Jer 51:52 Infinitive construct אֱנֹק Ezek 26:15, both of groaning of wounded (חָלָל).
Niph`al id., in mourning, lamentation; Infinitive construct הֵאָנֵק Ezek 24:17 || עָשָׂה אֵבֶל); Participle נֶאֱנָקִים Ezek 9:4 (|| נֶאֱנָחִים followed by עַל.
