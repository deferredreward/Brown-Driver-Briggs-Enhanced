הָדַף verb thrust, .push, drive (הֲדַף in ᵑ7 ; Arabic is ingressus ad aliquem fuit, propinquus fuit, also of time prope accessit) —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular suffix הֲדָפוֺ Num 35:22; Jer 46:15 1 singular suffix וַהֲדַפְתִּיךָ consecutive Isa 22:19 Imperfect 3 masculine singularיֶהְדֹּף Prov 10:3 suffix 3 masculine singular יֶהְדֳּפֶנּוּ Num 35:20 suffix 3 masculine plural יֶהְדֳּפֵם Josh 23:5 3 masculine plural suffix יֶהְדְּפֻהוּ Job 18:18 2 masculine plural תֶּהְדֹּ֑פוּ Ezek 34:21 Infinitive הֲדֹף Deut 6:19; Deut 9:4 suffix הָדְפָהּ 2Kgs 4:27 —
1. thrust, push, subject man, followed by accusative Num 35:20; Num 35:22 (here fatally); subject cattle, figurative of men, Ezek 34:21 (instrumental בְּכָתֵף; || נָגַח ); push away 2Kgs 4:27 (Gehazi, the Shunammite); thrust away (from oneself) figurative = reject , הַוַּת רְשָׁעִים Prov 10:3 (subject ׳ י).
2. thrust out, drive out, followed by מִן of place whence & accusative of person; of ׳ י driving out enemies of Israel from Canaan Deut 6:19; Deut 9:4; Josh 23:5 compare also indefinite subject יֶהְדְּפֻהוּ מֵאוֺר אֶלחֿשֶׁךְ Job 18:18 (|| וּמִתֵּבֵל יְנִדֻּהוּ); & Jer 46:15 (where no plural whence). Hence
3. = depose Isa 22:19 of ׳ י deposing Shebna (followed byמִן of station).
