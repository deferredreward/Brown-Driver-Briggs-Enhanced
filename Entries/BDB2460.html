BDB2459 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2461

[זָרַם] verb pour forth in floods, flood away (Assyrian zarâmu , overwhelm, VR36, 57 c DlPr 73 & in ZimBP 119) —

Qal Perfect 2 masculine singular suffix זְרַמְתָּם Ps 90:5 thou floodest them with rain, sweepest them (men) away.
Po. Perfect 3 plural זֹ֫רְמוּ מַ֫יִם עָבוֺת Ps 77:18 the cloud masses (Che) poured forth water.
