BDB1589 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1591

גְּזֵלָה noun feminine plunder, spoil. — גְּזֵלָה Lev 5:23 + 3 t.; construct גְּזֵלַת Isa 3:14, גְּזֵלוֺת Ezek 18:12 — thing seized, spoil Lev 5:23 (P) Ezek 33:15 גְּזֵלַת הֶעָנִי Isa 3:14 as accusative of congnate meaning with verb Ezek 18:7; Ezek 18:12; Ezek 18:16.
