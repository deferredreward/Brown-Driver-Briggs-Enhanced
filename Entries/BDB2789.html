BDB2788 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2790

I. [חָלַם] verb be healthy, strong (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic Ethpe`el be made healthy, strong, Aph`el causative, also derivatives; חֲלַם press firmly together) —

Qal Imperfect יַחְלְמוּ בְנֵיהֶם Job 39:4 their young are healthy. Hiph`il Imperfect 2 masculine singular וְתַחֲלִימֵנִי Isa 38:16 and restore me to health (|| וְהַחֲיֵ֫ינִי).
