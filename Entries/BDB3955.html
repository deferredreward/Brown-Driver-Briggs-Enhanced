BDB3955 [H3561]

Biblical Hebrew

[כַּוָּן] noun [masculine] cake, sacrificial cake (? √ כון; compare Hiph`il 2 a. prepare food, etc.)

probably Babylonian loan-word, from kamânu , cake used in worship of Ištar, ZimKAT 3. 441. — only plural absolute לַעֲשׂוֺת כַּוָּנִים לִמְלֶכֶת הַשָּׁמַיִם Jer 7:18 עָשִׂינוּ לְהַעֲצִבָהֿ ׳ לָהּ כ Jer 44:19.
