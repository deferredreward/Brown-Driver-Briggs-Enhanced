BDB4836 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4838

[מָרֹר] noun masculine bitter thing, bitter herb (compare Late Hebrew id., bitter herb; on form see BaNB 194); — only plural מְרֹרִים Exod 12:8; Num 9:11, מְרוֺרִים Lam 3:15 — bitter herbs, in Passover meal Exod 12:8; Num 9:11 (both P); compare ׳הִשְׂבִּיעַנִי בַּמּ Lam 3:15, figurative of distress inflicted (|| לַעֲנָה).
