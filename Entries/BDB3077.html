Job 8:8 + 2 t.; plural construct חִקְרֵי Judg 5:16 — חִקְרֵי לֵב Judg 5:16 searchings, questionings of heart; Job 11:7 canst thou reach אֱלוֺהַּ ׳ ח i.e. what is to be explored in him, the whole range of his nature, so ׳ ח תהום Job 38:16 the range of the deep; especially ׳ אֵין ח i.e. it is unsearchable Job 5:9; Job 9:10; Ps 145:3; Prov 25:3; Isa 40:28, compare ׳ לא ח Job 34:24; Job 36:26 אבותם ׳ ח Job 8:8 i.e. thing searched out by their fathers.
