BDB5029 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5031

[נוּב] verb bear fruit (poetry) (Aramaic נוֺבָא fruit (rare)); —

Qal Imperfect יָנוּב Ps 62:11; Prov 10:31 יְנוּבוּן Ps 92:15 — only figurative, absolute of righteous under figure of tree Ps 92:15 חַיִל כִּי יָנוּב Ps 62:11 if wealth beareth fruit; Prov 10:31 פִּי צַדִּיק יָנוּב חָכְמָה beareth the fruit of wisdom.
Po`l. Imperfect יְנוֺבֵב Zech 9:17, דָּגָן בְּתֻלוֺת ׳ בַּחוּרִים וְתִירוֺשׁ יְנ figurative for makes flourish.
