I. כְּנַעֲנִי adjective and name, [of a people] of I. כְּנַעַן; — usually masculine singular (הַ)כְּנַעֲנִי Gen 38:2; Num 21:1 +; feminine singular הַכְּנַעֲנִית Gen 46:10 = Exod 6:15; 1Chr 2:3 masculine plural (הַ)כְּנַעֲנִים Obad 1:20; Neh 9:24 —

1. adjective ׳ אִישׁ כ Gen 38:2 ( J ).
2. with article as substantive,
a. of individual, ׳ מֶלֶךְעֲֿרָדהַכּ Num 21:1 ( J ) and hence Num 33:40 ( P or R ); feminine הַכְּנַעֲנִיתּ Gen 46:10 = Exod 6:15 ( P ) בַּתשֿׁוּעַ הַכְּנַעֲנִית 1Chr 2:3.
b. usually collective (with article) of pre-Israel inhabitants of כְּנַעַן (q. v.)
