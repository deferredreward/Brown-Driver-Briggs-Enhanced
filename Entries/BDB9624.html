[כְּרַז] verb denominative Haph`el make proclamation (possibly directly dependent on κηρύξ (D§ 37, p. 146 (2nd ed. 183) Kl.c. M67*), but even then formed as denominative; compare ᵑ7 כְּרַו, Late Hebrew כָּרַז, Syriac Aph`el, Ethpe`el, so Christian-Palestinian Aramaic, SchwIdioticon 46 SchulthLex.97); — Perfect 3 masculine plural הַכְּ רִ֫צוּ Dan 5:29, with עַיל person concerning.
