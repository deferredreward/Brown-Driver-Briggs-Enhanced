[זָבַד] verb bestow upon, endow with (Arabic ; Aramaic זְבַד, Gen 30:20, dowry; in Sabean proper name, see below זָבָד below; also in Palmyrene proper name נבוזבד Vog73 and many others (זבד, זבדא , זבידא, זבדעתא, זבדבל = Ζαβδιβηλος, etc.): see EutSechs Inschr. aus Idalien p. 15), זְבָדַנִי אלהים אֹתִי זֶבֶד טוֺב Gen 30:20 a ( E ), apparently in explanation of name Zebulun, compare Thes Di; another explanation in vb, see below זבל.
