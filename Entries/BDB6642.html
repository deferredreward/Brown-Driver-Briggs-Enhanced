פָּלִיט noun masculineAmos 9:1 escaped one, fugitive; — ׳פ absolute 2Kgs 9:15 +; plural construct פְּלִיטֵי Judg 12:4 +; suffix פְּלִיטָיו Obad 1:14, etc.; — Amos 9:1; 2Kgs 9:15; Gen 14:13; Ezek 24:26; Ezek 24:27; Ezek 33:21 ( + מִן local), vEzek 33:22 ׳שָׂרִיד וּפ Josh 8:22; Jer 42:17 וְשָׂרִיד ׳פ Jer 44:14; Lam 2:22 with genitive subject Judg 12:5 fugitives of Ephraim (strike out in vJudg 12:4, see GFM), so with suffix Ezek 6:9; Ezek 7:16; Obad 1:14 with genitive object, escaped of, i.e. from Isa 45:20, so פְּלִיטֵי חֶרֶב Jer 44:28; Ezek 6:8.
