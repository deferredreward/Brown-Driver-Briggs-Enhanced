BDB5778 [H4569]

BDB5777 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5779

[מַעֲבָר] noun [masculine] ford, pass, passing; — only construct:

1. מַעֲבַר יַבֹּק Gen 32:23 (J) the ford of (the) Jabbok.
2. pass, מַעֲבַר מִכְמָשׂ 1Sam 13:23 the pass of M.
3. passing, sweep, כָּלמַֿעֲבַר מַטֶּה Isa 30:32 every sweep of the rod (עָבַר 4 c).
