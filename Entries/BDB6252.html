BDB6251 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6253

I. עֹ֫פֶל noun [masculine] mound, hill, only as acropolis; — ׳ ע absolute 2Kgs 5:24 +, construct Mic 4:8 — fortified mound or hill within city, or Jerusalem (compare RobBR i. 267 GuZPV v (1882), 326) Mic 4:8; Isa 32:14 south end of eastern hill Neh 3:26; Neh 11:21; 2Chr 33:14 ׳ חוֺמַת הָע Neh 3:27; 2Chr 27:3 (compare חמת העפל in קרחה MI21, 22 ); of Samaria 2Kgs 5:24.
