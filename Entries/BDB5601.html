BDB5600 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5602

I. [סָלָה] verb make light of, toss aside (compare Assyrian salû, throw off , shake off (yoke) Dl500 ; Arabic , is be forgetful, neglectful Lane1417; Aramaic סְלָא despise, reject); —

Qal Perfect 2 masculine singular סָלִיתָ Ps 119:118 thou dost make light of all those that err from thy statutes. — compare also סַלּוֺן below
Pi`el intensive Perfect 3 masculine singular סִלָּה Lam 1:15 Adonay hath flouted at my mighty ones.
