מְלָאכָה167 noun feminine occupation, work (for *מַלְאָכָה; compare Phoenician מלאכת labour) — ׳ מ Exod 12:16 + 102 t.; מְלָ֑אכֶת 2Chr 13:10 construct מְלֶאכֶת Exod 35:24 + 42 t.; suffix מְלַאכְתְּךָ Jonah 1:8 מְלַאכְתֶּ֑ךָ Exod 20:9 + 2 t.; מְלַאכְתּוֺ Gen 2:2 + 13 t.; plural construct מַלְאֲכוֺת 1Chr 28:19 suffix מַלְאֲכוֺתֶיךָ Ps 73:28 —

1. occupation, business, מַה מְּלַאכְתְּךָ what is thy occupation Jonah 1:8 business of a steward Gen 39:11 ( J ); diligent in business Prov 22:29 slack in business Prov 18:9.
2. property in which one is occupied, רעהוּ ׳ מ his neighbour's property Exod 22:7; Exod 22:10 ( E ); possessions of herds and flocks Gen 33:14 ( J ), 1Sam 15:9 (compare מִקְנֶה); רבה ׳ מ great property 2Chr 17:13 .
3. work as something done or made:
a. of God in creation Gen 2:2 (twice in verse); Gen 2:3 ( P ), in judgment Jer 50:25, in general Ps 73:28.
b. of men, עור ׳ מ leather-work Lev 13:48; Lev 13:51 ( P ), השׂדה ׳ מ work in the field 1Chr 27:26 in building Prov 24:27 +, the walls of Jerusalem Neh 4:5 +, making the tabernacle and its furniture Exod 36:2 +, the temple 1Kgs 5:30 +; work of the potter Jer 18:3, of the seaman Ps 107:23, of the Levites 1Chr 26:29, of priests in the sacrifices 2Chr 29:34 מְלָאכֶת (ה)עֲבֹדָה phrase of P , Exod 35:24; Exod 36:1; Exod 36:3; Lev 23:7; Lev 23:8; Lev 23:21; Lev 23:25; Lev 23:35; Lev 23:36; Num 28:18; Num 28:25; Num 28:26; Num 29:1; Num 29:12; Num 29:35 also 1Chr 9:13 ; 1Chr 9:19 ; 1Chr 28:13; 1Chr 28:20; 2Chr 24:12 לעבדה ׳ המ 1Chr 23:24 ׳ עבדה למ Exod 36:5 ( P ); compare the phrase ׳ עֹשֵׂה המ workmen Ezra 3:9; Neh 2:16 ׳ עֹשִׂים במ 1Kgs 5:30; 1Kgs 9:23 +; ׳ כל מ any work forbidden on the Sabbath Exod 20:9; Exod 20:10; Lev 23:3 ( P ) Deut 5:13 ; Deut 5:14 ; Jer 17:22; Jer 17:24 on holy convocations Exod 12:16; Lev 16:29; Lev 23:28; Lev 23:30; Lev 23:31; Num 29:7 ( P ).
4. workmanship, כל מלאכה in every kind of workmanship, phrase of P : Exod 31:3 ; Exod 31:5 ; Exod 35:29; Exod 35:31; Exod 35:33; Exod 35:35 and Chronicles: 1Chr 22:15; 1Chr 28:21 ; 1Chr 29:5; 1Kgs 7:14.
5. service, use Lev 7:24; Lev 11:32 ; Judg 16:11 ; Ezek 28:13 .
6. public business:
a. political 1Kgs 11:28 ; 1Chr 29:6; Dan 8:27; Esth 3:9; Esth 9:3.
b. religious, putting away foreign wives Ezra 10:13 יהוה ׳ מ 1Chr 26:30 הַקֹּדֶשׁ ׳ מ Exod 36:4 ; Exod 38:24 ( P ), קדשׁ הקדשׁים ׳ מ 1Chr 6:34 אהל ׳ מ מועד Exod 35:21 ( P ), ׳ בית י ׳ מ 1Chr 23:4 ; Ezra 3:8; Ezra 6:22; Neh 10:34; Neh 11:22.
