BDB5572 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5574

סֻכּוֺת בְּנוֺת proper name [of a divinity] 2Kgs 17:30, Assyrian-Babylonian deity, worshipped by Babylonians in Samaria; text of name corrupt; SchrCOT ad. location (after H. Rawlinson) proposes Zîr-bânit, or Zarpanituv, wife of Marduk (compare HptAR, May 1886 Muss-ArnJBL xi (1892), 167), rejecting Sakkut-binûtu = Marduk DlPar 215; JenZA iv. 352 makes בְּנוֺת = banîtu , epithet of Ištar; all doubtful.
