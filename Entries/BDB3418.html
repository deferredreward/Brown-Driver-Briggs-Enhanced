יָגוֺן noun [masculine] grief, sorrow; — absolute יָגוֺן Gen 42:38 + 12 t.; suffix (מִ)יגוֺנָם Jer 31:13 — sorrow, Gen 42:38; Gen 44:31 ( J ), Ps 13:3 ; Jer 8:18 || אֲנָחָה Ps 31:11; Isa 35:10; Isa 51:11 compare Ps 107:39 (|| עֹצֶר, רָעָה), Ps 116:3 (|| צָרָה), Jer 20:18 (|| עָמָל ), Jer 31:13 (|| אֵבֶל, opposed to שִׂמַּחְתִּים), Jer 45:3 (|| מַכְאֹבִי), Ezek 23:33 (|| שִׁכָּרוֺן, but Co.; שׁברון with Manuscripts; compare Ezek 21:11); Esth 9:22 (opposed to שִׂמְחָה).
