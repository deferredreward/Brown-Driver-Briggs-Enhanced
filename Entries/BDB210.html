II. אֵלוֺן proper name, masculine (= id. compare אֵלָה proper name)

1. a son of Zebulun Gen 46:14; Num 26:26.
2. אֵילֹן Gen 26:34 אֵיִלוֺן Gen 36:2 a Hittite, father-in-law of Esau.
3. אֵילוֺן Judg 12:11; Judg 12:12 a judge of Israel, of tribe of Zebulun
4. proper name [of a location] אֵילוֺן Josh 19:43 a town of Dan; so 1Kgs 4:9 (where read ׳ וּבֵית ח ׳ א Th Klo).
