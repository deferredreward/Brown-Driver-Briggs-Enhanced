עָשׂוֺר, and (Exod 12:3) עָשׂר noun [masculine] a ten, decade; —

1. usually of days:
a. a period of ten days Gen 24:55 ( J ).
b. in phrase, בֶּעָשׂוֺר (עָשׂר) לַחֹדֶשׁ on the tenth of the month Lev 25:9 ( H ), Ezek 20:1; Ezek 24:1; Ezek 40:1; Exod 12:3; Lev 16:29; Lev 23:27; Num 29:7; Josh 4:19 (all P ), 2Kgs 25:1 = Jer 52:4; Jer 52:12 (seventh day in || 2Kgs 25:8).
2. of strings of instrument: ׳ נֵבֶל ע harp, a ten (-stringed one) Ps 33:2; Ps 144:9 ׳ ע alone Ps 92:4 upon a ten (-stringed instrument).
