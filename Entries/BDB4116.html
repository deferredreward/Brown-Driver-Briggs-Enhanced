BDB4116 [H3683]

BDB4115 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4117

[כָּסַח] verb cut off or away, a plant (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic כְּסַח, (for Hebrew זָמַר); Arabic sweep off, away, destroy, do away with; compare Sabean כסח overpower, conquer, HomZMG 1892, 532) — only Qal Passive participle, feminine singular כְּסוּחָה Ps 80:17, cut away, of Israel under figure of vine; masculine plural קוֺצִים כְּסוּחִים Isa 33:12 thorns cut away, figurative of peoples destroyed by divine judgment.
