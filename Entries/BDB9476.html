Biblical Aramaic

הַמְנִיכָא Qr ( Kt המינכָא, also [Gi] המוניכא, etc.) noun [masculine] chain, necklace (Talmud הַמְנִכָּא DWB 108; Syriac ; loan-word from Greek μανιάκης K§ 64, 4; or < Persian hämy¹n , girdle [diminutive would be hamy¹nak ] (whence also Greek μαν., Bev Dr Dan 5:7 ) AndrM 60* (reading הַמְיָֽנְכָא ); see also Behrmix LagGes. Abh. 40; Arm. Stud. 1420; see Krauss5 f. (who compares also Talmud מוּנְיָיק, מָנְיָיק necklace, bracelet, LevyNHWB iii. 52 b, 158 b D230)); — of gold, Dan 5:7; Dan 5:16; Dan 5:29.
