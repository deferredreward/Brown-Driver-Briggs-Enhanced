BDB3102 [H2730]

Biblical Hebrew

חָרֵד verbal adjective of foregoing, trembling; — חָרֵד Judg 7:3 + 3 t.; חֲרֵדִים Isa 66:5; Ezra 10:3 — trembling (from fear), absolute Judg 7:3 (|| ירא), 1Sam 4:13 (subject לִבּוֺ, followed by עַל, for, in behalf of); in awe and reverence, at word of God, sq, בְּ Ezra 9:4; Isa 66:2 (followed by עַל), Isa 66:5 (followed by אֶל); compare ׳ בְּמִצְוַת אל ׳ ח Ezra 10:3.
