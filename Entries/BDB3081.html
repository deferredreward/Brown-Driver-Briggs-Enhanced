[חֶרֶא, or חֲרִי] noun [masculine] dung (Late Hebrew חרא (י_), id.; Aramaic id.; Mandean חרא (on this and cognate languages in general see NöM 56); Arabic id., whence verb of act, and noun of place; Amharic (DiLex. Aeth. 88)) — plural suffix חַרְאֵיהֶם Isa 36:12 Kt ( Qr צוֺאָתָם); || 2Kgs 18:27 has חֲרִיהֶם (see singular חרי 2Kgs 6:25 Qr as above); חֲרִי יוֺנִים dove's dung 2Kgs 6:25 Kt ( Qr דִּבִיוֺנִים); see, further, GeiUrschrift, 409.
