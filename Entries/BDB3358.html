BDB3357 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3359

טַ֫עַם noun masculineJer 48:11 taste, judegement — ׳ט absolute Job 6:6; Ps 119:66 טָ֑עַם Prov 11:22; Prov 26:16 construct טַעַם Num 11:8 + 2 t.; suffix טַעְמוֺ Exod 16:31 + 4 t.; טַעְמֵךְ 1Sam 25:33 —

1. taste of manna Exod 16:31 (P), Num 11:8 (JE), compare vNum 11:8 in juice of purslain Job 6:6 of Moab under figure of wine Jer 48:11 (|| רֵיחַ).
2. figurative judgement, discretion, discernment, 1Sam 25:33; Prov 11:22; Job 12:20, so טוּב טַעַם וָדַעַת לַמְּדֵנִי Ps 119:66 ׳מְשִׁיבֵי ט Prov 26:16 those answering discretion, i.e. discreetly: שׁנה טעם change, disguise one's judgement, sense 1Sam 21:13; Ps 34:1, of David feigning madness.
3. (late Aramaism, compare Dan 3:10 etc.) decision, decree טַעַם מֶלָךְ וּגְּדֹלָיו Jonah 3:7.
