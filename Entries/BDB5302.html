BDB5301 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5303

מַפֶּ֫לֶת noun feminine carcass, ruin, overthrow; —

1. carcass, of lion, ׳מ construct Judg 14:8.
2. elsewhere suffix
\ \ a. a ruin, מַפַּלְתּוֺ Ezek 31:13 (kg. under figure of tree).
\ \ b. overthrow, id. vEzek 31:16 (same figurative); מַפַּלְתֶּ֑ךָ (kg.) Ezek 32:10 מַפַּלְתֵּךְ of Tyre) Ezek 26:15; Ezek 26:18; Ezek 27:27 מַפַּלְתָּם (of wicked) Prov 29:16.
