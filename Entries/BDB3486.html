BDB3485 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3487

יַ֫הַץ, יַ֫הְצָה proper name [of a location] in Moab, site unknown ( MI19, 20 יהץ) — יהץ Isa 15:4; Jer 48:34 with ה locative יָהְ֑צָה, on border of territory of Amorites (under Sihon) Num 21:23; Deut 2:32 also בְּיָהְ֑צָה Judg 11:20 called יַהֲצָ֫ה Josh 13:18 (assigned to Reuben); יַ֫הְצָה 1Chr 6:63 (Levitical city in Reuben), Jer 48:21.
