III. שׁוּר proper name [of a location] southwest of Palestine, on east border of Egypt; — Gen 16:7 ( J ; ׳ דֶּרֶךְ שׁ), Gen 20:1 ( E ), towards Egypt Gen 25:18 ( J ), 1Sam 15:7 , שׁ֫וּרָה 1Sam 27:8 ׳ מִדְּבַּרשֿׁ Exod 15:22 ( J ). — Often supposed to denote properly the 'wall' or line of fortresses, built by Egyptian kings across isthmus of Suez; but dubious: compare DrHast.
