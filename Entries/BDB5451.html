BDB5450 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5452

[מַתַּת] noun feminine id.; — absolute מַתָּ֑ת 1Kgs 13:7 construct מַתַּת Prov 25:14 + 4 t.; — gift = reward 1Kgs 13:7 מַתַּת יָדוֺ the gift of his hand, i.e. of his power, what he is able to give Ezek 46:5; Ezek 46:11 (compare מַתָּנָה and יָד 2); אֱלֹהִים ׳ מ Eccl 3:13 the gift of God (of the enjoyment of man), so Eccl 5:18 שָׁ֑קֶר ׳ מ Prov 25:14 a gift of falsity, i.e. one promised and not given.
