שַׁ֫חַל noun masculine lion (poetry); — absolute ׳ שׁ Hos 5:14 +, שָׁ֑חַל Job 4:10 +; — lion, simile of ׳ י, toward Israel Hos 5:14 (|| כְּפִיר), Hos 13:7 (|| נָמֵר); of guilty men Job 4:10 (|| אַרְיֵה, כְּפִירִים); figurative of foes Ps 91:13 (|| כְּפִיר) lion, as hunted Ps 10:16 (in simile); literal Job 28:8; Prov 26:13.
