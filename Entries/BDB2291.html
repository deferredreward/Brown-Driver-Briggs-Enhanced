BDB2290 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2292

זוֹּ id quod זֹה (q. v.): only Hos 7:16b זוֺ לַעָגָּם this (i.e. the falling by the sword, va) is their derision; and (as, relative) Ps 132:12 וְעֵדֹ֫תִ֖י זֹוֺ and my testimony which . . . (neglecting the accentuation: see De, and compare the remark under זֶה 5).
