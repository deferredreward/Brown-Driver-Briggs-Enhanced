BDB1935 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1937

דַּךְ adjective crushed, oppressed — דַּךְ Ps 74:21 דָּ֑ךְ Ps 9:10; Ps 10:18 plural suffix דַּכָּ֑ו Prov 26:28 (Baer; and other editions דַּכָּיו) — in poetry; always figurative, oppressed, distressed Ps 9:10; Ps 18 (|| יתום) Ps 74:21(|| עָנִי and אביון); Prov 26:28 דַּכָּו = those crushed by it (a lying tongue).
