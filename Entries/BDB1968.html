BDB1967 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1969

דֻּמָּה noun feminine one silenced, brought to silence (?; destroyed?) מִי כְצוֺר כְּדֻמָּה בְּתוֺךְ הַיָּ֑ם Ezek 27:32 (form pecul. & sense dubious: Baer כְּדֻמָֿה; Co כְּבֻדָּה from כָּבֹד adjective, compare Ezek 23:41; Ps 45:14) AV What city is like Tyrus, like the destroyed (as though for מְדֻמָּה compare ההללה Ps 26:1 but Pi`el Punic not elsewhere) in the midst of the sea? RV like her that is brought to silence. — DlBaer Ezech p.xi. & Pr 64 derived, hesitantly, from II. דמם.
