BDB5509 [H7713]

BDB5508 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5510

[שְׂדֵרָה]

noun feminine 1. row, rank of soldiers in line.
2. architectural term. (probably for סְדֵרָה, see above and compare Ba§ 92 a); — only plural absolute שְׂדֵרוֺת 2Kgs 11:8; 2Chr 23:14, רֹת- 1Kgs 6:9; 2Kgs 11:15 —
1. rows, ranks, 2Kgs 11:8, and v2Kgs 11:15 = 2Chr 23:14 (where thought to be gloss by Benz, on account of מִבַּיִת, within).
2. technical term of building, גֵּבִים וּשְׂדֵרֹת בָּאֲרָוִים 1Kgs 6:9, meaning unknown.
