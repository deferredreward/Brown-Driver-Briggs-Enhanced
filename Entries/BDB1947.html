BDB1946 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1948

דְּלָיָ֫הוּ, דְּלָיָה proper name, masculine (Yah(u) hath drawn) —

1. דְּלָיָהוּ,
a. a priest, contemporary of David, 1Chr 24:18.
b. a prince of Judah, contemporary of Jeremiah Jer 36:12; Jer 36:25.
2. דְּלָיָה,
a. head of a family contemporary with Zerubbabel Ezra 2:60 = Neh 7:62.
b. descendant of Zerubbabel. 1Chr 3:24.
c. father of contemporary of Nehemiah Neh 6:10.
