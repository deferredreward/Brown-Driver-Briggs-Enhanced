BDB7839 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7841

[רָזַם] verb wink, flash (of eyes) (transposed [textual error ?] for Arabic , Aramaic רְמַז, ; so Late Hebrew רָמַז (rare), רֶמֶז sign, intimation); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine plural וּמַהיִּֿרְזְמוּן עֵינֶיךָ Job 15:12 (Manuscripts ירמזון) why do thine eyes flash (in wrath)? but possibly read יְרוּמוּן (Prov 6:17; Prov 30:13, etc., compare Bu).
