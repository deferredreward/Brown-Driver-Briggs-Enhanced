BDB368 [H375]

Biblical Hebrew

אֵיפֹה (from אַי & פֹּה here) adverb

1. where? Gen 37:16; 1Sam 19:22; 2Sam 9:4; Isa 49:21; Jer 3:2; Job 4:7; Job 38:4; Ruth 2:19 in indirect sentence Jer 36:19 (less common than אַיֵה, used of persons [contrast אֵיוֶֿה] and with a verb [contrast אַיֵּה]).
2. of what kind? (quails ?) only Judg 8:18.
