BDB5835 [H5736]

Biblical Hebrew

[עָדַף] verb remain over, be in excess, synonym of סרח q. v., only P H (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic עָדִיף superior (to); Arabic be profuse, IV. let down veil, or curtain, etc.; plentifulness); —

Qal Participle סֶרַח הָעֹדֵף Exod 26:12 the surplus (of curtains) that remains over, so feminine הָעֹדֶ֫פֶת Exod 26:12, and הָעֹדֵף as substantive Exod 26:13 the excess in (ב) length; הָעֹדֵף = the surplus of food Exod 16:23, of price of field Lev 25:27 הָעֹדְפִים עַלֿ those over and above, Num 3:46; Num 3:49, compare Num 3:48 (no עַלֿ).
Hiph`il Perfect 3 masculine singular הֶעְדִּיף Exod 16:18 have a surplus (of manna; opposed to הֶחְסִיר).
