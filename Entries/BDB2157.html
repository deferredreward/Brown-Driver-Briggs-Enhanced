[הָלָא] verb denominative only

Niph`al participle feminine Mic 4:7 הַנַּהֲלָאָה (of sheep) removed far off.
We proposes הַנַּחֲלָה (√ חלה) her that is diseased (note || הַצֹּלֵעָה, and compare Ezek 34:21), so Now; Gr הַנִּלְּאָה her that is exhausted; Marti allows either of these.
