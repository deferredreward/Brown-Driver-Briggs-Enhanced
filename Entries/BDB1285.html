II. [בָּקַק] verb empty (compare probably Arabic make a gurgling noise, of a mug dipped in water, or emptied of water) —

Qal Perfect וּבַקֹּתִ֫י Jer 19:7, בָּֽקֲקוּ Nah 2:3 Participle בּוֺקֵק Isa 24:1, בִּקֲקִים Nah 2:3 empty, lay waste land, accusative, Isa 24:1; Nah 2:3, also absolute v.Nah 2:3 figurative make void (object עֵצָה) Jer 19:7.
Niph`al Perfect וְנָָֽבְקָה (compare Ges«GKC:67»§ 67, R. 11) Isa 19:3 Imperfect 3 feminine singular תִּבּוֺק Isa 24:3 Infinitive absolute הִבּוֺק Isa 24:3 — be emptied (laid waste) Isa 24:3 הִבּוֺק תִבּוֺק, of land (|| הִבּוֺז תִּבּוֺז); figurative of spirit, courage Isa 19:3.
Po. Imperfect יְבֹקֲקוּ Jer 51:2 empty out (devastate) land.
