כֶּ֫סֶל noun masculine

1. loins,
2. stupidity,
3. confidence (Late Hebrew id. lion; Aramaic כִּסְלָא id.); —
1. loins כָּ֑סֶל Job 15:27 plural כְּסָלִים Lev 3:4; Lev 3:10; Lev 3:15; Lev 4:9; Lev 7:4 suffix כְּסָלַי, Ps 38:8.
2. stupidity, folly ׳ כ Eccl 7:25 (|| סִכְלוּת), כֵּסֶל Ps 49:14.
3. confidence, suffix כִּסְלִי Job 31:24 כִּסְלֶ֑ךָ Prov 3:26 כִּסְלוֺ Job 8:14 כִּסְלָם Ps 78:7.
