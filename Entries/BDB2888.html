BDB2887 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2889

I. חֹ֫מֶר noun masculine cement, mortar, clay; — ׳ח absolute Gen 11:3 + 13 t.; construct Isa 10:6 + 2 t.; —

1. mortar, cement for holding building-stones (brick) together Gen 11:3 (J), Exod 1:14 (P; both || לְבֵנִים); Nah 3:14 (|| טיט).
2. clay:
\ \ a. as material of vessels Jer 18:4; Jer 18:6, היצר ׳ח Isa 29:16, in simile of God's fashioning man Isa 45:9; Isa 64:7; Job 10:9 as material of human bodies ׳בָּתֵּי ח Job 4:19 compare Job 33:6 as material of bulwarks (disparagingly) Job 13:12 חותם ׳ח = seal-clay i.e. clay upon which seal is pressed.
\ \ b. = mire חוצות ׳ח Isa 10:6 ׳הֹרָנִי לַח Job 30:19 he hath cast me into the mire, i.e. deeply humiliated me (עָפָר and אֵפֶר in || clause); as simile of commonness, abundance Job 27:16 (|| עָפָר).
