לָבֵשׁ לָבַשׁ verb put on (a garment), wear, clothe, be clothed (Late Hebrew id. ; Aramaic לָבֵישׁ, Assyrian labâšu ; Arabic ; Ethiopic ); —

1.
a. literally put on (one's own) garment (accusative) Gen 38:19 ; 1Sam 28:8 ; 2Sam 14:2 ; 1Kgs 22:30 = 2Chr 18:29 ; Exod 29:30 ; Lev 6:3; Lev 6:4; Lev 16:4 (twice in verse); Lev 16:23 ; Lev 16:24 ; Lev 16:32 ; Lev 21:10 ; Deut 22:5 ; Ezek 44:17 ; Ezek 44:19 , and so Ezek 42:14 Qr (see above), Jonah 3:5; Song 5:3; Esth 4:1 ; Esth 5:1 with accusative garment + עַלבְּֿשָׂרוֺ Lev 16:3 of putting on armour Jer 46:4 = wear (more or less habitually), with accusative of garment, Isa 4:1; Deut 22:11 ; Zeph 1:8 ; Zech 13:4 of Jerusalem under figure of woman Jer 4:30 , of rulers under figure of shepherds Ezek 34:3, compare כֵּן תִּלְבַּשְׁןָ 2Sam 13:18 (no accusative)
b. very often figurative, put on, be clothed with, with accusative of garment; — לָבַשׁ בְּשָׂרִי רִמָּה Job 7:5 my flesh is clothed (i.e. covered) with worms; of Jerusalem, be clothed with inhabitants Isa 49:18 of pasture, with flocks Ps 65:14 more often the garment is some abstract quality, e.g. righteousness, majesty, beauty, strength, etc.; — וַיִּלְבַּשׁ צְדָקָה כַּשִּׁרְיָן Isa 59:17 he ( ׳ י) hath put on righteousness as a breastplate, compare vIsa 59:17 (vengeance), Ps 93:1 (majesty), v Ps 93:1 (strength), Ps 104:1 (honour and majesty), Isa 51:9 (strength); also Job 40:10 said of men, 2Chr 6:41 (salvation), Ps 132:9 (righteousness), Job 29:14 (id. ); of Zion Isa 52:1 (twice in verse); also in bad sense, clothed with terror, trembling, shame, etc., Ezek 7:27; Ezek 26:16 ; Job 8:22 ; Ps 35:26; Ps 109:29, compare vPs 35:18 (cursing).
c. literally put on, object omitted, נָתַןבֶּ֗֗֗גֶד לִלְבּשׁ Gen 28:20 ; Job 27:17 Infinitive absolute לָבוֺשׁ Hag 1:6 (there is) a clothing, but he has nothing for warmth.
d. once with בְּ לְבוּשׁאֲ֗֗֗שֶׁר לָבַשׁ בּוֺ Esth 6:8.
e. Passive participle, clothed with, chiefly Ezek.; — with accusative of garment, Ezek 9:2; Ezek 9:3; Zech 3:3; Prov 31:21 ; Dan 10:5 of wearing armour 1Sam 17:5 construct before garment, לְבֻשׁ הַבַּדִּים Ezek 9:11 ; Ezek 10:2 ; Ezek 10:6; Ezek 10:7, compare Ezek 23:6 ; Ezek 23:12; Ezek 38:4 ; Dan 12:6 ; Dan 12:7 .
f. figurative: — לָֽבְשָׁה אֶתגִּֿדְע֑וֺן ׳ וְרוּחַ י Judg 6:34 and the spirit of ׳ י clothed itself with Gideon, i.e. (GFM) took possession of him (compare possessed); so 1Chr 12:18; 2Chr 24:20 צֶדֶק לָבַשְׁתִּי וַיִּלְבָּשֵׁנִי Job 29:14 and it clothed itself in me, as it were, became incarnate in me.
