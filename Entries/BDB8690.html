Biblical Hebrew

[שָׁלַב] verb

Pu`al be bound, joined (Late Hebrew Punic = Biblical Hebrew; שְׁלִיבָה rung of ladder; Aramaic שְׁלִיבִין apparently only 1Kgs 7:28; 1Kgs 7:29, , ravine, narrow path between mountains; Arabic is seize, carry off, by force); — Participle feminine plural מְשֻׁלָּבֹת Exod 26:17 tenons joined each to (אֶל) its fellow, Exod 36:22 (both P ).
