אֲבִירָם proper name, masculine ((the) Exalted One is (my) father (see BaeRel 156) compare Assyrian Aburamu (?) KAT2 479 compare DlL2, p. 91, 1. 225).

1. a Reubenite, son of Eliab Num 16:1; Num 16:12; Num 16:24; Num 16:25; Num 16:27 (twice in verse); Num 26:9; Deut 11:6; Ps 106:17.
2. son of Hiel the Bethelite 1Kgs 16:34. compare also following.
