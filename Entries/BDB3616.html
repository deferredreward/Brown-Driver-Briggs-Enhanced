BDB3615 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3617

[יָעַז] verb only

Niph`al Participle as adjective (precise meaning dubious; Arabic is give a nod or sign, then command , whence Hebrew might have meaning nodding, making signs (not intelligibly speaking), so Hi — Che barbarous, De 'ungeberdig' — which context favours, or arrogant; Thes proposes durus, saevus, and compare עזז ) — in phrase עַם נוֺעָז Isa 33:19 a barbarous (?) people, of foreign invader.

יעזיאל, יעזיהו see below עזה.

יַעְזִרִ see below עזר. p. 741.
