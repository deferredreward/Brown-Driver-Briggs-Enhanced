BDB3156 [H2742]

BDB3155 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3157

I. חָרוּץ adjective sharp, diligent (on this and following see BaNB 173 ) — חָרוּץ Isa 28:27 + 11 t.; plural חֲרוּצִים Prov 10:4; Prov 12:24 חֲרֻצִים Prov 13:4 חֲרֻצוֺת Amos 1:3 —

1. sharp: of threshing instrument חָדָשׁ ׳ מוֺרָג ח Isa 41:15 without ׳ מ, as substantive, Isa 28:27 (where יוּדַשׁ); הַבַּרְזֶל ׳ ח Amos 1:3; Job 41:22 (figurative of crocodile).
2. figurative diligent: as substantive Prov 21:5 opposed to רְמִיָּךְ Prov 10:4; Prov 12:24 ; Prov 12:27 opposed to עָצֵּל Prov 13:4. — Dan 9:25 see IV. חרוץ .
