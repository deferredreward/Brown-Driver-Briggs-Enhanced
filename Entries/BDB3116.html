BDB3116 [H2747]

Biblical Hebrew

חֶ֫רֶט noun [masculine] graving-tool, stylus —

1. graving-tool, with which Aaron fashioned (וַיָּצַר) the molten calf Exod 32:4 ( E ).
2. stylus, for writing on tablet (גִּלָּיוֺן): כְּתֹב עָלָיו בְּחֶרֶט אֱנוֺשׁ Isa 8:1 write on it with a man's (i.e. an ordinary) stylus = in common characters, intelligible to all (see BenzArchaeology 290).
