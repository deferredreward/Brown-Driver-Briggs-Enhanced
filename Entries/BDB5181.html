BDB5180 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5182

נִיסָן proper name of 1st month (loan-word from Babylonian Nisannu , compare SchrCOT Neh 1. 1 Muss-ArnJBL xi. 1 (1892). 76; Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; so Nabataean, Palmyrene, see Lzb323); — only post-exile: ׳ חֹדֶשׁ נ Neh 2:1; Esth 3:7 ( + הָרִאשׁוֺן הַחֹדֶשׁ), = pre-exile הָאָבִּיב (הֹדֶשׁ). — On sacredness of Nisan compare also RSSemitic i. 387; 2d ed. 406 f.

נִיצוֺץ see נצץ . נֵיר see נֵר .
