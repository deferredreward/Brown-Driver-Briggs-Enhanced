BDB2049 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2051

הֶ֫גֶה noun masculine Job 37:2 a rumbling, growling, moaning: — ׳ ה absolute Ezek 2:10 + 2 t.; —

1. a rumbling, growling sound מִפִּיו יֵצֵא ׳ וְה Job 37:2 of thunder, as sound going forth from God's mouth.
2. a moaning וָהִי ׳ קִינִים וָה Ezek 2:10 lamentations and moaning and woe.

3. a sigh or moan, as transient, כִּלִּינוּ שָׁנֵינוּ כְמוֺהֶֿ֑גֶה Ps 90:9 we bring our years to an end as a sign, i.e. a fleeting sound (compare RVm VB ).
