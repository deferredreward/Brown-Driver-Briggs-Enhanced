בְּאֵר שֶׁ֫בַע proper name, of a location Beersheba (well of seven, explained Gen 21:30; Gen 21:31 as place of swearing by seven lambs, or, well of oath, see שְׁבֻעָה; compare same meaning otherwise derived Gen 26:33) — בְּאֵר שֶׁבַע Gen 26:33 + 13 t., בְּאֵרשֶֿׁבַע Josh 19:2 בְּאֵר שָׁ֑בַע Gen 21:14 + 18 t.; בְּאֵ֫רָה שֶׁבַע (ָ  ה locative) Gen 46:1 — south from Hebron, according to Onomasticon approximately 20 miles LagOnom. 103, 234, 2nd ed. 138, 248; modern Bîr-es-Seba, 12 hours from Hebron RobBR i. 204 f. Surveyiii. 394 Gen 21:14; Gen 21:31; Gen 21:32; Gen 21:33; Gen 22:19 (twice in verse); Gen 26:23; Gen 26:33; Gen 28:10; Gen 46:1; Gen 46:5; Josh 15:28; Josh 19:2; Judg 20:1; 1Sam 3:20; 1Sam 8:2; 2Sam 17:11; 2Sam 24:7; 1Kgs 19:3; 2Kgs 12:2; 2Kgs 23:8; 2Kgs 1:28; 2Kgs 2:4; 2Kgs 24:1; in phrase ׳מִדָּן וְעַדבּֿ from Dan to Beersheba (i.e. all the territory of Israel, see דָּן) Amos 8:14; Judg 20:1; 1Sam 3:20; 2Sam 3:10; 2Sam 17:11; 2Sam 24:2; 2Sam 24:15; 1Kgs 5:5, & (only Chronicles) מִבְּאֵר שֶׁבַע וְעַדדָּֿן from Beersheba to Dan 1Chr 21:2; 2Chr 30:5.
