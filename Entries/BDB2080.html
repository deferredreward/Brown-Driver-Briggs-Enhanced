BDB2080 [H1927]

BDB2079 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2081

[הֲדָרָה] noun feminine adornment, glory — only construct הַדְרַת; —

1. קֹֿדֶשׁ ׳ ה holy adornment (ChePsalm), always in connection with public worship of ׳ י 1Chr 16:29; 2Chr 20:21; Ps 29:2; Ps 96:9.
2. מֶֿלֶךְ ׳ ה the glory of a king, figurative of רָבעַֿם, Prov 14:28.
