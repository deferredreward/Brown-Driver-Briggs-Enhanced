BDB7944 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7946

I. רְמִיָּה noun feminine deceit, treachery; — always absolute ׳ ר; — deceit, treachery: especially of speech, predicate of לָשׁוֺן Mic 6:12 (|| שֶׁקֶר), apposition of לָשׁוֺן (or read לְשֹׁוֺן ?) Ps 120:2 (|| שְׂפַת שֶׁקֶר), vPs 120:3 of man, ׳ עשֵֹׁה ר Ps 52:4 that workest treachery, Ps 101:7 (|| דֹּבֵר שְׁקָדִים); also Job 13:7; Job 27:4 (both || עַוְלָה ); deception Ps 32:2 (|| עָוֺן); in phrase ׳ קֶשֶׁת ר treacherous bow (simile) Hos 7:16; Ps 78:57 (failing him who trusts to it).
