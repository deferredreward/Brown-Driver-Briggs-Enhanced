BDB3153 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3155

IV. [חָרַף] verb acquire (compare Arabic turn a thing from its proper way or manner, but also gain, acquire [subsistence] for one's family) — only Niph`al Participle f. שִׁפְחָה נֶחֱרֶפֶת לְאִישׁ a maidservant acquired for a man (namely as his concubine) Lev 19:26 (H; compare Late Hebrew חֲרוּפָה of woman designated for a man).
