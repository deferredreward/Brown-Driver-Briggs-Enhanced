BDB3593 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3595

מוּסָדָה noun feminine foundation, appointment, מַטֵּה מוּסָדָה Isa 30:32 rod of appointment (compare Hab 1:12 יָסַד), appointed rod (of punishment); plural construct מוּסְדוֺת Ezek 41:8 Qr, must mean foundations (si vera 1.); Kt perhaps intends Pu`al Participle מְיסָֻּדוֺת founded, and furnished with foundation (conjectural emendation by Co).
