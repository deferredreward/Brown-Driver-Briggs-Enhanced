קוֺל (sometimes, with prefix and suffix, קֹל)506 noun masculine Gen 39:14 sound, voice; — absolute קוֺל Gen 4:10 + ( הַקֹּל Gen 27:22; Gen 45:16); construct קוֺל Gen 3:8 + (וְקֹל Exod 19:16, לְקֹל Exod 4:8 (twice in verse)); suffix קוֺלִי 1Sam 26:17 +, קוֺלְךָ Judg 18:25 +, etc. (קֹלִי Gen 22:18, קֹלֶ֑ךָ Exod 3:18, etc.); plural (usually of thunder) קֹלֹת Exod 9:23 + 4 t., קוֺלֹת Exod 20:18 ( Gi Exod 20:15), קֹלוֺת 1Sam 2:17 + 5 t.; —
