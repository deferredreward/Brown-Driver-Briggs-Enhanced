II. חָלַם verb dream (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic , חֲלַם; Ethiopic Arabic , dream, experience an emission of the seminal fluid, attain to puberty) —

Qal Perfect ׳ ח Gen 42:9; Dan 2:1, חָלַמְתִּי Gen 37:9 + 3 t., חָלָ֑מְתִּי Gen 37:6 + 2 t., etc., + 3 t. Perfect; Imperfect יַחֲלֹם Isa 29:8 + 5 t., יַחְלְמוּ Job 39:4, וַיַּחַלְמוּ Gen 40:5, יַחֲלֹמ֑וּן Joel 3:1, נַחַלְמָה Gen 41:11 Participle חוֺלֵם Deut 13:4, חֹלֵם Gen 41:1; Deut 13:2; Deut 13:6, חֹלְמִים Ps 126:1 — dream:
a. of ordinary dreams of sleep Isa 29:8 (twice in verse); Ps 126:1.
b. of dreams with prophetic meaning: of Jacob Gen 28:12, Joseph Gen 37:5; Gen 37:6; Gen 37:9 (twice in verse); Gen 37:10; Gen 42:9, of Pharaoh and his servants Gen 40:5; Gen 40:8; Gen 41:1; Gen 41:5; Gen 41:11 (twice in verse); Gen 41:15 (all E , not elsewhere in Hexateuch); of Midianite Judg 7:13, Nebuchad. Dan 2:1; Dan 2:3, old men in latter days Joel 3:1.
c. of dreams of false prophets Deut 13:2 ; Deut 13:4; Deut 13:6; Jer 23:25 (twice in verse).
