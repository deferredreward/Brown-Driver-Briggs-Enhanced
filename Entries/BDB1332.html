I. בְּרָכָה noun feminine blessing (Arabic ; Ethiopic Aramaic בִּרְכָּא ; Late Hebrew as Hebrew) — ׳ ב Gen 12:2 + 39 t.; construct בִּרְכַּת Gen 28:4 + 9 t.; suffix בִּרְכָתוֺ Gen 49:28 + 7 t.; plural בְּרָכוֺת Ps 21:7 + 4 t.; construct בִּרְכוֺת, בִּרְכֹת Gen 49:25 + 5 t.; suffix בִּרְכוֺתֵיכֶם Mal 2:2 —

1. blessing:
a. of parent Gen 27:12; Gen 27:41; Gen 49:28 ( J E ), of Moses Deut 33:1.
b. of God Exod 32:29 ( E ) Lev 25:21 ( P ) Deut 11:26; Deut 11:27; Deut 11:29; Deut 23:6 (= Neh 13:2) Deut 28:2; Deut 28:8; Deut 30:1; Deut 30:19; Josh 8:34 ( D ) 2Sam 7:29; Ps 3:9 ; Ps 21:4 ; Ps 133:3; Isa 44:3; Ezek 34:26 ; Ezek 44:30 ; Joel 2:14 ; Mal 3:10 ברכת יהוה Gen 39:5 ( J ) Deut 12:15; Deut 16:17; Deut 33:23; Ps 129:8; Prov 10:22 ׳ ברכה מאת י Ps 24:5 ברכת אברהם Gen 28:4 ( P ), the blessing given to Abraham.
c. of the people, in recognition of good men Ps 109:17; Prov 10:6; Prov 11:26; Prov 24:25 ; Prov 28:20 .
d. of a poor man, in recognition of benefits Job 29:13.
2. source of blessing: Abraham Gen 12:2 ( J ); Israel Isa 19:24; Ezek 34:26; Zech 8:13 seed of the righteous Ps 37:26 the king Ps 21:7 memory of the righteous Prov 10:7 new wine Isa 65:8.
3. blessing, prosperity: בברכת ישׁרים by the prosperity of the upright (the city is exalted) Prov 11:11 גם ברכות יעטה מורה yea, the early rain covereth with blessings Ps 84:7 compare Gen 49:25 ; Gen 49:26 וארותי את ברכותיכם and I will curse your prosperity Mal 2:2.
4. blessing, praise of God Neh 9:5.
5. a gift, present Gen 33:11 ( E ) Josh 15:19 ( J ) Judg 1:15 ; 1Sam 25:27; 1Sam 30:26; 2Kgs 5:15 נֶפֶשׁ בְּרָכָה a liberal person Prov 11:25 (compare Syriac , Ethiopic ).
6. treaty of peace 2Kgs 18:31 = Isa 36:16.
