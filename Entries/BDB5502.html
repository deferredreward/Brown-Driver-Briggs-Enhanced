מִסְגֶּ֫רֶת noun

1. border, rim.
2. fastness;
— absolute ׳ מ Exod 25:27; Exod 37:14 construct ׳ מ Exod 25:25; Exod 37:12 suffix מִסְגַּרְתּוֺ Exod 25:25; Exod 37:12 plural absolute מִסְגְּרוֺת 1Kgs 7:28 +; suffix מִסְגְּרֹתֶיהָ 1Kgs 7:35 1Kgs 7:36, etc.; —
1. border, rim (enclosure), of sacred table in tabernacle Exod 25:25 (twice in verse); Exod 25:27 = Exod 37:12 (twice in verse); Exod 37:14 of bases of the sea, in temple 1Kgs 7:28 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 7:29; 1Kgs 7:31; 1Kgs 7:32; 1Kgs 7:35; 1Kgs 7:36 (compare Benz), also 2Kgs 16:17.
2. fastness, ׳ וְיַחְרְגוּ מִמּ Ps 18:46 = 2Sam 22:46 (see חרג ) and they shall come quaking out of their fastnesses, so Mic 7:17 (יִרְגְּזוּ , like reptiles), all of nations, in awe of ׳ י.

II. סגר (√ of following; compare apparently Arabic fill with water Lane1308, torrent that fills everything Idib.; Syriac imber vehemens, and Samaritan אַסְגָּר, compare GeiNachgel. Schr. iv. 186).
