אַדִּיר adjective majestic — ׳ א Ps 8:2 +; feminine אַדֶּ֫רֶת Ezek 17:8 ? (see below) etc. —

1. majestic (wide, lofty) of waters of sea Exod 15:10; Ps 93:4 a ship Isa 33:21 a tree Ezek 17:23 a vine Ezek 17:8 (גֶּפֶן אַדֶּרֶת , so Fü; or ׳ א abstract noun see below אַדֶּרֶת below); also figurative of kings Ps 136:18 nations Ezek 32:18 gods 1Sam 4:8 of ׳ י Ps 93:4; Ps 76:5 of name of ׳ י Ps 8:2; Ps 8:10.
2. substantive majestic one, of nobles, chieftains, etc., Judg 5:13; Judg 5:25; Nah 2:6; Nah 3:18 Jer 14:3; Jer 25:34 ( אַדִּירֵי הַצּאֹן figurative so): Jer 25:35; Jer 25:36; Jer 30:21; Zech 11:2; 2Chr 23:20; Neh 3:5; Neh 10:30 of ׳ י Isa 10:34; Isa 33:21 of servants of ׳ י Ps 16:3 (= priests ? compare 1Chr 24:5 & see Che).
