BDB5201 [H5230]

Biblical Hebrew

[נָכַל] verb be crafty, deceitful, knavish (Assyrian nakâlu, be crafty, cunning; ᵑ7 נְכֵל be crafty, Pa`el deceive; Syriac deceive, plot; compare perhaps act faithlessly, faithless, PräAmhar. Spr. 90); —

Qal Participle נוֺכֵל Mal 1:14 knave.
Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine plural with accusative of cognate meaning with verb נִכְלֵיהֶם אֲשֶׁר נִכְּלוּ לָכֶם Num 25:18 their wiles with which they beguiled you.
Hithpa`el Imperfect וַיִּתְנַכְּלוּ אֹתוֺ לַהֲמִיתוֺ Gen 37:18 they knavishly planned against him (Köiii. 9), etc.; Infinitive construct לְחִתְנַכֵּל בְ Ps 105:25 to deal knavishly with.
