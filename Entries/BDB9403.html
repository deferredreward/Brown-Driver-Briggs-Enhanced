BDB9402 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9404

דְָֽבְרַיָּא noun masculine plural emphatic Dan 3:2; Dan 3:3, dubious: most think || form of [גַּזְבָּר] treasurer (or, MeyEntst. J. 23, textual error for ׳ גז); Gr Bev and others read הַדָּֽבְרַיָּא ministers (Dan 3:24); possibly (see Lag Dr and others) dittograph for following דְּתָֽבְרַיָּא.
