Biblical Hebrew

אֲפֵלָה noun feminine darkness, gloominess, calamity — ׳ א Exod 10:22 +; אֲפֵלָתְךָ Isa 58:10 plural אֲפֵלוֺת Isa 59:9 —

1. darkness Deut 28:29; Prov 7:9 (|| אִישׁוֺן לַיְלָה); of supernatural darkness in Egypt Exod 10:22 of day of .׳ י (compare Amos 5:20 below אָפֵל) Joel 2:2; Zeph 1:15 (both || חשׁך ענן, רְפל) simile of wickedness Jer 23:18; Prov 4:19.
2. figurative of calamity Isa 8:22 (|| חֲשֵׁכָה), Isa 58:10 (|| חשׁך, opposed to צהרים ), Isa 59:9 (|| id., opposed to נגהות)
