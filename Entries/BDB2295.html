BDB2295 [H2092]

Biblical Hebrew

[זָהַם] verb be foul, loathsome, but only Pi`el causative (Late Hebrew id., be foul, Pi`el make foul; Aramaic זְהִים foul, make foul, fetid; Arabic stink, be greasy) —

Pi`el Perfect 3 feminine singular suffix 3 masculine singular וְזִהֲמַתּוּ חַיָּתוֺ לָ֑חֶם (consecutive) Job 33:20 his life maketh it, bread, loathsome to him (|| ונפשׁו מַאֲכַל תַּאֲוָה) (On suffix see Ges«GKC:131»§ 131, 2, R. 4; Dr 1Sam 21:14: according to Siegf the word is hopelessly corrupt.)
