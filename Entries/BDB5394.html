BDB5394 [H4864]

Biblical Hebrew

מַשְׂאֵת noun feminine uprising, utterance, burden, portion (on formation compare Ol§ 109 a Sta§ 272 b Köii. 1, 183; Phoenician (Pun) has משאת = payment, tax see Lzb326); — absolute ׳ מ Judg 20:40 +; construct מַשְׂאַת Gen 43:34 +; plural absolute מַשְׂאֹת Gen 43:34, + (probably) מַשְׂאוֺת Lam 2:14 (see below); construct מַשְׂאֹת Gen 43:34 —

1. that which rises, uprising, uplifting:
a. הֶעָשָׁן ׳ מ Judg 20:38 uprising of smoke, compare (׳ הֶע omitted) Judg 20:40.
b. in general = signal Jer 6:1 (= נֵס Gf.).
c. uplifting of hands (כַּפַּי) in prayer Ps 141:2 (|| תְּפִלָּה ).
2. utterance, oracle (= III. מַשָּׂא), Lam 2:14 (Bu Löhr).
3. burden Zeph 3:18 (of reproach, figurative).
4.
a. portion (carried to some one), especially from table of king or superior, Gen 43:34 (3 t. in verse) ( J ; on custom see Di and references), 2Sam 11:8 so read perhaps also 2Sam 19:43 (for ᵑ0 נִשֵּׂאת) Gr HPS.
b. perhaps also more generally, present, largess from a superior Jer 40:5 ( + אֲרֻחָה food-allowance), Esth 2:18 (כְּיַד הַמֶּלֶךְ).
c. exacted or enforced gift, from inferior, בַּר ׳ מ Amos 5:11 (compare Phoenician above; see also מנחה ).
d. sacred contribution, tax משֶׁה ׳ מ (׳ י) עֶבֶד 2Chr 24:6 ; 2Chr 24:9 .
e. offering to ׳ י Ezek 20:40 .
