Biblical Hebrew

אֲחַשְׁוֵרוֺשׁ proper name, masculine Ahasuerus = Xerxes (Persian Khshayârshâ = mighty + eye or man, see Spiegl. with 216 ; in Aramaic חשיארש, CISII. i. 122 [ B.C. 481]) king of Persia Ezra 4:6; Esth 1:1 (twice in verse); Esth 1:2; Esth 1:9; Esth 1:10 + 18 t. Esther + Esth 10:1 Qr ( Kt אחשׁרשׁ); אֲחַשְׁוֵרשׁ Esth 1:16; Esth 2:21; Esth 3:12; Esth 8:7; Esth 8:10 also Dan 9:1 where made father of 'Darius the Mede,' compare Meinh.
