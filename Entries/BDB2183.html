BDB2183 [H4112]

Biblical Hebrew

מַהֲלֻמֿוֺת noun feminine plural strokes, blows (compare Phoenician מהלם stroke, stamp, impression, once on a coin מהלם אגדר coinage of Gader (Gades), = percussura, κόμμα, GesMon.pl.40,xiv. c also p. 307) — Prov 18:6 (compare Baer) Prov 19:29, both times of blows on the body.
