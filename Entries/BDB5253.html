BDB5252 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5254

[נָעַל] verb denominative furnish with sandals, shoe (ᵑ7 נְעַל (rare), bind on sandal; Arabic furnish with sandals, shoe horse or camel; Syriac shoe horse); —

Qal Imperfect 1 singular suffix וָאֶנְעֲלֵךְ תָּ֑חַשׁ Ezek 16:10 and I shod thee with תחשׁ (q. v.). Hiph`il Imperfect 3 masculine plural suffix וַיַּנְעִלוּם 2Chr 28:15 and gave them sandals.
