שֶׁ֫קֶץ noun masculine Lev 7:21 detestation, detestable thing; — ׳ שׁ of the ceremonially unclean. Lev 7:21 creatures forbidden as food: water animals without fins or scales Lev 11:10; Lev 11:11; Lev 11:12, birds of prey, etc., Lev 11:13, winged creeping things Lev 11:20 Lev 11:23, creeping vermin Lev 11:41 Lev 11:42 (all P ), compare Isa 66:17 (+ בְּשַׂר הַחֲזִיר, הָעַכְבָּר); see RSSemitic i. 275, 2nd ed. 293; K 309 f., 2nd ed. 311 f. — Ezek 8:10 read probably שִׁקּוּצִים, see שִׁקּוּץ .
