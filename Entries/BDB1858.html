BDB1858 [H1727]

Biblical Hebrew

[דּוּב] verb pine away (compare דאב, דאבון) —

Hiph`il Participle feminine plural מְדִיבֹת נפשׁ Lev 26:16 (diseases) causing to pine away the soul (life), compare Di ; in 1Sam 2:33 read probably Infinitive לְהָדִיב את נפשׁך (for ᵑ0 לַאֲדִיב) so Dr; transitive to cause thy (al. his) soul to pine away (compare VB ).
