BDB8808 [H8082]

Biblical Hebrew

שָׁמֵן adjective fat, robust; — ׳ שׁ masculine Judg 3:29 +, feminine שְׁנֵנָה Gen 49:20 +; —

1. fat, rich, of food, Gen 49:20 (poem in J ), Isa 30:23 (+ דָּשֵׁן), Hab 1:16 (||); of land, festile Num 13:20 ( E ), Neh 9:25 ; Neh 9:35 , pasturage Ezek 34:14; 1Chr 4:40. (1Sam 15:9 see מִשְׁנֶה 3).
2. stout, robust, of men Judg 3:29 (|| אִישׁ חַיִל; see GFM); feminine collective (figurative of flock) Ezek 34:16 (+ הַחֲזְקָהִ).
