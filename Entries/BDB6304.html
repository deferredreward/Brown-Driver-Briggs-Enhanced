BDB6304 [H6115]

BDB6303 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6305

עֹ֫צֶר noun [masculine] restraint, coercion; — ׳ ע absolute: וּמִמִּשְׁפָט ׳ מֵע Isa 53:8 from (as a result of) coercion and judgment he was taken off; construct רָעָה ׳ ע Ps 107:39 רָ֑חַם ׳ ע Prov 30:16 restraint of womb, barrenness (so Ps 107:39 PerlesAnal. 85).
