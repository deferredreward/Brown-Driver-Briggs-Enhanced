BDB1112 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1114

בִּכּוּרָה noun feminine first ripe fig, early fig (regarded as a delicacy) (Löw391; compare Arabic , Spanish albacora, Moorish bokkôre) Mic 7:1; Hos 9:10 suffix בִּכּוּרָהּ = בִּכּוּרָתָהּ (read בִּכּוּרָה Di), Isa 28:4 plural בַּכֻּרוֺת Jer 24:2

**probably a different word, plural abstract = early ripeness, compare Du.
