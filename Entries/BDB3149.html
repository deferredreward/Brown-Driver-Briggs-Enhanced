חֹ֫רֶף noun masculine harvest-time, autumn (Arabic freshly gathered fruit, autumn (also rain of autumn or beginning of winter) = Sabean חֿרפ (ןׅ DHMZMG 1875, xxix. 597; Sabean חֿרף, חֿרפם = year, compare Ethiopic annus currens Os (Levy)ZMG 1865, 168, 174 DHMZMG 1883, 369; Assyrian —arpu , SchrJPTh 1875, 341 KAT 2 53 n. = COTi. 54 n.) — absolute ׳ ח Zech 14:8 + 5 t.; suffix חָרְפִּי Job 29:4 — קַיִץ וָחֹרֶף Gen 8:22 ( J ), Ps 74:17 ; Zech 14:8 ׳ בֵּית הַח autumn-house or palace Amos 3:15; Jer 36:22 (+ בַּחֹדֶשׁ הַתְּשִׁיעִי in the 9th month, i.e. Nov-Dec.); ׳ מח עָצֵל לֹא יַחֲרשׁ Prov 20:4 a sluggard ploughs not after harvest; as implying maturity, בִּימֵי חָרְפִּי Job 29:4 in the days of my autumn (prime).
