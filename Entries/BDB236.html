[אוֺר]43 verb be or become light (compare Arabic enkindle, Aramaic (Nasar.) Aph`el illumine, & derivatives in Assyrian) —

Qal Perfect אוֺר Gen 44:3 + אֹ֫רוּ 1Sam 14:29 Imperfect 3 feminine plural וַתָּאֹרְנָה 1Sam 14:27 Qr; Participle אוֺר Prov 4:18 (Ges«GKC:72»§ 72 R. 1) Imperative feminine singular א֫וֺרִי Isa 60:1; Prov 4:18 — become light, shine of sun (especially in early morning) Gen 44:3 (J) 1Sam 29:10; Prov 4:18; Isa 60:1 of eyes (owing to refreshment) 1Sam 14:27 (Qr) 29. Niph`al Imperfect וַיֵּאֹר 2Sam 2:32 Infinitive לֵאוֺר Job 33:30 Participle נָאוֺר Ps 76:5 — become lighted up of day-break 2Sam 2:32 by light of life, revival Job 33:30 by light of glory, enveloped in light Ps 76:5. Hiph`il Perfect וְהֵאִיר Exod 25:37, etc.; Imperfect יָאִיר Job 41:24 +; יָאֵר Num 6:25 +; וַיָּ֫אֶר Ps 118:27 +, etc.; Imperative הָאֵר Ps 80:20 +, הָאִ֫ירָהPs 31:17 +; Infinitive הָאִיר Gen 1:15 +; Participle מֵאִיר Prov 29:13 מְאִירַתPs 19:9 מְאִירוֺת Isa 27:11 —
1. give light, of sun, moon & stars Gen 1:15; Gen 1:17 (P), of moon Isa 60:19 of pillar of fire (followed by ל) Exod 13:21; Exod 14:20 (both J E) Ps 105:39; Neh 9:12; Neh 9:19 of scared lamp Exod 25:37; Num 8:2 (both P); figurative of the words of God Ps 119:130.
2. light up, cause to shine, shine, followed by accusative הֵאִירוּ בְרָקִים תֵּבֵל lightnings lighted up the world Ps 77:19; Ps 97:4 absolute הָֹאָרֶץ הֵאִירָה מִכְּבֹדוֺ Ezek 43:2 the earth shined with his glory (of theophany); of leviathan, which makes path shine behind him Job 42:24 לַיְלָה כַּיּוֺם יָאִיר night shineth as day Ps 139:12.
3. light a lamp Ps 18:29 wood Isa 27:11 alter (-fire) Mal 1:10.
4. lighten, of the eyes, הָאִיר עֵינַיִם, subject ׳י, his law, etc. Prov 29:13; Ps 13:4; Ps 19:9; Ezra 9:8.
5. make shine, of face of God פָּנָיו אֵלֶיךָ ׳יָאֵר י Yahweh make his face shine upon thee Num 6:25 (priest's blessing), reappearing Ps 31:17 (followed by עַלֿ), Ps 67:2 (followed by אֶתֿ), Ps 80:4; Ps 80:8; Ps 80:20; Ps 119:135 (followed by ב), Dan 9:17 compare Ps 4:7 without פָּנִים (followed by ל) Ps 118:27 once, of face of man, Eccl 8:1 פָּנָיו חָכְמַת אָדָם תָּאִיר the wisdom of a man lighteth up his face.
