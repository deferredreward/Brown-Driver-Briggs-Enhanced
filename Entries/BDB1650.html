BDB1649 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1651

[גֵּל] noun [masculine] dung (ball of dung, Arabic etc., dung of camels etc., compare Aramaic גַּלָּא; on form see Ew§ 255 b Ol§ 150. 163) — suffix גֶּלֲלוֺ Job 20:7, plural construct הָאָדָם (צֵאַת) גֶּלֲלֵי Ezek 4:12; Ezek 4:15 — human dung used as fuel Ezek 4:12; Ezek 4:15 (צֵאַת) הָאָדָם ׳ ג; perhaps also Job 20:7, but compare Di, who thinks no reference to fuel, & De who thinks of cattle-dung; (see on Arabic word Wetzst in De). — See also II. גָּלָל.
