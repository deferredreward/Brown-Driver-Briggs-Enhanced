BDB445 [H461]

Biblical Hebrew

אֱלִיעֶ֫זֶר (עֶֿ֑זרֶ Gen 15:2 +) proper name, masculine (God is help, compare Exod 18:4 see also אֶלְעָזָר below, עֲזַרְאֵל, Phoenician אשמנעזר, בעלעזר, עזרבעל)

a. Abraham's steward (אֱלִיעֶ֑זֶר), a Damascene Gen 15:2.
b. a son of Moses Exod 18:4; 1Chr 23:15; 1Chr 23:17 (twice in verse).
c. Benjamite 1Chr 7:8.
d. several priests 1Chr 15:24; 1Chr 26:25; Ezek 10:18.
e. Reubenite 1Chr 27:16.
f. prophet in time of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 20:37.
g. Levite chief Ezek 8:16; Ezek 10:23.
h. son of Harim Ezra 10:31.
