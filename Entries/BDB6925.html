BDB6924 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6926

אֶצְבַּע noun feminine Lev 14:6 finger; — ׳ א absolute Isa 58:9, construct Exod 8:15 +; suffix אֶצְבָּעוֺ Lev 4:6 +; plural אֶצְבָּעוֺת Jer 52:21, construct אֶצְבְּעֹת 2Sam 21:20 +, etc.; —

1. finger, especially
a. forefinger, of priest, applying blood ( P ), Exod 29:12; Lev 4:6; Lev 4:17; Lev 4:25 + 8 t.; applying oil Lev 14:16 (twice in verse); Lev 14:27 .
b. ׳ שְׁלַח א of any one Isa 58:9, sign of contempt.
c. of God, as writing Exod 31:18 ( E ) Deut 9:10 = act of God Exod 8:15 ( J ).
d. plural of all the fingers 2Sam 21:20 a; fingers in general Isa 2:8; Isa 17:8; Isa 59:3; Ps 144:1; Prov 7:3; Song 5:5 of ׳ י Ps 8:4 ׳ מֹרֶה בְא Prov 6:13 (of contemptuous gesture); as measure of thickness, Jer 52:21 four fingers.
2. toes: אֶצְבְּעֹת רַגְלָיו 2Sam 21:20 b, literally fingers of his feet, compare ׳ א || 1Chr 20:6 = fingers and toes.
