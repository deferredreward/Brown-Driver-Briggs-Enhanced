חוֺל noun masculineHos 2:1 sand (perhaps as turning or whirling; otherwise explained by Sta, see below חֲוִילָה; Late Hebrew id., Aramaic חָלָא, ) — ׳ח absolute Gen 22:17 + 15 t.; construct Gen 32:13 + 6 t.; — sand Exod 2:12 (E); set as bound for sea Jer 5:22 ׳שְׂפֻנֵי טמוני ח Deut 33:19 hidden treasures of sand, poetic for glass (regarded as mysteriously produced out of sand, see Di and references);
\ \ a. usually sand of seashore, simile of numberlessness, vastness, so of Abraham's seed Gen 22:17 (J; compare Gen 15:5); Jacob's Gen 32:13 (E) of a host Josh 11:4 (JE), Judg 7:12; 1Sam 13:5; 2Sam 17:11 a people 1Kgs 4:20; Hos 2:1; Isa 10:22; Isa 48:19 (|| וְצֶאֱצָאֵי מֶעֶיךָ כִּמְעֹתָ֑יו), Jer 33:22; Hab 1:9 of corn gathered by Joseph Gen 41:49 of days of Job Job 29:18 (on interpretation = phoenix, see Di); of birds עוף כנף (i.e. quails) Ps 78:27 (|| כֶּעָפָר); of vast extent of Solomon's wisdom 1Kgs 5:9.
\ \ b. in compare, more than the sand Jer 15:8 (widows of Judah); thoughts of God Ps 139:18.
\ \ c. of weight (in balances), metaphor of Job's vexation (כַּעַשׂ) Job 6:3 heavier than the sand, compare וְנֵטֶל הַחוֺל Prov 27:3 (|| כֹּבֶד אֶבֶן), said of כַּעַס אֱוִיל. — Combinations are: ׳כח אשׁר על שׂפת הים Gen 22:17; Josh 11:4; 1Sam 13:5; 1Kgs 5:9 הַיָּם ׳כח Gen 32:13; Gen 41:49; Isa 10:22; Hos 2:1 (+ אשׁר לא יִמַּד ולא יִסָּפֵר); הַיָּם ׳ח Jer 33:22 שֶׁעַלשֿׂפת הים לרב ׳כח Judg 7:12 אשׁרעֿלהֿים לרב ׳כח 2Sam 17:11; 1Kgs 4:20 (מ)חול יַמִּים Job 6:3; Ps 78:27; Jer 15:8.
