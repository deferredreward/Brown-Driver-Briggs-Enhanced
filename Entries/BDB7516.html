Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִקְצָכעֵץֿ 2Kgs 6:6 he cut off a stick; Passive participle feminine plural עֵדֶר הַקְּצוּבוֺת Song 4:2 a flock of (sheep) that are shorn (?).
