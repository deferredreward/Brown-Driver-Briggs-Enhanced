I. [פּוּק] verb reel, totter; —

Qal Perfect 3 plural פָּק֫וּ פְּלִילִיָּה Isa 28:7 they reel (drunken, in giving) judgement (|| שָׁגוּ, תָּעוּ, etc.); read probably also Imperfect 3 feminine singular תָּפוּק Amos 2:13 tottereth (see עוק). Hiph`il Imperfect
1. totter: 3 masculine singular יָפִיק Jer 10:4 (of idol).
2. read probably also Participle מֵפִיק Amos 2:13 cause tottering (see עוק).
