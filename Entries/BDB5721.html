Biblical Hebrew

סתו Kt , סְתָיו Qr noun masculine winter (loan-word from Aramaic; compare Syriac , ᵑ7 סִיתְוָא; Arabic Lane1504; probably originally ׳ שׂ, compare SchwIdioticon 64; on ו as old nominative ending (׳ ס for šutayu) compare LagBN 190 Anm.); — הַסְּתָ֯ו עָבָ֑ר Song 2:11.
