BDB6729 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6731

[פָּצַץ] verb break (Arabic break, break asunder; Nabataean פצץ break asunder; Syriac crush); —

Po`. Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְפֹצֵץ סָ֑לַע Jer 23:29 a hammer which shatters rock. Hithpo`el Imperfect 3 masculine plural וַיִּתְפִּצְצוּ Hab 3:6 and the eternal mountains were shattered (elsewhere √ פּוּץ were scattered). Pilpel Imperfect 3 masculine singular suffix וַיְפַצְפְּצֵנִי Job 16:12 he [God] dashed me in pieces (figurative).
