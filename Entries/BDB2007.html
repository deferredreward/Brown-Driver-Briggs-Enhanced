דָּֽרְיָ֫וֶשׁ proper name, masculine Darius (Old Persian Dârayaua'ush compare SpiegAPK 81) —

1. Darius Hystaspis, 522-485 Ezra 4:5; Hag 1:1; Hag 1:15; Hag 2:10; Zech 1:1; Zech 1:7; Zech 7:1.
2. Darius Codomannus, 336-332 Neh 12:22.
3. 'Darius the Mede,' Dan 9:1; Dan 11:1, compare BevDan. p. 19.
