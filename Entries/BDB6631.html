[פְּלָדָה] noun feminine usually iron, steel (origin dubious; steel in Arabic is , but Vulgar Arabic ; Syriac (in Lexicons); loan-word from Persian according to LagGes. Abh. 75, compare NöZMG xxx(1876), 769, but uncertain, compare Now and references); — plural [absolute!] בְּאֵשׁפְּֿלָדֹת הָרֶכֶב Nah 2:4 < ׳ כָּאֵשׁ פִּלְדֹת וגו We Now, like fire the steel (fittings) of the chariots.
