מִשְׁלַח noun [masculine]

1. outstretching;
2. place of letting loose; — ׳ מ only construct:
1. ׳ יַד פ ׳ בְּכָלמֿ in every outstretching of one's hand, = every undertaking, Deut 12:7; Deut 12:18; Deut 15:10; Deut 23:21; Deut 28:8; Deut 28:20.
2. ׳ למ שׁוֺר וּלְמִרְמַס שֶׂה Isa 7:25 (√ Pi`el 3 especially Isa 32:20).
