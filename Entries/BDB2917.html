מַחֲנֶה214 noun masculineGen 33:8 + 16 t. and femininePs 27:3; 1Chr 11:15 (on Gen 32:9 a see Di) encampment, camp — absolute ׳מ Gen 32:9 (twice in verse) + 126 t. (including Ezek 1:24, strike out Co); construct מַחֲנֵה Gen 32:3 + 56 t.; suffix מַחֲנֵהוּ Num 1:52 + 5 t.; מַחֲנֶיךָ Deut 23:15; Deut 29:10, מַחֲנֶךָ Deut 23:15, מַחֲנֵיכֶם Amos 4:10, מַהֲנֵיהֶם Num 5:3; Judg 8:10 (compare below, and on form of noun with suffix see Ges«GKC:93»§ 93. 3, R 3); plural absolute מִחֲנות Gen 32:8 + 9 t.; construct 1Chr 9:18 + 2 t., מַחֲנִים Num 13:19 suffix (apparetnly plural noun) מַחֲנֵיהֶם Josh 10:4 + 5 t.; — on dual see ׳מ proper name below

1. camp, place of encampment:
\ \ a. of caravan of travelers Gen 32:22 (J), especially of Israel at Exodus and in wilderness Exod 16:13 (twice in verse); Exod 19:16; Exod 19:17 (all P; distinguished from עַם); ׳שַׁעַר מ Exod 32:26 (JE); especially ׳מִחוּץ המ in connection with uncleanness, etc., Exod 29:14; Lev 4:12 (P) + often, of position of tent of meeting Exod 33:7 (twice in verse) (JE), contr. Num 2:17 (P), where of encampments of several tribes, compare Num 1:52; Num 2:3; Num 10:2; Num 10:5; Num 10:6; Num 10:25 etc., (all P), already passing over to 3 (compare הַחֹנִים Num 10:5; Num 10:6); specifically of camp of Levites, surrounding tabernacle, so that P can say אֹהֶלמֿוֺעֵד מַחֲנֵה הַלְוִיִם Num 2:17 and so (late) of temple הַשֹּׁעֲרִים לְמַחֲנוֺת בְּנֵי לֵוִי 1Chr 9:18, as well as ׳שַׁעֲרֵי מַחֲנוֺת י 2Chr 31:2 plural (opposed to מִבְצָרִים = fortresses) Num 13:19.
2. camp of armed host Josh 6:11 (twice in verse); Josh 6:14 (JE), 1Sam 4:3; 1Sam 4:5; 1Sam 4:6 (twice in verse); 1Sam 4:7; 1Sam 17:53; 2Kgs 7:16 etc.
3. a. those who encamp Num 10:5; Num 10:6; 1Chr 11:15; Ps 27:3 (all with verb חנה) compare (probably) Isa 37:36 = 2Kgs 19:35, etc.
\ \ b. company, body of people; people and beasts Gen 32:8; Gen 32:9 (twice in verse); Gen 32:11; Gen 33:8, funeral company of Jacob Gen 50:9 of Israel on march Exod 14:19; Exod 14:20 compare Num 10:5; Num 10:6.
\ \ c. army, host Josh 8:13; Josh 10:5; Josh 11:4; Judg 4:15; Judg 4:16 (twice in verse); Judg 7:1; Judg 7:8; Judg 8:10 (twice in verse); Judg 8:11 (twice in verse); Judg 8:12; 1Sam 17:1; 1Sam 17:46; 2Kgs 3:9 even while in the thick of the fight; 1Kgs 22:34 = 2Chr 18:33 etc.
