BDB7587 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7589

קָרְחָה noun feminine baldness, bald spot, made as sign of mourning; — absolute ׳ק Isa 3:24 +; suffix קָרְחָתֵח Mic 1:16 — bald spot, עַלכָּֿלרֿאֹשׁ Amos 8:10, ׳בְּכָלרֿ Isa 15:2; Ezek 7:18 בְּראֹשׁ ׳ק Lev 21:5 (accusative of congnate meaning with verb); ׳כָּלרֿאֹשׁ ק Jer 48:37 כַנֶּשֶׁר ׳ק Mic 1:16 בֵּין עֵינֵיכֶם Deut 14:1 opposed to מַעֲשֶׂה מִקְשֶׁה Isa 3:24 also Jer 47:5 and (accusative of congnate meaning with verb) Ezek 27:31.
