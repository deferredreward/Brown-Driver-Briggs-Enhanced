BDB9048 [H8292]

Biblical Hebrew

[שְׁרִיקָה] noun [feminine] hissing, perhaps also whistling, piping; — plural absolute (intensive) שְׁרִיקֹת Jer 18:16 Qr hissing, in derision, + שַׁמָּה (see שְׁרֵקָה ), Kt שרוקת; construct שְׁרִקוֺת עֲדָרִים Judg 5:16 as signal hissings (or whistlings, pipings) for flocks.
