BDB6409 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6411

עָרִיץ adjective awe-inspiring, terror-striking; — ׳ע of ׳י, כְּגִבּוֺר עָרִיץ Jer 20:11 plural as substantive עָרִיצִים, in bad sense of formidable adversaries, personal, Jer 15:21 (|| רָעִים), Job 6:23 (|| צָר), and national, Ps 54:5 (|| זָרִים), = ׳עֲדַת ע Ps 86:14 (|| זֵדִים), ׳הֲמוֺן ע Isa 29:5 (|| זָרָ֑יִח); especially of Chaldeans Isa 13:11, construct עָרִצֵי גוֺיִם most terrifying of the nations Ezek 28:7 (|| זָרִים) Ezek 30:11; Ezek 31:12 (|| זָרִים), Ezek 32:12 so (probably) singular עָרִיץ Isa 49:25 (read ׳ע also for צַדִּיק vIsa 49:24, so Lo Ew Che Gr and others); without specific reference גּוֺיִם עָרִיצִים Isa 25:3 awe-inspiring nations (Du Che take ׳ע as substantive), ׳רִוּחַ ע vIsa 25:4 (strike out as gloss Di and others), ׳זְמִיר ע vIsa 25:5 (strike out verse as gloss Du Che); late, of wicked in general, as ruthless ׳רָאִיתִי רָשָׁע ע Ps 37:35 I have seen a wicked man ruthless; as substantive, Isa 29:20 (|| לֵץ), Job 15:20 (|| רשׁע), plural Job 27:13 (|| id.), Prov 11:16 read also עָרִיצִ(יםׅ Isa 11:4 (for ᵑ0 אֶרֶץ), so Che BrMP Du Gr and others (|| רָשָׁע).
