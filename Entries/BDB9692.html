1. volunteer, followed by infinitive, Ezra 7:13.
2. give, or offer freely, vEzra 7:15, accusative of thing + ל of God; vEzra 7:16, with ל of temple; freewill gift (infinitive, properly freewill giving) vEzra 7:16.
