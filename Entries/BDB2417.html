1. be indignant, have indignation, of hostile prince עַל בְּרִית קוֺדֶשׁ Dan 11:30 elsewhere of Yahweh, who is אֵל זֹעֵם Ps 7:12 (|| שׁוֺפֵט), with accusative of enemy Isa 66:14 cities of Judah Zech 1:12 people Mal 1:4 a man Prov 22:14 .
2.
[2 is probably denominative from זַעַם (see Buhl).] express indignation in speech, denounce, curse || יִקְּבֻהוּ Prov 24:24 || אֶקֹּב and אָֽרָהֿ Num 23:7; Num 23:8 (twice in verse) ( E ); זְעוּמָה אֵיפַת רָזוֺן Mic 6:10 an ephah of scantiness, denounced, or cursed.
