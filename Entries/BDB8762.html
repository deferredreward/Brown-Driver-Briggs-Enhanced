(3 + 10 = 13 are additional; on rare exceptions to rule of gender compare Ges «GKC:97c»§ 97c), suffix שְׁלָשְׁתְּכֶם Num 12:4, שְׁלָשְׁתָּם vNum 12:4; Ezek 40:10; Ezek 41:16 — three (Hexateuch chiefly P ):

1. no other number:
a. before plural noun, שָׁלשׁ 51 t., e.g. אַמוֺת ׳ שׁ Exod 27:1 +, בָּאַמָּה ׳ שׁ 1Kgs 7:27, שְׁלשָׁה 90 t., e.g. Gen 18:2 + (so always שְׁלשׁ, שְׁלשֶׁת; read מִשְׁלשֶׁת Gen 38:24 Sam Di; = ׳ מִשְּׁ, from מִןֿ); after noun (late: on 1Sam 1:24 see [שִׁלֵּשׁ) 1Chr 25:5 + 17 t.; noun omitted 1Sam 17:4 ; 2Sam 24:12 = 42 t.
b. = ordinal יָמִים ׳ לִשׁ Exod 19:5 on third day; ׳ הַיּוֺם שׁ הַיָּמִים 1Sam 9:20, ׳ הַיּוֺם הַשּׁ 1Sam 30:13, both = three days ago; especially לְ ׳ בִּשְׁנַת שׁ 1Kgs 15:28 + 7 t. (Ges«GKC:134o»§ 134o).
2. 13, with noun feminine: שְׁלשׁ עֶשְׂרֵהשָׁנַה Gen 17:25; 1Kgs 7:1 + (as ordinal) Gen 14:4 ( שְׁלשֿׁ), Jer 1:2; Jer 25:3 before ערים Josh 21:19 after, Josh 19:16 + 3 t., before עִיר Josh 21:33; 1Chr 6:43, אַמּוֺת Ezek 40:11 with noun masculine שְׁלשָׁה עָשָׂר before פָּרִים Num 29:14 after, vNum 29:13 ordinal before יוֺם Esth 3:12; Esth 9:1 after, vEsth 9:17 יוֺם omitted Esth 3:13; Esth 8:12; Esth 9:18 גוֺרָל omitted 1Chr 24:13; 1Chr 25:20; 1Chr 26:11.
3. 300 = שְׁלשׁ מֵאוֺת Gen 5:22 + 59 t. ( 1Chr 11:11 perhaps intentional change [HPSSm] from שְׁמֹנֶה || 2Sam 23:8 which certainly original, Vrss Th We Dr and modern); 3000 = שְׁלשֶׁת אֲלָפִים Exod 32:28 +28 t.
