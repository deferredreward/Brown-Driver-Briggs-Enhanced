נֶ֫שֶׁק, נֵ֫שֶׁק noun [masculine] equipment, weapons (collective) armoury; — absolute נֶ֫שֶׁק Ezek 39:9 + 2 t., נֵ֫שֶׁק 1Kgs 10:25; 2Chr 9:24, נָָֽשֶׁק 2Kgs 10:2 + 2 t., construct ׳נֶ Isa 22:8, ׳נֵ Job 20:24 —

1. equipment, weapons 1Kgs 10:25; 2Kgs 10:2; Isa 22:8; 2Chr 9:24; Ezek 39:9 (general term, followed by specific), vEzek 39:10 בַרְזֶל ׳נ Job 20:24 (קֶשֶׁת נְחשֶׁת); יוֺם נָָֽשֶׁק Ps 140:8 i.e. day of battle; so ׳נ alone Job 39:21.
2. apparently armoury Neh 3:19.
