צֹאָה noun feminine filth; — absolute ׳ צ Isa 28:8 (of drunkards' vomit, compare קִיא); ׳ צ figurative of iniquity, construct צֹאַת Isa 4:4, suffix צֹאָתוֺ Prov 30:12 specifically of human excrement ( = [צֵאָה]) suffix צֵאָתָם 2Kgs 18:27 = Isa 36:12. (both Qr ; Kt חר(א)יהם, compare [חֶרֶא] p. 351 above).
