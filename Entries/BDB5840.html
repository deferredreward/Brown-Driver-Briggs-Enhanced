III. [עָדַר] verb Niph`al be lacking, fail (Arabic remain or lag behind); —

Niph`al Perfect, all with לֹא 3 masculine singular נֶעְדַּר לָהֶם 1Sam 30:19 not anything was lacking to them; נֶעְדָּ֑ר none was lacking 2Sam 17:22 ; Isa 40:25 of ׳ י, Zeph 3:5 he doth not fail; 3 feminine singular נֶעְדָּ֔רָה Isa 34:16 no wild beast is lacking. Participle feminine נֶעְדֶּ֫רֶת Isa 59:15 truth has become lacking.
Pi`el Imperfect 3 masculine plural לֹא יְעֲדְּרוּ דָּבָּ֑ר 1Kgs 5:7 they left nothing lacking.
