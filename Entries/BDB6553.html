BDB6552 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6554

פּוּנֹן proper name, of a location 2nd station of Israel from Hor Num 33:42; Num 33:43, Φ(ε)ινω; between Petra and Zoar, famous for mines, Greek Φινων, Φαινων EusebOnom. ed. Lag. 299, 85, see also RitterErdkunde, Asien. xiv, 125 ff. Seetzen:Reise iii, 17 (Kálaét Phenân); perhaps = Edomite proper name, masculine פִּינֹן q. v.
