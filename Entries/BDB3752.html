BDB3751 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3753

יָרָק noun [masculine] herbs (collective), herbage (green, greens) — absolute יָרָק Deut 11:10 + 2 t.; construct יְרַק Isa 37:27 = 2Kgs 19:26 — גַּןיָֿרָק Deut 11:10; 1Kgs 21:2 אֲרֻחַת יָרָק Prov 15:17 as substantive, יְרַק דֶּשֶׁא Isa 37:27 = 2Kgs 19:26 green shoots of grass (|| עֵשֶׂב שָׂדֶה, etc.)
