BDB2298 [H2096]

BDB2297 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2299

זֹ֫הַר noun [masculine] shining, brightness (Arabic , Late Hebrew זְהוֺר, compare Aramaic זִיהֲרָא); — absolute Ezek 8:2 ׳ כְּמַרְאֵה ז like an appearance of shining (explained, vb, as resembling amber, חַשְׁמַל compare Ezek 1:27); also construct Dan 12:3 see foregoing.
