BDB244 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB246

אוּרִיאֵל proper name, masculine (flame of El or my light is El, see אֵל below אלה; compare Phoenician ארמלך in Assyrian Urumilki (SenTaylor Cylind.ii.50, compare COT2Kgs 18:13))

1. chief of Levitical line of Kohath, in time of David 1Chr 6:9; 1Chr 15:5; 1Chr 15:11.
2. maternal grandfather of Abijah 2Chr 13:2.
