BDB9201 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9203

I. [תָּנָה] verb hire (si vera lectio, akin to נתן); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine plural יִתְּנוּ בַגּוֺיִם Hos 8:10 hire (allies) among the nations;
Hiph`il Perfect 3 plural הִתְנוּ vHos 8:9 have hired lovers. — We in both יִתְּנוּ give: vHos 8:9 give amorous favours, vHos 8:10 give (these), compare Now Marti Harper. — תְּנָה Ps 8:2 see נתן Qal 2.b.
