I. מַסֵּכָה noun feminine

1. molten metal, or image;
2. libation; — ׳ מ Exod 32:4 +; construct מַסֵּכַת Isa 30:22 plural מַסֵּכוֺת 1Kgs 14:9 +; suffix מַסֵּכֹתָם Num 33:52 —
1. libation, with covenant sacrifice in making covenant Isa 30:1 (= 1. נֶסֶךְ; see, however, below II. [נָסַךְ]).
2. molten metal, or image: עֵגֶל מַסֵּכָה molten calf Exod 32:4; Exod 32:8; Deut 9:16; Neh 9:18, compare Ps 106:19 אֱלֹהֵי מַסֵּכָה molten gods Exod 34:17; Lev 19:4 ׳ צַלְמֵי מ Num 33:52 פֶּסֶל וּמַסֵּכָה Deut 27:15; Judg 17:3; Judg 17:4; Judg 18:14; Nah 1:14; 2Chr 34:3; 2Chr 34:4, elsewhere (variously) Deut 9:12; Judg 18:17; Judg 18:18; 1Kgs 14:9; 2Kgs 17:16; 2Chr 28:2; Isa 30:22; Isa 42:17; Hos 13:2; Hab 2:18.
