[מַפָּל] noun masculine Job 41:15 refuse, hanging parts; —

1. fallings, refuse, construct מַפַּל בַּר Amos 8:6 the refuse of wheat.
2. plural construct מַפְּלֵי בְשָׂרוֺ Job 41:15 the hanging (falling, drooping) parts of his flesh (of crocodile).
