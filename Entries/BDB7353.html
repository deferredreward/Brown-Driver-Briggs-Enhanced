II. [קָוָה] verb collect (Late Hebrew Hiph`il collect); —

Niph`al be collected, Perfect 3 plural וְנִקְווּ consecutive Jer 3:17 (of nations); Imperfect 3 masculine plural יִקָּווּ Gen 1:9 ( P ; of waters; both with אֶל location); so probably of ships (with ל person) Isa 60:9 (for ᵑ0 יְקַוּוּ), see I. קזה Pi`el 1.
