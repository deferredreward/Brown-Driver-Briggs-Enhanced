BDB4455 [H4457]

Biblical Hebrew

מֶלְקָחַ֫יִם, [מַלְקָחַיִם] noun [masculine] dual tongs, snuffers; —

1. tongs used at altar of temple, for lifting coal, in Isaiah's vision, Isa 6:6.
2. lamp snuffers, in temple 1Kgs 7:49 = 2Chr 4:21 in tabernacle suffix ( reference to lamp-stand, מְנוֺרָה) מַלְקָחֶיהָ Exod 25:38; Exod 37:23; Num 4:9 (all P ).
