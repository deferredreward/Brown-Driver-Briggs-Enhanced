מַתְכֹּ֫נֶת noun feminine measurement, tale, proportion; —

1. construct הַלְּבֵנִים ׳ מ Exod 5:3 ( J ) the tale of bricks (compare 1.תֹּכֶן).
2. suffix בֻּנְתּוֺ- measurement, proportion, of ephah and bath Ezek 45:11 of composition of sacred oil Exod 30:32, תָּהּ- vExod 30:37 ( P ); of temple, its measurement, proportion תּוֺ- 2Chr 24:13.

תַּכְּרִיךְ see כרך . תֵּל and compounds see תלל .
