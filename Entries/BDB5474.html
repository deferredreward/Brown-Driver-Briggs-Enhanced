BDB5473 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5475

סְבָאִים noun plural, of a people Seba'im, ᵐ5 οἱ Σαβαειμ, A Σεβωειμ; — אַנְשֵׁי מִדָּה ׳וּס Isa 45:14 (+ כּוּשׁ, || מִצְֹרַיִם, compare Isa 43:3 above); this perhaps intended also by Ezek 23:42 Qr (see סָבָא), perhaps confounding it with שְׁבָא, compare ᵑ6.
