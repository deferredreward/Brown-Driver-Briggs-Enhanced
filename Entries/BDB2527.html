BDB2527 [H4225]

BDB2526 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2528

מַחְבֶּ֫רֶת noun feminine thing joined, place of joining; — absolute ׳ מ Exod 26:4 + 3 t.; מַחְבָּ֑רֶת Exod 36:11; Exod 36:17 suffix מַחְבַּרְתּוֺ Exod 28:27; Exod 39:20 —

1. thing joined, of curtain-pieces of tabernacle (compare חֹבֶרֶת), Exod 26:4; Exod 26:5; Exod 36:11 (twice in verse); Exod 36:12.
2. place of joining:
a. of the curtains Exod 36:17.
b. of shoulder-pieces of the ephod Exod 28:27; Exod 39:20 (all P ).
