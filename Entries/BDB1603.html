BDB1602 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1604

גִּזְרָה noun feminine cutting, separation — גִּזְרָה Ezek 41:12 + 6 t.; suffix גִּזְרָתָם Lam 4:7 —

1. cutting, i.e. polishing ( AV RV or carving, i.e. beauty of from, shape, compare VB ) סַפִּיר גִּזְרָתָם Lam 4:7 their polishing, (or beauty of form) was as sapphire.
2. separation, הַגִּזְרָה Ezek 41:12 ; Ezek 41:13; Ezek 41:14; Ezek 41:15; Ezek 42:1; Ezek 42:10; Ezek 42:13, separate place AV RV compare Da; i.e. yard, or space adjoining temple on three sides.
