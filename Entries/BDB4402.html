BDB4402 [H3908]

BDB4401 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4403

לַ֫חַשׁ noun [masculine] a whispering, charming; — ׳ ל Isa 26:16 לָ֑חַשׁ Eccl 10:11; Isa 3:3; Jer 8:17 plural לְחָשִׁים Isa 3:20. —

1. serpent-charming Eccl 10:11 נְבוֺן לָ֑חַשׁ Isa 3:3 (probably more general).
2. charms, amulets, worn by women, Isa 3:20.
3. whisper of prayer, צָקוּן לַחַשׁ Isa 26:16 they pour forth a whisper of prayer (but Koppe Bö Gr Di Du read צְקוֺן לַחַשׁ 'Zauberzwang,' compulsion of (by) magic).
