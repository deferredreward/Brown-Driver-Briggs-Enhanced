BDB9057 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9059

שֹׁ֫רֶשׁ noun masculine Deut 29:17 root; — ׳ שׁ absolute Deut 29:17 +, construct Isa 11:10 +; suffix שָׁרְשִׁי Job 29:19, etc.; plural construct שָׁרְשֵׁי Job 13:27 (see below) Job 36:31 0, suffix שָֽׁרָשָׁיו Amos 2:9 +, שָֽׁרָשֶׁיהָ Ezek 17:7 +; —

1. root of people under figure of tree, involving firmness, permanence, Amos 2:9; Hos 9:16; Hos 14:6; Isa 14:30; 2Kgs 19:30 = Isa 37:31 — שָׁרְשָׁם Judg 5:14 is probably corrupt, see GFM; — so of person (compare Phoenician, Eshmunazar11 GACookepp. 30, 36) Isa 5:24; Mal 3:19; Job 8:17; Job 18:16; Job 29:19; Prov 12:3; Prov 12:12 = stock, family Isa 11:1; Isa 11:10; Dan 11:7, compare (of serpent) Isa 14:29 = source or cause Deut 29:17, דָּבָר ׳ שׁ Job 19:28.
2. literally root of tree or shrub Jer 17:8 (simile), Ezek 31:7 (metaphor of people). Isa 53:2 (simile), Job 14:8; Job 30:4 of vine (metaphor of people) Ezek 17:6; Ezek 17:7; Ezek 17:9 (twice in verse); Ps 80:10 (accusative of cognate meaning with verb with שׁרשׁ Hiph`il).
3. root, figurative = lowest stratum, of mountain Job 28:9, of sea Job 36:30= bottom (text strange and dubious: Du רָאשֵׁי הָרִים, with אֵרוֺ in va); of feet, שָׁרְשֵׁי רַגְלַי Job 13:27 , i.e. soles Ew Di De Da and others (elsewhere כַּף), Bu place of treading, footholds; Du (arbitrarily) שָׁרְשִׁי, strike out רגלי .
