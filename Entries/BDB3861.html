BDB3860 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3862

יִתְרוֺן noun [masculine] advantage, profit, only Ecclesiastes; — absolute Eccl 1:3 + 4 t.; construct Eccl 2:13 + 4 t.; — advantage to (ל) any one, מַהיִֿתְרוֺן לָאָדָם Eccl 1:3, i.e. what advantage hath a man ? Eccl 5:15; Eccl 10:11 followed by מִן comparative = advantage beyond, more than Eccl 2:13 (twice in verse); construct יִתְרוֺן הָעוֺשֶׂה Eccl 3:9 advantage of (for) him that worketh; יִתְרוֺן אֶדֶץ Eccl 5:8 יִתְרוֺן דַּעַת Eccl 7:12 advantage of knowledge; וְיִתְרוֺן הַכְשֵׁיר חָכְמָה Eccl 10:10 an advantage for giving success is wisdom; absolute אֵין יִתְרוֺן תַּחַת הַשָּׁ֑מֶשׁ Eccl 2:11.
