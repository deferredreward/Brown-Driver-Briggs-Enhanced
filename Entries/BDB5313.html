נֹ֫פֶת noun masculine Prov 24:13 flowing honey, honey from the comb (Phoenician נפת CISi.166, 8b); — ׳ נ absolute Prov 5:3 + 3 t., construct Ps 19:11 — sweet Prov 24:13 (|| דְּבַשׁ), compare Prov 27:7 figurative of sweet words Prov 5:3, and perhaps Song 4:11 (Bu of kisses; || דְּבַשׁ , חָלָב); in full ׳ נ צוּפִים Ps 19:11 honey of (the) combs (compare with ׳ מִשְׁפְטֵי י; || דְּבַשׁ).

נֶפְתּוֺחַ see פתח .

נַפְּתּוּלִים, נַפְתָּלִי see פתל .
