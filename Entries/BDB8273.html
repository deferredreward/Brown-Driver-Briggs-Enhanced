שְׂנִיר proper name [of a mountain] Σανειρ: Amorite name of Hermon Deut 3:9, whence came cypress-trees Ezek 27:5, probably northern peak(s) of Hermon 1Chr 5:23; Song 4:8 (where + חֶרְמוֺן); so Arabic Abulfed.
