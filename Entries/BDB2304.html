BDB2303 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2305

[זָוִית] noun feminine corner (late (Aramaic זָוִיתָא, (often for Hebrew פִּנָּה, מִקְצוֺעַ), hence, as loan-word ( FräAramaic Fremdw. 11, 168) Arabic ); — Plural absolute זָוִיּוֺת Ps 144:12 construct זָוִיֹּת Zech 9:15 — וּמָֽלְאוּ כַמִּזְרָק כְּזָוִיֹּת מִוְבֵּךְַ Zech 9:15 simil. of warriors streaming with blood, see Lev 1:5; Lev 1:11 etc.; ׳ בְּנוֺתֵינוּ כְּז ׳ וגו Ps 144:12 out daughters like corner (-pillar)s, carved (so Che and others, but see II. חטב ) in palace-fashion.
