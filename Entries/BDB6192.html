BDB6192 [H8588]

BDB6191 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6193

תַּעֲנוּג noun [masculine] daintiness, luxury, exquisite delight; —

1. luxury: ׳ת Prov 19:10 elsewhere plural; construct תַּעֲנֻגוֺת Eccl 2:8.
2. suffix בְּנֵי תַּעֲנוּנָ֑יִךְ Mic 1:16 thy dainty sons; בֵּית תַּעֲנֻגֶיהָ Mic 2:9 her (their) dainty house(s), > of tender love (reading בְּנֵי Mic 2:9 We Now.
3. delight of love, plural absolute בַּתַּעֲנוּגִים Song 7:7 (PerlesAnal. 22 f. conjecture בַּת עַמִּינָדָב).
