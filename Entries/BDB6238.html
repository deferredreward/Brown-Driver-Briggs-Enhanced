II. עֲנָק noun masculine Song 4:9 necklace, neck-pendant (Aramaism according to LagBN 175); — absolute ׳ ע Song 4:9 apparently part of necklace, perhaps neck-pendant; plural עֲנָקוֺת Judg 8:26 (ornament of camels), עֲנָקִים Prov 1:9 (of youth; in figure).
