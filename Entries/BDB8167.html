Biblical Hebrew

[שָׂדַד] verb

Pi`el harrow (שׂדד id. Ecclus 38:25 margin 26; compare Assyrian šadâdu , draw, drag); — Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְשַׂדֵּד Job 39:10 (of beast); human subject, with accusative of ground Isa 28:24 (+ יִפְתַּח, || יַחֲרשׁ); יְשַׂדֶּדלֿוֺ Hos 10:11 (|| יַחֲרוֺשׁ; figurative of Jacob).
