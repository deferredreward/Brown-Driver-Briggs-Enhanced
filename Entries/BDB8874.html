שֶׁ֫מֶשׁ133 noun feminine Judg 19:14 and masculine Gen 19:23 (AlbrZAW xv (1895), 324) sun; — absolute ׳ שׁ Josh 10:12 +, שָׁ֑מֶשׁ Judg 20:43 +; suffix שִׁמְשֵׁךְ Isa 60:20, שִׁמְשָׁךְ Jer 15:9 plural suffix שִׁמְשֹׁתַ֫יִךְ Isa 54:12 —

1. sun, Gen 37:9 ( E ; + moon, stars); marking time: as rising (verb זָרַת q. v.) Exod 22:2 ( E ) + 8 t. + Mal 3:20 (figurative צְדָקָה ׳ שׁ, of reward of the faithful, with wings, i.e. rays ? Now GASm; or winged sun-disc [compare We ] ?); verb יְצָא Gen 19:23 ( J ), Isa 13:10; Judg 5:31 (simile of splendour; see יְצָא 1.f.); setting (verb בּוֺא) Gen 15:17 ( J ), Amos 8:9 + 19 t., + לָבוֺא ׳ וַיְהִי הַשּׁ Gen 15:12 ( J E ), יְדַע מְבוֺאוֺ ׳ שׁ Ps 104:19 (see בּוֺא 1.i., מָבוֺא 2); as giving daylight Isa 60:9 hot 1Sam 11:9; Neh 7:3 ; Exod 16:21 ( P ), compare Isa 49:10; Jonah 4:8; Ps 121:6 as ripening crops Deut 33:14 (poem), compare (figurative) Job 8:16, tanning the face Song 1:6 standing still at Joshua's word Josh 10:12.13 a (poem in J ; || יָרֵחַ), Josh 10:13 b ( J ), compare Hab 3:11 (+ יָרֵךְַ); casting shadow (on dial), ׳ יָָֽרְדָה בְמַעֲלוֺת אָחָז בַּשּׁ Isa 38:8, ׳ וַתָּשָׁב הַשּׁ Isa 38:8 set in sky by ׳ י Jer 31:25; Ps 19:5; Ps 74:16; Ps 136:8, enduring Ps 72:5; Ps 72:17 (compare עִם 1.g.; [פָּנֶה] 11 4 a (e), p. 817), also Ps 89:37 personified, called to praise ׳ י Ps 148:3 (+ יָרֵךְַ); to be darkened by ׳ י's judgment, Ezek 32:7; Joel 2:10; Joel 3:4; Joel 4:15 (compare Amos 8:9 above, Mic 3:6).
2. of direction: ׳ מִמִּוְרַח הַשּׁ Num 21:11 ( E ) toward sun-rise, = East, compare Deut 4:41; Deut 4:47 + 16 t. (see מִוְרָךְ p. 280); ׳ מְבוֺא הַשּׁ Deut 11:30 + 3 t. (see מָבוֺא 2, p. 99).
3. object of worship (usually + moon, stars, etc.) 2Kgs 23:5; Jer 8:2; Ezek 8:16; Deut 4:19 horses given to ׳ שׁ 2Kgs 23:11, ׳ מַרְכְּבוֺת השּׁ 2Kgs 23:11. — See also ׳ בֵּית שׁ p. 112:b.
4. other phrases:
a. ׳ נֶגֶד חַשּׁ, i.e. openly, publicly, 2Sam 12:12; Num 25:4 ( P ), so ׳ לְעֵינֵי חַשּׁ 2Sam 12:11 .
b. figurative of living, ׳ חָזָה שׁ Ps 58:9, ׳ רָאָה שׁ Exod 6:5; Exod 7:11; Exod 11:7, opposed to ׳ תֶּחְשַׁךְ הַשׁ Exod 12:2.
c. ׳ תַּחַת הַשּׁ, i. e. on the earth, Eccl 1:3 + 28 t. Ecclesiastes (compare Greek ὐφ’ ἠλίῳ, KueOnd. iii. 196; Ein1. iii. 192).
5. pinnacle, as glittering, shining, plural Isa 54:12 thy pinnacles, battlements (of Zion), so perhaps singular אֱםהִים ׳ וּמָנֵן י ׳ שׁ Ps 84:12 battlement and shield is ׳ א ׳ י (Gr Bae Du Buhl; most sun, and so Thes De Che SS; Hup-Now hesitates).
