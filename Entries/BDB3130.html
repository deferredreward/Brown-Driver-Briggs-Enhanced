BDB3129 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3131

חָרִם proper name, masculine (consecrated; compare Sabean proper name, masculine חרם, יחרמאל Hal411. 504 DHMl.c.) —

1. priest of the third course, David's time, according to 1Chr 24:8.
2. priest of time of Nehemiah Neh 10:6.
3. heads of families of returning exiles:
\ \ a. Ezra 2:39 = Neh 7:42; Ezra 10:21; Neh 12:15.
\ \ b. Ezra 2:32 = Neh 7:35; Ezra 10:31; Neh 3:11.
4. a prince Neh 10:28.
