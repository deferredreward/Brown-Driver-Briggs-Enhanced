שָׁפַךְ113 verb pour out, pour (Late Hebrew id.; Assyrian Šapâku, pour out, especially (DlWB JenKosmol. 41) earth, to form mound; Arabic pour out, compare melt and pour into mould; Ethiopic Aramaic שְׁפַח, (rare) = Biblical Hebrew); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳שׁ 1Kgs 2:31 +; 3 feminine singular suffix שְׁפָכַ֫תְהוּ Ezek 24:7, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִשְׁפֹךְ 2Kgs 19:32 +; 2 masculine singular suffix 3 masculine singular תִּשְׁפְּכֶנּוּ Deut 12:16 +, etc.; Imperative masculine singular שְׁפוֺךְ Judg 6:20 +, שְׁפָךְֿ Ps 69:25 feminine singular שִׁפְכִי Lam 2:19, etc.; Infinitive construct שְׁפָךְֿ 1Kgs 18:28 +, לִשְׁפֹךְ Isa 59:7 +, etc.; Participle active שֹׁפֵךְ Gen 9:6 +, feminine שֹׁפֶ֫כֶת Ezek 22:3, etc.; passive שָׁפוּךְ Ps 79:10, שְׁפוּכָה Ezek 20:33; Ezek 20:34 —
1. literal:
\ \ a. pour out, pour water Exod 4:9 (J; accusative of location). 1Sam 7:6 (+ ׳לִפְנֵי י, unique, symbol of contrition), עַלפְּֿנֵי הָאָרֶץ (subject ׳י) Amos 5:8; Amos 9:6 blood like water (to flow away and be absorbed), עַלהָֿאָרֶץ Deut 12:16; Deut 12:24; Deut 15:23, compare Lev 17:13 (H) Ezek 24:7 also + אֶל location Exod 29:12; Lev 4:7; Lev 4:18; Lev 4:25; Lev 4:30; Lev 4:34 + עַל person reflexive 1Kgs 18:28 pour out broth Judg 6:20, libation (נֶסֶךְ, to (ל) false gods) Isa 57:6 dust Lev 14:41 (P; אֶל location); with accusative סִלְלָה mound (in siege), = made by pouring (earth, originally from baskets, see especially Assyrian šapâku DlWB 679 b JenKosmol. 41), + עַלֿ of city 2Kgs 19:32 = Isa 37:33; Jer 6:6; Ezek 4:2; Ezek 26:8, אֶלֿ 2Sam 20:15 absolute Ezek 17:17; Ezek 21:27; Dan 11:15 מֵעָיו אַרְצָה ׳וַיִּשׁ 2Sam 20:10 (by a sword-thrust in the belly), compare לָאָרֶץ מְרֵרָתִי ׳יִשׁ Job 16:13 (metaphor, ׳י subject).
\ \ b. techn., accusative דָּם shed blood Gen 37:22 (E) Gen 9:6 (P) 1Sam 25:31; Ezek 22:4 + 9 t. Ezekiel, + 15 t. (passive Ps 79:10) + Ezek 36:18 (with עַלהָֿאַרֶץ), 1Chr 22:8 (with אַ֫רְצָה); accusative
\ \ a. דָּמִים 1Kgs 2:31; 1Chr 28:3; Ps 79:3 (כַּמַּיִם).
2. figurative:
\ \ a. ׳י subject, pour out anger, etc.: ׳עֲלֵיהֶם אֶשׁ כַּמַּיִם עֶבְרָתִי Hos 5:10 חֲמָתִי עַל ׳שׁ Ezek 14:19 + 8 t. Ezekiel; Jer 10:25; Isa 42:25; Ps 79:6 (אֶל and עַל); accusative ׳ח omitted Jer 6:11 with ב of location Lam 2:4 שְׁפוּכָה ׳ח Ezek 20:33; Ezek 20:34 accusative חֲרוֺן אַפּוֺ Lam 4:11 accusative זַעַם (עַל person) Ezek 21:36 + 3 t.; accusative בּוּז contempt, עַל person, Job 12:21 = Ps 107:40 pour their own wickedness עֲלֵיהֶם Jer 14:16 (i.e. requite it); pour out רוּחִי עַל Ezek 39:29, compare Zech 12:10; Joel 3:1; Joel 3:2.
\ \ b. hum. subject pour out one's heart, etc., כַּמַּיִם לִבֵח Lam 2:19, לְפָנָיו לְבַבְכֶם Ps 62:9 (i.e. before ׳י), ׳אֶתנַֿפְשִׁי לִפְנֵי י 1Sam 1:15, עָלַי (reflexive; see עַל 1 d) Ps 42:5 ׳אֶתשִֿׂיחוֺ לִפְנֵי י Ps 102:1 (title), compare Ps 142:3 in bad sense, וַתִּשְׁפְּכִי אֶתתַּֿזְנוּתַיִח עַלֿ Ezek 16:15, compare Ezek 23:8.
