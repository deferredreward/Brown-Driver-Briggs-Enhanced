3. in a comparative sense, above, beyond, Dan 3:19; Dan 6:4.
4. expressing direction:
a. to, of a person, after a verb of motion (compare Biblical Hebrew 7.c . a a), Dan 2:24; Dan 4:31; Dan 4:33; Dan 6:7; Dan 6:16; Ezra 4:12 ; Ezra 4:23 + often; after to send (a letter, etc.) Ezra 4:11; Ezra 4:17 ; Ezra 4:18; Ezra 5:6 +, write Ezra 4:7, prophesy Ezra 5:1 of the direction of the mind (ib. 7.c. c), Dan 3:12; Dan 6:14 (twice in verse); Dan 6:15 compare Ezra 5:5 הֲוַת עַל ׳ וְעֵין אֱל (compare Jer 40:4 and אֶל Ps 34:16 ).
