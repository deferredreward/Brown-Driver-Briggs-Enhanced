BDB5188 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5190

נְכאֹת noun feminine a spice, (usually understood of tragacanth gum, exuding from astralagus bush, Di Gen 27:25 and references ; NowArchaeology i. 64 (and 249); compare also Löw§ 24; but = Arabic = summitas rubicunda sive flores plantae (i.e. a certain plant, Frey), according to HomAufsätze 4 ); — absolute ׳ נ Gen 37:25 ( + צְרִי, לּט as merchandise), Gen 43:11 (+ id., also דְּבַשׁ , בָּטְנִים, שְׁקֵדִים, as gift).
