מִצְרִי adjective [of a people, Egyptian]; — masculine singular ׳ מ Gen 39:1 + 16 t.; feminine singular מִעְרִית Gen 16:1 + 3 t.; masculine plural מִצְרִים Gen 12:12 + 5 t.; feminine plural מִצְרִיֹּת Exod 1:19 —

1. adjective ׳ אִישׁ מ = an Egyptian Gen 39:1; Exod 2:11; Exod 2:19; Lev 24:10; 1Sam 30:11; 2Sam 23:30 ׳ הָאִישׁ הַמּ 1Chr 11:23 ׳ נָעָר מ 1Sam 30:13, ׳ עֶבֶד מ 1Chr 2:34 שִׁפְחָה מִצְרִית Gen 16:1, כַּנָּשִׁים הַמִּצְרִיֹּת Exod 1:19.
2. with article = substantive the Egyptian Gen 39:2; Gen 39:5; Exod 2:12; Exod 2:14; 2Sam 23:30 (twice in verse); 1Chr 11:23 (twice in verse); so once without article מִצְרִי Deut 23:8 הַמִּצְרִי once (late) collective = the Egyptians Ezra 9:1 (where with Canaanitish peoples); plural = the Egyptians, הַמִּצְרִים Gen 12:12; Gen 12:14; Gen 43:22; Deut 26:6; Josh 24:7 feminine singular הָגָּר הַמִּצְרִית Hagar the Egyptian woman Gen 16:3; Gen 21:9; Gen 25:12.
