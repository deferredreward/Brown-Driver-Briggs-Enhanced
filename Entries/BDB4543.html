BDB4542 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4544

מוֺט noun [masculine] shaking, pole, bar of yoke; — absolute ׳ מ Num 4:10 + 5 t.; suffix מֹטֵ֫הוּ Nah 1:13 —

1. a shaking; tottering (c. negative, of security) לַצַּדִּיק לֹאיִֿתֵּן ׳ מ Ps 55:23 רַגְלֵנוּ ׳ לֹא נָתַן לַמ Ps 66:9, compare Ps 121:3 .
2. pole, or bar for carrying (named from springing motion) Num 4:10; Num 4:12 compare especially Num 13:23.
3. bar of yoke Nah 1:13 (אֶשְׁבֹּר מֹטֵהוּ מֵעָלַיַךְ), figurative of oppression. compare following.
