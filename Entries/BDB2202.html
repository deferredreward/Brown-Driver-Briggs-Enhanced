BDB2201 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2203

[מַהֲמֹרָה] noun feminine flood (compare Arabic √), or watery pit (properly place of flowing waters; compare Symm βοθύνους, and Late Hebrew, NHWBiii. 40) — only plural מַהֲמֹרוֺת Ps 140:11 (Gr Che מכמרות, nets, see below כמר; compare רֶשֶׁת vPs 140:6).
