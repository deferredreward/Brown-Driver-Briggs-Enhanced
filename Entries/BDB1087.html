בֵּיתרְֿחוֺב proper name, of a location (place of street, or market ?) near Dan on road to Hamath Judg 18:28; 2Sam 10:6 (where אֲרַם בֵּיתרְֿחוֺב) = רְחֹב Num 13:21 compareאֲרַם צוֺבָא וּרְחוֺב 2Sam 10:8 — location dubious (compare Robiii. 371 f.).
