BDB8622 [H7849]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׁטַח] verb spread, spread abroad (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic spread out; so Ethiopic Aramaic שְּׁטַח ); —

Qal Perfect 3 plural suffix consecutive לַשֶּׁמֶשׁ וּטָחוּם Jer 8:2 they shall spread them abroad, etc., accusative of many separate things, so Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַקתִּשְׁטַךְ 2Sam 17:19 ( עַל location; distinguished from פָּרַשׂ spread covering); 3 masculine plural + Infinitive absolute וַיִּשְׁטְחוּ לָהֶם שָׁטוֺחַ Num 11:32 ( J ) they spread (that is, the quails) for themselves all abroad round about the camp (i.e. to dry them; ᵑ9 siccaverunt; compare Di Gray); Participle שֹׁטֵחַ לַנּוֺיִם Job 12:23 expanding the nations (compare ל 3.b.).
Pi`el Perfect 1 singular שִׁטַּחְתִּי אֵלֶיךָ כַפָּ֑י Ps 88:10 I have spread out unto thee my palms.
