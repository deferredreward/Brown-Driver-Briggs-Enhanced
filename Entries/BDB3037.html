מַחֲצִית noun feminine half, middle — construct מַחֲצִית Exod 30:13 (twice in verse) + 8 t.; מַחֲצִת Num 31:30; Num 31:47 suffix מַחֲצִיתוֺ Exod 30:23, מַחֲצִיתָהּ Lev 6:13 (twice in verse), מַחֲצִיתָם Num 31:29 —

1. half of a shekel Exod 30:13 (twice in verse); Exod 30:15 compare vExod 30:23; Exod 38:26 the flour for offering Lev 6:13 (twice in verse), of spoils Num 31:29; Num 31:30; Num 31:42; Num 31:47 of tribe Josh 21:25 (all P), 1Chr 6:46; 1Chr 6:55 of chariots 1Kgs 16:9.
2. middle היּוֺם ׳מ = midday, noon Neh 8:3 (compare midnight, חָצוֺת, חֲצִי 2).
