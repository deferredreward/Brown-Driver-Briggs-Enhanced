עָמֹק17 adjective deep (compare BaNB § 23 b); —

1. ׳ ע literal Lev 13:3 + 6 t. Lev 13 feminine עֲמֻקָּה of cup Ezek 23:32, trench Prov 22:14; Prov 23:27 (all 3 in simile); מִשְּׁאוֺל ׳ ע Job 11:8 (figurative); plural מַיִם עֲמֻקִּים (simile) Prov 18:4; Prov 20:5.
2. = unsearchable עָמֹקּ Ps 64:7; Eccl 7:24 (twice in verse); plural עֲמֻקוֺת (Baer עֲמוּקוֺת, van d. H. עֲמֻקּוֺת) Job 12:22.
