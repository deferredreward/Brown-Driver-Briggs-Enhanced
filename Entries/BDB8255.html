[שִׂמְאֵל] verb denominative Hiph`il take the left (always opposed to הֵמִין): — Imperfect cohortative אַשְׂמְאִ֫ילָה Gen 13:9 2 masculine plural, תַּשְׂמְאִ֫ילוּ Isa 30:21 Imperative feminine singular הַשְׂמִילִי (Ges«GKC:23f»§ 23f Kö1. 276) Ezek 21:21, compare Infinitive construct הַשְׂמִיל 2Sam 14:19 Participle מַשְׂמִאלִים 1Chr 12:2 —

1. = go to the left Gen 13:9 (J), Ezek 21:21.
2. figurative turn (aside) to the left (from true way) 2Sam 14:19; Isa 30:21.
3. use the left hand 1Chr 12:2.
