אָפַף verb surround, encompass (Assyrian apâpu ZimBP 59) —

Qal Perfect אָֽפְפוּ Ps 40:13 ;אְפָפוּנִי Ps 18:5 +; — encompass (poetry) literal Jonah 2:6 (subject מַיִם); figurative, subject evils & misfortunes רָעוֺת Ps 40:13 (followed by עָלַי); מִשְׁבְּרֵי מות 2Sam 22:5, compare ׳חֶבְלֵי מ Ps 18:5, also Ps 116:3.
