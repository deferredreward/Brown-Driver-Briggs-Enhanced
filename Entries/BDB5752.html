BDB5751 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5753

עָבָה verb be thick, fat, gross (Late Hebrew Pi`el עִבָּה make thick, and derivatives; Syriac swell up, passive participle swollen, thick, dense, stupid, and many derivatives; Arabic be dense, stupid, dense foliage; Ethiopic be great Di985); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular מִמָּתְנֵי אָבִי ׳קָֽטֳנִּי ע 1Kgs 12:10 my little finger is thicker (stouter) than, etc., = 2Chr 10:10 be thick, gross, of rebell. Israel under figure of highly fed beast, 2 masculine singular שָׁמַנְתָּ עָבִיתָ כָּשִׂיתָ Deut 32:15.
