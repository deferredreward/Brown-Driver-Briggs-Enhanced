BDB9850 [H6927 H6928]

BDB9849 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9851

[קַדְמָה] noun feminine former time (compare Biblical Hebrew קַדְמָה , Zinjirli קדמה (Cooke177), former state); — construct Dan 6:11 מִןקַּֿדְמַת דְּנָה = before this, formerly; so Ezra 5:11 ׳ מִקַּדְמַת דְּ. compare Gen 28:19 ᵑ7 j , Ezek 38:17 ᵑ7 .
