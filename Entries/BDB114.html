Biblical Hebrew

אָדֹם adjective red — ׳ א Isa 63:2; Zech 1:8 + Gen 25:30 (twice in verse), see below; אָדוֺם Song 5:10 feminine אֲדֻמָּה Num 19:2, plural אֲדֻמִּים 2Kgs 3:22 + 2 t.; — ruddy, red, of man Song 5:10 horse Zech 1:8 (twice in verse) (|| שָׂרֹק compare Assyrian DlW 87) Zech 6:2 (|| שָׁחֹר); heifer Num 19:2 water 2Kgs 3:22 (כַּדָּם ׳ א); compare as substantive red, redness on garment Isa 63:2 הָאָדֹם = the (red) lentils Gen 25:30 (twice in verse), but read הָאֱדֹם see below; compare also מַעֲלֶה.
