הֵרָיוֺן noun [masculine] conception, pregnancy; — ׳ ה absolute Hos 9:11; Ruth 4:13 suffix הֵרֹנֵךְ Gen 3:16, either contracted, so Ew§ 214 a, or erroneous for הריונך which stands in ⅏ compare Di; — conception, in combination מִלֵּדָה וּמִבֶּטֶן וּמֵהֵרָי֑וֺן Hos 9:11 וַתֵּלֶד ׳ לָהּ ה ׳ וַיִּתֵּן י Ruth 4:13 עִצְּבוֺנֵךְ וְהֵרֹנֵךְ Gen 3:16 (|| תֵּלְדִי).
