BDB6242 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6244

[עָנַשׁ] verb denominative (Gerber61 f.) fine, mulct; —

Qal Perfect consecutive וְעָֽנְשׁוּ Deut 22:19 (2 accusative); Imperfect וַיַּעֲנשׁ 2Chr 36:3 (2 accusative; || 2Kgs 23:33 above); Infinitive construct עֲנוֺשׁ Prov 17:26 (לְ person); = punish (in general) עֲנָשֿׁ Prov 21:11 Passive participle עֲנוּשִׁם Amos 2:8 those fined, mulcted. Niph`al Imperfect + Qal Infinitive absolute עָנוֺשׁ יֵעָנֵשׁ Exod 21:22 (E) he shall be strictly fined; more generally Perfect נֶעֱנָ֑שׁוּ Prov 22:3 be mulcted, punished = Prov 27:12.
