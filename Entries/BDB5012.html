BDB5011 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5013

נְהִי noun [masculine] wailing, lamentation, mourning song; — ׳נ absolute Amos 5:16 + 5 t.; וָנֶהִ֑י Jer 9:9 — wailing, at ׳י's judgement Mic 2:4 as accusative of congnate meaning with verb; Jer 9:9 (c.נָשָׂא עַלֿ, + ׳בְּכִ, || קִינָה ), v.Jer 9:17 (c, id.,|| דִּמְעָה), v.Jer 9:18 (׳קוֺל נ ), Jer 31:15 (|| בְּכִי, תַּמְרוּרִים); mourning song ׳יוֺדְעֵי נ Amos 5:16 of professional mourners, skilled in mourning song; compare Jer 9:19, where taught (|| קִינָת), also הִי Ezek 2:10 (|| אֵכֶל, מִסְפֵּד q. v. (Ol Co read נתי, compare Berthol).
