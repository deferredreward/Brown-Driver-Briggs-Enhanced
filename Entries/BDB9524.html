BDB9524 [H2428 H2429]

BDB9523 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9525

חַ֫יִל noun masculine power, strength, army (see Biblical Hebrew II. חול , חיל); —

1. power: absolute בְּאֶדְרָע וְחָ֑יִל Ezra 4:23 by force and power; ׳ קרא בְח i.e. call loudly Dan 3:4, בְּחַיִל Dan 4:11; Dan 5:7 ׳ גִּבָּרֵיאח Dan 3:20 mighty men of power.
2. force, army, suffix חֵילֵהּ Dan 3:20 construct חֵיל שְׁמַיָּא Dan 4:32 (= Biblical Hebrew צְכָא הַשָּׁמַיִם).
