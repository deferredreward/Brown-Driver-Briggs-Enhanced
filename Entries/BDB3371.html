BDB3371 [H2954]

Biblical Hebrew

טָפַשׁ verb be gross (Late Hebrew Hithpa`el grow stupid, and derivatives; Aramaic טְפֵשׁ, for Hebrew שׁמן Isa 6:11, Ithpe`el be stupid; compare Assyrian ‰apâšu , be abundant, large, GuyJAS 1883, Août-Sept. 189) — metaphor טָפַשׁ כַּחֵלֶב לִבָּ֑ם Ps 119:70 their heart is gross, like fat.
