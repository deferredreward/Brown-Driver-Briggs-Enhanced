BDB4660 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4662

III. [מָלַח] verb denominative salt, season; Qal Imperfect 2 masculine singular תִּמְלָ֑ךְ followed by accusative Lev 2:13 (object offering). Pu`al Participle מְמֻלָּח Exod 30:35 salted, i.e. the incense (see Di.) Hoph`al Perfect 2 feminine singular + Infinitive absolute הָמְלֵחַ לֹא הֻמְלַ֫חַתּ Ezek 16:4, i.e. rubbed or washed with salt (of infant, in personification).
