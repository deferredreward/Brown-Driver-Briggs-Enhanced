BDB4235 [H3787]

BDB4234 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4236

כָּשֵׁר verb be advantageous, proper, suitable, succeed, late Aramaic (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic כְּשָׁר, ); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular וְכָשֵׁר הַדָּבָר לִפְנֵי הַמֶּלֶךְ Esth 8:5 and the thing be proper in the view of the king; Imperfect יִכְשָׁר֨ Eccl 11:6 thou knowest not whether this shall succeed, or this. Hiph`il Infinitive construct וְיִתְרוֺן הַכְשֵׁיר חָכְמָה Eccl 10:10 an advantage for giving success is wisdom.
