[עָגַב] verb have inordinate affection, lust (compare Arabic wonder, admire, beloved; and (on sens. obsc. in Late Hebrew) LevyNHWB iii. 616); —

Qal Perfect 3 feminine singular עָֽגְבָה Ezek 23:7; Ezek 23:9; Ezek 23:12 Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַתַּעְגַּב Ezek 23:5, + Ezek 23:16 Kt ( Qr וַתַּעְגְּבָה), וַתַּעְגְּבָּה Ezek 23:20 ( Ew§ 191 c Ol§ 228 b); Participle plural עֹגְבִים Jer 4:30 — lust after (עַלֿ), only in figure of relations of Samaria and Jerusalem (personified) with foreigners Ezek 23:5; Ezek 23:9; Ezek 23:12; Ezek 23:16; Ezek 23:20, so with accusative Ezek 23:7 participle as substantive paramours Jer 4:30.
