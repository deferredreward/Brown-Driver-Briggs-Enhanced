BDB9620 [H3729]

Biblical Aramaic

כְּפַת verb bind ( ᵑ7 id.; Late Hebrew כָּפַת; Syriac form knots, twist into a knot, Arabic draw together, Assyrian [ kapâtu ] II. bring together); —

Pe`il Perfect 3 masculine plural כְּ פִ֫תוּ, (WCG 224) Dan 3:21 they were bound.
Pa`el bind: Infinitive לְכַפָּתָךְ Dan 3:20 ( ל accusative of person); Passive participle plural מְכַפְּתִין bound Dan 3:23 Dan 3:24.
