BDB6333 [H6135]

Biblical Hebrew

עָקָר adjective barren; — ׳ ע of male Deut 7:14, עֲקָרָה of female Deut 7:14 (mankind and beasts), Exod 23:26 ( E ; female); elsewhere of women Gen 11:30; Gen 25:21; Gen 29:31 (all J ), Judg 13:2 ; Judg 13:3; 1Sam 2:5; Job 24:21; Ps 113:9 ( עֲקֶרֶת construct); of personified Zion Isa 54:1.
