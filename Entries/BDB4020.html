BDB4019 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4021

כָּלָה noun feminine completion, complete destruction, consumption, annihilation; always this form; —

1. completion, but only as adverb, and dubious: completely, altogether Gen 18:21 (J; read כֻּלָּם Ol Kau Buhl), Exod 11:1 (si vera lectio)
2. complete destruction, consumption, annihilation:
\ \ a. almost always by God, especially ׳עָשָׂה כ Isa 10:23 (+ וְנֶחֱרָצָה), Nah 1:8 (2 accusative, si vera lectio; BuhlZAW 1885, 181 proposes בְּקָמָיו for מְקוֺמָהּ see IdHWB 12 KauAT, Text Krit. Erläut. p. 65; see also מָקוֺם below קום), vNah 1:9; Jer 4:27; Neh 9:31 (2 accusative); + אֵת person = with, pregnant sense, = in dealing with (compare II. אֵת 1 d), Zeph 1:18 (אַךְ נִבְהָלָה ׳כ), Jer 5:18; Jer 30:11 = Jer 46:28 (אֹתְָךָ = אִתְּךָ), Ezek 11:13; Ezek 20:17 (אוֺתָם = אִתָּם); + בְּכָלגּֿוֺיִם Jer 30:11; Jer 46:28 without וְנֶחֱרָצָה שָׁמַעְתִּי ׃עשׂה ׳כ Isa 28:22, ׳וְנ ׳עַדכֿ Dan 9:27 (on both compare Isa 10:23; Zeph 1:18); לְכָלָה for annihilation Ezek 13:13 (si vera lectio, see Co), ׳וְלֹא לְהַשְׁחִית לְכ 2Chr 12:12.
\ \ b. by men, אַלֿ ׳וְכ תַּעֲשׂוּ Jer 5:10 בְיָדוֺ ׳וְכ Dan 11:16 and (i.e. with) annihilation in his hand.
