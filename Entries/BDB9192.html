Biblical Hebrew

תָּם adjective complete — ׳ ת Gen 25:27 + 10 t.; suffix תַּמָּתִי Song 5:2; Song 6:9 (late, except Gen 25:27); —

1. complete, perfect: in beauty Song 5:2; Song 6:9 of physical strength Ps 73:4 (read לָ֑מוֺ תָּם Ew De DrSam. xxxi Che Bae and others).
2. sound, wholesome: אישׁ תָּם, of Jacob as tent-dweller, opposed to Esau, hunter, Gen 25:27 ( J ; De leutseliger Mann, Di ruhig, still, AV RV plain man; ׳ ת here possibly redactional, = 3).
3. complete, morally innocent, having integrity: אִישׁ תָּם Job 1:8; Job 2:3 ׳ ת alone Job 1:1; Job 8:20; Job 9:20; Job 9:21; Job 9:22; Prov 29:10; Ps 37:37; Ps 64:5. — תַּמִּים see [תָּאם ]. p. 1060f
