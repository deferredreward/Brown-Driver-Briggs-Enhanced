Biblical Hebrew

מִמְשָׁל noun [masculine] dominion, ruler (late); — absolute ׳ מ Dan 11:3; Dan 11:5 plural הַמִּמְשָׁלִים 1Chr 26:6 —

1. רב ׳ מ great dominion Dan 11:3 (accusative of cognate meaning with verb with מָשַׁל), Dan 11:5 (predicate of מֶמְשַׁלְתּוֺ ).
2. abstract for concrete (plural) = rulers 1Chr 26:6 (? read הַמּשְׁלִים).
