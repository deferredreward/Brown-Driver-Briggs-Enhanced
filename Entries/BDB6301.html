BDB6301 [H6110 H6099]

Biblical Hebrew

[עֲצֻמָה] noun feminine defence; — plural suffix עֲצֻמוֺתֵיכֻם (so Baer Ginsb; van d. H. ׳ עַצֻּמ) Isa 41:21 bring forward your defences, defensive arguments ( CheHpt עַצְּבוֺתֵיכֻם your idols, after Gr, [so Lo Klo explain ׳ עצמ, as mightiest aid, I. עצם]).

(ה)עצנו 2Sam 23:8, see I. עָדִין below I. עדן.
