תֹּף noun masculine timbrel, tambourine; — absolute ׳ת Gen 31:27 +; plural תֻּפִּים Judg 11:34 +; suffix תֻּפֶּ֫יךָ Ezek 28:13, תֻּפַּ֫יִח Jer 31:4 — timbrel, held and struck with hand, especially by dancing women, often with other musical instruments: — sign of merriment, gladness Gen 31:27 (E) Job 21:12, reverly Isa 5:12 ׳מְשׂוֺשׂ ת Isa 24:8, compare Jer 31:4; Ezek 28:13 exultation, triumph Exod 15:20 (twice in verse) (E) Judg 11:34; 1Sam 18:6; Isa 30:32 used by prophets in ecstasy 1Sam 10:5 in praise of ׳י 2Sam 6:5 || 1Chr 13:8; Ps 81:3; Ps 149:3; Ps 150:4. — See PrinceEB Music.§ 3.
