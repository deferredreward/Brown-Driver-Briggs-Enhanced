III. מַשָּׂא noun masculineIsa 14:28 utterance, oracle (compare נשׂא 1 b (6); GfJer 23:33); — ׳מ absolute Isa 14:28 +; usually construct Isa 13:1 +; — of prophetic utterance: 2Kgs 9:25; Isa 14:28; Ezek 12:10; 2Chr 24:27 (with עַל); בָבֶל ׳מ Isa 13:1, מוֺאָב ׳מ Isa 15:1, compare Isa 17:1; Isa 19:1; Isa 21:1; Isa 21:11; Isa 22:1; Isa 23:1; Isa 30:6; Nah 1:1 בערב ׳מ Isa 21:13 is later insert (Di); ׳מ = revelation, אֲשֶׁר חָזָה חֲבַקּוּק הַנָּבִיא ׳הַמּ Hab 1:1 ׳י ׳מ utterance of ׳י Jer 23:33; Jer 23:34; Jer 23:36; Jer 23:38 (3 t. in verse) (compare 2Kgs 9:25), see also v2Kgs 9:33 b (read אַתֶּם הַמַּשָּׂא), with word-play, — followed by וְנָטַשְׁתִּי v2Kgs 9:36 b; ׳דְבַר י ׳מ Zech 9:1 (+ בארץ), Zech 12:1 (+ עַליִֿשְׂרָאֵל), Mal 1:1 (+ ׳אֶליִֿשׂ). — Prov 31:1 see I. מַשָּׂא, p. 601 above; in Prov 30:1 read מִמַּשָּׂא from Massa (Mühlau, De), or הַמַּשָּׂאִי (Wild), which however is perhaps not original, but borrowed from Prov 31:1 (compare Wildon the passage FrankenbSpr. p. 5; corrupt name of Agur's home or tribe, Kau).
