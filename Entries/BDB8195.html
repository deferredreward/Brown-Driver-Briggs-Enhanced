[שָׂחָה] verb swim (Late Hebrew סָחוּ swimming; Targum סְחָא, Syriac swim, bathe, also סָחְיָא, סַחְיָנָא, שָׂחְיָנָא swimmer; Aph`el wash); —

Qal Participle + Infinitive construct כַּאֲשֶׁר יְפָרֵשׂ הַשּׂחֶה לִשְׂחוֺת Isa 25:11. Hiph`il Imperfect 1 singular אַשְׂחֶה Ps 6:7 I make my bed swim (with my tears; another view below שִׂיחַQal 1).
