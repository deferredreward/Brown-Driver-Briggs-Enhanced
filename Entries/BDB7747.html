BDB7747 [H4772]

Biblical Hebrew

[מַרְגְּלוֺת] noun [feminine] plural denominative place of the feet, feet (compare [מְרַאֲשׁוֺת] below I. ראשׁ); &; suffix מַרְגְּםתָיו place of his feet Ruth 3:4; Ruth 3:7 adverb = at his feet Ruth 3:8 + Ruth 3:14 Qr ( Kt מרגלתו); = his feet Dan 10:6 (opposed to זְרֹעֹתָיו).
