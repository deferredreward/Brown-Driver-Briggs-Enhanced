[דָּבַב] verb move gently, glide, glide over (Late Hebrew דָּבַב flow slowly, drop; Arabic walk leisurely, gently, any animal that walks or creeps, compare דֹּב; Assyrian dabâbu, plot, plan, COTGloss, compare דִּבָּה) — only Qal Participle דּוֺבֵב שִׂפְתֵי יְשֵׁנִים Song 7:10, of wine gliding over the lips of sleepers; (שְׂפָתַי וְשִׁנָּי֑ ׳ד i.e. my lips and teeth ᵐ5 ᵑ6 Aq GeiUrschrift 405).
