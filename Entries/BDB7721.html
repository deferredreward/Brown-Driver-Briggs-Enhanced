BDB7720 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7722

[רְבַע] verb denominative, only Passive participle squared, square; —

Qal Passive participle רָבוּעַ square, of altar Exod 27:1; Exod 30:2; Exod 37:25; Exod 38:1, compare Ezek 43:16 breastplate Ezek 30:2; Ezek 39:9 plural רְבֻעִים, of doorways, etc. [on text see Kit Benz] 1Kgs 7:5 feminine singular רְבֻעָה as substantive = a square Ezek 41:21 (accusative Co Toy, omit מְזוּזַת, and joining ׳ ר to Ezek 41:20 Krae reads מְזוּזתֹ רְבֻעֹת, compare 1Kgs 6:33, רְבִייְִ 2.).
Pu`al Participle square, מְרֻבַּע Ezek 45:2, of piece of land; feminine singular מְרֻבָּ֑עַת Ezek 40:47, of court; feminine plural מְרֻבָּעוֺת 1Kgs 7:31 (opposed to עֲנֻלּוֺת), of borders of bases (compare Sabean רבעתם, a kind of square building, SabDenkm31).
