BDB599 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB601

[אָנֵף, אָנַף] verb (breathe, snort) be angry ( MI id., compare Arabic Assyrian etc. in derivatives)

in Hebrew, verb denominative from אַף. —
Qal Perfect אָנַפְתָּ Ps 60:3, etc.; Impefect יֶאֱנַף Ps 2:12, etc.; — be angry, of ׳ י; usually followed by בְּ 1Kgs 8:46 = 2Chr 6:36; Isa 12:1; Ps 85:6; Ezra 9:14 absolute Ps 60:3; Ps 79:5 compare also Ps 2:12.
Hithpa`el id. always followed by בְּ & always of ׳ י, Perfect הִתְאַנַּף Deut 1:37; Deut 4:21; Deut 9:20 Imperfect וַיִּתְאַנַּף Deut 9:8; 1Kgs 11:9; 2Kgs 17:18 .
