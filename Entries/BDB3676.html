I. יֵ֫צָר noun masculine Isa 26:3 form, framing, purpose; — ׳ י Gen 6:5 + 5 t.; יִצְדוֺ Deut 31:21; Hab 2:18 יִצְרֵנוּ Ps 103:14 —

1. pottery, formed by the potter Isa 29:16.
2. form of a graven image Hab 2:18.
3. form of man as made of the dust Psalm Hab 103:14 .
4. of what is framed in the mind (compare יָצַר 1 c, 2 b), imagination, device, purpose: יצר מחשׁב(ו)ת לב(בׅ Gen 6:5 ( J ), 1Chr 29:18 מחשׁבות ׳ י 1Chr 28:9 לב ׳ י Gen 8:21 ( J ); יֵצֶר alone Deut 31:21 ( J ); יֵצֶר סָמוּךְ Isa 26:3 a stedfast purpose (or frame of mind). (In Late Hebrew יֵצֶר is common in sense of impulse: יצר הטוב and יצר הרע of good and bad tendency in man.)
