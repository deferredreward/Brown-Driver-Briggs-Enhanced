מַשְׁחִית noun [masculine] ruin, destruction; — ׳מ absolute Jer 5:26 + 10 t.; — physical destruction Ezek 5:16; Ezek 9:6; Ezek 21:36; Ezek 25:15; Exod 12:13 (P), 2Chr 20:23; 2Chr 22:4 ׳בַּעַל מ Prov 18:9 = destroyer; Dan 10:8 (disfigurement); ׳הַרמֿ Jer 51:25 (figurative of Babylonian as destroyer), in 2Kgs 23:13 = mountain of corruption (where bamoth were, southern end of Mt. of Olives, possibly adapted from הַרמִֿשְׁחָה so HoffmZAW ii (1882), 175 Benz Kit Bur; ᵑ9 mons offensionis).
