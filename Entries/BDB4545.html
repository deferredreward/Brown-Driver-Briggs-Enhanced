BDB4545 [H4134]

Biblical Hebrew

[מוּךְ] verb be low, depressed, grow poor (Late Hebrew id., Niph`al; Aramaic מוּךְ, sink or bend down; compare מָכַח, Syriac , be brought low, humble); —

Qal Perfect consecutive וּמָח Lev 27:8 ( P ); Imperfect יָמוּח Lev 25:25; Lev 25:35; Lev 25:39 (all H ); Participle מָח Lev 25:47 ( P ): all of impoverished Israelites.
