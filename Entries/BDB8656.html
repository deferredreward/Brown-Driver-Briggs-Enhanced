[שְׁכָבָה] noun feminine act of lying, layer; — only construct שִׁכְבַת all P ; —

1. act of lying, as accusative of cognate meaning with verb with שׁכב 3: שִׁכְבַתזָֿ֑רַע Lev 15:18 a man lies with her a lying of seed (in copulation), compare Lev 19:20; Num 5:13 then ( = semen), תֵּצֵא שִׁכְבַתזָֿ֑רַע Lev 15:16; Lev 15:32; Lev 22:4 (+ מִמֶּנּוּ), ׳ ז ׳ שׁ alone Lev 15:17.
2. layer of dew, שִׁכְבַת הַטָּ֑ל Exod 16:13; Exod 16:14 ( P ).
