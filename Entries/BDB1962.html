דְּמוּת noun feminine likeness, similitude (mostly late) (according to LagBN 12. 147 ff. mispunct. for דִּמְוַת from דִּמְוָה; according to WeProl. 413. Eng. Tr. 389 an Aramaic Loan-word, but see Di Gen 5:1, DrJPh xi. 216 CheOP. 474) — absolute ׳ ד Isa 40:18 + 3 t.; construct ׳ ד Gen 5:1 + 16 t.; suffix דְּמוּתוֺ Gen 5:3 דְּמוּתֵנוּ Gen 1:26 —

1. likeness, similitude, of external appearance, chiefly in Ezek.: Ezek 1:5 (likeness , i.e. something that appeared like) so vEzek 1:26; Ezek 8:2 דְּמוּת כְּמַרְאֵה (אֵשׁׅ (compare Co), Ezek 10:1 כִּסֵּא ׳ כְּמַרְאֵה ד; compare also Dan 10:16 כִּדְמוּת בְּנֵי אָדָם i.e. one like the sons of man; similitude, resemblance Ezek 1:5; Ezek 1:10; Ezek 1:16; Ezek 1:22; Ezek 1:26; Ezek 10:10 ; Ezek 10:21; Ezek 10:22 דְּמוּת כְּמַרְאֵה אָדָם Ezek 1:26 ׳ מַרְאֵה ד ׳ כְּבוֺד י vEzek 1:28 also 2Kgs 16:10 (pattern of altar), 2Chr 4:3 (images of oxen); of son in likeness of father Gen 5:3 ( P ); so also of man in likeness of God Gen 1:26 (|| צֶלֶם) Gen 5:1 (both P ); compare Isa 40:18 what ׳ ד will ye compare to him (אֵל) ? || דִּמָּה q. v.
2. adverbially, in likeness of, like as Isa 13:4 compare Ezek 23:15 & ׳ כִּד Ps 58:5. — Ezek 1:13 read וּבֵינוֺת, see בֵּין and J P PetersJBL 1892 40. 42. On דְּמוּת אֶחָד Ezek 1:16; Ezek 10:10 (apparently masculine) compare Thes & Sm who translate the likeness of one had they all four; Co reads אחת.
