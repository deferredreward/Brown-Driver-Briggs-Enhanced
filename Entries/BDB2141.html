BDB2141 [H2050]

BDB2140 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2142

[הוּת] verb shout at, si vera l. (Arabic with shout at, threaten, dialect. for , compare De Ps 62:4 who camp. Damascene rush upon one with cries and raised fist, compare further Wetzst in De; so MV SS: Thes and others assign to [התת], attack; text dubious) — only Po. Imperfect תְּהוֺתֲתוּ עַלאִֿישׁ Ps 62:4 but Hup Che read תְּהוֺלֲלוּ be frantic against, compare Ps 102:9.
