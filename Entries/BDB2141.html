Biblical Hebrew

[הוּת] verb shout at, si vera l. (Arabic with shout at, threaten, dialect. for , compare De Ps 62:4 who camp. Damascene rush upon one with cries and raised fist, compare further Wetzst in De; so MV SS: Thes and others assign to [ התת], attack; text dubious) — only
