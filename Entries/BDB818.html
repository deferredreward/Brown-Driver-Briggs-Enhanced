אַשְׁכְּנַז

proper name, masculine 1. a descendant of Japhet Gen 10:3 = 1Chr 1:6 =
2. a northern people Jer 51:27 מַמְלְכוֺת אֲרָרָט מִנִּי וְאַשְׁכְּנָ֑ז (perhaps a people Bithynia, = Ascan(ians) + az ending of Armen. patronymics, see especially LenOr. ii. 388 f., also LagGes, Abhandl. 254 f. Di Gen 10:3 but see LagArmen. Stud. 143).

אֶשְׁכָּר see II. שׁכר .
