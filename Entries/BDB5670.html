BDB5670 [H5595]

Biblical Hebrew

[סָפָה] verb sweep away or snatch away, catch up (Late Hebrew ׳ ספ, סָפָא collect (rare), ᵑ7 ׳ סְפִ (rare), Syriac collect, pick up; Arabic of wind, raise dust and carry it away Lane1377); —

Qal
1. intransitive: Perfect 3 feminine singular סָֽפְתָה Jer 12:4 be snatched away (read possibly סָ֫פָה [סוף]).
2. transitive: Imperfect 3 feminine singular תִּסְפֶּה Isa 7:20 sweep away beard; 2 masculine singular תִּסְפֶּה Gen 18:23; Gen 18:24 sweep away indiscriminately (good and bad); Infinitive construct סְפוֺת Deut 29:18 to snatch away the moist with the dry (proverb. expression, compare Dr), suffix לִסְפּוֺתָהּ Ps 40:15 to snatch it away (i.e. my life). — וְסָפ֫וּ Amos 3:15 see סוף .
