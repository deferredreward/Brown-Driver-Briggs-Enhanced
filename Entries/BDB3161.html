BDB3161 [H2785]

Biblical Hebrew

חַרְצַנִּים noun masculine plural some insignificant vine-product, usually taken as grape-kernels, grape-stones, from acrid taste, so Thes (after Onk , Mishna), see also זָּג above: וְעַדזָֿג ׳ מִגֶּפֶן הַיַּיִן מֵח Num 6:4 of the wine-vine, including both חרצנים and זג, he shall not eat.
