BDB1733 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1735

[גָּרָה] verb only Pi`el stir up strife, Hithpa`el engage in strife (compare Late Hebrew Pi`el stir up, excite, against ( בְּ) Hithpa`el; Aramaic Pa`el גָּרֵא ; especially Assyrian garû , Qal attack, be at war with, garû, girû , enemy, etc. AsrbAnnals iv. 49, 50 (KBii. 190) also SASmithAssurbanipal i. ii. Glossary; Arabic is run, especially of water, flow, etc.) —

Pi`el Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְגָרֶה Prov 15:18 ; Prov 28:25; Prov 29:22, all with object מָדוֺן excite, stir up, strife. — On גֵּרָה Deut 14:8 see I. גֵּרָה below גרר.
Hithpa`el Perfect 2 feminine singular הִתְגָּרִיתְ Jer 50:24 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִתְגָּרֶה Dan 11:25, וְיִתְגָּרֶ֯ו Dan 11:10 2 masculine singular תִּתְגָּרֶה 2Kgs 14:19; 2Chr 25:19 jussive with apocope תִּתְגָּר Deut 2:9; Deut 2:19 3 masculine plural יִתְגָּרוּ Prov 28:4 ; Dan 11:10, compare Dan 11:10 Kt ., see above; 2 masculine plural תִּתְגָּרוּ Deut 2:5 (jussive); Imperative masculine singular הִתְגָּר Deut 2:24 —
1. excite oneself against (בְּ person), engage in strife with, literally Deut 2:5; Deut 2:19; 2Kgs 14:19 = 2Chr 25:19 (followed by בְּרָעָה; — רעה personified as challenged opponent); also + accusative of manner, מִלְחָמָה Deut 2:9 ; Deut 2:24 figurative וְשֹׁמְרֵי תוֺרָה יִתְגָּרוּ בָם Prov 28:4 while they that keep the law are at strife with them, i.e. with those that forsake it.
2. absolute, excite oneself (against foe), wage war , only Dan 11: יִתְגָּרוּ וְאָֽסְפוּ הֲמוֺן חֲיָלִים רַבִּים Prov 28:10 they shall wage war, etc.; וְיָשֹׁב וְיִתְגָּרֶ֯ו עַדמָֿעֻזֹּה v Prov 28:10 and he shall return and war, even to his stronghold; Prov 28:25 followed by לַמִּלְחָמָה + בְּחַיִל גָּדוֺל (instrumental)
