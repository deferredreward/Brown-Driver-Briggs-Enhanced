Biblical Hebrew

יָעָה verb sweep together (with collateral idea of carrying away) (Arabic is collect, gather) —

Qal Perfect וְיָעָה consecutive Isa 28:17 subject בָּרָד with accusative (מַחְסֵה כָזָב ׳ ויעה ב).
