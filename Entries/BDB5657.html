BDB5657 [H5585]

Biblical Hebrew

[סָעִיף] noun [masculine]

1. cleft.
2. branch; —
1. cleft of a crag, construct סְעִיף סֶלַע Judg 15:8; Judg 15:11 plural construct וּבִסְעִפֵֿי הַסְּלָעִים Isa 2:21 ׳ סְעִיפֵי הַסּ Isa 57:5.
2. branches, boughs, plural construct סְעִפֵֿי הַפֹּרִיָּה Isa 17:6 (so divide, Hi Kn Di DrSm xxx Du and others) the boughs of the fruit-tree; suffix סְעִיפֶיהָ Isa 27:10 (of ruined city; compare קְצִירָהּ Isa 27:11).
