BDB2030 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2032

דָּת noun feminine Esth 3:15 decree, law, usage, only in Persian period (Biblical Aramaic Late Hebrew id. , Syriac or ; Persian loan-word, Old Persian dâta , law, SpiegAPK 225) — ׳ ד absolute Esth 1:8 + 9 t.; construct דַּת Esth 2:12, דָּת Esth 9:13 suffix דָּתוֺ Esth 2:8 + 4 t.; plural construct דָּתֵי Ezra 8:36 + 2 t.; suffix דָּתַיהֶם Esth 3:8 —

1. decree, edict, commission of Persian king Ezra 8:36 (word elsewhere only in Esther) Esth 2:8 (|| דְּבַר הַמֶּלֶךְ), Esth 4:3 (|| id.) Esth 8:17 (|| id.) Esth 9:1 (|| id.; with עשׂה execute) compare also Esth 9:13 with verb נָתַן Esth 3:14; Esth 3:15; Esth 8:13; Esth 8:14; Esth 9:14 written כְּתָבהַֿדָּת Esth 4:8 (compare Esth 3:14; Esth 8:13).
2. law, permanently valid and applicable, וְיִכָּתֵב בְּדָתֵי פָּרַסוּֿמָדַי וְלֹא יַעֲבוֺר Esth 1:19 compare Esth 1:15 ; Esth 4:11 ; Esth 4:16 יֹדְעֵי דָּת וָדִין Esth 1:13 those that understood law and judgment; דָּתֵי הַמֶּלֶךְ Esth 3:8 of laws of the Jews in Persia Esth 3:8 even of rules for drinking at a feast Esth 1:8 for purifying of women Esth 2:12. — The distinction between 1 & 2 is not absolute. The king's will was law, and the royal edict (דְּבַר מַלְכוּת ) concerning Vashti was reckoned among the laws (דָּתֵי) of the Persians & Medes ( Esth 1:19). — In Deut 33:2 ᵑ0 אֵשׁ דָּת לָמוֺ fire was a law for them is corrupt; many emendations proposed, compare Di; Di suggests דֹת [לַפִּ] אֵשׁ fire of flames, flaming fire, compare הַלַּפִּידִם Exod 20:18 (but לַפִּדֹת plural in תֹ֯ not in OT unless as proper name), or (better), דֶת[יֹקֶ] אֵשׁ a burning fire, compare Isa 65:5 (different sense).
