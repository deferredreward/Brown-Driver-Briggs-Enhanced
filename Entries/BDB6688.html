פְּנִינִים noun [feminine] plural corals (? as branching compare Arabic branch of tree; so Mich Thes and others; > pearls BoHieroz. 601 DiJb after Ra and others); — ׳פ figurative of value Prov 20:15 usually ׳מִפּ, camp. Job 28:18; Prov 3:15 (Kt מפניים), Prov 8:11; Prov 31:10 (all of value), Lam 4:7 (of red colour); + Ps 45:14 (perhaps; for פְּנִימָה, q. v. ).
