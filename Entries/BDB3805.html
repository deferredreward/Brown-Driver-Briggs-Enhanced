יֵ֫שַׁע noun masculine Isa 51:5 deliverance, rescue, salvation, also safety, welfare; — יֵ֫שַׁע Ps 20:7 + 4 t.; יֶ֫שַׁע Job 5:11 + 4 t.; suffix יִשְׁעִי 2Sam 22:3 + 11 t., + 14 t. suffixes; —

1. safety, welfare, prosperity 2Sam 23:5; Ps 12:6; Job 5:4; Job 5:11.
2. salvation, i.e. primarily physical rescue, by God, often with added spiritual idea: Isa 62:11; Ps 69:14; Ps 85:8; Ps 85:10 יֵשַׁע אֱלֹהִים salvation from God Ps 50:23 used as infin. with accusative לְיֵשַׁע Hab 3:13 (twice in verse) (see Ew§ 239 a); accordingly Yahweh is אוֺרִי וְיִשְׁעִי my light and my salvation Ps 27:1 צוּר ישׁע Ps 95:1 קֶרֶן ישׁע Ps 18:3 = 2Sam 22:3 אֱלֹהֵי ישׁע Isa 17:10; Mic 7:7; Hab 3:18; Ps 18:47 = אֱלֹהֵי צוּר ישׁע 2Sam 22:47 ; Ps 24:5 ; Ps 25:5; Ps 27:9; Ps 65:6; Ps 79:9; Ps 85:5; 1Chr 16:35 ׳ מָגֵן ישׁ Ps 18:36 = 2Sam 22:36 על אלהים ישׁעי Ps 62:8 || צְדָקָה Isa 45:8; Isa 51:5; Isa 61:10 שְׂשׂוֺן יִשְׁעֶ֑ךָ Ps 51:14 joy of thy salvation ׳ כֹּהֲנֶיהָ אַלְבִּישׁ ישׁ Ps 132:16 her priests will clothe with salvation.
3. victory: גְּבוּרוֺת יֵשַׁע יְמִינוֺ Ps 20:7 the mighty deeds of the victory of his right hand.
