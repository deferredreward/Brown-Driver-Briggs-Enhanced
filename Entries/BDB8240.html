שֵׂ֫כֶל, שֶׂ֫כֶל noun masculine prudence, insight; — ׳שֵׂ֫ absolute Ps 111:10 +, construct Prov 19:11 +; ׳שֶׂ absolute 1Sam 25:3 +; שָׂ֑כֶל Job 17:4 suffix שִׂכְלוֺ Prov 12:8; Dan 8:25 —

1. prudence, good sense: ׳טוֺבַת שׂ woman of good sense 1Sam 25:3.
2. insight, understanding: || בִינָה 1Chr 22:12; 2Chr 2:11, compare Job 17:4 טוֺב ׳שׂ Prov 3:4; Prov 13:15; Ps 111:10; 2Chr 30:22 ׳אִישׂ שׂ Ezra 8:18 ׳יוֺעֵץ בְּשׂ 1Chr 26:14 בְּעָלָיו ׳מְקוֺר חַיִּים שׂ Prov 16:22 restrains from anger Prov 19:11, wins praise Prov 12:8, fool despises מִלִּים ׳שׂ Prov 23:9 ׳שׂוֺם שׂ Neh 8:8 (|| הֵבִין) set forth (the) understanding (i.e. the meaning).
3. bad sense, cunning, craft, Dan 8:25.
