BDB89 [H98]

Biblical Hebrew

אֲגַם noun [masculine] troubled pool (Aramaic id., , Assyrian agammu DlW) — ׳ א absolute Isa 35:7 construct Isa 41:18 + 2 t.; אֲגַמִּים Exod 8:1 + 2 t.; אַגְמֵי Isa 14:23 אַגְמֵיהֶם Exod 7:19 —

1. troubled or muddy (gloomy) pools or marshes, plural מַיִם ׳ א Isa 14:23.
2. any pool, pond, singular מַיִם ׳ א Isa 41:18; Ps 107:35; Ps 114:8 plural without מַיִם Exod 7:19; Exod 8:1 ( P ) Isa 42:15 .
3. swamp-reed, rush (= אַגְמוֺן) Jer 51:32.
