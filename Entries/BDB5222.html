BDB5222 [H5246]

Biblical Hebrew

נָמֵר noun masculine Isa 11:6 leopard; — absolute ׳ נ Hos 13:7 + 3 t.; plural נְמֵרִים Hab 1:8; Song 4:8 — leopard, as spotted Jer 13:23, swift Hab 1:8, keen-eyed Jer 5:6 (symbol of calamities of Israel), so Hos 13:7 (simile of ׳ י); עִםגְּֿדִי יִרְבָּ֑ץ ׳ נ Isa 11:6 (predict); חַרֲרֵי נְמֵרִים Song 4:8 (|| מְעֹנוֺת אֲרָיוֺת).
