BDB2331 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2333

מָזוֺר noun [masculine] wound (as needing to have its matter pressed out) — figurative of injury to, or sufferings of, Israel and Judah: מָזוֺר Hos 5:13 also Jer 30:13 ( Gf Che RVm ; but accents Ew Ges Gie AV RV for pressing, i.e. binding up); מְזֹרוֺ Hos 5:13 (compare III. זור Isa 1:6). — מָזוֺר Obad 1:7 see below מזר.
