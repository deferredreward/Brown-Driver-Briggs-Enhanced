זֶ֫רֶשׁ proper name, feminine wife of Haman Esth 5:10; Esth 5:14; Esth 6:13 (twice in verse) (according to OppEsth. 25 = Persian zaris, Zend zairis, la dorée, compare aurum ductile, Vullii. 128 b; JenVOJ, 1892, 64 compare Elamite goddess Kiriša).
