BDB154 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB156

[אָהַל] verb denominative tent, move tent from place to place (compare Assyrian â'ilu (?) DlW No. 4 & Agl) וַיֶּאֱהַל Gen 13:12; Gen 13:18 ( J ), compare ישֵׁב אֹהֶל (see אֹהֶל );

Pi`el Imperfect יַהֵל (contracted for יְאַהֵל) pitch one's tent like nomad Isa 13:20.
