Biblical Hebrew

III. [גּוּר] verb dread (compare יָגֹר) —

Qal Imperfect וַיָּגָר Num 22:3; 1Sam 18:15 2 masculine singular תָּגוּר Deut 18:22 אָגוּר Deut 32:27 plural יָגוּרוּ Ps 33:8; Job 41:17 + Hos 10:5, תָּגוּרוּ Deut 1:17 Imperative גּוּרוּ Ps 22:24; Job 19:29 —
1. be afraid of, followed by מִפְּנֵי Num 22:3; Deut 1:17; 1Sam 18:15, גּוּרוּ לָכֶם מִפְּנֵי Job 19:29 with מִן Job 41:17; Deut 18:22 with accusative Deut 32:27 (poetry) Hos 10:5 read probably ינודו lament (|| אבל, used with לְ e.g. Nah 3:7; Jer 15:5 and elsewhere; compare Che).
2. stand in awe of, followed by מִן Ps 22:24; Ps 33:8 ( יָרֵא).
