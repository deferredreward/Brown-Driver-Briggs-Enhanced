פּוּר noun masculine lot (Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; perhaps Assyrian loanword from puru, buru, stone JenZA x. 339 f. and in WildEst p. 173 f.);

Scheft49 f. compare Old Iranian fravi , etc., progress, fortune, fate, lot. — explained by הַגּוֺרָל Esth 3:7; Esth 9:24 שֵׁם הַפּוּר vEsth 9:26 explains name of feast פּוּרִים vEsth 9:26 יְמֵי הַפֻּרִים days of Purim vEsth 9:28 vEsth 9:31 ׳ אִגֶּרֶת הַפּ vEsth 9:29, ׳ דִּבְרֵי הַפּ vEsth 9:32. — See on Purim especially NowArchaeology ii. 194 ff and references.
