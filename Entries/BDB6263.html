עֶפְרוֺן proper name Εφρων: —

1. masculine Hittite, Abraham's time, according to Gen 23:8; Gen 23:10 (twice in verse); Gen 23:13; Gen 23:16; Gen 23:17; Gen 49:29, עֶפְרֹן Gen 23:6; Gen 25:9; Gen 49:30; Gen 50:13 (all P).
2. location 2Chr 13:19 Kt (עֶפְיִן Qr), compare עָפְרָה 1 a. 3. mont. ׳הַרעֿ, on northern border of Judah Josh 15:9.
