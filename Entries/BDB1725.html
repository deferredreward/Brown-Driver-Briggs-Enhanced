BDB1725 [H1610]

BDB1724 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1726

[גַּף] noun masculine body, self, height, elevation (compare Late Hebrew גּוּף) — singular suffix גַּפּוֺ Exod 21:3 (twice in verse); Exod 21:4 plural construct גַּפֵּי Prov 9:3 —

1. body, self, only in phrase יָבאֹ (יֵצֵא) בְּגַפּוֺ) he shall come in (go out) by himself (with his body) Exod 21:3 (twice in verse); Exod 21:4 in law of slave (compare Di).
2. height, elevation עַלגַּֿפֵּי מְרֹמֵי קָ֑רֶת Prov 9:3.
