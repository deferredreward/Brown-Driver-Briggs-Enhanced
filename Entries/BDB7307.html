BDB7307 [H6926]

Biblical Hebrew

[קִדְמָה] noun feminine front, East; — only construct קִדְמַת as preposition:

1. in front of, over against (compare NöZMG xxxiii (1879), 532), Gen 2:14 (i.e. from standpoint of writer; really, on geographic grounds, = west of; yet see Died. 6), Gen 4:16 (both J ),
2. on the east of, Ezek 39:11; 1Sam 13:5 may be either.
