צָרַ֫עַת35 noun feminine leprosy; — absolute ׳ צ Deut 24:3 +, צָרָ֑עַת Lev 13:2 +; construct צָרַ֫עַת 2Kgs 5:27 +; suffix צָרַעְתּוֺ 2Kgs 5:3; 2Kgs 5:6; 2Kgs 5:7 — leprosy 2Kgs 5:3; 2Kgs 5:6; 2Kgs 5:7; 2Kgs 5:27; 2Chr 26:19 elsewhere only in laws: ׳ נֶגַע צ Deut 24:8, and Lev 13:14 ( P ; 29 t.): ׳ תּוֺרַת הַצ Lev 14:57 specific of

a. human disease (as above) ׳ נֶגַע צ Lev 13:2 + 8 t.; without ׳ נ Lev 13:8 + 10 t.
b. leprosy in a garment Lev 13:47; Lev 13:49; Lev 13:51; Lev 13:52; Lev 13:59 (בֶּגֶד ׳ תּוֺרַת נֶנַע צ).
c. in house Lev 14:34 (׳ נֶגַע צ), Lev 14:44 house or garment Lev 14:55. — Exact meaning of b. and c. dubious, perhaps some fungus or mould; see especially Di Dr-Wh Baen.
