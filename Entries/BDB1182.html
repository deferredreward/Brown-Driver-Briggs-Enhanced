BDB1181 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1183

בַּתשֿׁוּעַ proper name, feminine (?) (daughter of opulence?) —

1. wife of David, mother of Solomon, etc. 1Chr 3:5 = בַּתשֶֿׁ֫בַּע q. v.; We Klo בַּתשֶֿׁ֫וַע compare Be; but probably textual error. see Dr on 2Sam 11:3.
2. wife of Judah 1Chr 2:3 הַכְּנַעֲנִית ׳ שֿׁ ׳ ב, RV Bathshua but in || Gen 38:2; Gen 38:12 not a proper name (compare V:2).
