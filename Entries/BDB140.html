אֲדָר proper noun [masculine] 12th (Babylonian) month = Feb.-Mar. (late Hebrew loan-word, = Babylonian A(d)-daru see DlWp. 188, compare Al 3 93, meaning dubious perhaps adâru, be darkened, eclipsed, but see DlW p. 190) Esth 3:7; Esth 3:12; Esth 8:12; Esth 9:1; Esth 9:15; Esth 9:17; Esth 9:19; Esth 9:21 compare Palmyrene, Nabataean אדר Vog8 EutNab 24.
