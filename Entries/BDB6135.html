BDB6134 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6136

[עָמִית] noun masculine Lev 18:20 associate, fellow, relation (perhaps originally feminine abstract association , compare פֶּחָה); — always suffix: גֶּבֶר עֲמִיתִי Zech 13:7 a man (who is) my fellow; elsewhere only Leviticus: עֲמִיתוֺ Zech 5:21 (twice in verse); reciprocal אִישׁ בַּעֲמִיתוֺ Zech 19:11; Zech 24:19, cf . Zech 25:17 עֲמִיתְךָ Zech 18:20, עֲמִיתֶ֑ךָ Zech 19:15; Zech 19:17; Zech 25:14 (twice in verse); Zech 25:15.

עַמִּיאֵל and similar proper names see below I. עמם.
