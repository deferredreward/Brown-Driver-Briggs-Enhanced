II. נִיר noun [masculine] the tillable, untilled or fallow ground; — ׳ נ absolute as accusative of cognate meaning with verb נִירוּ לָכֶם נִ֑יר Hos 10:12; Jer 4:3 till you the untilled ground (figurative of unaccustomed moral action); construct only רָבאֹֿכֶל נִיר רָאשִׁים Prov 13:23 abundant food (yields) the fallow ground of poor men (i.e. with ׳ י's blessing). — I. נִיר see נור . p. 632f
