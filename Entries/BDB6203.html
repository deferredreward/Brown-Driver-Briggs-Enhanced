BDB6203 [H4617]

BDB6202 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6204

מַעֲנֶה noun masculine Prov 15:1 answer, response; — absolute רַֿךְ ׳ מ Prov 15:1 a gentle answer; construct מַעֲנִה פִּיו vProv 15:23, compare לָשׁוֺן ׳ מ Prov 16:1 (i.e. ability to answer); אֱלֹהִים ׳ מ Mic 3:7 i.e. response for guidance; absolute also Prov 29:19 response = obedience; Job 32:3; Job 32:5 reply, refutation; suffix לַמַּעֲנֵהוּ Job 16:4 = for its purpose, that for which it answers (see Toy).
