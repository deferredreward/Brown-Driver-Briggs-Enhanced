קֹ֫רַח37 proper name, masculine Κορε (baldness ?); —

1. Edomite name:
a. Gen 36:5; Gen 36:14; Gen 36:18; 1Chr 1:35.
b. Gen 36:16 (not ⅏ ; probably gloss from Gen 36:18 Di Kau Ball and others; Holz hesitates).
2. Levite, rebel against Moses, Num 16:1 + 10 t. 16, 17, Num 26:9; Num 26:10; Num 27:3 in geneal. Lists Exod 6:21; Exod 6:24; 1Chr 6:7; 1Chr 6:22 ׳ בֶּןקֿ 1Chr 9:19 ׳ בְּנֵיקֿ Num 26:11 especially ׳ לִבְנֵיקֿ as company of Psalm-collectors (or singers), in titles: Ps 42:1; Ps 44:1; Ps 45:1; Ps 46:1; Ps 47:1; Ps 48:1; Ps 49:1; Ps 84:1; Ps 85:1; Ps 87:1; Ps 88:1.
3. a 'son' of Hebron, i.e. Judahite clan 1Chr 2:43 Κορεε, etc.
