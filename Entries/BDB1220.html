BDB1220 [H1187]

Biblical Hebrew

בַּ֫עַל פְּעוֺר proper name, masculine Num 25:3; Num 25:5; Deut 4:3 (twice in verse); Ps 106:28 ; Hos 9:10, Baal of Peor ( VB ) i.e. worshipped at פְּעוֺר q. v.; or Baal-P. (whence Peor as proper name, of a location); compare Di Num 25:3 BaudStud. ii. 233 BaeRel 14, 210.
