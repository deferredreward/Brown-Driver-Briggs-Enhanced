II. [נָשַׁק] verb exact meaning uncertain; probably either handle, or be equipped with (compare perhaps Arabic etc., below I. נשׁק); — only

Qal Participle plural construct נשְׁקֵי: — קֶשֶׁת ׳ נ 1Chr 12:2 equipped with the bow, so 2Chr 17:17 (+ מָגֵן); רוֺמֵי קֶשֶׁת ׳ נ Ps 78:9 is difficult, ׳ ר appositive of ׳ נ according to Hup-Now Bae and others (Ges «GKC:130e»§ 130e); strike out רוֺמֵי as explanatory gloss Hup Hi Che Kau; strike out ver. as gloss Hup Kau Bae.
