BDB6641 [H6405]

BDB6640 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6642

פַּלֵּט noun [masculine] deliverance (si vera lectio, properly Infinitive of foregoing); — ׳רָנֵּי פ Ps 32:7 shouts of deliverance (dubious, see Commentaries); עַלאָֿוֶן פַּלֶּטלָֿ֑מוֺ Ps 56:8, read פַּלֶּסֿ (Ew Hup and others) for (their) iniquity weigh to them (retribution), or עַלאָֿוֶן אֵין פַּלֶּטֿ Bae.
