BDB5421 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5423

נְשָׁמָה noun feminine breath; — ׳נ Deut 20:16 + 9 t.; construct נִשְׁמַת (compare LagBN 82) Gen 2:7 + 10 t.; suffix נִשְׁמָתִי Job 27:3 נִשְׁמָתוֺ Job 34:14 plural נְשָׁמוֺת Isa 57:16 —

1. breath of God as hot wind kindling a flame Isa 30:33 as destroying wind 2Sam 22:16 = Ps 18:16; Job 4:9 as cold wind producing ice Job 32:8; Job 33:4.
2. breath of man 1Kgs 17:17; Isa 42:5; Job 27:3; Dan 10:7 breath of life נשׁמת חיים Gen 2:7 (J); compare נִשְׁמַת רוּחַ חַיִּים Gen 7:22 (J); as breathed in by God it is God's breath in man Job 34:14; Job 36:4 and is characteristic of man אדם אשׁר נשׁמה באפו Isa 2:22 man in whose nostrils is but a breath (late gloss).
3. synonym נפשׁ כָּל נְשָׁמָה every breathing thing Deut 20:16; Josh 11:11; Josh 11:14; 1Kgs 15:29 כָּלהַֿנְּשָׁמָה Josh 10:14; Ps 150:6 נְשָׁמוֺת Isa 57:16.
4. spirit of man, || רוּחַ, אָדָם ׳נ ׳נֵר י Prov 20:27 the spirit of man is a lamp of ׳י.
