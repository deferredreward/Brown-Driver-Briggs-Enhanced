BDB6657 [H6414]

BDB6656 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6658

[פָּלִיל] noun masculine judge; plural פְּלִילִים Deut 32:31 our enemies being judges, umpires; ׳עָוֺן פ Job 31:11, read ׳עֲוֺן פ according to Di Siegf, compare De; < עָוֺן פְּלִילִי as vJob 31:28, so Bu Du; בִּפְלִלִים Exod 21:22, read perhaps בַּנְּפָלִים for the abortion BuZAW xi (1891), 107, compare also Di-Ry.
