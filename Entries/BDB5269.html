II. נָעִים adjective perhaps singing, sweetly sounding, musical; — absolute ׳ כִּנּוֺר נ Ps 81:3 sweetly-sounding lyre; construct, as substantive, ׳ נְעִים זְמִרוֺת יִשׂ 2Sam 23:1 Israel's sweet singer of songs (compare Dr 2Sam 8:10 Löhr ; ᵑ9 egregius psaltes Israel, and so Ges Ew and others, from I. נעם; joy of the songs of Israel HPS, compare Klo Kit).
