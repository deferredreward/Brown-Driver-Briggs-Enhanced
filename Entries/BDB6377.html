BDB6377 [H4626 H4629]

BDB6376 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6378

[מַעֲרֶה], מַ֫עַר noun [masculine] bare, naked place (hence Μαραθών, Marathon, according to LewyFremdw. 144); —

1. bare place, or space: construct מִמַּעֲרִהגָּֿ֑בַע Judg 20:33 bare (open) space of Geba' (si vera 1.; but see II. מַעֲרָב ); כְּמַעַראִֿישׁ 1Kgs 7:36 according to the clear space on each (plate, see לוּחַ ).
2. suffix מַעְרֵח Nah 3:5 figurative of Nineveh (pudenda exposed in shameful punishment; compare עֶרְוָה).
