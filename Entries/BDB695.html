BDB694 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB696

[אָצַל] verb denominative lay aside, reserve, withdraw, withhold —

Qal Perfect אָצַלְתָּ Gen 27:36 אָצַלְתִּי Eccl 2:10 וְאָֽצַלְתִּ֫י consecutive Num 11:17 — reserve, לִי בְרָכָה Gen 27:36 (withdraw), set apart מִןהָֿרוּחַ Num 11:17 withhold מֵהֶם Eccl 2:10. Niph`al Perfect נֶאֱצַל Ezek 42:6 be withdrawn, i.e. shortened or narrowed. Hiph`il Imperfect וַיָּאצֶל Num 11:25 (Kö1. 390) = Qal Num 11:17.
