BDB7728 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7730

רֵ֫בֶץ noun [masculine] (place of) lying down, resting- or dwelling-place; — construct בָּקָר ׳ ר Isa 65:10 (|| נְוֵה צאֹן); suffix בִּנְוֵה תַנִּים רִבְצָהּ Isa 35:7 (compare Ges«GKC:145m»§ 145m, but Du רָֽבְצָה, that is, flock); רִבְצָם Jer 50:6 (people as sheep); of man, רִבְצוֺ Prov 24:15 (|| נְוֵה צַדְּיק).
