BDB7111 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7113

צֶ֫מַח noun masculine Jer 23:5 sprout, growth; — absolute ׳ צ Hos 8:7 +, צֶ֑מַח Zech 3:8 construct צֶ֫מַח Gen 19:25+; suffix צִמְחָהּ Ezek 17:9 +; —

1. collective sprouting, growth, הָאֲדָמָה ׳ צ Gen 19:25 ( J ), הַשָּׂדֶה ׳ צ Ezek 16:7 (simile), compare Isa 61:11; Ps 65:11 so יהוה ׳ צ Isa 4:2, ׳ צ absolute Hos 8:7 growth (= foliage) of vine, ׳ טַרְפֵּי צ Ezek 17:9.
2. process of growth, of vine, עֲרֻגֹת צִמְתָהּ Ezek 17:10 = the beds where it grew.
3. future ruler, under figure of sprout from Davidic tree (compare Ps 132:17) וַהֲקִמֹתִי צַדִּיק ׳ לְדָוִר צ Jer 23:5 a righteous sprout, shoot, = צְדָקָה ׳ אַצְמִיחַ לְדָוִד צ Jer 33:15 hence (as proper name) ׳ עַבְדִּי צ Zech 3:8, of Zerubbabel שְׁמוֺ ׳ אִישׁ צ Zech 6:12.
