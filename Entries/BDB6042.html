עַ֫יִר noun masculine Gen 32:16 male ass (young and vigorous); — plural וַעְיָרִם Gen 32:16 ( E ); suffix עִירֹה Gen 49:11 (poem; || בְּנִי אֲתֹנוֺ); construct עַיִר פֶּרֶא (Ges «GKC:131c»§ 131c N. 2; or appositive KöSynt. § 333 s Di and others) Job 11:12 wild ass's colt; plural עֲיָרִים, for riding Judg 10:4a (Judg 10:4b see עִיר ), עֲיָרִם), Judg 12:14, singular absolute עַיִר Zech 9:9 עֲיָרִים for carrying Isa 30:6 Qr ( Kt עורים), עֲיָרִם for tillage vIsa 30:24.
