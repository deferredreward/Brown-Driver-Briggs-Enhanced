BDB3469 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3471

[יְהָב] noun [masculine] lot (as that which is given) — יְהָֽבְךָ ׳ הַשְׁלֵךְ עַל י Ps 55:23 cast on ׳ י thy lot (the care, anxiety, etc. which are thy portion; compare ׳ גּוֺל עַליֿ דרכך Ps 37:5).

compare ᵑ7 יַהֲבַת Ps 11:6, יְהָבִית Eccl 5:10.
