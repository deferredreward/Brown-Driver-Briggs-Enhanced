BDB8661 [H7913]

BDB8660 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8662

[שָׁכֵחַ] adjective forgetting, forgetful; — plural ׳ הַשְּׁכֵּ חִים אֶתהַֿר ק Isa 65:11 they who forget the mountain of my holiness (|| ׳ עֹזְבֵי י); construct ׳ שְׁכֵחֵי א Ps 9:8 all nations forgetful of God (compare שֹׁכְחֵי participle, Job 8:13; Ps 50:22).
