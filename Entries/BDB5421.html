[נשׁם] verb pant (Late Hebrew in nouns נְשִׁימָה, נְשָׁמָה, ᵑ7 נִשְׁמָא, נִשְׁמְתָא; late Aramaic Ithpe`el; Syriac breathe, blow; Arabic gently breathe (of wind), etc.; see seek a thing with labour and perseverance (Lane3032); a soul, Laneib.); — pant, of the deep and strong breathing of a woman in travail; Qal Imperfect 1 singular אֶשֹּׁם Isa 42:14.
