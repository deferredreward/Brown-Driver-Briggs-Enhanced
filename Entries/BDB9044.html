שָׁרַץ verb swarm, teem (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic crawl, = שֶׁרֶץ, so שִׁרְצָא (rare); Ethiopic germinate, sprout); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ שׁ Ps 105:30, שָֽׁרְצוּ Gen 1:21, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִשְׁרֹץ Ezek 47:9, etc.; Imperative masculine plural שִׁרְצוּ Gen 9:7 Participle הַשֹּׁרֵץ Gen 7:21 +, feminine הַשֹּׁרֶ֫צֶת Lev 11:46 Hexateuch only P : —
1. swarm, teem with (accusative animal.), subject water Gen 1:20; Gen 1:21, compare Exod 7:28, land Ps 105:30.
2. swarm, subject animal. Gen 8:17 (+ פָּרָה, רָבָה), Ezek 47:9, הַּשֹׁרֵּץ Gen 7:21; Lev 11:29 ; Lev 11:41; Lev 11:42; Lev 11:46 (all עַלהָּֿרֶָּֿץ), vLev 11:43 subject men Gen 9:7 (+ פָּרָה, רָבה), Exod 1:7 (id., + עָצַם ).
