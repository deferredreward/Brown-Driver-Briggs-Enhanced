[אֹ֫פֶס noun [masculine] only in the dual אָפְסָ֑ים not ׳ א; see Baer), literally the two extremities, i.e. either the soles of the feet (so AW Ges; compare Aramaic פִּיסְּתָא, , or the ankles (so ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ᵑ9 , & most): only Ezek 47:2 ׳ מֵי א water of (i.e. reaching to) the soles (or ankles ); compare Ezek 47:4 מֵי מָתְנָ֑יִם water reaching to the loins.
