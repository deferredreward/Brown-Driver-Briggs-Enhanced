פֶּ֫סַח noun masculine passover (usually (Thes and most) from I. פסח, with reference to Exod 12:13 etc.; ReussGeschichte. AT § 58 passing over into new year; SchaeferPassah-Mazzoth (1900), 346 compare Assyrian pašâ—u, soothe, placate [deity], < be soothed, Pi`el transitive, JenZA iv (1889), 275 ZimBabylonian Rel. i (1896). Glossary; ii (1899), 92; SchwIdiot, 124 from II. פסח, of sacred dance); — absolute ׳פ Exod 12:11 +; פָּ֑סַח vExod 12:21 +; plural פְּסָחִים 2Chr 30:17 + 3 t.; —

1. sacrifice of Passover, involving communion-meal, hence a species of peace-offering (compare I. זֶבַח II. 2), ׳הוא לי ׳זֶבַח פ Exod 12:27 (J), compare vExod 12:11 (P); ׳זָבַח פ Deut 16:2; Deut 16:5; Deut 16:6 ׳אבל הפ 2Chr 30:18 the special feature lay in the application of blood to homes to consecrate them: compare simile Babylonian rite of purification Zimib. ii. 126-7.
2. the animal victim of the passover: ׳שׁחט פ Exod 12:21 (J) 2Chr 30:15; 2Chr 35:1; 2Chr 35:6; 2Chr 35:11; Ezra 6:20, compare 2Chr 30:17 באשׁ ׳בשׁל הפ 2Chr 35:13 לפסחים 2Chr 35:7; 2Chr 35:8; 2Chr 35:9. [Passover animals (compare BrHex.206) were צאֹן flock Exod 12:21 (J), שֶׁה vExod 12:3 vExod 12:4 vExod 12:5, including כֶּבֶשׁ and עֵז vExod 12:5 (P); צאן ובקר Deut 16:2 large numbers of all these (שֶׂה not used) in Josiah's Passover, active to 2Chr 35:7; 2Chr 35:8; 2Chr 35:9, but evident mingling of whole burnt-offerings for the passover with special passover victim.]
3. festival of the passover : ׳חג הפ Exod 34:25 (J); ׳(ה)פ Lev 23:5; Num 28:16; Num 33:3; Josh 5:11 (P) Ezek 45:21 ׳חֻקַּת הפ Exod 12:43; Num 9:12; Num 9:14 (P); ׳עשׂה פ Exod 12:48; Num 9:2; Num 9:4; Num 9:5; Num 9:6; Num 9:10; Num 9:13; Num 9:14; Josh 5:10 (P) Deut 16:1; 2Kgs 23:21; 2Kgs 23:22; 2Kgs 23:23; 2Chr 30:1; 2Chr 30:2; 2Chr 30:5; 2Chr 35:1; 2Chr 35:16; 2Chr 35:17; 2Chr 35:18 (twice in verse); 2Chr 35:19; Ezra 6:19 it was held in month הָאָבִיב Deut 16:1, בָּעָ֑רֶב vDeut 16:6 on 14th day Josh 5:10 (P), of 1st month Ezek 45:21 בֵּין הערבים (Exod 12:6) Lev 23:5; Num 9:5 (P); if impossible at that time, then on 14th of 2nd month Num 9:10; Num 9:12. [No reference to פֶּסַח in E; J subordinates it to מַצּוֺת (the great feast of J E); in D it predominates over מצות; P makes it first in importance (BrHex.195f.)].]
