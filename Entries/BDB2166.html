BDB2165 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2167

[מַהֲלָךְ] noun masculine Neh 2:6 walk, journey, going (late); construct מַהֲלַךְ Ezek 42:4 + 2 t.; suffix מַהֲלָֽכְךָ Neh 2:6 plural מַהְלְכִים Zech 3:7 —

1. walk 'of 10 cubits' breadth before the chambers' in Ezekiel's temple Ezek 42:4 (compare Da).
2. journey from Babylonian to Jerusalem Neh 2:6 of dimension of Nineveh, a journey of three days Jonah 3:3 i.e. diameter or length, compare Jonah 3:4 (according to Herodotsee 58 one day's journey = 150 stadia, = length of Nineveh according to Diodii.3).
3. plural goings, i.e. free access ( VB ) Zech 3:7.
