BDB9237 [H8626]

BDB9236 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9238

[תָּקַן] verb become straight (Late Hebrew תִּקֵּן arrange, put right; Eccl 47:9 תיקן set in order; Assyrian takânu , be well ordered, especially Pi`el as Late Hebrew; Aramaic be established, firm, Pa`el , תֵּקֵּין fix, arrange, prepare, etc.; Palmyrene Pa`el erect); —

Qal Infinitive construct לִתְקֹן Eccl 1:15 (opposed to מְעֻוָּת bent).
Pi`el Infinitive construct לְתַקֵּן Eccl 7:13 make straight (opposed to עִוָּה); Perfect 3 masculine singular תִּקֵּן Eccl 12:9 put straight, arrange in order (proverbs).
