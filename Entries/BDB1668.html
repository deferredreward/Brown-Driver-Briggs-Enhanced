BDB1668 [H1565]

BDB1667 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1669

גַּלְמוּד adjective hard, barren (Late Hebrew id. lonely NHWB; Arabic rock, stony (of land); Aramaic גַּלְמוּדָה compare BaNB 208) — ׳ ג Job 3:7 + 2 t. Job; feminine גַּלְמוּדָה Isa 49:21 — hard, barren, unproductive, mostly figurative: Job 15:34 of company of wicked men, where probably a substantive see De Di; Job 30:3 through want and famine (they are) stiff (lifeless; RV gaunt); of exiled Zion as bereaved and barren woman Isa 49:21 so of night of Job's birth Job 3:7.
