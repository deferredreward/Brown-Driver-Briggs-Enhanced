[פָּלַשׁ] verb

Hithpa`el act of mourning, possibly roll in (dust, etc.; weakened from burrow into) (Late Hebrew Pi`el break open, or through; Assyrian palâšu , dig a hole; Syriac dig or break through; but connection dubious, Vrss mostly be-sprinkle oneself, Aq roll oneself); — Perfect 1 singular עָפָר הִתְפַּלָּ֑שְׁתִּי Mic 1:10 Kt ( Qr Imperative פַּלָּ֑שִׁי-), others masculine plural פַּלָּ֑שׁוּ-); Imperfect 3 masculine plural בָּאֵפֶר יִתְפַּלָּ֑שׁוּ Ezek 27:30, so Imperative feminine singular הִתֵפַּלֵּשִׁי בָאֵפֶר Jer 6:26 (compare Mic 1:10 Qr ); absolute, masculine plural הִתֵפַּלְּשׁוּ Mic 25:34.
