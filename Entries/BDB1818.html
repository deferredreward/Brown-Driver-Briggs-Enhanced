BDB1817 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1819

[דֹּ֫בֶר] noun [masculine] pasture (compare Aramaic דַּבְרָא, Syriac ) — suffix כְּעֵדֶר בְּתוֺךְ הַדָּֽבְרוֺ as a flock in the midst of its pasture Mic 2:12 (article with suffix, compare Ges«GKC:127»§ 127, 4 b, who attaches ו as conjunction to following word); וְרָעזּ כְבָשִׂים כְּדֳבְרָם and lambs shall feed as (in) their pasture Isa 5:17.
