Perfect; Imperfect 3 masculine וַיִּ֫יצֶר Gen 2:7 וַיִּ֫צֶר Gen 2:19 suffix יִצְּרֵהוּ Isa 44:12 1 singular suffix אֶצֳּו֯רְךָ Jer 1:5 Participle יוֺצֵר Isa 41:25 + 20 t.; יֹצֵר Isa 45:18 + 6 t.; suffix יֹצְרִי Isa 49:5 + 11 t. suffix; plural יוֺצְרִים Isa 30:14; 1Chr 4:23 construct יֹצְרֵי Isa 44:9 —
1. of human activity:
a. of a potter who forms out of clay a vessel Isa 29:16; Isa 41:25; Jer 18:4 (twice in verse); Jer 18:6 (twice in verse); 1Chr 4:23; Lam 4:2; Zech 11:13 (twice in verse) (these last two according to Thes SS et al. error for אוֺצָר); ׳ כְּלִי (ה)י potter's vessel 2Sam 17:28 ; Ps 2:9; Jer 19:11 ׳ חֹמֶר הי potter's clay Isa 29:16 ׳ נֵבֶל י Isa 30:14 ׳ בַּקְבֻּק י Jer 19:1 ׳ בית הי Jer 18:2; Jer 18:3.
b. of a carver of wood, graven images Isa 44:9; Isa 44:10; Isa 44:12; Hab 2:18 (twice in verse).
c. frame, devise in the mind עָמָל ׳ י Ps 94:20 .
2. of divine activity:
a. (as a potter) forming Adam out of עפר from אדמה Gen 2:7; Gen 2:8 ( J ), beasts and birds, also from אדמה Gen 2:19 ( J ); Israel as a people Isa 27:11; Isa 43:1; Isa 43:21; Isa 44:21; Isa 45:9 (twice in verse); Isa 45:11 ; Isa 64:7 , even from the womb Isa 44:2; Isa 44:24 the servant of Yahweh from the womb Isa 49:5 of the formation of the individual man Isa 43:7 Jeremiah in the womb Jer 1:5 the eye of man Ps 94:9 the locust Amos 7:1 Leviathan Ps 104:26 the dry land Ps 95:5 the earth Isa 45:18 (twice in verse); the mountains Amos 4:13 הַכֹּל Jer 10:16 = Jer 51:19 . Figurative perhaps lost sight of in some of the above, and quite certainly in the forming of light Isa 45:7, of summer and winter Ps 74:17 , the רוּחַ of man Zech 12:1, and the לב of men Ps 33:15.
b. figurative for frame, pre-ordain, plan (in divine purpose), of a situation מֵרָחוֺק Isa 22:11 of an occurrence מִימֵי קֶדֶם Isa 37:26 = 2Kgs 19:25 יָצַרְתִּי אַף אֶעֱשֶׂנָּה I have planned, I will also do it 2Kgs 46:11 רעה על ׳ י Jer 18:11 לַהֲכִינָהּ ׳ י devised it to establish it Jer 33:2.
