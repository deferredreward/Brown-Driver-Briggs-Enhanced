BDB7259 [H6872]

Biblical Hebrew

I. צְרוֺר noun masculine Hag 1:6 bundle, parcel, pouch, bag (properly a binding, i.e. something bound up); — ׳ צ absolute Amos 9:9 +, construct Gen 42:35 +; plural construct צְרֹרוֺת Gen 42:35 — bundle or pouch (purse) of money Gen 42:35 (twice in verse) ( E ), Prov 7:20 הַמֹּר ׳ צ Song 1:13 (figurative of lover); ׳ צְרוּרָה בִּצ הַחַיִּים 1Sam 25:29 (figurative; see I. צרר ); פִּשְׁעִי ׳ חָתֻם בִּצ Job 14:17 (i.e. hidden, forgotten Hi Bu and others; > al. treasured up), נָקוּב ׳ צ Hag 1:6 (see I. נקב ).
