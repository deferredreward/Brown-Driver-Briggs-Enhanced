BDB8072 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8074

[רָצַד] verb

Pi`el watch stealthily, or with envious hostility (Arabic watch or wait (often lie in wait) for; ירצר Eccl 14:22 observe stealthily, so once Aramaic רְצַר LevyNHWB iv. 464); — only Imperfect 2 masculine plural תְּרַצְּדוּן Ps 68:17 (figurative of mountains, with accusative).
