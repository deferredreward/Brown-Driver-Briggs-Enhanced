חֵל, חֵיל noun masculineObad 1:20 rampart, fortress (perhaps originally surrounding wall, compare SabDenkm91 n. 2) — absolute חֵיל Nah 3:8, חֵל Lam 2:8 + 3 t.;(+ Ps 10:10 Qr see חלכה); construct חֵל 1Kgs 21:23 suffix חֵילֵךְ Ps 122:7, חֵילָהּ Zech 9:4 חֵילָה = חֵילָהּ Ps 48:14, Ol§ 40 approximately 75g. 96 e Sta§ 347 c; —

1. rampart (defined by Jews as בֶּן חוֺמָה, i.e. a little wall, compare Ki under the word), of an outer fortification 2Sam 20:15 (others, by metonymy, of space between outer and inner fortification, including moat, see Dr Kit); Isa 26:1; Lam 2:8 (both || חוֺמוֺת); in general of defences, or sea-power of Tyre וְהִכָּה בַיָּם חֵילָהּ Zech 9:4 (see StaZAW i.1881, 15), of No-Amon (Thebes) אֲשֶׁר חֵיל יָם מִיָּם חוֺמָתָהּ Nah 3:8 of Zion Ps 122:7, also Ps 48:14 (חילה = חֵילָהּ, compare above; both || ארמנותיך). — For חֵל 1Kgs 21:23 read probably חֵלֶק, q. v.
2. fortress גָּלֻת הַחֵל הַזֶּה Obad 1:20 the exiles of this fortress (Hi-St; Or, AV RV this army, חֵל = חַיִל).
