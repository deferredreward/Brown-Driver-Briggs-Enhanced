BDB2685 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2687

חָזְקָה noun feminine strength, force, violence, always בְּחָזְקָה with strength, force, etc.; —

1. of oppression Judg 4:3 forcibly, violently; capture by force 1Sam 2:16; Ezek 34:4 of ruling וּבְפָּ֑רֶךְ ׳ בְּח with force and with rigour.
2. rebuke severely, sharply Judg 8:1 cry mightily, insistently Jonah 3:8. — לֲחָזְקָה 2Kgs 12:13 see חָזַק Qal Infinitive
