פֶּ֫לַח noun feminine Job 41:16 cleavage, mill-stone (mill cleft between the stones ? compare GFMJu; ᵑ7 פִּלְחָא); — ׳ פ absolute Job 41:16, elsewhere construct; —

1.
a. mill-stone, רֶכֶב ׳ פ mill-stone of riding, i.e. upper stone, which is turned (compare רֶכֶב), Judg 9:53; 2Sam 11:21 תַּחְתִּית ׳ פ Job 41:16 lower mill-stone (simile
of hardness).
b. דְּבֵלָה ׳ פ 1Sam 30:12 a cake of figs (from shape ?).
2. cleavge, split, hence slice הָרִמּוֺן ׳ פ Song 4:3=Song 6:7 simile of human temple (from colour, compare Wetzst in DeComm. Excurs.A).
