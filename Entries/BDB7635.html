BDB7634 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7636

I. [קָשָׁה] verb be hard, severe, fierce (Late Hebrew קָשָׁה be severe; Arabic ( ) be hard, dry, III. endure, struggle against, severity ; Aramaic קְשָׁא, be hard, difficult; Jewish-Aramaic קְשִׁי, קַשְׁיָה adjective hard); —

Qal Perfect 3 feminine singular קָֽשְׁתָה 1Sam 5:7, קָשָׁ֑תָה Gen 49:7 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִקְשֶׁה Deut 1:17; Deut 15:18, וַיִּ֫קֶשׁ 2Sam 19:44 —
1. be hard, difficult Deut 1:17 (of a legal case; מִן comparative).
2. be hard, severe, יָדוֺ ׳ ק עָלֵינוּ 1Sam 5:7 (of ׳ י, in judgement); בְעֵינֶ֑ךָ ׳ יִק. Deut 15:18 it shall not be hard in thine eyes (seem a severe trial, an injustice); of wrath Gen 49:7 (poem in J ; || עָ֑ז); of fierce words 2Sam 19:44.
Niph`al Participle נִקְשֶׁה Isa 8:21 hardly bestead, hard pressed.
Pi`el Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַתְּקַשׁ בְּלִדְתָּהּ Gen 35:16 ( E ) she made hard in her bearing (had severe labour).
Hiph`il Perfect 3 masculine singular הִקְשָׁה Exod 13:15 +, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיֶּקֶשׁ 2Chr 36:13 3 masculine plural וַיַּקְשׁוּ Jer 7:26 +, etc.; Infinitive construct suffix בְּהַקְשֹׁתָהּ Gen 35:17 Participle מַקְשֶׁה Prov 28:14 ; Prov 29:1 —
1. make difficult, difficulty: הִקְשָׁה לְשַׁלְּחֵנוּ Exod 13:15 Pharaoh made difficulty about sending us away; of travail Gen 35:17 ( E ; as Pi`el); הִקְשִׁיתָ לִשְׁאוֺל 2Kgs 2:10 thou hast made hard to ask, asked a hard thing.
2. make severe, burdensome, yoke imposed by king 1Kgs 12:4 = 2Chr 10:4.
3.
a. make hard, stiff, stubborn, figurative of obstinacy: וַיַּקְשׁוּ אֶתעָֿרְפָּם 2Kgs 17:14 they stiffened their neck, so Jer 7:26; Jer 17:23; Jer 19:15; Deut 10:16; 2Chr 30:8; 2Chr 36:13; Neh 9:16; Neh 9:17; Neh 9:29; Prov 29:1 אַלתַּֿקְשׁוּ לְבַבְכֶם Ps 95:8, compare Prov 28:14 subject ׳ י, ׳ אַקְשֶׁה אֶתלֵֿב פ Exod 7:3 ( P ), accusative אֶתרֿוחוֺ Deut 2:30,
b. shew stubborness Job 9:4 (with אֶל person [׳ י]).
