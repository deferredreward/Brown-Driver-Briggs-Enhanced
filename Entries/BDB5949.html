עֻזָּא proper name, masculine Οζα, Αζα :

1. driver of the ark 2Sam 6:3 = עֻזָּה 2Sam 6:6 2Sam 6:7 2Sam 6:8 = עֻזָּא 1Chr 13:7; 1Chr 13:9; 1Chr 13:10; 1Chr 13:11.
2. in גַּןעֻֿזָּא burial-place of Manasseh and Amon 2Kgs 21:18; 2Kgs 21:26 (׳ ע perhaps = עֻזִּיָּה, compare StaG i. 569, ii. 679 Kit).
3. Benjamite name 1Chr 8:7.
4. head of family of returned exiles Ezra 2:49 = Neh 7:51.
