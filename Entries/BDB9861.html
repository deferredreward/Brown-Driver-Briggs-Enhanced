Biblical Aramaic

[קְטַר] noun masculine Dan 5:6 joint, knot; — literal plural construct קִטְרֵי Dan 5:6 joints of his loin; figurative absolute קִטְרִין Dan 5:12 Dan 5:16, usually knotty things, difficulties (Syriac PS3591); but probably of magic spells, banns (also Syriac PSl.c. , compare Brock319 b), so Bev, see also Dr.
