[כָּוָה] verb burn, scorch, brand (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic especially Pa`el; Arabic cauterize) —

Niph`al Imperfect 2 masculine singular תִּכָּוֶה Isa 43:2 thou shalt not be scorched (figurative of Israel, || בָּעַר); 3 feminine plural וְרַגְלָיו לֹא תִּכָּוֶי֑נָה Prov 6:28 shall a man walk on the coals and his feet not be burned ? (|| שָׂרַף of garments v Prov 6:27).
