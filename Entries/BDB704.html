BDB704 [H686]

Biblical Hebrew

[אָצַר] verb lay up, store up (Mishna id., Aramaic אֲצַר, , Arabic confine, restrict) —

Qal Perfect 3 plural אָֽצְרוּ 2Kgs 20:17; Isa 39:6 Participle הָאֹצְרִים Amos 3:10 — store up treasure 2Kgs 20:17 = Isa 39:6 object חָמָס וָשֹׁד Amos 3:10, i.e. treasure gained by violence robbery.
Niph`al Imperfect יֵאָצֵר be stored up, of the merchandise of Tyre Isa 23:18.
Hiph`il Imperfect וָאוֺצְרָה (compare Köi, 391) ( denominative from אוֺצָר) Neh 13:13 and I appointed treasurer, followed by accusative of person + עַלאֿוֺצָרוֺת .
