BDB6119 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6121

[עָלַס] verb rejoice (|| with עָלַז, עָלַץ, q. v.; Late Hebrew עֲלִיסָה rejoicing (once LevyNHWB iii. 657)); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular יַעֲלֹס Job 20:18 he shall not rejoice. Niph`al Perfect 3 feminine singular נֶעֱלָ֑סָה Job 39:13 (of ostrich's wing) = flap joyously. Hithpa`el Imperfect 1 plural cohortative נִתְעַלְּסָה Prov 7:18 let us delight ourselves in (ב) love.
