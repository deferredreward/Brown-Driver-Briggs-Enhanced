BDB5873 [H5756]

Biblical Hebrew

[עוּז], עוֺז verb take or seek refuge (Arabic , take refuge, seek protection, a refuge); —

Qal Infinitive construct לָעוֺז בְּמָעוֺז Isa 30:2 (|| לַחְסוֺת בְּ).
Hiph`il bring into safety, Perfect 3 masculine plural הֵעִ֫יזוּ Isa 10:31, object omitted, and so Imperative masculine plural הָעִ֫יזוּ Jer 4:6, הָעִ֫זוּ מִקֶּרֶב Jer 6:1 with accusative, masculine singular הָעֵז Exod 9:19.
