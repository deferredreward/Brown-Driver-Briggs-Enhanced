[הָמַן] verb rage, be trubulent (? denominative from המון Thes, after Rabb ; compare Ke RV ; AV multiply; but very dubious) — only

Qal Infinitive suffix יַעַן הֲמָנְכֶם מִןהַֿגּוֺיִם Ezek 5:7 (Sm queries; Ew, after ᵑ6 Aq , הִמָּנֵכֶם from מנה); read rather, with Bö Co Da, המרותכם (from מרה) because ye rebelled.
