BDB8706 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8708

I. שֶׁ֫לַח noun [masculine] missile, weapon, sprout (late); — absolute ׳ שׁ Joel 2:8 +, שָׁ֑לַח Neh 4:11; Job 33:18, suffix שִׁלְחוֺ Neh 4:17 plural שְׁלָחִים 2Chr 23:10, etc.; —

1. missile, weapon, Neh 4:11 and v.Neh 4:17 (where הַמַּיִם corrupt; read probably בִּימִינוֺ or בְּיָדוֺ; see Ryle BeRy Berthol), 2Chr 23:10 (distinguished from חֲנִית, מָנֵן); collective 2Chr 32:5 and ׳ עָבַר בַּשׁ Job 33:18; Job 36:12 (perish by or < rush upon [and perish]), Joel 2:8 (see בְּעַד ).
2. sprout, shoot (compare [שְׁלוּחָה], and √ Ps 80:12; Jer 17:8), שְׁלָחַיִךְ פַּרְדֵּס Song 4:13 (figurative; meaning ? PerlesAnal. 63 proposes שְׁנֵי לְחָיָיִךְ thy two cheeks); + probably Ezek 31:5 (read בִּשְׁלָחָיו Co Toy and others, or בְּשִׁלְחֵי [with vEzek 31:6] Krae).
