BDB3663 [H4163]

BDB3662 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3664

[מוֺצָאָה] noun feminine only plural:

a. Mic 5:1 מוֺצָאוֺתָיו (compare future ruler out of Bethlehem), his origin.
b. 2Kgs 10:27 Qr מוֺצָאוֺת places of going out to, i.e. a privy (compare Arabic ; German Abtritt), euphemistically for Kt מַחֲרָאוֺת.
