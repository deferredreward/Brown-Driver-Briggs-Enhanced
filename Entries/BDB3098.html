BDB3098 [H2727]

Biblical Hebrew

[חָרַג] verb quake (compare (si vera lectio) Arabic be straitened, unable to move for fear and rage (Lane ), also put oneself in a rage (Dozy); ᵑ7 חַרְגַת מוֺתָא Deut 32:25 terror of death) — only

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine plural וְיַחְרְגוּ מִמִּסְגְּרוֺתֵיהֶם Ps 18:46 pregnantly and come quaking out of their fortresses (compare יִרְגְּזוּ in like connection Mic 7:17 ); read the same also in || 2Sam 22:46 (for ᵑ0 וְיַחְגְרוּ, see חגר ; possible would be also ויחרדו in both, as Hos 11:10; Hos 11:11 etc.)
