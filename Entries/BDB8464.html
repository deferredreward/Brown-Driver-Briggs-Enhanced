[שָׁגַח] verb

Hiph`il gaze (Late Hebrew id., Eccl 40:29; Eccl 50:5 late Rabb הַשְׁגָּחָה = providence (from Ps 33:14 Jewish-Aramaic שְׁגַח gaze, consider); — with אֶלֿ person; Perfect 3 masculine singular הִשְׁגִּיחַ Ps 33:14 (of ׳ י; מִן local); Imperfect 3 masculine plural רֹאֶיךָ אֵלֶיךָ יַשְׁגִּיחוּ Isa 14:16 they that see thee, at thee shall they gaze (|| אֵלָיךָ יִתְבּוֺנָ֫נוּ; compare משגיח על שלחן Eccl 40:29); absolute: Participle מַשְׁגִּיחַ Song 2:9 (with מִן from (the outside of) the windows).
