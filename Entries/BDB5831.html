BDB5831 [H4574 H4575]

Biblical Hebrew

[מַעֲדָן] noun [masculine] dainty (food), delight only plural; — מַעֲדַגֵּי מֶלָךְ Gen 49:20 royal dainties; הָאֹכְלִים לְמַעֲדַנִּים Lam 4:5 those who ate (according to dainties) daintily; more generally וְיִתֵּן מַעֲדַנִּים לְנַפְשֶׁ֑ךָ Prov 29:17 he shall give delight to thy soul (|| וִינִידֶ֑ךָ). — מַעֲדַנּוֺת , also below ענד.
