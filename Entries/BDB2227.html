II. הָרָה adjective feminine pregnant — absolute ׳ ה Gen 16:11 + 10 t.; construct הֲרַת Jer 20:17 plural construct הָרוֺת Amos 1:13 suffix הָרוֺתֶיהָ 2Kgs 15:16 הָרֹתֵיהֶם 2Kgs 8:12 — pregnant, as attributive, ׳ אִשָּׁה ה Exod 21:22 as predicate Gen 38:24 (לִזְנוּנִים), Gen 38:25 (לְאִישׁ), 1Sam 4:19; 2Sam 11:5; Isa 7:14 (+ וְיֹלֶדֶת); in phrase הִנָּךְ הָרָה וְיֹלַדְתְּ Gen 16:11 (on resemblance to Isa 7:14 compare PetersHebraica. Apr. 1885, 243; Apr. 1886, 175), Judg 13:5; Judg 13:7 = substantive pregnant women, women with child Amos 1:13; 2Kgs 8:12; 2Kgs 15:16; Jer 31:8, and, in simile, Isa 26:17 once of womb וְרַחְמָהּ הֲרַת עוֺלָם Jer 20:17.
