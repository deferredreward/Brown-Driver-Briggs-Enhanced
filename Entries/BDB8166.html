[שָׂגָה] verb grow, increase (late; || form of שׂגא); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִשְׂגֶּה Ps 92:13 righteous shall grow great like cedar (|| יִפְרַח), compare Job 8:7 (opposed to מִצְעָר; Job 8:11 see שׂגא ).
Hiph`il Perfect 3 plural הִשְׂגּוּחַֿיִל Ps 73:12 they have increased riches.
