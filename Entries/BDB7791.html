BDB7790 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7792

[מָרוֺד, Köil. l, 127 f.] noun [masculine] restlessness, straying (?); — suffix עָנְיִי וּמְרוּדִי Lam 3:19 plural suffix יְמֵי עָנְיָהּ וּמְרוּדֶיהָ Lam 1:7 (read probably מְרוּדָהּ NöZMG xxxvii (1883), 539); plural וַעֲנִיִּם מְרוּדִים Isa 58:7, usually concrete the wandering (homeless) poor (Di thinks old Qal passive Participle; CheComm. reads Hoph`al מוּרָדִים; BuhlLex Hiph`il מָרִידִים; Köii. 1, 128 takes ᵑ0 abstract for concrete [apposition of ׳ וַע]; so Du CheHpt [׳ וַע gloss], read perhaps participle רָדִים (מ dittograph)).
