[נוּן, נִין] verb propagate, increase (si vera lectio) ( > denominative from נין Hup-Now SS; — Late Hebrew נוּן = fish, so Aramaic נוּנָא, , Assyrian nunu; —

Qal or Hiph`il ?) Imperfect ינין Kt; > Niph`al ינּוֺן Qr Ps 72:17 let his name have increase (or, if Niph`al, be propagated, spread); but dubious, Vrss perhaps יִכּוֺן be established, endure.
