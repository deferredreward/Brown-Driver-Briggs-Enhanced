BDB6094 [H5955]

Biblical Hebrew

עוֺלֵלוֺת noun feminine plural intensive gleaning (going over a second time); — gleaning (of grapes and olives; לֶקֶט of grain), always figurative of remnant: absolute ׳ ע Isa 17:6 (|| כְּנֹקֶף זַיִת), Isa 24:13 (simile; || id., + בָּצִיר), Jer 49:9 (compare בֹּצְרִים va) = Obad 1:5 (id.); construct עֹלִלֹת בָּצִיר Mic 7:1 figurative of warlike achievement עֹלְלוֺת אֶפְרַיִם Judg 8:2 (opposed to בָּצִיר).
