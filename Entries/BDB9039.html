Biblical Hebrew

שִׁרְיוֺן, שִׁרְיָ֑ן noun [masculine] body-armour; — absolute שִׁרְיוֺן 1Sam 17:5; 1Sam 17:38 שִׁרְיָ֑ן (Ges«GKC:29u»§ 29u) 1Kgs 22:34 = 2Chr 18:33, שִׁרְיָ֔ן Isa 59:17 plural שִׁרְיֹנִים Neh 4:10, שִׁרְיֹנוֺת 2Chr 26:14 — body-armour, perhaps more exactly breast-armour, Neh 4:10 with appendages ( הַדְּבָקִים) 1Kgs 22:34 = 2Chr 18:33 with verb לבשׁ 1Sam 17:5, made of scale-like plates (קַשְׂקֵשִּׂים) of bronze 1Sam 17:5, named with helmet here, so 1Sam 17:38; 2Chr 26:14 and (figurative) Isa 59:17. — ׳ שׂ proper name see שׂריון.
