מִשְׁחָה

noun 1. ointment;
2. consecrated portion; — absolute ׳ מ Exod 25:6 +; construct מִשְׁחַת Exod 30:25 +; —
1. ointment, the holy oil used in the consecration of the Aaronic priests, only in P ; שֶׁמֶן הַמִּשְׁחָה Exod 25:6; Exod 29:7; Exod 29:21; Exod 31:11; Exod 35:8; Exod 35:15; Exod 35:28; Exod 37:29; Exod 39:38; Exod 40:9; Lev 8:2; Lev 8:10; Lev 8:12; Lev 8:30; Lev 21:10; Num 4:16 מִשְׁחַת קֹדֶשׁ ׳ שׁ Exod 30:25 (twice in verse); Exod 30:31 יהוה ׳ מ ׳ שׁ Lev 10:7 אֱלֹהָיו ׳ מ ׳ שׁ Lev 21:12.
2. consecrated portion, of Aaron and his sons Lev 7:35 (twice in verse) ( P ).
