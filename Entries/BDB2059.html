הַגְרִי

1. proper name, of a people only plural הַגְרִים Ps 83:7 הַהַגְרִאִים 1Chr 5:10, הַהַגְרִיאִים 1Chr 5:19; 1Chr 5:20 a tribe (Aramaean ? Arabian ?) with which the east Jordan Israelites waged successful war; see dubious conjecture as to identity in GlasSkizze ii. 407.
2. adjective, of a people of an officer of David יָזִיז הַהַגְרִי 1Chr 27:31.
3. proper name, masculine father of one of David's warriors בֶּןהַֿגְרִי 1Chr 11:38 (but || 2Sam 23:36 בָּנִי הַגָּדִי so here Öttli, compare Be ThSm DrSm).

הַגְרִיאִים, הַגְרִאִים see foregoing.
