כָּבוּל proper name [of a location]

1. city on border of Asher Josh 19:27, modern Kâbûl, 4 h. (9 miles) southeast from Akko, RobBR iii, 88 GuérinGal, i. 422 f.
2. אֶרֶץ כָּבוּל district in Galilee 1Kgs 9:13 containing 20 cities given by Solomon to Hiram (popular etymology possibly כּ as + בֻּל = בַּל not, i.e. as good as nothing, compare 1Kgs 9:12, so Ew Th; Klo conjectures ארץ גָּלִיל), BuhlGeogr. § 116.
