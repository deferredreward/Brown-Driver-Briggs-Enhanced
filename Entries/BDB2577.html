BDB2576 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2578

[חָדַשׁ] verb only Pi`el (and Hithpa`el) renew, repair (in poetry and late) (Late Hebrew id. , Phoenician חדש; noun חדש new moon, also in קרתחדשת new-city = Carthage; Aramaic and חַדֵּשׁ; Arabic be new, Ethiopic 1. 2. renew; Sabean החדתֿ, see Os (Levy):ZMG 1865, 204 MordtZMG 1876, 30; Assyrian [adâšu], uddiš, renew, eššu , new, etc. DlW 199 ff. ) —

Pi`el Perfect וְחִדְּשׁוּ consecutive Isa 61:4 Imperfect וַיְחַדֵּשׁ 2Chr 15:8 2 masculine singular תְּחַדֵּשׁ Job 10:17 ; Ps 104:30 וּנְחַדֵּשׁ 1Sam 11:14 Imperative חַדֵּשׁ Ps 51:21 Infinitive לְחַדֵּשׁ 2Chr 24:4 ; 2Chr 24:12 —
1. renew, make anew הַמְּלוּכָה 1Sam 11:14 (editorial); פני אדמה Ps 104:30, רוּחַ Ps 51:12 (|| בּרא); = bring back יָמֵינוּ כְּקֶדֶם Lam 5:21 עדיך ׳ ח Job 10:17 i.e. bringest fresh (new) witnesses.
2. repair, cities Isa 61:4, temple 2Chr 24:4; 2Chr 24:12, altar 2Chr 15:8.
Hithpa`el Imperfect 3 feminine singular תִּתְחַדֵּשׁ with plural subject כַּנֶּשֶׁר נְעוּרָ֑יְכִת ׳ ת Ps 103:5 thy youth reneweth itself, like the eagle.
