BDB167 [H88]

Biblical Hebrew

אֹבֹת proper name [of a location] (water-skins) station of Israel in wilderness Num 21:10; Num 34:43 not yet determined, probably on eastern skirts of Idumea not far from Moab; accusative to Wetzst in DeCt 168 Wêba, in the Arabah, but identification not probably; compare Di on Num 21:10.
