BDB3039 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3041

חֹ֫צֶן noun masculine bosom; — חָצְנִי נָעַרְתִּי Neh 5:13 I shook out my bosom (the bosom of my garment); וְהֵבִיאוּ בָּנַיִךְ בְּחֹ֫צֶן Isa 49:22 and they shall bring thy sons in the bosom (i.e. in the arms, clasped to the bosom, like infants; || עַלכָּֿתֵף).
