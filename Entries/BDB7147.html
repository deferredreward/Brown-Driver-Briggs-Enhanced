BDB7147 [H6794]

BDB7146 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7148

צִנּוֺר noun masculine pipe, spout, conduit (so Late Hebrew); — absolute וְיִגַּע בַּצִּנּוֺר 2Sam 5:8 (i.e., si vera lectio, of Jerusalem, but very dubious, compare Dr HPS); plural suffix קוֺל צִנּוֺרֶיךָ Ps 42:8 the sound of thy (water-) spouts figurative, of sluices of heaven opened, compare אֲרֻבֶּה, פֶּלֶג.
