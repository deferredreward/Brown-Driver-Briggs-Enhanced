רִקְמָה noun feminine variegated stuff (woven or embroidered); — absolute ׳ ר Judg 5:30 +; suffix רִקְמָתֵךְ Ezek 16:18, תָם- Ezek 26:16 dual רִקְמָתַיִם Judg 5:30 plural רְקָמוֺת Ps 45:15 — (piece of) variegated stuff, Judg 5:30 (twice in verse), for garments Ezek 16:10; Ezek 16:13; Ezek 27:16; Ezek 27:24; Ps 45:15 explicitly, ׳ בִּגְדֵי ר Ezek 16:18; Ezek 26:16 ׳ ר apparently = variegated work Ezek 27:7 figurative of variegated plumage Ezek 17:3 of variegated stone 1Chr 29:2.
