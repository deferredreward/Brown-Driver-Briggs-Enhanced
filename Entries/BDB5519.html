BDB5519 [H5475]

Biblical Hebrew

סוֺד noun [masculine] council, counsel; — ׳ ס absolute Prov 11:13 +; construct Jer 6:11 +; suffix סוֺדִי Jer 23:22; Job 19:19 סוֺדוֺ Amos 3:7; Prov 3:32 סֹדָם Gen 49:6 —

1. council, in familiar conversation; —
a. divan or circle of familiar friends, בַּחוּרִים ׳ ס Jer 6:11, משׂחקים ׳ מ Jer 15:17 ׳ מְתֵי סוֺדִ Job 19:19 men of my intimate circle; ׅ ׳ עמד בסוד (י Jer 23:18 ; Jer 23:22 in the intimate circle of ׳ י; בסוד אלוה Job 15:8 in bad sense, מְרֵעִים ׳ ס Ps 64:3.
b. assembly, company, יְשָׁרִים ׳ ס Ps 111:1 (|| עֵדָה; סוֺד עמי Ezek 13:9 קדשׁים ׳ ס Ps 89:8 (of angels); in bad sense, בסוד בא Gen 49:6 (|| קהל).
2. counsel, taken by those in familiar conversation:
a. counsel itself, מחשׁבות באין סוד Prov 15:22 thoughts without counsel; of intimate friendship, Ps 55:15 נַמְתִּיק סוֺד; in bad sense, of crafty plotting Ps 83:4 יַעֲרִימוּ סוֺד
b. secret counsel, which may be revealed (גלה), Amos 3:7; Prov 11:13; Prov 20:19; Prov 25:9.
c. familiar converse with God, intimacy, ׳ י ׳ ליראיו ס Ps 25:14 intimacy with ׳ י have those who fear him (|| ברית), אתיֿשׁרים סודוֺ Prov 3:22 with the upright is his intimacy; here also Job 29:4 (si vera lectio) Di De and others; Siegf Du compare BuhlLex בְּסֹךְ when Eloah sheltered my tent.
