BDB1153 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1155

[בָּלַק] verb waste, lay waste (Assyrian balâ‡u , Pi`el destroy, LyonSargon 61 ; compare Arabic a desert) —

Po`el Participle without מ, suffix בּוֺלְקָהּ Isa 24:1 (|| בּוֺקֵק, subject ׳ י, object הארץ); — on form (which might also be Qal Participle) compare Ol§ 254 LagGN 1882, 403; also בקק Po. Jer 51:2, and intensive use of Infinitive absolute Niph`al Isa 24:3 further Punic (i.e. intensive) Participle Nah 2:11 (this however perhaps largely influenced by assonance).
Pu`al Participle מְבֻלָּקָ֑ה Nah 2:11 ( ׳ בּוּקָה וּמְבוּקָה ומב), devastated, or as substantive a devastated city.
