BDB1766 [H1643]

Biblical Hebrew

גֶּ֫רֶשׂ noun [masculine] a crushing, but only concrete of that which is crushed, groats, grits (compare Arabic , Late Hebrew גְּרִיס, Aramaic (rare) גִּרְשָׂא, גְּרוּסְיָא ) — אָבִיב קָלוּי בָּאֵשׁ גֶּרֶשׂ כַּרְמֶל Lev 2:14 young ears parched with fire, groats (&) fresh fruit (see Sifra on the passage); מִגִּרְשָׂהּ (וּמִשַּׁמְנָהּׅ Lev 2:16.
