פּוֺטִי פֶ֫רַע proper name, masculine priest of On, Joseph's father-in-law (Egyptian P°-di°-p°-R±, i.e. he whom the Ra gave, see SetheDe aleph prosthetico in ling. aeg. (1892), 31; Dr in HastingsDB); — Gen 41:45; Gen 41:50 (E), Gen 46:20 (P), Πετεφρης, A Πετρεφης.
