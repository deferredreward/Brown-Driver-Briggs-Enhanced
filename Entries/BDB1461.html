גָּבַר verb be strong, mighty (Late Hebrew id. Aramaic גְּבַר; Arabic (conjugation I. dialect; usually in derived conjugation) compel, force; overbearing behaviour, constraint; Ethiopic : I, 1, subigere; II, 2, cogere; Syriac play the man, is denominative from man) —

Qal Perfect ׳ג Ps 103:11 + 5 t.; גָּבְרוּ Gen 7:19 + 6 t.; גָּבֵ֑רוּ 2Sam 1:23 Imperfect יִגְבַּר 1Sam 2:9 וַיִּגְבְּרוּ Gen 7:18; Gen 7:24 —
1. be strong, mighty, absolute גָּבְרוּ חַיִל mighty in power Job 21:7 with מִן stronger than 2Sam 1:24; Ps 65:4 with בְּ mighty among 1Chr 5:2.
2. prevail: —
a absolute e.g. enemies Exod 17:11 (twice in verse) (E) 1Sam 2:9; Lam 1:16, waters Gen 7:18; Gen 7:19; Gen 7:20; Gen 7:24 (P), power Jer 9:2
\ \ b. with עַל prevail over, subject enemies 2Sam 11:23, blessings Gen 49:26(J), mercy of God Ps 103:11; Ps 117:2.
