BDB4230 [H3785]

BDB4229 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4231

[כֶּ֫שֶׁף] noun masculine 2Kgs 9:22 sorcery, only plural (Assyrian kišpu , id.); — כְּשָׁפִים Mic 5:11; Nah 3:4 suffix כְּשָׁפַיִךְ Isa 47:9; Isa 47:12 כְּשָׁפֶיהָ Nah 3:4; 2Kgs 9:22 — sorceries:

1. literal מִיָּדֶיךָ ׳ וְהִכְרַתִּי כ Mic 5:11, said of Israel; ׳ רֹב כ Isa 47:9; Isa 47:12 || חֲבָרַיִךְ, said of Babylon.
2. figurative of seductive and corrupting influences: of Jezebel 2Kgs 9:22 || זְנוּנִים; of Nineveh personified as harlot Nah 3:4 (|| id.), called ׳ בַּעֲלַת כ vNah 3:4 (following זוֺנָהמֵרֹב זְנוּנֵי).
