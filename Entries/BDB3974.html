BDB3973 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3975

כּוּתָה, כּוּת proper name [of a location] whence king of Assyria (Sargon) transported colonists into Northern Israel, מִכּוּתָה 2Kgs 17:4, אַנְשֵׁיכֿוּת 2Kgs 17:30 Babylonian Kûtû, Kûtê, modern Tel-Ibrahim, approximately 20 miles northeast from Babylon, see COT 2Kgs 17:24 DlPar 217 M-AJBL 1892, xi. 169.
