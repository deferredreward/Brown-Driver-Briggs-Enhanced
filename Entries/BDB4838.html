BDB4838 [H4846]

Biblical Hebrew

[מְרֹרָה] noun feminine bitter thing, gall, poison; construct מְרוֺרַת Job 20:14 suffix מְרֹרָתוֺ Job 20:25 plural מְרֹרֹת Deut 32:32, מְרֹרוֺת Job 13:36 —

1. gall, i.e. seat of gall, gall-bladder Job 20:25 (|| גֵּוָה back; compare מְרֵרָה Job 16:30 ).
2. = poison, פְּתָנִים ׳ מ Job 20:14.
3. מְרֹרוֺת bitter thing Job 13:26 (of God's edict of judgment).
4. plural abstract = bitterness; אַשְׁכְּלֹת ׳ מ Deut 32:32 clusters of bitterness (|| עִנְּבְי רוֺשׁ).
