[מִשְׁמָן] noun [masculine] fatness; — construct וּמִשְׁטַן בְּשָׁרוֺ יֵרָוֶה Isa 17:14 (figurative); plural concrete: stout, vigorous, ones, suffix מִשְׁמַנָּיו Isa 10:16 (opposed to רָווֺן on form see Ges«GKC:93pp»§ 93pp), מִשְׁמַנֵּיהֶם Ps 78:31 = fertile spots, construct מִשְׁמַנֵּי מְדִינָה Dan 11:24. compare also 1Sam 15:9 , see מִשְׁנֶה 3.
