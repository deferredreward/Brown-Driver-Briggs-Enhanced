BDB5975 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5977

עֲזַרְאֵל proper name, masculine (El hath helped); — Οζρειηλ, Εζριηλ, etc.;

1. warrior of David 1Chr 12:7 (van d. H. v.1Chr 12:6).
2. Levite musician 1Chr 25:18 (= עֻזִּיאֵל 1Chr 25:4), B Αζαρια, A Εζριηλ.
3. a Danite 1Chr 27:22, Αζαραηλ, Εζριηλ.
4. one with foreign wife Ezra 10:41.
5. priestly name:
a. Neh 11:13.
b. Neh 12:36.
