BDB3903 [H3529]

BDB3902 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3904

II. כְּבָר proper name, of a river Kebar, a river (or perhaps a canal) of Babylonia, not at present identified, by which the exiles, among whom Ezekiel ministered, were settled; always in the phrase נְהַר כְּבָר Ezek 1:1; Ezek 1:3; Ezek 3:15; Ezek 3:23; Ezek 10:15; Ezek 10:20; Ezek 10:22; Ezek 43:3 (compare SmEzek 1:1 DelPar. 47 f., 184).
