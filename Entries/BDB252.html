III. אור proper name [of a location] Ur (Babylonian Uru; seat of moon-god worship; hence Eupolemos in EusebPraep.Ev.ix. 17 says Καμαρίνῃ ἥν τινας λέγειν πόλιν Οὐρίην), ancient city in Southern Babylonia; OT always אור כַּשְׂדִּים i.e. Ur of the Kasdim (Chaldeans) see כַּשְׂדִּים below כשׂד; home of Terah, Abram's father, & A.'s point of departure for Mesopotamia & Canaan Gen 11:28 ; Gen 15:7 (both J ), & hence Neh 9:7 also Gen 11:31 ( P ); — modern Muqayyar, south of Euphrates, approximately 150 miles southeast of Babylon; see KG94 f DlPa 226 f COT on Gen 11:28.
