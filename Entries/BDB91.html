BDB90 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB92

אַגְמֹן, אַגְמוֺן noun [masculine] rush, bulrush.

1. used as cord or line Job 40:26 (of twisted rushes, or spun of rush-fibre, compare Di on the passage); as fuel Job 41:12 simile of bending head Isa 58:5.
2. metaphor of the lowly, insignif. (|| כִּפָּה) Isa 9:13; Isa 19:15.
