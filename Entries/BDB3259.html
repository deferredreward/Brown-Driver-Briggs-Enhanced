II. חתן verb denominative only

Hithpa`el make oneself a daughter's husband (Late Hebrew Hithpa`el id., Aramaic Ithpa`al id.) — Perfect 2 masculine plural וְהִתְחַתַּנְתֶּם consecutive Josh 23:12 Imperfect וַיִּתְחַתֵּן 1Kgs 3:1; 2Chr 18:1 2 masculine תִּתְחַתֵּן Deut 7:3; 1Sam 18:21 Imperative הִתְחַתֵּן 1Sam 18:22, הִתְחַתְּנוּ Gen 34:9 Infinitive הִתְחַתֵּן 1Sam 18:23 + 3 t.; —
1. make oneself daughter's husband (son-in-law) to, with בְּ 1Sam 18:21 ; 1Sam 18:22; 1Sam 18:23; 1Sam 18:26; 1Sam 18:27 with אֶת 1Kgs 3:1; Gen 34:9 ( P ); with לְ 2Chr 18:1 , of marriage of Jehoshaphat's son and Ahab's daughter (compare 2Kgs 8:18 = 2Chr 21:6).
2. in general form marriage-alliance with, with בְּ Deut 7:3; Josh 23:12 ( D ), Ezra 9:14.
