BDB3124 [H2762]

BDB3123 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3125

חֲרַכִּים noun [masculine] plural lattice or other opening through which one may look (Aramaic חֲרַכָּא ᵑ7 Josh 2:15 and elsewhere=Hebrew חַלּ֖וֺן; but Late Hebrew חֲרָךְ is an opening smaller than a window) — only ׳ מֵצִיץ מִןהַֿח Song 2:9 peeping in at the lattice (|| מַשְׁגִּיחַ מִןהַֿחַלֹּנוֺת).
