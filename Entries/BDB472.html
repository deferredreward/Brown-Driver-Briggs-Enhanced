אָלָה noun feminine oath Gen 26:28 +; suffix אָלָתִי, אָלָתוֺ Gen 24:11 + (4 t.); plural אָלוֺת Deut 29:20 + (4 t).

1. oath in testimony Lev 5:1; Num 5:21 (twice in verse) ( P ) Prov 29:23 בא באלה come into an oath Neh 10:30 הביא באלה bring into an oath Ezek 17:13 נשׂא אלה 1Kgs 8:31 ( = 2Chr 6:22).
2. oath of convenant Gen 24:41 (twice in verse); Gen 26:28 ( J ) Deut 29:11; Deut 29:13 בזה אלה despise an oath Ezek 16:59 ; Ezek 17:16; Ezek 17:18; Ezek 17:19.
3. curse
(a) from God Num 5:23 ( P ) Deut 29:18; Deut 29:19; Deut 29:20; Deut 30:7; Deut 2:24;
(b) from men Job 31:30; Ps 10:7; Ps 59:13.
4. execration in the phrase היה לאלה become an execration Num 5:27 ( P ) Jer 29:18; Jer 42:18; Jer 44:12.
