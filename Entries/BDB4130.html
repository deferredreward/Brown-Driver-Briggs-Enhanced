כֻּסֶּ֫מֶת noun feminine spelt, triticum spelta (Late Hebrew plural כוּסְמִין, compare ᵑ7 ᵑ6 כונתא, ; compare Löwp. 104 ff. Di Exod 9:23 NowArch i, 111); — absolute ׳ כ Exod 9:32; Isa 28:25 plural כֻּסְּמִים Ezek 4:9 (in all distinguished from wheat, barley, etc.)
