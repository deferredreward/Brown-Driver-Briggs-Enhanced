II. סָפִיחַ noun [masculine] growth from spilled kernels ((kernels) poured out, accidentally, in harvesting, according to most; BuhlLex 13 and others think of additional growth, √ I. ספח); — it is what springs up of itself in second year, and serves as food when no grain could be sown: absolute ׳ ס 2Kgs 19:29 = Isa 37:30 construct סְפִיחַ קְצִירְךָ Lev 25:5 suffix תִּקְצְרוּ אֶתסְֿפִיחֶיהַ Lev 25:11.
