BDB7318 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7320

קֵדָר proper name, of a people (swarthy? black-tented?); Κηδαρ:

1. tribe of nomads in Arabian desert Isa 21:16; Isa 42:11 (with verb feminine), Isa 60:7, Jer 2:10; Jer 49:28 (twice in verse); Ezek 27:21 ׳בְּנֵיקֿ Isa 21:17 ׳אָהֳלֵי ק Ps 120:5; Song 1:5 (made of black goat-skins JacobBeduinenleben (2), 41 or black woven stuff DoughtyArab. Des. i. 224 f.; simile of swarthy hue).
2. ancestor of 1, son of Ishmael, Gen 25:13 = 1Chr 1:29. — compare Assyrian †idru COTGen 25:13, PlinNH see 11 (12) Cedrei; also Sabean tribe-name קדר Hal623 (compare DHMZMG xxxvii (1883), 14).
