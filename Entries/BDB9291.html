BDB9290 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9292

אחרין Dan 4:5 Kt, וְעַד אחרין עַל קֳדָ מַי דָּנִיֵּאל; very dubious (see Commentaries); usually and unto the last (= at last) came in, etc.; but word in this sense unknown: Qr אָחֳרָן; read perhaps (Bev) וְעֹד אָחֳרָן (or ? Kt אַחֲרִין; compare) and yet another came in before me.
