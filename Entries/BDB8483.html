BDB8483 [H7710]

Biblical Hebrew

[שְׁדַף] verb scorch, blight (Late Hebrew id.; Niph`al, Hithpa`el be blighted ; Jewish-Aramaic שְׁדַף Ithpe`el burn (up); Arabic IV. be dark); —

Qal Passive participle construct, of ears of grain, שְׁדוּפֹת קָדִים Gen 41:6 scorched by a sirocco, so ׳ שְׁדֻפוֺת ק Gen 41:23, ׳ הַקּ ׳ שׁ Gen 41:27 (all E ).
