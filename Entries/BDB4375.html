Biblical Hebrew

מָלוֺן noun masculine lodging-place, inn, khan; — absolute ׳ מ Gen 42:27 + 5 t.; construct מְלוֺן 2Kgs 19:23; Jer 9:1 — lodging-place, inn, khan (?) Gen 42:27; Gen 43:21; Exod 4:24 אֹרְתִים ׳ מְ Jer 9:1 = camp (of Israel) for a night, Josh 4:3 ( + לין), Josh 4:8 of Assyrians Isa 10:29 figurative מְלוֺן קִצֹּה֯ 2Kgs 19:23, i.e. its (Lebanon's) remotest camping-ground, hyperb. of Assyrian conquest; so read probably in || Isa 37:24 for ᵑ0 ׳ מְרוֺם ק, compare Di Du Kit CheHpt.
