Biblical Hebrew

אָמָּן (omm¹n) noun masculine master-workman, artist Song 7:2 (Mishna, Talmud אוּמָן, אוּמָנוּת handiwork, Syriac ᵑ6 Exod 28:11, where = Hebrew חָרָשׁ & is likewise used of gems; Nabataean אמנא Vog94, Assyrian mar umm'ni, LyonSargontexte 65, compare ZimBP 12; compare II. אָמוֺן).
