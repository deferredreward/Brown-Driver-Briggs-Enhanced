BDB8712 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8714

שִׁלּוּחִים noun [masculine] plural sending away, parting gift; —

1. אַחר שִׁלוּחֶיהָ Exod 18:2 ( J ) after she had been sent away.
2. שִׁלֻּחִים 1Kgs 9:16 he gave (city) as parting gift (i.e. dowry) to his daughter; compare figurative שִׁלּוּחִים Mic 1:14 thou shalt give a pearting gift (= say farewell to, lose) Morešeth of Gath.
