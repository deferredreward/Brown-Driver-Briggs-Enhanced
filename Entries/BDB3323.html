BDB3323 [H2918]

BDB3322 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3324

[טִירָה] noun feminine encampment, battlement (from idea of surrounding, enclosure; Syriac sheepfold, ἔπαυλις) — construct טִירַת Song 8:9 טִירָתָם Ps 69:25 plural טִירוֺת Ezek 46:23 suffix טִירֹתָם Num 31:10; Gen 25:16 טִירוֺתָם 1Chr 6:39 טִירוֺתֵיהֶם Ezek 25:4 —

1. encampment, especially of circular encampment of nomad tribes, mentioned with חצר Gen 25:16 with ערים Num 31:10; Ezek 25:4 (|| מִשׁכניהם); encampment (poetry) = habitation Ps 69:26 (|| אהליהם ); more generally, 1Chr 6:39 (|| מושׁבות).
2. in metaphor כָּ֑סֶף ׳ ט Song 8:9 a battlement of silver.
3. row of stones, only plural טִירוֺת Ezek 46:23 , virtually plural of טוּר 1, q. v.
