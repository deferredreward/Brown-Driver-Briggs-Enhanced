סָרָה noun feminine a turning aside, defection, apostasy, withdrawal; — always absolute ׳ ס; —

1. defection, used apparently of any moral or legal offence Deut 19:16, so probably Isa 59:13 (+ עשֶׁק , דִּבְרֵי שָׁ֑קֶר).
2. apostasy, Deut 13:6; Isa 1:5; Isa 31:6; Jer 28:16; Jer 29:32.
3. סָרָה בִּלְתִּי non-withdrawal Isa 14:6 (compare verb, Isa 14:25 ; Amos 6:7).

סִרָה see ׳ בּוֺר הַסּ above יסור, only in יסורי Jer 17:13 Kt , but see Qr סור Qal Participle
