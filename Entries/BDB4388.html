BDB4388 [H3892]

Biblical Hebrew

לַח adjective moist, fresh, new; — ׳ ל Gen 30:37; Ezek 21:3 לָ֑ח Ezek 17:24 plural לַחִים Num 6:3 + 2 t.; —

1. moist, fresh, of trees, fruit, etc.; לִבְנֶה לַח Gen 30:37 ( J ); ׳ עֵץ ל Ezek 17:24 ; Ezek 21:3 grapes ׳ עֲנָבִים ל Num 6:3.
2. new, of cords, ׳ יְתָרִים ל Judg 16:7; Judg 16:8, probably made of fresh sinews of animal (compare Syriac PS1652; GFMon the passage)
