[דּוּדַי] noun masculine Gen 30:14 mandrake (as love-producing, compare Di Gen 30:14) — plural דּוּדָאִים (compare Ew189 g Sta§§ 301. 122 ) Gen 30:14 ; Song 7:14 construct דּוּדָאֵי Gen 30:14 + 3 t.; — mandrakes, as exciting sexual desire, and favouring procreation Gen 30:14 (twice in verse); Gen 30:15 (twice in verse); Gen 30:16 ( J ); also Song 7:14 where odour referred to. On דּוּדָאֵי Jer 24:1 vessels, baskets, see דּוּד .
