BDB5668 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5670

מִסְפֵּד noun masculine Gen 50:10 wailing; — absolute ׳ מ Amos 5:16 + 11 t.; construct מִסְפַּד Mic 1:11 + 2 t.; suffix מִסְפְּדִי Ps 30:12 — wailing:

1. for dead, Gen 50:10 ( J ; as accusative of cognate meaning with verb), Zech 12:10 (with עַלֿ; compare Zech 12:11 v Zech 12:11 below).
2. for calamity experienced Amos 5:16 (twice in verse) (|| אֵבֶל), Amos 5:17; Mic 1:8 (כַּתַּנִּים ׳ מ, || אֵבֶל), Mic 1:11; Jer 48:38; Ezek 27:31, anticipated Jer 6:26; Esth 4:3.
3. in contrition Isa 22:12 (+ בְּכִי), Joel 2:12 (+ צוֺם, בְּכִי ), compare Zech 12:11 (twice in verse).
4. in general, Ps 30:12 (opposed to מָחוֺל, compare Eccl 3:4).
