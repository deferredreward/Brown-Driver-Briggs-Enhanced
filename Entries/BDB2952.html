חַנָּתֹן proper name [of a location] place in the tribe of Zebulun Josh 19:14 ᵐ5 Αμωθ, Αενναθωθ, ᵐ5 L Αναθωθ; site unknown; according to Conder = Talm, Caphar Hanania ( NbrGeogr. 176. 226) on the border of Upper and Lower Galilee, modern Kefr' Anân, SurveyWP i. 205, 207.
