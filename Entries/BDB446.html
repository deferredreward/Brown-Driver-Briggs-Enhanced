אֱלִיעָם proper name, masculine (God is kinsman; < = El is kinsman (see GrayEncy. Bib. AMMI, NAMES WITH Nöib. NAMES, § 46) Phoenician אלעם )

a. father of Bathsheba 2Sam 11:3 compare עַמִּיאֵל 1Chr 3:5.
b. one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:34 (according to some = a.).
