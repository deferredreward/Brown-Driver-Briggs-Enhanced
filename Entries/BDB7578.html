BDB7577 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7579

קִרְיַת אַרְבַּע proper name, of a location older name of Hebron; πόλις Αρβο(κ), Καρ(ι)αθαρβοκ, etc. (probably = fourfold city (otherwise Josh 14:15; Josh 15:13; Josh 21:11), see especially GFM); — Josh 14:15 (J E D), Judg 1:10b (אַרְבַּ֑ע ׳ק), also Gen 23:2; Josh 15:13; Josh 15:54; Josh 20:7; Josh 21:11 (all P); ׳הָא ׳ק Gen 35:27 (P) Neh 11:25. — see חֶבְרוֺן.
