I. חֵ֫לֶק noun masculine Josh 18:5 portion, tract, territory — ׳ ח absolute Gen 31:14 +; construct Gen 14:24 +; suffix חֶלְקִי Ps 16:5 + 6 t.; חֶלְקְךָ Num 18:20; Eccl 9:9 חֶלְקֶ֑ךָ Ps 50:18 חֶלְקֵךְ Isa 57:6 חֶלְקוֺ Hab 1:16 + 5 t.; חֶלְקָם Gen 14:24 + 3 t.; plural חֲלָקִים Josh 18:5 + 5 t.; suffix חֶלְקֵיהֶם Hos 5:7 —

1. portion, share:
a. of booty Gen 14:24 (twice in verse); Num 31:36 ( P ) 1Sam 30:24 (twice in verse).
b. of food Lev 6:10 ( P ); יאכלו ׳ כּח ׳ ח Deut 18:8 = food Hab 1:16 (|| מַאֲכָל); so (of unrestricted charity) Eccl 11:2 see Now.
c. followed by בְּ, share or interest in Eccl 9:6, with idea of obligation to Gen 31:14 ( E ; in father's house), 2Sam 20:1 = 1Kgs 12:16 = 2Chr 10:16 (in royal line; all || נַחֲלָה ); of right (privilege) in ביהוה ׳ ח Josh 22:25; Josh 22:27 ( P ); Neh 2:20 (in Jerusalem; + צְדָקָה וְזִכָּרוֺן),
d. proper share or part אַעֲנֶה חֶלְקִי Job 32:17 i.e. I will do my part in replying. Especially
2. portion, tract, of land:
a. as distributive at conquest Josh 19:9, seven portions Josh 18:5; Josh 18:6; Josh 18:9 (all J E ) vJosh 18:7 ( J E ; + נחלה in || clause), Josh 15:13; Josh 19:9 (both P ); none for tribe of Levi Deut 10:9; Deut 12:12; Deut 14:27; Deut 14:29; Deut 18:1; Josh 18:7 (all D ; || נחלה), Josh 14:4 ( P ), none for priests Num 18:20 ( P ; || verb נחל).
b. of land possessed by people Hos 5:7; Mic 2:4.
c. portion to be assigned after exile Isa 61:7; Ezek 45:7; Ezek 48:8; Ezek 48:21, חֶלְקוֺ עַלאַֿדְמַת הַקֹּדֶשׁ Zech 2:16.
d. specifically of town-land, district about a town, יִזְרְעֵאל ׳ ח 2Kgs 9:10; 2Kgs 9:36 + 1Kgs 21:23 ( ᵑ0 חֵל), ׳ יִזְ ׳ הַשָּׂדֶה בְּח v1Kgs 21:37.
e. הַחֵלֶק Amos 7:4 (opposed to תְּהוֺם) apparently of land of Israel
3. hence portion, i.e. (acquired) possession:
a. of people as ׳ י's possession Deut 32:9 (|| חֶבֶל נחלתו); but especially
b. of ׳ י as possession of his servants: of priests, as enjoying perquisites of altar Num 18:20 ( P ; || נחלה), in spiritual sense Ps 73:26; Ps 119:57; Lam 3:24 מְנָת חֶלְקִי ׳ י Ps 16:5 בְּאֶרֶץ הַחַיִּים ׳ ח Ps 142:6 hence יעקב ׳ ח of ׳ י Jer 10:16 = Jer 51:19 ( שֵׁבֶט נַחֲלָתוֺ in || clause)
4. chosen portion, habitual mode of life ׳ עִםמְֿנָאֲפִים ח Ps 50:18 with adulterers is thy chosen life (companionship, shewing character); בְּחַלְּקֵינַֿחַל חֶלְקֵךְ Isa 57:6 among smooth (things i.e. stones) of a brook-bed is thy chosen life (of idolatrous habit; see חָלָק ); חֶלְקָם בַּחַיִּים Ps 17:14.
5. portion, award, from God אֱלוֺהַּ ׳ ח Job 31:2 (|| נחלה); specifically of punishment of wicked Isa 17:14 (|| גּוֺרָל), עִםאֵֿל Job 27:13, מֵאֱלֹהִים Job 20:29 (|| נחלה); = profit, reward חֶלְקִי מִכָּלעֲֿמָלִי Eccl 2:10, compare vEccl 2:21 ; Eccl 3:22 ; Eccl 5:17 ; Eccl 5:18 ; Eccl 9:9. — לְחֵלָק Job 17:5 is dubious: a share of feast, or of booty, Kmp Hi; a prey, spoil, Ew Di Da; AV flattery does not suit context.
