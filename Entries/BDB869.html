BDB868 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB870

III. [אֵת] noun [masculine] a cutting instrument of iron, usually translated ploughshare — singular suffix אֵתוֺ 1Sam 13:20 plural אֵתִים 1Sam 13:21, אִתִּים Isa 2:4 = Mic 4:3 suffix אִתֵּיכֶם Joel 4:10 according to Klo and others also 2Kgs 6:5 אֶתהַֿבַּרְזֶל, i.e. the axe of iron.
