BDB2571 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2573

חֵ֑דֶק noun [masculine] brier (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic חִדְקָא Löwp. 147, No. 104; compare Arabic a kind of night-shade, see Lane532); — טוֺבָם כַּחֵדֶק Mic 7:4 the best of them (si vera lectio) is like the brier (|| יָשָׁר מִמְּסוּכָה); דֶּרֶךְ עָצֵל כִּמְשֻׂכַת חָ֑דֶק Prov 15:19 the way of a sluggard is like a brier-hedge.
