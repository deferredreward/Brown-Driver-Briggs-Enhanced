דֶּ֫בֶק noun masculine Isa 41:7 joining, soldering, appendage —

1. ׳ דּ joining, soldering Isa 41:7.
2. plural הַדְּבָקִים appendages of breastplate (?) 1Kgs 22:34 = 2Chr 18:33 ובין הַשִּׁרְיָן ׳ בֵּין הַדּ. (The Be and others compare VB : the jointed attachment or appendage to the rigid breast-armour, which covered the abdomen; > Klo proposes appendage of helmet; Thes suggests armpits, literally joints (compare מַדְבְּקֵי יָד Jer 38:12 ᵑ7 ); Ew the soft muscles etc. connecting the chest with the bottom of the back.)
