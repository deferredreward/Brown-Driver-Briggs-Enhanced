BDB1185 [H1131]

Biblical Hebrew

בִּנּוּי proper name, masculine

1. head of a family that returned with Zerubbabel Neh 7:15 (= בָּנִי || Ezra 2:10 & perhaps Ezra 10:34 compare SmListen 14; but BeRy proposes here בִּגְוַי ).
2. a Levite of Ezra's time Ezra 8:33; Neh 10:10 (properly = בָּנִי Neh 8:7, בֻּנִּי Neh 9:4) Neh 12:8 compare Neh 3:24 ( בַּוַי, textual error Neh 3:18) see BeRy; also Ezra 2:40 = Neh 7:43 לִבְּנֵי read perhaps לְבִנֻּי compare SmListen 16 but BeRy otherwise; compare further GrGeschichte.ii.2, 389.
3. Israelites of Ezra's time,
a. Ezra 10:30
b. Ezra 10:38 .
