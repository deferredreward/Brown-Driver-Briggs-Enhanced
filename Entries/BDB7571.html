BDB7571 [H7147]

BDB7570 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7572

[קְרִי] noun [masculine] opposition, contrariness; — only (בְּ)קֶ֑רִי in Lev 26, as adverb modifier of הָלַךְ (in figurative sense), + עִם person, of Israel's relation to ׳ ׃י תֵּלְכוּ עִמִּי קָ֑רִי Lev 26:21 if ye walk with me contrariwise, so vLev 26:23, בְּקָ֑רִי vLev 26:27.40; ׳ י subj בַּקֶּ֑רִי וְהָלַכְתי עִמָּכֶם vLev 26:24, so vLev 26:41 and (׳ בַּחֲמַת ק in wrath of opositioin) vLev 26:28.
