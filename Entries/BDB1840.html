BDB1839 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1841

דּוּגָה noun feminine fishing, fishery, only in וְנִשָּׂא בְּסִירוֺת דּוּגָה Amos 4:2 (|| בְּצִנּוֺת) of ׳י, metaphor for dragging Israel captive (compare for practice referred to, in case of fish, Ezek 29:4 & Job 40:26 scornful summons to do it with crocodile, if possible ! see Herodii. 70 on mode of capturing crocodiles).
