BDB4009 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4011

כִּלְאַיִם noun [masculine] dual two kinds (Arabic , both; Ethiopic two; perhaps also כלאי MI23 both, two, NöLCB 1889, 61, cf DrSm xcii; Sm u. SoMI, 1886 suggest reservoirs; see also כְּלִי) — בְּהֶמְתְּךָ לֹא ׳תַּרְבִּיעַ כ Lev 19:19 not cause thy cattle to breed (in) two kinds; ׳שָֽׂדְךָ לֹא תִּזְרַע כ vLev 19:19 (H); לֹא תִּזְרַע ׳כַּרְמְךָ כ Deut 22:9 (both כִּלְאָ֑יִם); ׳בֶּגֶד כ Lev 19:19 (H); — only in law of H and D against mixtures.
