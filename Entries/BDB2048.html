BDB2048 [H1901]

Biblical Hebrew

הָגִיג noun [masculine] murmuring (Che), whisper, musing (on form compare BaNB 136) — only suffix הֲגִיגִי

1. Ps 5:2 ׳ בִּינָה ה understand my murmuring (whispering, faint utterance), addressed to ׳ י.
2. Ps 39:4 בַּהֲגִיגִי in my musing i.e. while I was musing.

הַגֻּדְגֹּ֫דָה see below גדד p. 151.
