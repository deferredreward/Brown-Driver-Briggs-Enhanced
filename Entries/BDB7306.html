BDB7306 [H6927]

BDB7305 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7307

[קַדְמָה] noun feminine antiquity, former state; —

1. antiquity, beginning, suffix קַדְמָתָהּ Isa 23:7 (of Tyre).
2. former state, suffix קַדְמָתָן Ezek 16:55 (twice in verse), קַדְמַתְכֶן Ezek 16:55 plural suffix קַדְמוֺתֵיכֶם Ezek 36:11.
3. construct singular = conjunction before (temporal; Aramaic) שֶׁקַּדְמַת שָׁלַף Ps 129:6.
