[שׁוּף] verb bruise (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic שׁוּף, שְׁפַף , rub off, away, grind (Exod 32:20 ᵑ7 ᵑ9 for טָחַן); see especially DrGn; > Di and others think || form of I. שׁאף); —
