BDB2030 [H1879]

BDB2029 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2031

דָּשֵׁן adjective fat, דָּשֵׁן Isa 30:23 (|| שָׁמֵן), of לֶחֶם as product of ground; figurative of righteous as trees דְּשֵׁנִים Ps 92:15 (|| רעננים) fat, full of oil (?) or sap (Che; compare דֶּשֶׁן Judg 9:9); as substantive vigorous, stalwart ones (opposed to יוֺרְדֵי עָפָר compare Che) Ps 22:30 דִּשְׁנֵיֿ אֶרֶץ (Brüll שֹׁכְנֵי, RenHist. iii. 134 ישְׁבֵי).
