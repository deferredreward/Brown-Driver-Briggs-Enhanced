BDB1443 [H1382]

BDB1442 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1444

גִּבְלִי adjective, of a people of foregoing with article = collective noun Josh 13:5 והארץ הַגִּבְלִי, but read ׳גְבוּל הַגּ, compare Di. 1Kgs 5:32 הַגִּבְלִים (but probably text error; Th reads וַיַּגְבִּלוּם and they bordered them, made a border for them (the stones); ᵐ5 ἔβαλον, compare also Klo).
