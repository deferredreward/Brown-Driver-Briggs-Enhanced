BDB6173 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6175

עָמִיר noun [masculine] swath, row of fallen grain (hay, as Mishna, according to Vogelstl.c. 74 f. (who is then compelled to read עֹמֶר Mic 4:12), compare Syriac grass, SchwIdioticon 69); — Amos 2:13 (on cart), Jer 9:21 (falling behind reaper), Mic 4:12 (brought to threshing-floor), Zech 12:6 (inflammable).
