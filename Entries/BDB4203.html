BDB4203 [H3758]

Biblical Hebrew

כַּרְמִיל noun [masculine] crimson, carmine, i.e. crimson stuff, cloth; late (probably Persian loan-word, and a derive. from Persian worm, see DeZDuth. Th. 1878, 593); — only 2Chr 2:6; 2Chr 2:13; 2Chr 3:14 (in all + אַרְגָּמָן (ו־ָןׅ תְּכֵלֶת 2Chr 2:13; 2Chr 3:14 + בּוּץ also ); read perhaps also for כַּדְמֶל Song 7:6 (|| אַרְגָּוָן: so Gi, of hair formed spirally, like shell-fish; Gr, of glossy hair).
