BDB1796 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1798

[דָּאָה] verb fly swiftly, dart through the air (compare perhaps Arabic run vehemently (of camel)) —

Qal Imperfect יִדְאֶה Deut 28:49 + 2 t.; וַיֵּ֫דֶא Ps 18:11(> || 2Sam 22:11 וַיֵּרָא); fly swiftly, dart, of eagle Deut 28:49, in simile of swift army; of Chaldaeans camp. with eagle, in judgment against Moab & Edom Jer 48:40; Jer 49:22 (in both || פרשׂ כנפיו); of ׳ י in theoph. Ps 18:11 (compare 2Sam 22:11) וַיֵּדֶא עלכֿנפי רוח (|| וַיִּרְכַּב עַלכְּֿרוּב וַיָּעֹף).
