BDB7645 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7647

קָשַׁר verb bind, league together, conspire (Late Hebrew id., bind, join, קֶשֶׁר knot; ᵑ7 קְטַר, Syriac bind (ט for ת after ק) NöZMG xi.735, who compare also ('perhaps') Arabic force to do a thing, Ethiopic bind); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular קָשַׁר Amos 7:10; 1Kgs 16:16 2 masculine singular suffix וּקְשַׁרְתָּם consecutive Deut 6:8, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּקְשֹׁר 1Kgs 15:27 +, וַיִּקְשָׁרֿ 2Kgs 15:30, etc.; Imperative masculine singular suffix קָשְׁרֵם Prov 3:3 +; Participle active plural קשְׁרִים 2Sam 15:31 +; passive feminine singular קְשׁוּרָה Gen 44:30; Prov 22:15 plural קְשֻׁרִים Gen 30:42 —
1. bind:
a. literal, with accusative of thing + עַל Gen 38:28 ( J ), Jer 51:63, + ב, Josh 2:18; Josh 2:21 ( J E ); bind, confine, with accusative רֵים Job 39:10 (+ בְּתֶלֶם), accusative of crocodile Job 40:29.
b. figurative, with accusative + עַל Deut 6:8; Deut 11:18 ; Prov 3:3 ; Prov 6:21; Prov 7:3 (all of religious and moral precepts); with ב, נַפְשׁוֺ קְשׁוּרָה בְנַפְשׁוֺ Gen 44:30 his life is bound up with his [namely the boy's] life ( J ; of strong affection); בְלֶבנָֿ֑עַר ׳ אִוֶּלֶת ק Prov 22:15 .
c. passive participle vigorous (properly well- knit; opposed to עֲטֻפִים) Gen 30:42 ( J ).
2. league together, conspire: with עָ person against 1Sam 22:8; 1Sam 22:13 ; 1Sam 1:27; 1Sam 16:9; 1Sam 2:9; 1Sam 15:10 ; 2Kgs 21:24 = 2Chr 33:24; 2Chr 33:25; 2Chr 24:21 with accusative of cognate meaning with verb קֶשֶׁר, 1Kgs 16:20; 2Kgs 12:21; 2Kgs 15:15, + עַל person 2Kgs 14:19 = 2Chr 25:27; 2Kgs 15:30 absolute 1Kgs 16:16; Neh 4:2 (+ infinitive purpose); with עִם, together with, 2Sam 15:31.
Niph`al Perfect 3 feminine singular נִקְשְׁרָה 1Sam 18:1 the life of Jonathan was bound up with the life of David ( Qal 1.b.); Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַתִּקָּשֵׁר כָּלהַֿחוֺמָה Neh 3:38 all the wall was joined together (the circuit complete).
Pi`el Imperfect
1. bind on (as ornament), 2 feminine singular suffix וּתְקַשְּׁרִים Isa 49:18 (figurative; || תִּלְבְּשִׁי).
2. bind fast, 2 masculine singular תְקַשּׁר (הַ) מַעֲדַנּוֺת כִּימָה Job 38:31 canst thou bind fast the bands of the Pleiades?
Pu`al Participle הַצּאִן הַמְּקֻשָּׁרוֺת Gen 30:41 ( J ), = Qal 1c.
Hithpa`el conspire: Perfect 3 plural הִתְקַשְּׁרוּ עַלֿ 2Chr 24:25 (|| 2Kgs 12:21 Qal 2); Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּתְקַשּׁר אֶלֿ 2Kgs 9:14 Participle plural הַמִּתְקַשְּׁרִים עָלָיו 2Chr 24:26.
