BDB8731 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8733

שָׁלָל75 noun masculine 1Sam 30:16 prey, spoil, plunder, booty; — absolute ׳ שׁ Gen 49:27 +; construct שְׁלַל Judg 5:30 +, suffix שְׁלָלֵךְ Zech 14:1, שְׁלַלְכֶם Isa 33:4, etc.; —

1. prey, of Benjamin as wolf Gen 49:27 (poem in J ; with חִלֵּק).
2. booty, spoil of war, of all kinds (often || בַּז): including garments, gold and silver Josh 7:21 ( J ), ornaments Judg 8:24; Judg 8:25, fabrics and women Judg 5:30 a, b, flocks and herds 1Sam 30:20; Jer 49:32; 2Chr 15:11 , + persons Deut 20:14 (twice in verse), garments, food and drink 2Chr 28:15, etc. (58 t.; among these) שְׁלַל הֶעָרִים Deut 2:25; 2Sam 12:30 ; 1Chr 20:2 and (+ cattle in addition) Deut 3:7; Josh 8:27; Josh 11:14 (both R D ), compare (also of city) Isa 8:4; Deut 13:17 (twice in verse); Zech 14:1 and (+ cattle) Josh 8:2 ( R D ), but including person and cattle Deut 20:14, compare Deut 20:14 figurative of entire nation Jer 50:10; Ezek 7:21; Zech 2:13 ׳ שׁ in symbolic proper name ׳ מַהֵר שָׁלָל וגו Isa 8:1; Isa 8:3, see above p.555:a; ׳ שׁ in metaphor of future majesty of ׳ עֶבֶד י Isa 53:12 ׳ וְהֳיְתָהאלּוֺ נַפְשׁוֺ לְשׁ i.e. life shall be spared, Jer 21:9; Jer 38:2; Jer 39:18, compare Jer 45:5.
3. private plunder Isa 10:2 ( אַלְמָנוֺת שְׁלָלָם), Prov 1:13 and (perhaps in current saying) Prov 16:19.
4. = gain Prov 31:11. — ׳ לְצַוִּארֵי שׁ Judg 5:30 c is clearly wrong; meaning dubious (see especially GFM); plausible conjectures are ׳ לְצַוָּארַי שׁ for my neck as spoil, ReussBr and others, and לְצַוְּארֵי שֵׁגָל for the neck of the queen Ew Be Kit and others (but שֵׁגָל elsewhere late); Now strike out שָׁלָל and reads לְצַוָּארָיו.
