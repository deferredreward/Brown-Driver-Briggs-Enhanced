אַפְּתֹם or אַפְּתוֺם (so editions: > Gi Baer from MSS, אַפְּתֹס) Ezra 4:13 מַלְכִים ׳וְא תְּהַנְוִק; of uncertain meaning: Thes NaugJBW 1853, 156 in the end (from Pahlavi afdom, Persian end); Scheft79 treasuries (from Zend pathm¹, plural of pathma, treasury, √ path, to fill up), will endamage the treasuries of kings.

[אֶצְבַּע] see צכע. אַרְבַּע see רבע.
