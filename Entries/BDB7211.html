Biblical Hebrew

I. צִפּוֺר, צִפֹּר40 noun feminine Amos 3:5 (masculine Ps 102:8, compare KöSynt § 252 a, so Ps 104:17, yet compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896). 71), bird; — absolute צִפּוֺר Hos 11:11 + 15 t., צִפֹּר Gen 15:10 + 11 t., construct צִפּוֺר Deut 4:17 + 5 t., צִפֹּרֹ Ps 8:9 plural צִפְָּרִים Lev 14:4 + 5 t.; —

1. single bird, Amos 3:5; Hos 11:11 (simile; || יוֺנָה), Lam 3:52; Job 40:29; Ps 102:8; Ps 124:7; Prov 6:5; Prov 7:23; Prov 26:2; Prov 27:8 (all simile), Lev 14:5 + 10 t. Lev 14 ׳ בָּנָף כָּלצֿ Deut 4:17, טְהוֺרָה ׳ כָּלצֿ Deut 14:11 ( כל distributive); ׳ קַןצֿ Deut 22:6 bird's nest, ׳ קוֺל הַצּ Eccl 12:4 plural Isa 31:5 (simile), Neh 5:18; Lev 14:4 ( שְׁתֵּי צִפְָּרִים, in purify.; compare WeHeid. 2.171 RSSemitic i. 402, 2nd ed. 422), Lev 14:49 (id.), Ps 104:17; Eccl 9:12.
2. less often collective, either def. Gen 15:10 (two), or indefinite Ps 11:1 (figurative), צִפֹּר שָׁמַיִם Ps 8:9, especially כָּלכָּֿ֠נָף ׳ צ Ezek 39:17; Ps 148:10, כָּלכָּֿנָף ׳ כֹּל צ Ps 17:23; Gen 7:14, also redund. with עַיִט, כָּלכָּֿנָף ׳ עֵיט צ Ezek 39:4 birds of prey of all kinds.
