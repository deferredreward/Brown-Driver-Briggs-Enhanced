[רָצַץ] verb crush (Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; Arabic bruise, bray, crush; Aramaic רְעַ, crush, shatter); —

Qal Perfect 2 masculine singular suffix רַצּוֺתָנוּ 1Sam 12:4 1 singular רַצּוֺתִי v1Sam 12:3 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יָּרוּץ (Ges«GKC:67q»§ 67q) Isa 42:4 (Babylonian Manuscript יֵרוֺץ, Niph`al, so SS here and Eccl 12:6a), 3 feminine singular תָּרוּץ Eccl 12:6a, 1 singular אָרֻץ Ps 18:30 = אָרוּץ 2Sam 22:30 (see below); Participle active feminine plural רֹצְצוֺת Amos 4:1 passive רָצוּץ Isa 42:3 +, construct רְצוּץ Hos 5:11 plural רְצוּצִים Isa 58:6 — crush:
1. literal,
\ \ a. passive participle in (הַ)קָּנֶה (הָ)רָצוּץ 2Kgs 18:21 = Isa 36:6; Isa 42:3 (all metaphor of weak person).
\ \ b. imperfect intransitive get crushed (read תֵּרוֺץ?), of bowl Eccl 12:6a (in metaphor).
2. figurative crush, oppress, accusative of person 1Sam 12:3; 1Sam 12:4; Amos 4:1 passive participle Deut 28:33, as substantive the oppressed Isa 58:6 רְצוּץ מִשְׁפָּט Hos 5:11 (We Now רֹצֵץ, i.e. perverting judgment, but dubious); intransitive get crushed (read יֵרוֺץ?), of servant of ׳י, under figure of wick, Isa 42:4 (+ יִכְהֶה) he shall not grow dim or be crushed out. Niph`al be crushed, broken: Perfect 3 masculine singular consecutive נְרֹץ (Ges«GKC:67t»§ 67t) Eccl 12:6b (of wheel at cistern); Imperfect 2 masculine singular תֵּרוֺץ Ezek 29:7 (of Pharaoh as reed, compare Qal 1 a); Job 20:10 read perhaps יֵרֹצּוּ (for יְרַצּוּ, see רצה Pi`el), are crushed (as) poor men, Bu, compare Hoffm SS Gerber. — Isa 42:2; Eccl 12:6 a see Qal. Pi`el crush in pieces:
1. literal (in metaphor) Perfect 2 masculine singular רִצַּצְתָּ Ps 74:14 with accusative of thing.
2. figurative = grievously oppress, 3 masculine singular רִצַּץ Job 20:19 Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיְרַצֵּץ מִן הָעָם 2Chr 16:10. Po`el Imperfect 3 masculine plural וַיְרֹצְצוּ Judg 10:8, accusative of person (= Pi`el 2; || רעץ; רצץ here perhaps doublet, so GFMHpt, compare Bu Now). Hiph`il Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַתָּ֫רִץ (Köi. 352; BaZMG xliii (1889), 181 thinks Qal) Judg 9:53 she crushed his skull. Hithpo`el reciprocal, Imperfect 3 masculine plural וִיִּתְרֹצְצוּ הַבָּנִים בְּקִרְבָּהּ Gen 25:22 (J) the children crushed (thurst, struck) one another within her.
