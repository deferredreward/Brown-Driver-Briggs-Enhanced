BDB8052 [H4832]

Biblical Hebrew

מַרְפֵּא, מַרְפֵּה noun masculine Eccl 10:4 healing, cure, health (usually figurative); — absolute מַרְפֵּא Jer 14:19 +, מַרְפֵּה Jer 8:15 construct מַרְפֵּא Prov 15:4 —

1. healing, cure, of national woes, Jer 8:15; Jer 14:19b (|| שָׁלוֺם), Jer 36:6 (+ אֲרֻכָה); especially ׳ אֵין לָנוּ מ Jer 14:19a there is no cure for us, ׳ עַד לְאֵין מ 2Chr 36:16 .
2. figurative of person, health, profit, Prov 4:22 (|| חַיִּים), Prov 12:18; Prov 13:17; Prov 16:24 with spiritual implication Mal 3:20 ׳ אֵין מ Prov 6:15 = Prov 29:1 ׳ לֵב מ Prov 14:30 a mind of health (healthy, composed, mind), compare ׳ מ alone = composure Eccl 10:4 לְשׁוֺן ׳ מ Eccl 15:4 healing of the tongue, = a soothing tongue (Toy).
3. literally, of disease, ׳ לְאֵין מ 2Chr 21:18.
