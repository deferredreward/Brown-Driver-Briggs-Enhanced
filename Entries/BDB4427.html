BDB4426 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4428

לִמֻּד adjective taught; — ׳ ל Jer 2:24 plural לִמּוּדִים Isa 50:4 (twice in verse); construct לִמּוּדֵי Isa 54:13 לִמֻּדֵי Jer 13:23 suffix לִמֻּדַי Isa 8:16 —

1. taught, as disciples Isa 8:16; Isa 50:4 (twice in verse); Isa 54:13.
2. accustomed to something, לִמֻּד מִדְבָּר Jer 2:24 (wild ass) accustomed to the wilderness; לִמֻּדֵי הָרֵעַ Jer 13:23 accustomed to do evil.
