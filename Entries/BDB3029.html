BDB3028 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3030

חָצָה verb divide (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic is be fortunate, happy with one's husband or wife, etc., i.e. have a share in happiness; a small arrow; Di134 puts here Ethiopic arrow, compare חֵצִי) —

Qal Perfect חָצָה Num 31:42 וְחָצִיתָ consecutive Num 31:27 וְחָצוּ consecutive Exod 21:35 Imperfect יֶחֱצֶה Isa 30:28 וַיַּ֫חַץ Gen 32:8 + 2 t.; וַיֶּחֱצֵם Judg 9:43 יֶחֱצוּ Ps 55:24 יֶחֱצוּן Exod 21:35 יֶחֱצוּהוּ Job 40:30 —
1. divide, followed by כסף Exod 21:35 שׁור vExod 21:35 (both J E), לִוְיָתָן crocodile Job 40:30 prey, מלקוח, Num 31:27 (P; with לְ before parts); a company of people Gen 32:8 (followed by לְ as foregoing), Gen 33:1 (both J) Judg 9:43 (לְ as foregoing) but compare Judg 7:16.
2. (apparently denominative from חֲצִי) Ps 55:24 ימיהם ׳לא יח shall not halve their days, i.e. enjoy even half of the normal number; Isa 30:28 עד ׳צואר יח shall halve unto the neck = shall reach to the neck and so divide the man in half. Niph`al Imperfect 3 feminine singular with apocope תֵּחָץ Dan 11:4 be divided (of kingdom), followed by לְ; ולא יֵחָ֫צוּ עוד לשׁני גוים Ezek 37:22 (Judah and Israel); waters of Jordan 2Kgs 2:8; 2Kgs 2:14.
