מֶרְחָב noun [masculine] broad, roomy, place; — absolute ׳מ, of pasture Hos 4:16 (in simile); figurative of freedom from distress and anxiety 2Sam 22:20 = Ps 18:20; Ps 31:9; Ps 118:5 (ᵑ0 construct in מֶרְחַבְיָה spacious place of Yah, i.e. extraordinarily spacious, < מֶרְחָב יָהּ, יָהּ being subject of verb); plural construct מֶרְחֲבֵיאֶֿרֶץ Hab 1:6 expanses of the earth.
