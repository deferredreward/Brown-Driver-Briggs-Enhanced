BDB4680 [H4468]

BDB4679 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4681

[מַמְלָכוּת] noun feminine kingdom, dominion, reign (on form compare We 1Sam 15:28 Dron the passage id.); — construct מַמְלְכוּת Josh 13:12 + 8t.;

1. kingdom of Og Josh 13:12; Josh 13:30; Josh 13:31 of Sihon Josh 13:21 Josh 13:27.
2. dominion, royal power with קָרַע מֵעַל (subject ׳ י) 1Sam 15:28 with הֵשִׁיב לְ (hum. subject) 2Sam 16:3 with הִשְׁבִּית Hos 1:4.
3. reign ׳ בְּרֵאשִׁית מַמ Jer 26:1 (compare מַמְלָכָה 3, מַלְכוּת 2).
