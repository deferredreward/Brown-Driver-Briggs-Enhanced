BDB1760 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1762

גַּרְגְּרוֺת noun feminine plural neck (on formation compare BaNB 204) — גַּרְגְּרֹתֶ֑יךָ Prov 1:9; Prov 3:22, גַּרְגְרוֺתֶ֑ךָ Prov 3:3, גַּרְגְּרֹתֶ֑ךָ Prov 6:21 — always in figure, instruction of parents an ornament, a chain upon neck Prov 1:9, bind them upon thy neck Prov 6:21 so of mercy & truth Prov 3:3 , compare Prov 3:22.
