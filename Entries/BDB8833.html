BDB8833 [H8095]

BDB8832 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8834

שִׁמְעוֺן44 proper name, masculine (compare proper name שמעון in Palmyrene Lzb378; meaning doubtful; Rs and others compare said to mean offspring of hyena and wolf; see HoggEBiv. 4531); —

1. second son of Jacob and Leah, Συμεωη: Gen 29:33 (name explanation from שָׁמַע ), Gen 35:23 ; Exod 1:2 + 14 t. (associated especially with Levi Gen 34:25; Gen 34:30; Gen 49:5).
2. tribal name Judg 1:3 (twice in verse); Judg 1:17; Num 1:6 +; so ׳ גְּנֵי שׁ Num 1:22 + 7 t., ׳ מַטֵה שׁ Num 1:23; Num 2:12; Num 13:5 ׳ מַטֵּה גְנֵי שׁ Num 10:19; Num 34:20; Josh 19:1; Josh 19:8; Josh 21:9; 1Chr 6:50.
3. post-exilic Jew with foreign wife Ezra 10:31.
