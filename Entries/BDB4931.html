BDB4931 [H5031]

Biblical Hebrew

נְבִיאָה noun masculine prophetess; —

1.
a. of the ancient type endowed with gift of song, Miriam Exod 15:20 Deborah Judg 4:4.
b. of the later type consulted for a word of ׳ י, Huldah 2Kgs 22:14 = 2Chr 34:22.
2. false prophetess, Noadiah Neh 6:14.
3. wife of Isaiah Isa 8:3.
