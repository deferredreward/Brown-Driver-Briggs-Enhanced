[חָשׁוּק] noun [masculine] fillet or ring clasping (binding) a pillar of the tabernacle, only plural suffix חֲשֻׁקֵיהֶם Exod 27:10 + 5 t.; חֲשׁוּקַיהֶם Exod 38:12; Exod 38:17 those of the pillars at door of tabernacle (הָאֹהֶל) were overlaid with gold Exod 36:38 those of the pillars of the court with silver Exod 27:10; Exod 27:11; Exod 38:10; Exod 38:11; Exod 38:12; Exod 38:17; Exod 38:19 (all P ), see Di on Exod 27:10 > Thes and most who understand of connecting-rods, joining tops of pillars, from which curtains were hung.
