BDB6634 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6636

[פָּלַח] verb cleave (Arabic cleave; Aramaic פְּלַח (usually),, are till, work, serve [=עָבַד]); —

Qal Participle פֹּלֵחַ Ps 141:7 of cleaving, ploughing, בָּאָרֶץ (|| בקע). Pi`el Imperfect
1. cleave open, through, 3 masculine singular יְפַלַּח of piercing kidneys Job 16:13, liver Prov 7:23 (both figurative); וַיְּפַלַּח אֶל 2Kgs 4:39 of cutting up (fruit) into pot.
2. cause to cleave open, 3 feminine plural יַלְדֵיהֶן תְּפַלַּחְנָה Job 39:3 of animals bringing forth young; read perhaps תְּפַלַּטְנָה (Job 21:10).
