BDB2610 [H2427]

Biblical Hebrew

חִיל noun masculine Exod 15:14 a writhing, anguish; — ׳ ח absolute Exod 15:14 + 5 t. —

1. writhing (contortions of fear) Exod 15:14 (|| verb רָגַז).
2. anguish, always in simile כַּיּוֺלֵדָה Ps 48:7 (allied kings against Jerusalem); Zion, before Assyrians Mic 4:9 before Scythians or Chaldeans Jer 6:24 (|| צָרָה), = Jer 50:43 (of king of Babylon; || צָרָה); Jer 22:23 (|| חבלים q. v.)
