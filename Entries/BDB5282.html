II. [נַעֲרָה] proper name, of a location on border of Ephraim — only with ה locative, נַעֲרָ֫תָה Josh 16:7 (ᵐ5 αἱ Κῶμαι αὐτῶν; ᵐ5L id. + Αναραθα); apparently = נַעֲרָן, q. v. below; Νοοραθ lay 5 Roman miles from Jericho LagOnom. 283, 11 compare BuhlGeogr. 181.
