BDB2375 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2377

זַלְעָפָה noun feminine raging heat (on formation compare GesLbg 863 Ol§ 196 a. 1. 2) — ׳ ז Ps 119:53 plural absolute זִלְעָפוֺת Ps 11:6 construct זִלְעֲפוֺת Lam 5:10 —

1. of fever heat of famine רָעָב ׳ ז Lam 5:10.
2. of burning wind ׳ רוּח ז Ps 11:6 (in figurative of ׳ י's judgment, with אֵשׁ, גָּפְרִית).
3. figurative of zeal אֲחָזַתְנִי ׳ ז Ps 119:53.
