BDB1983 [H1749]

BDB1982 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1984

דּוֺנַג noun masculine wax, דּוֺנַג Mic 1:4 + 2 t.; דּוֺנָג֑ Ps 22:15 — always in simile, of melting; at theophany hills melt like wax Ps 97:5 like wax before fire Mic 1:4 wicked perish as wax melteth before fire Ps 68:3 heart melteth like wax Ps 22:15 (simile of fear, despair). On ׳ ד in Ps 118:12 compare Bae Checritical note.
