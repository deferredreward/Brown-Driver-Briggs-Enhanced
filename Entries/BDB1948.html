[דָּלַח] verb make turbid (Assyrian dalâ—u, disturb, ZimBp 81 also in derivatives; Palestinian ᵑ7 דְּלַח figurative be anxious, fear, turbavit, conturbavit) —

Qal Imperfect 2 masculine singular, וַתִּדְלַח Ezek 32:2 3 feminine singular suffix תִּדְלָחֵם Ezek 32:13 + vEzek 32:13 (Co תרפשׂם); — stir up, trouble, make turbid (always with feet) followed by מים Ezek 32:2 (|| תִּרְפֹּס, — ס Baer, and not שׂ); so Ezek 32:13 a, b (but vEzek 32:13 b. Co reads תרפשׂ (compare vEzek 32:2) for תדלחם).
