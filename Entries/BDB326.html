Biblical Hebrew

II. אָח noun feminine fire-pot, brasier (Arabic in Thes, is an error) is loan-word from Egyptian &las— according to WMMOLZ 3, 51; Jer 36:22 read וְאֵשׁ הָאָח; always with article Jer 36:22 לְפָנָיו ׳ הַא מְבֹעָ֑רֶת the brasier before him was burning; Jer 36:23 ׳ הָאֵשׁ אֲשֶׁר אֶלהָֿא; Jer 36:23 ׳ הָאֵשׁ אֲשֶׁר עַלהָֿא (I. אָח, brother see below אחה; III. אָח interjection, p. 25).
