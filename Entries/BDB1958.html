דָּלַק verb burn (intransitive), hotly pursue (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic דְּלַק, all intransitive burn, kindle; Hiph`il, Aph`el transitive) —

Qal Perfect 2 masculine singular דָּלַקְתָּ Gen 31:36 3 masculine plural וְדָֽלְקוּ consecutive Obad 1:18 suffix דְּלָקֻנִי Lam 4:19 Imperfect יִדְלַק Ps 10:2 Infinitive construct דְּלֹק 1Sam 17:53 Participle active plural דֹּלְקִים Ps 7:14; Prov 26:23 —
1. burn, וְדָֽלְקוּ בָהֶם Obad 1:18 (figurative of Israel ravaging among Edomites, || וַאֲכָלוּם; on construction with בְּ compare 2Kgs 17:25); also in חִצָּיו לְדֹלְקִים יִפְעָ֑ל Ps 7:14 his arrows he maketh burning ones (subject God) RV De Che and others; & שְׂפָתַיִם דֹּלְקִים Prov 26:23 burning (fervent) lips (opposed to לֵב רָ֑ע).
2. hotly pursue Gen 31:36 (E; followed by אַחֲרֵי) 1Sam 17:53 (followed by id.); Ps 10:2; Lam 4:19 (both followed by accusative) Hiph`il Imperfect suffix יַדְלִיקֵם Isa 5:11 Imperative הַדְלֵק Ezek 24:10 — inflame, with accusative, subject wine Isa 5:11 kindle object הָאֵשׁ Ezek 24:10.
