BDB1190 [H1146]

Biblical Hebrew

בִּנְיָן noun masculine structure (loan-word = according to LagBN 205 ) applied

a. to enclosing wall of Ezekiel's temple Ezek 40:5
b. to rear-building of same Ezek 41:12 (twice in verse); Ezek 41:15, but read probably בִּנְיָה q. v.; so Sm Co;
c. apparently to whole temple Ezek 42:1; Ezek 42:10 (Co reads הבית); compare Ezek 42:5 (strike out Co); — Sm refers Ezek 42:1 to enclosing wall, see a.
