[יָסַר] verb discipline, chasten, admonish (Talmud ᵑ7 יִ(י)סּוֺר chastisement; but Aramaic יְסַר is bind ) —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine suffix וְיִסְּרֵנִי Isa 8:11 (Di De SS see Bo§ 1103 Ew§ 249 d, but Thes MV Che and others Pi`el Perfect); 1 singular suffix וְאֶסְֳּרֵם Hos 10:10 (Ges«GKC:71»§ 71); Infinitive absolute יָסֹר 1Chr 15:22 (noun Ke, point as participle Öttli); Participle יֹסֵר Prov 9:7; Ps 94:10 יסורי Jer 17:13 Kt see סוּר; —
1. admonish Isa 8:11 (followed by suffix + מִלֶּכֶת away from walking, i. e. not to walk), Prov 9:7 (|| מוֺכִיחַ).
2. instruct, 1Chr 15:22.
3. discipline, of God Hos 10:10; Ps 94:10. Imperfect; Imperative יַסֵּר Prov 19:18; Prov 29:17 suffix יַסְּרֵנִי Jer 10:24 Infinitive absolute יַסֹּר Ps 118:18 construct יַסְּרָה Lev 26:18 suffix יַסְּרֶ֑ךָּDeut 4:36 Participle מְיַסְּרֶ֑ךָּ Deut 8:5 —
1. discipline, correct (the moral nature, with more or less severity according to circumstances):
\ \ a. of God, with accusative Hos 7:15 (of training arms), Deut 4:36 (see Dr), Deut 8:5 (Israel as son), Isa 28:26, happy the man אשׁר תיסרנו יהּ Ps 94:12; Ps 118:18 (twice in verse)
\ \ b. of man, with accusative Deut 8:5; Prov 19:18; Prov 29:17 (all of a father his son), Job 4:3; Prov 31:1; Ps 16:7 my reins (the emotions of my own heart) correct, admonish me.
2. more severely, chasten, chastise:
\ \ a. of God, followed by accusative of person, Jer 31:18 אַלבַּֿחֲמָֽתְךָ תְיַסְּרֵנִי Ps 6:2; Ps 38:2 בְּמשׁפט אל באף Jer 10:24 למשׁפט Jer 30:11 = Jer 46:28 על חטאת Lev 26:18; Lev 26:28 בתוכחות על עון Ps 39:12.
\ \ b. of man, a father his son Deut 21:18 elders a man Deut 22:18 (including, as perhaps Deut 21:18, bodily chastisement); a king his subjects בַּשּׁוֺטִים and בָּעַקְרַבִּים 1Kgs 12:11; 1Kgs 12:14 = 2Chr 10:11; 2Chr 10:14.
\ \ c. תְּיַסְּרֵךְ רָֽעָתֵךְ Jer 2:19 thy badness will chastise thee. Nithp. Perfect נִוַּסְּרוּ Ezek 23:48 (for נִתְוַסְּרוּ Ges«GKC:55»§ 55 R. 7); passive be disciplined, corrected. Hiph`il Imperfect 1 singular suffix אַיְסִירֵם (Ew§ 131 c, but error for אֲיַסְּרֵם Bö§ 437 f SS), with accusative chasten Hos 7:12.
