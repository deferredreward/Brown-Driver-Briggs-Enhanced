BDB5580 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5582

[סֹךְ] noun [masculine] thicket, covert, lair; — only suffix סֻכּוֺ Jer 25:38 lair of ׳י, under figure of lion (but Gie סֻבְּכוֺ compare Jer 4:7), סֻכֹּה Ps 10:9 of lion (simile of wicked; Bae סֻכָּה; Lag Che We סֻבְּכוֺ); סוּכּוֺ Ps 76:3 his covert (of ׳י under figure of lion; || מְעוֺנָתוֺ); for סֻכֹּה in his covert Ps 27:5 Qr read perhaps, with Kt סֻכָּה a booth (Ol Hup-Now Bae compare Ps 31:21).
