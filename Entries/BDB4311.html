BDB4311 [H3828]

Biblical Hebrew

I. לְבֹנָה, לְבוֺנָה noun feminine frankincense (from white colour, compare LagBN 33; Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic id., לְבוּנְתָּא, Phoenician לבנת; Arabic (Lane3007); Ethiopic is from λιβανος); — ׳ ל Exod 30:24 + 18 t.; לְבֹנָתָהּ Lev 2:2; Lev 2:16 — used in preparation of holy incense Exod 30:34 (|| סַמִּים); used as incense Jer 6:20, on meal-offering Lev 2:1; Lev 2:2; Lev 2:15; Lev 2:16; Lev 6:8, compare Lev 5:11; Num 5:15 also Jer 17:26; Jer 41:5; Isa 43:23; Isa 66:3 with shew-bread Lev 24:7; Neh 13:5; Neh 13:9; 1Chr 9:29 (|| בְּשָׂמִים); burned as perfume (article of luxury) Song 3:6 (|| מֹר); גִּבְעַת הַלְּבוֺנָה Song 4:6 (|| הַר הַמּוֺר); עֲצֵי לְבוֺנָה Song 4:14 (|| נֵרְדְּ, כַּרְכֹּם , קָנֶה, קִנָּמוֺן, מֹר, אֲהָלוֺת); as tribute to Zion Isa 60:6 (|| זהב). — In Hexateuch only P .
