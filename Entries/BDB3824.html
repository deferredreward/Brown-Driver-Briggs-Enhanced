יָשַׁר verb be smooth, straight, right (Late Hebrew id., and derivatives; Arabic be gentle, tractable easy, II. make easy; manageable, easy, but Sabean ותֿר, הותֿר, אהל יתֿרן OsZMG 1865, 270 CISiv, 29, 2; Assyrian Išâu, be or go straight, right, mêšêru, justice, mêšêriš, righteously, DlHWB 310) —

Qal Perfect יָשַׁר Jer 18:4 + 2 t.; 3 feminine יָשְׁרָה Judg 14:3; Hab 2:4 3 masculine plural יָָֽשְׁרוּ 1Kgs 9:12 Imperfect יִישַׁר Num 23:27 + 2 t.; יִשַׁר 1Sam 18:20; 1Sam 18:26 3 feminine singularתִּישַׁר Judg 14:7 3 feminine plural יִשַּׁרְנָה 1Sam 6:12 (Ges«GKC:47»§ 47, n. 3; 71) —
1. go straight, בַּדֶּרֶךְ in the way 1Sam 6:12.
2. figurative, be pleasing, agreeable, right (בְּעֵינֵי in the yees of); said of a woman Judg 14:3; Judg 14:7 a man Jer 27:5 of matters and things (הַדָּבָר) 1Sam 18:20; 1Sam 18:26; 2Sam 17:4; 1Chr 13:4; 2Chr 30:4 of cities 1Kgs 9:12 a place בְּעֵינֵי הָאֱלֹהִים Num 23:27 (E); with כַּאֲשֶׁר Jer 18:4.
3. in ethical sense, straightforward, upright; לֹאיָָֽשְׁרָה נַפְשׁוֺ בּוֺ Hab 2:4 his soul is not upright in him (opposed to עֻפְּלָה swollen). Pi`el Perfect 1 ׳יִשָּׁ֑רְתִּ Ps 119:128 (יִשְׁרֵהוּ Job 37:3, 3 masculine suffix Thes; but < Ew Di Hi De Da, Imperfect from שָׁרָה); Imperfect יְיַשֵּׁר Prov 3:6 יְיַשֶּׁרֿ Prov 15:21 suffix וַיְיַשְּׁרֵם (Masoretes abbreviated וַיַּשְּׁרֵם) 2Chr 32:30 3 feminine singular תְּיַשֵּׁר Prov 11:4 1 singular אֲיַשֵּׁר Isa 45:13 אֲיַשֵּׁר vIsa 45:2 Qr (Kt Hiph`il אוֺשִׁר Ges«GKC:70»§ 70, 2 doubtless copyist's error); Imperative plural יַשְּׁרוּ Isa 40:3 Participle plural מְיַשְּׁרִים Prov 9:15 —
1. make smooth, straight, accusative מְסִלָּה Isa 40:3 הֲדוּרִים Isa 45:2 figurative, with דֶּרֶךְ make smooth or straight, i.e. free from obstacles, successful vIsa 45:13; Prov 11:5, אֹרַח Prov 3:5 in ethical sense, הַמְיַשְּׁרִים אָרְחוֺתָם Prov 9:15 those making straight their paths, going straightforward on their paths; יְיַשֶּׁרלָֿ֑כֶת Prov 15:21 maketh straight his going, goeth straightforwards.
2. lead straight along direct, waters of an aqueduct 2Chr 32:30 (of the thunder Job 37:3 according to some; but see rather שָׁרָה)
3. esteem right, approve Ps 119:128 (read כל פקודיך לי יִשָּׁ֑רְתִּי). Pu`al Participle זָהָב מְיֻשָּׁר 1Kgs 6:35 gold made level, laid smoothly out, upon the graven work. Hiph`il Imperfect 3 plural עַפְעַפֶּיךָ יַיְשִׁרוּ נֶגְדֶּ֑ךָ Prov 4:25 let thine eyelids look straight before thee; Imperative הַיְ֯שַׁר דַּרְכֶּךָ make thy way even Ps 5:9 Qr (Kt הוֺשַׁר; see Ges70 (2)).
