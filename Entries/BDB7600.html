קַרְנַ֫יִם proper name [of a location] conquered by Israel Amos 6:13 (so Gr We Now GASm, compare also לֹא דְבָר above; ᵐ5 and most below קֶרֶן 2); probably in Bashan, = Καρναιν 1 Macc 5:43; 5:44, Καρνιον 2 Macc 12:21; also in ׳ עַשְׁתְּרוֺת ק Gen 14:5 (see III. עַשְׁתָּרוֺת above).
