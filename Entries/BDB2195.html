הָמַם verb make a noise, move noisily, confuse, discomfit (compare [הום]) —

Qal Perfect וְהָמַם consecutive Isa 28:28 suffix הֲמָמַ֫נִי Jer 51:34 Qr (Kt הֲמָמָ֫נוּ), הֲמָמָם 2Chr 15:6 1 singular וְהַמֹּתִ֫י Exod 23:27 Imperfect וַיָּ֫הָם Exod 14:24; Judg 4:15 suffix וַיְּהֻמֵּם Josh 10:10; 1Sam 7:10; Ps 18:15 + 2Sam 22:15 Kt (doubtless right; Qr וַיָּהֹ֑ם); 2 masculine singular suffix וּתְהֻמֵּם Ps 144:6 Infinitive suffix לְהֻמָּם Deut 2:15; Esth 9:24 —
1. move noisily, transitive, drive a wagon in threshing גִּלְגַּל עֶגְּלָתוֺ וּפָרָשָׁיו ׳ה Isa 28:28 (|| דושׁ).
2. confuse, discomfit, with accusative Exod 14:24; Exod 23:27; Josh 10:10; Judg 4:15; 1Sam 7:10; Ps 18:15 = 2Sam 22:15 (|| הפיץ), Ps 144:6 (|| id.); discomfit, vex, 2Chr 15:6 with accusative & בְּכָלצָֿרָה of instrumental; with accusative also Deut 2:15 (followed by עַד תֻּמָּם), Esth 9:24 (followed by לְאַבְּדָם); probably also Jer 51:34 (|| אָכַל), compare הוּם Deut 7:23.
