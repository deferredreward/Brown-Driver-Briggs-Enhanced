BDB4305 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4307

I. לְבָּנָה noun feminine moon, in poetry (Late Hebrew id.); — ׳ ל Isa 24:23 + 2 t.; shall pale before ׳ י Isa 24:23 (|| חַמָּה); shall become like sun (חַמָּה) in day of ׳ י's redemption Isa 30:26 (׳ אוֺר הַלְּ); simile of woman's beauty, ׳ יָפָה כַלְּ Song 6:10 (|| חַמָּה).
