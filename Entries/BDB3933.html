כְּהֻנָּה noun feminine priesthood; — absolute ׳ כ Exod 29:9 + 5 t.; construct כְּהֻנַּת Num 25:13 + 2 t.; suffix כְּהֻנַּתְכֶם Num 18:1; Num 18:7 (twice in verse); כְּהֻנָּתָם Num 3:10 plural כְּהֻנּוֺת 1Sam 2:36. — כהנת יהוה priesthood of ׳ י, the portion of the Levites Josh 18:7 ( D ); plural priest's offices 1Sam 2:36 elsewhere Exod 29:9; Exod 40:15; Num 3:10; Num 16:10; Num 18:1; Num 18:7 (twice in verse); Num 25:13 (all P ), Ezra 2:62 = Neh 7:64; Neh 13:29 (twice in verse).
