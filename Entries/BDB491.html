[אָלַם] verb bind (perhaps compare Assyrian [ al¹mu ], almattu , fortress Dl in ZimBP 114 & in BDEzek.:xi; Arabic be in pain, Aramaic retain anger).

Niph`al (be bound =) be dumb, Perfect 3 feminine singular נֶאֱלָ֑מָה Isa 53:7 וְנֶאֱלַמְתָּ֫ Ezek 3:26, etc.; Imperfect 2 masculine singular תֵּאָלֵם Ezek 24:27 3 feminine plural תֵּאָלַמְנָה Ps 31:19. —
1. be dumb, i.e. silent Ps 39:3 דוּמִיָּה ׳ נ (|| הֶחֱשֵׁיתִי); compare Ps 39:10 (|| לֹא אֶפְתַּחפִּֿי ); simile of sheep Isa 53:7 (|| id.); be dumb, i.e. unable to speak Ezek 3:26 (result of וּלְשׁוֺנְךָ אַדְבִּיק אֶלחִֿכָּ֑ךָ) compare Ezek 24:27 יִפָּתַח פִּיךָ ֗֗֗ וּתְדַבֵּר וְלֹא תֵאָלֵם עוֺד; Ezek 33:22 וַיִּפְתַּח פִּי וְלֹא נֶאֱלַמְתִּי עוֺד; also Dan 10:15 (compare Dan 10:16); be made dumb subject שִׂפְתֵי שָׁ֑קֶר lying lips Ps 31:19 (|| יִדְּמוּ לִשְׁאוֺל Ps 31:18).
Pi`el Participle bind Gen 37:7 ( E ) מְאַלְּמִים אֲלֻמִּים binding sheaves.
