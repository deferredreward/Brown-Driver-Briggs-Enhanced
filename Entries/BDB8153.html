Biblical Hebrew

שׂ֫בַע noun [masculine] satiety, abundance; — absolute ׳ לָשׂ Exod 16:3 +, cstr שׂבַע Ps 16:11, ׳ לְשׂ Prov 13:25 suffix שָׂבֶעֶ֑ךָ Deut 23:25, שָׂבְעָהּ Ruth 2:18 —

1. satiety as to food Ruth 2:18 ׳ לָשׂ (לֶחֶםׅ אָכַל Exod 16:3 ( P ), Lev 25:19 ; Lev 26:5 (both H ), compare ׳ לָשׂ Ps 78:25 ׳ אָכַלכְּ֗֗֗נַפְשְׁךָ שׂ Deut 23:25 eat grapes according to thine appeitite, (namely) thy fill , compare נַפְשׁוֺ ׳ אָכַל לְשׂ Prov 13:25.
2. satisfying abundance, שְׂמָחוֺת ׳ שׂ Ps 16:11.
