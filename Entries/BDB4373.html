BDB4373 [H3924]

Biblical Hebrew

[לוּלַי] noun feminine loop (probably from לולו; compare [דוּדַי] p. 188 above; ZehnpfBAS i. 635, compare Assyrian lu-lu, 'Schleife' ); — only plural absolute לָֻלָאֹת Exod 26:5 (3 t. in verse) + 8 t., construct לֻלְאֹת Exod 26:4; Exod 36:11 — loops on edges of curtains of tabernacle, matching the hooks (קְרָסִיםׅ, for joining the ourtains: Exod 26:4; Exod 26:5 (3 t. in verse); Exod 26:10 (twice in verse); Exod 26:11; Exod 36:11; Exod 36:12 (3 t. in verse); Exod 36:17 (twice in verse) (all P ).

לוּלֵי see לוּלֵא below לוּ.
