בַּרְזֶל noun masculine Deut 19:5 iron (with ל afform.; from piercing ? Late Hebrew id., Aramaic id., פַּדְזֶל, , Phoenician ברזל, Assyrian parzillu COTGloss, (Arabic fetter is loan-word)) — always absolute ׳ ב Gen 4:22 + 74 t. ( בַּרְזֶל֑ Gen 4:22 etc.); —

1. iron, literal:
a. iron-ore, stone containing iron, Deut 8:9; Job 28:2
b. as raw material (to be worked) Gen 4:22; 1Chr 22:3; 1Chr 22:14; 1Chr 22:16; 1Chr 29:2 (twice in verse); 1Chr 29:7; 2Chr 2:6; 2Chr 2:13; 2Chr 24:12 ;
c. as article of commerce Ezek 27:12; Ezek 27:19
d. as material of furniture, utensils, implements, etc., ׳ עֶרֶשׂ ב Deut 3:11, ׳ רֶכֶב ב Josh 17:16; Josh 17:18; Judg 1:19; Judg 4:3; Judg 4:13, ׳ כְּלֵי ב Josh 6:19; Josh 6:24 compare 1Kgs 6:7; Num 35:16, ׳ עֵטבֿ Job 19:24 & figurative Jer 17:1, ׳ חֲרִצֵי הַבּ 2Sam 12:31 = 1Chr 20:3 , ׳ מַגְזְרוֺת הַבּ 2Sam 12:31 cutting instrument of iron, see Amos 1:3, ׳ כַּבְלֵי ב Ps 149:8 (|| זִקִּיִם), ׳ נֵשֶׁק ב Job 20:24, ׳ קַרְנֵי ב 1Kgs 22:11 = 2Chr 18:10, ׳ קִיר ב & ׳ מַחֲבַת ב Ezek 4:3 ׳ בְּרִיחֵי ב, i.e. of Babylon, ag. Cyrus Isa 45:2 compare 1Sam 17:7 & Isa 60:17 (twice in verse) (figurative)
2. tool of iron Deut 27:5; Josh 8:31; Prov 27:17 (twice in verse); Eccl 10:10; Isa 10:34 (metaphor); head of an axe Deut 19:5 ; 2Kgs 6:5 ; 2Kgs 6:6 so weapon 2Sam 23:7 ; Job 41:19 compare also Num 31:22; Josh 22:8 where iron as spoil of war.
3. iron in figure of unwatered earth Deut 28:23 of Egyptian bondage, ׳ כּוּר הַבּ Deut 4:20; 1Kgs 8:51 ; Jer 11:4 of oppression ׳ עֹל ב Deut 28:48 ; Jer 28:14 compare Jer 28:13 of strength Jer 15:12 (twice in verse); compare מִנְעָלֶיךָ ׳ ב Deut 33:25 & Mic 4:13 ׳ קֶרֶן ב; of prophet, firm through Yahweh's might, עַמּוּד ׳ ב Jer 1:18 of distress, ׳ בְּרִיחֵי ב Ps 107:16 compare Ps 107:10 of judgments of ׳ י, & Ps 105:18 ׳ שֵׁבֶט ב Ps 2:9 of evil-doers, Jer 6:28 ; Ezek 22:18; Ezek 22:20 simile of scorching sky Lev 26:19 ׳ מְטִיל ב of bones of hippopotamus Job 40:18 ׳ גִּיד ב of obstinate neck of Israel Isa 48:4.
