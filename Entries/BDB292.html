BDB291 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB293

אַחְיוֺ proper name, masculine (= אחיון, i.e.fraternal).

1. a son of Aminadab, brother of Uzzah 2Sam 6:3; 2Sam 6:4 (ᵐ5 אֶחָיו, We אָחִיו, but see Dr) 1Chr 13:7 (ᵐ5 אֶחָיו).
2. a Benjamite 1Chr 8:14, but read אָחִיו ᵐ5 Be.
3. a Benjamite 1Chr 8:31 = 1Chr 9:37 (> ᵐ5 אֶחָיו).
