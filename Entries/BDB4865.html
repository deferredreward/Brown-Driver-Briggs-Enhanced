מִמְשַׁח noun [masculine] dubious; only in כְּרוּב מִמְשַׁח הַסּוֺכֵךְ Ezek 28:14 cherub of expansion (then with Assyrian mašâhu , measure, Aramaic מְשַׁח id., etc.) that covereth, after ᵑ9 cherub extentus (far-reaching wings), "far-covering cherub," Ew (Or "umspannender"); the anointed cherub that covereth, AV RV , so Hä. Co thinks gloss, and reads אֶתכְּֿרוּב נְתַתִּיךָ; text dubious according to SiegfKau SS Buhl Berthol.
