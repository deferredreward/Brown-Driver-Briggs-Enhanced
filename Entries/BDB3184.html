Biblical Hebrew

חָרִישׁ noun masculine ploughing, ploughing-time — absolute וְקָצִיר ׳ ח Gen 45:6 ( E ) ploughing and harvesting; also (= time of ploughing and harvest ) Exod 34:21 ( J E ); with suffix as accusative of cognate meaning with verb חָרַשׁ חֲרִישׁוֺ 1Sam 8:12 (see above חָרַשׁ 2).
