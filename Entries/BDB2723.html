מִחְיָה noun feminine preservation of life, sustenance, ׳מ Gen 45:5 + 4 t.; construct מִחְיַת Lev 13:10; Lev 13:24 suffix מִחְיָתֶ֑ךָ Judg 17:10 —

1. preservation of life Gen 45:5 (E), 2Chr 14:12.
2. sustenance Judg 6:4; Judg 17:10.
3. reviving, Ezra 9:8; Ezra 9:9.
4. the quick (בָּשָׂר) מִחְיַת the quick (or raw spot) of the flesh Lev 13:10; Lev 13:24 (P).
