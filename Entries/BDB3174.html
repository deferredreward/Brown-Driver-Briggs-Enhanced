III. חֹר, חוֺר noun [masculine] hole; — absolute חֹר 2Kgs 12:10; Ezek 8:7 (strike out Co B etc.), חוֺר Song 5:4, חֹרִים 1Sam 14:11 construct חֹרֵי Job 30:6 suffix חֹרָיו Nah 2:13, חֹרֵיהֶן Zech 14:12 — hole in lid of chest 2Kgs 12:10 (made by boring, נקב), in door Song 5:4 in wall Ezek 8:7 (see above); = eye-socket ׳עיניו תִּמַּקְנָה בְּח Zech 14:12 (plague of enemies of Jerusalem); holes as hiding-places for men 1Sam 14:11, so also 1Sam 13:6 Ew We Dr (for ᵑ0 חוחים), dwelling of outcast people חרי עָפָר Job 30:6 of dens of lions Nah 2:13. — I. חֹר, see II. חור.
