BDB6267 [H6089]

BDB6266 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6268

I. עֶ֫צֶב noun [masculine] pain, hurt, toil; —

1. ׳ ע pain Gen 3:16 (of travail), Prov 10:22.
2. hurt (of mind), ׳ דְּבַרעֿ Prov 15:1 a word that hurts (opposed to מַעֲנֶהרַֿךְ).
3. toil Prov 14:23 pluralלֶחֶם הָעֲצָבִים Ps 127:2 bread of (gained by) toils; suffix עֲצָבֶיךָ Prov 5:10.
