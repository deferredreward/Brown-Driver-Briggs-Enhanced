BDB2846 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2848

II. [חָם] noun masculine husband's father (Late Hebrew id., of husband's or wife's father, so Aramaic חֲמָא; Arabic husband's male relation (father, brother, paternal uncle), but also wife's father , etc.; Ethiopic Assyrian êmû , ZimBP 48); — only suffix חָמִ֫יךָ Gen 38:13; Gen 38:25, of Judah as Tamar's father-in-law; חָמִיהָ 1Sam 4:19; 1Sam 4:21 of Eli, father-in-law of Phinehas' wife.
