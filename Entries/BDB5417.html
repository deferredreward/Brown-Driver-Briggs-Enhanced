BDB5417 [H5392]

BDB5416 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5418

נֶ֫שֶׁךְ noun [masculine] interest, usury (literally something bitten off); — ׳ נ absolute Exod 22:24 +; construct Deut 23:20 — with שִׂים עַלֿ Exod 22:24 ( E ); with לָקַח מִן Lev 25:36, הֵשִׁיב מִן Ezek 18:17, לָקַח Ezek 22:12 (no preposition), בְּנֶשֶׁךְ Lev 25:37 at interest, so Ps 15:5; Ezek 18:8; Ezek 18:13 ׳ בְּנ Prov 28:8 by usury; as accusative of cognate meaning with verb Deut 23:20 (3 t. in verse).
