BDB6699 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6701

פָּסַל verb hew, hew into shape (Late Hebrew Pi`el hew off boughs; Aramaic פְּסַל, = Biblical Hebrew; Nabataean פסלא stone-cutter); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular suffix פְּסָלוֺ Hab 2:18 Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּפְסֹל Exod 34:4, etc.; Imperative masculine singular פֲּסָלֿ Exod 34:1; Deut 10:1 — hew out stone tablets Exod 34:1; Exod 34:4; Deut 10:1; Deut 10:3 building-stones 1Kgs 5:32 with cognate accusative (suffix referring to פֶּסֶל) Hab 2:18.
