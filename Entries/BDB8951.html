BDB8950 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8952

מִשְׁפְּתַ֫יִם noun [masculine] dual probably fire-places or ash-heaps (namely, of the villages or encampments of the tribe) (> Ki Thes and most sheepfolds, see StuJu); — בֵּין הַמִּשְׁפְּתַ֫יִם Judg 5:16 (poem; compare GFM); so (תָּ֑יִם-) Gen 49:14 (poem in J ).
