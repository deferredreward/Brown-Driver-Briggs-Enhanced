עַ֫יִט noun masculine Jer 12:19 bird(s) of prey (from scream); — absolute ׳ ע Gen 15:11+, עָ֑יִט Job 28:7, with article (perhaps) ׳ הַע Jer 12:9b (compare Gie; ׳ הַע interrogative va); construct עֵיט Isa 18:6; Ezek 39:4 — usually collective Gen 15:11 ( J E ), Isa 18:6 ; Job 28:7, עֵיט הָרִים Isa 18:6, עֵיט צִפּוֺר כָּלכָּֿנָף Ezek 39:4 figurative of foes of Judah Jer 12:9 b; of single bird vJer 12:9 a (figurative of Judah), Isa 46:11 (figurative of invader, || אִישׁ עֲצָתִי).
