BDB6206 [H6045]

Biblical Hebrew

עִנְיָן noun masculine Eccl 1:13 occupation, task, only Ecclesiastes (Aramaic loan-word LagBN 205; common in Late Hebrew); — ׳ ע absolute Eccl 2:26; Eccl 3:10; Eccl 5:2; Eccl 8:16 suffix כַּעַס עִנְיָנוֺ Eccl 2:23 his task is (sheer) vextion; עִנְיָן רָע Eccl 1:13 an evil (worthless) task, so Eccl 4:8 (Masora עִנְיַן as if construct, see Baer1; Baer13; but Köii, 1, 99), and, in weakened sense, Eccl 5:12 a bad business, bad affair.
