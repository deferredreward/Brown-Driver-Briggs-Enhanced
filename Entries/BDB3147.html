BDB3146 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3148

חֶרְפָּה noun feminine reproach — ׳ ח Gen 34:14 + 35 t.; construct חֶרְפַּת Josh 5:9 + 15 t.; suffix חֶרְפָּֽתְךָ Ps 74:22 etc. + 17 t. suffixes; plural חֲרָפוֺת Ps 69:11; Dan 12:2 construct חֶרְפּוֺת Ps 69:10. —

1. taunt of enemy 1Sam 17:26 so also 1Sam 25:39 reproach cast upon another, scorn, contumely Neh 3:36; Neh 5:9; Ps 69:20; Ps 69:21; Ps 71:13; Ps 89:51; Ps 119:22 ; Prov 18:3 ; Isa 51:7 ; Ezek 21:33 ׳ שׁמע ח Jer 51:51; Zeph 2:8; Lam 3:61 על ׳ נתן ח Jer 23:40 על ׳ נשׂא ח Ps 15:3 (slander); ׳ נשׂא ח Jer 31:19; Ezek 36:15 ; Mic 6:16 (reproach); נשׂא על ׳ ח bear reproach for Ps 69:8; Jer 15:15; Zeph 3:18 ׳ השׁיב ח Hos 12:15; Neh 3:36 ׳ חרף ח Ps 69:10; Ps 79:12; Ps 89:51 מעל ׳ הסיר ח 1Sam 17:26; Isa 25:8 ׳ העביר ח Ps 119:39 reproaches against God Ps 69:10 (חֶרְפּוֺת חוֺרְפֶיךָ), Ps 74:22; Ps 79:12.
2. reproach which rests upon one, condition of shame, disgrace:
a. sexual 2Sam 13:13; Isa 47:3; Ezek 16:57; Prov 6:33.
b. barrenness of womb Gen 30:23 ( E ) Isa 4:1 widowhood Isa 54:4 .
c. hunger Ezek 36:30 disease Job 19:5.
d. ritual, uncircumcision Gen 34:14 ( P ) Josh 5:9 ( J E ).
e. injuries from enemies Lam 3:30; Lam 5:1; Neh 1:3; Neh 2:7; Job 16:10; Dan 11:18 (twice in verse).
3. a reproach, the object of reproach, the person of thing reproached חֶרְפַּת אָדָם a reproach of man Ps 22:7 נבל ׳ ח Ps 39:9 ׳ ל ׳ היה ח become an object of reproach to Ps 31:12; Ps 79:4; Ps 89:42; Ps 109:25 ; Ezek 5:15 ׅ ׳ (היה) לח (ל Isa 30:5; Jer 6:10; Jer 20:8; Jer 42:18; Jer 44:8; Jer 44:12; Jer 49:13; Ps 69:11; Dan 9:16; Dan 12:2 ׅ ׳ (ל ׳ נתן ח Ezek 22:4; Joel 2:19; Ps 78:66 ׳ נתן לח Jer 24:9; Jer 29:18; Ezek 5:14; Josh 2:17 ׳ ל ׳ שׂים ח Ps 44:14 על ׳ שׂים ח 1Sam 11:2.
