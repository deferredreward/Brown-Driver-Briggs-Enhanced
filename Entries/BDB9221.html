BDB9221 [H8591]

BDB9220 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9222

[תָּעַע] verb Pilpel, Hithpa`el mock (compare Arabic stammer, also shake violently ; onomatopoetic); —

Pilpel Participle מְתַעְתֵּ֑עַ Gen 27:12 = substantive a mocker.
Hithpa`el Participle plural מִתַּעְתְּעִים 2Chr 36:16, with ב person, mocking at his prophets (+ מַלְעִבִים, בּוֺזִים).
