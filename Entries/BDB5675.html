BDB5675 [H4939]

Biblical Hebrew

מִשְׂפָּח noun [masculine] probably outpouring (of blood), bloodshed (שׂ substantive for ס; word chosen for assonance with מִשְׁפָּט); — absolute, וַיְקַו ׳ לְמִשְׁפָּט וְחִנֵּה מ 1Sam 5:7 and he waited for justice and lo! bloodshed, so Ges Hi Ew Di Gu Du CheComm. CheHpt.(dubious).
