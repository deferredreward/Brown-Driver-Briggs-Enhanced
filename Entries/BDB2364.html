BDB2363 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2365

אַזְכָּרָה noun feminine memorial-offering, only P (an Aramaic infinitive form; compare BaNB 90 Sta§ 224) — absolute ׳א Lev 24:7 suffix אַזְכָּֽרָתָהּ Lev 2:2 + 5 t.; — used of the frankincense burned for the shew-bread והיתה ׳אִשֶּׁה לי ׳ללחם לאז Lev 24:7 elsewhere always after הקטיר, only with suffix, referring to מִנְחָה Lev 2:2; Lev 2:9; Lev 2:16; Lev 6:8; Num 5:26 of the meal used as חַטָּאת by the very poor Lev 5:12 always connected with אִשֶּׁה, except Lev 6:8; Num 5:26.
