BDB3306 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3308

טוֺב אֲדוֺנִיָּה proper name, masculine (good is my Lord, ׳ י) — וַאֲדֹנִיָּהוּ וְטוֺבִיָּהוּ וְטוֺב אֲדוֺנִיָּה in list of Levites assigned to reign of Jehoshaphat 2Chr 17:8, but text suspicious; ᵐ5 L τωβαδωνια, but ᵐ5 only τωβαδωβεια for all three names.
