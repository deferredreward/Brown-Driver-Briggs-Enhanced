BDB3041 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3043

חָצָץ noun [masculine] gravel (as divided, comminuted; Aramaic חֲצִיצָא, ) — only absolute ׳ח Prov 20:17; Lam 3:16 [חֲצָצֶיךָ Ps 77:18 see חֵץ]. — ׳יְמַלֵּאפִֿיהוּ ח Prov 20:17 figurative of a liar; שִׁנָּ֑י ׳וַיַּגְרֵס בח Lam 3:16 and he hath crushed my teeth with gravel-stones, figurative of ׳י's dealings with sufferer.
