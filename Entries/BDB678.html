[אָפַק] verb hold, be strong (so Thes who compare Arabic excel = multum valuit; MV compare also ) — only Hithpa`el Perfect 3 plural הִתְאַפָּ֑קוּ Isa 63:15 Imperfect וַיִּתְאַפַק Gen 43:21; Esth 5:10 2 masculine singular תִּתְאַפַּק Isa 64:11 אֶתְאַפָּ֑ק Isa 42:14 וָאֶתְאַפַּק 1Sam 13:12 —

1. force, compel oneself 1Sam 13:12.
2. restrain oneself, refrain Gen 43:31; Gen 45:1; Isa 42:14; Esth 5:10 of ׳י Isa 64:11 of Yahweh's compassion Isa 63:15, מֵעֶיךָ וְרַחֲמֶיךָ אֵלַי הִתְּאַפָּ֑קוּ (almost passive).
