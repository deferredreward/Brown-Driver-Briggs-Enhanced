טָלֶה noun masculine1Sam 7:9 lamb (Late Hebrew id., lamb; Aramaic טַלְיָא lamb, youth, טַלְיְתָא girl, boy, youth, girl (compare Mark 5:41 ᵑ6); Arabic young of cloven-footed animals, especially young gazelle HomNS 235, Nedj. tully, male lamb, DoughtyArab. Desert. i. 429, ii. 269; Ethiopic flock of goats, goat, kid) — טְלֵה חָלָב 1Sam 7:9 sucking lamb (for burnt-offering); in prediction זְאֵב וְטָלֶה יִרְעוּ Isa 65:25 wolf & lamb shall feed; טְלָאִים plural of foregoing, only Isa 40:11 in metaphor of ׳י shepherding his people.
