BDB6928 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6930

[צָבַר] verb heap up (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic צְבַר Syriac is prate, chatter, rave; Arabic is collect, bind together; compare further Dozy); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִצְבֹּר Gen 41:35; Ps 39:7 3 feminine singular וַתִּצְבָּרֿ Zech 9:3, etc.; — heap up, in great quantity: with accusative corn Gen 41:35; Gen 41:49 ( E ), dead frogs Exod 8:10 ( J ), dust Hab 1:10, silver like dust Zech 9:3; Job 27:16, compare (object omitted) Ps 39:7.
