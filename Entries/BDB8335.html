שְׂרֵפָה noun feminine burning; — absolute ׳שׂ Isa 9:4 +, construct שְׂרֵפַת Num 19:6 +; — ׳לשׁ of brick-burning Gen 11:3 (+ שָׁרַף); destructive Amos 4:11; Isa 9:4, of land Deut 29:22, of temple, הָיָה לִשְׂרֵפַת אֵשׁ Isa 64:10, ׳הַר שׁ Jer 51:25 i.e. a burnt-out volcano (figurative of Babylonian ); of heifer Num 19:6, compare vNum 19:17 of spices (probably) as funeral rite 2Chr 16:14 (as accusative of congnate meaning with verb), 2Chr 21:19 (object of עָשָׂה), v2Chr 21:19 of person (penal) Lev 10:6 (accusative of congnate meaning with verb, ׳י subject), Num 17:2.
