BDB7430 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7432

קַ֫יִץ noun masculine Jer 8:20 summer, summer-fruit (compare Greek Θέρος in both meanings); — absolute ׳ ק Gen 8:22 +; קָ֑יִץ Amos 3:15 +; suffix קֵיצֵךְ Isa 16:9; Jer 48:32 —

1.
summer-season, opposed to חֹרֶף Gen 8:22 ( J ), Amos 3:15 ; Zech 14:8 ; Ps 74:17 || קָצִיר Jer 8:20; Prov 6:8; Prov 10:5; Prov 26:1, also (without קָצִיר) Prov 30:25 as fruit harvest Isa 28:4 time of drought Ps 32:4 (figurative).
2. summer-fruit 2Sam 16:1; 2Sam 16:2; Amos 8:1; Amos 8:2; Jer 40:10; Jer 40:12 also, || בָּצִיר, Jer 48:32; Mic 7:1 (in simile), but || קציר Isa 16:9 (assimilated to ק of קַיִץ; read probably בציר).
