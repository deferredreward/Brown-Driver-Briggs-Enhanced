[שָׁחר] verb denominative look early, diligently for (late; originally look for dawn); —

Qal Participle שֹׁחֵר טוֺב Prov 11:27 (|| בִּקֵּשׁ, דָּרַשׁ), elsewhere Pi`el with accusative (suffix) Perfect 3 masculine singular suffix שִׁחֲרוֺ מוּסָר Prov 13:24 seeks him early (with) discipline (chastises him be-times); 2 masculine singular suffix consecutive וְשִׁחַרְתַּ֫נִי וְאֵינֶנִּי Job 7:21 literally Infinitive לְשַׁחֵר פָּנֶיךָ Prov 7:15 of wild asses, Participle מְשַׁחֲרֵי לַטָּ֑רֶף Job 24:5 ( Ges«GKC:130a»§ 130a) seeking eagerly for their food; usually of seeking God (׳ י) earnestly, Perfect וְשִׁחֲרוּאֵֿל Ps 78:34 (|| דָּרַשׁ), Imperfect 2 masculine singular תְּשַׁחֵר אֶלאֵֿל Job 8:5 (si vera lectio), 3 masculine singular suffix יְשַׁחֲרֻנְנִי (Ges «GKC:60e»§ 60e) Hos 5:15 (|| בִּקֵּשׁ), + Hos 6:3 (see שַׁחַר ), and, accusative wisdom, Prov 1:28, Participle suffix מְשַׁחֲרַי Prov 8:17 seek with longing, long for (׳ י), אֲשַׁחֲרֶ֑ךָּ Isa 26:9; Ps 63:2.
