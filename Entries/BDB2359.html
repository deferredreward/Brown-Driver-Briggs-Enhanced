BDB2359 [H2138]

BDB2358 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2360

[זָכוּר] noun masculine male, i. q. זָכָר, but only with suffix; — collective כָּלזְֿכוּרְךָ Exod 23:17 = Exod 34:23 (both J E), = Deut 16:16, of attendance at feasts; כָּלזְֿכוּרָהּ (of city refusing terms of peace) Deut 20:13 commanded to be slain (opposed to הַנָּשִׁים, הַטַּף, etc., vDeut 20:14).
