גֵּרְשֹׁם proper name, masculine

1. son of Moses & Sipporah Exod 2:22 (explanation as if from גור) = Exod 18:3 גֵּרְשׁוֺם 1Chr 23:15; 1Chr 23:16; 1Chr 26:24 so also גֵּרְשֹׁם Judg 18:30 ᵑ0 בֶּןֿ מְנַשֶּׁה compare VB .
2. a son of Levi גֵּרְשֹׁם 1Chr 6:1; 1Chr 6:28; 1Chr 15:7 (compare גֵּרְשׁוֺן); גֵּרְשׁוֺם 1Chr 6:2; 1Chr 6:5; 1Chr 6:47; 1Chr 6:56.
3. a son of Phinehas Ezra 8:2.
