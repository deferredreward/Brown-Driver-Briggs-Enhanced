[פִּגּוּל] noun masculineLev 7:18 foul thing, refuse, but only as technical term of unclean sacrificial flesh; — it is ׳פ if eaten on third day Lev 7:18 (P), Lev 19:7 (H); compare ׳בְּשַׂר פ Ezek 4:14 (undefined); plural מְרַק פִּגֻּלִים Isa 65:4 broth of refuse things (Kt פרק), RSSem i. 325, 2nd ed. 343 thinks of flesh with the blood; Duon the passage of mice and other unclean animals.
