BDB7938 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7940

[רֹ֫כֶס] noun [masculine] dubious, Thes Hup-Now snare, or band (Dr banding together), or (most) league, conspiracy (RV plottings); Ol reads derivatives of רכל = slander, so (רְכִילֵי) Che Du; Bae despairs; — plural construct רֻכְסֵיאִֿישׁ Ps 31:21 (|| דִיב לְשֹׁנוֺת).
