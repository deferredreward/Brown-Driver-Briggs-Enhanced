BDB7608 [H7171]

Biblical Hebrew

קֶ֫רֶץ noun masculine dubious word, apparently nipping, concrete nipper usually interpreted (since Hi) of a nipping or stinging insect (compare Aramaic קָרוֺצָא, Arabic biting insect); e.g. gadfly (compare ThesAdd. 111), figurative of Nebuchadr. מִצָּפוֺן בָּא ׳ ק Jer 46:20 a gadfly cometh from the north; ᵑ9 ( Aq Symm ) stimulator, i.e. קֹרֵץ; Thes and others, more generally, excidium.
