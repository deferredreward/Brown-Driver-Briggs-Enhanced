II. [חֶלְקָה] noun feminine smooth part, smoothness, flattery; — construct חֶלְקַת Gen 27:16; Prov 6:24 plural חֲלָקוֺת Ps 12:3 + 3 t.; —

1. smoothness, smooth part חֶלְקַת צַוָּארָיו Gen 27:16.
2. plural slippery places Ps 73:18 figurative of situation of wicked.
3. smoothness = flattery ׳ שְׂפַת חִ Ps 12:3, compare Ps 12:4 חֶלְקַת לָשׁוֺן Prov 6:24 = substantive plural smooth things, i.e. agreeable, flattering things Isa 30:10. — 1. חֶלְקָה see below I. חלק.
