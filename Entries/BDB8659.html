[שָׁכָה, so most] verb

Hiph`il Participle as adjective in סוּסִים מוּזָנִים מַשְׁכִּים הָיוּ Jer 5:8 usually, horses… roaming at large they have become (compare Ethiopic ); — but meaning lustful needed; Aq Theod ἔλκοντες, Jerome trahentes (that is, genitalia), i.e. משְׁכִים; read with Arnheim Du Dr מַאֲשָׁכִים, i.e. fed stallions (literally growing אֲשָׁכִים, Lev 21:20 compare מַקְרִין, מַפְרִים).
