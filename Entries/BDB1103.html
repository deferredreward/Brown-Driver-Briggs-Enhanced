[בָּכַר] verb (Late Hebrew בְּכֵּר, Aramaic בַּכֵּר, ; compare Arabic rise early, do anything early; , virgin, woman having her first child; Ethiopic primogenitus; Assyrian bukru, first-born, Dl§ 65, 5) —

Pi`el Imperfect יְבַכֵּר Ezek 47:12 Infinitive לְבַכֵּר Deut 21:16 —
1. bear early, new fruit Ezek 47:12.
2. make or constitute as first-born Deut 21:16 (denominative of בכיר). Pu`al Imperfect יְבֻכַּר Lev 27:26 born or made a firstling. Hiph`il Participle f. מַבְכִּירָה Jer 4:31 one bearing her first child.
