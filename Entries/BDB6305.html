עֲצָרָה, עֲצֶ֫רֶת noun feminine assembly (? as confined, held in); — absolute עֲצָרָה Isa 1:13 + 2 t.; עֲצֶ֫רֶת Deut 16:8 + 3 t., עֲצָ֑רֶת 2Chr 7:9 construct עֲצֶ֑רֶת Jer 9:1 plural suffix עַצְרֹתֵיכֶם Amos 5:21 —

1. sacred assembly, rejected by ׳ י, Isa 1:13 and (at Bethel, etc.) Amos 5:21 for Baal 2Kgs 10:20; Deut 16:8 (last day of Maƒƒoth), Lev 23:36 ( P ), Num 29:35 ( P ), Neh 8:18 (all of day after feast of booths), compare 2Chr 7:9 [Late Hebrew of feast of weeks, ᵑ7 עֲצַרְתָּא JosAnt. «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 3:10»iii. 10, 6 Ἀσάρτα]; occasional, Joel 1:14; Joel 2:15.
2. assemblage, company (in general), בֹּגְדִים ׳ ע Jer 9:1 an assemblage of deceivers (Gr עֲדַת; Che חֶבְרַת).
