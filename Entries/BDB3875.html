מַכְאוֺב noun masculine Ps 32:10 pain; — ׳ מ absolute Eccl 1:18 + 2 t.; construct Ps 69:27 suffix מַכְאוֺבִי (מַכְאֹבִי ) Ps 38:18 + 3 t., etc.; plural מַכְאוֺבִים Ps 32:10; Eccl 2:23 מַכְאֹבוֺת Isa 53:3 suffix מַכְאֹבָיו Exod 3:7 מַכְאֹבֵינוּ Isa 53:4 —

1. pain, physical, Exod 3:7 (|| עֳנִי; compare מִפְּנֵי נֹגְשָׂיו in context); 2Chr 6:29 (|| נֶגַע); Job 33:19.
2. of mental pain, Ps 32:10 (of troubles of wicked), of Babylon Jer 51:8; Ps 38:18; Ps 69:27 (as result of sin; of ׳ י's servant); in ׳ י's word to Baruch Jer 45:3 (|| יָגוֺן, אֲנָחָה), compare of Israel in distress Jer 30:15 (|| שֵׁבֶר), of Jerusalem Lam 1:12 (twice in verse); Lam 1:18, Eccl 1:18 (׳ יוֺסִיף דַּעַת יוֺסִיף מַכ); Eccl 2:23 (|| כַּעַם עִנְיָנוֺ); particular of suffering servant of ׳ י Isa 53:3; Isa 53:4 (both || חֳלִי).
