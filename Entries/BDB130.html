BDB129 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB131

(אֲדֹיִּנָה) אֲדֹנִיָּ֫הוּ proper name, masculine (my Lord is Yahweh, compare Phoenician אדנאשמן , אדנבעל, אדנשמש etc., in Assyrian Aduniba' al SchrKB II, 172)

1. fourth son of David 1Kgs 1:8 + 11 t., 1Kgs 2:13 + 5 t. (= אֲדֹנִיָּה 2Sam 3:4; 1Kgs 1:5; 1Kgs 1:7; 1Kgs 1:18; 1Kgs 2:28; 1Chr 3:2).
2. a Levite 2Chr 17:8.
3. a chief of the people Neh 10:17 (= אֲדֹנִיקָם Neh 7:18; Ezra 2:13 compare Ezra 8:13).
