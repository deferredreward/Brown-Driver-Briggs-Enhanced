[יָבַם] verb denominative, only

Pi`el do the duty of יָבָם to a brother's widow; Perfect וְיִבְּמָהּ consecutive Deut 25:5 and shall do a brother-in-law's office to her; Imperative יַבֵּם אֹתָהּ Gen 38:8 (i.e. אֵשֶׁת אָחִיךָ va); Infinitive suffix לֹא אָבָה יַבְּמִי Deut 25:7 (on nominal suffix as object of infinitive, see Ges«GKC:115»§ 115 R. 2 near the end).
