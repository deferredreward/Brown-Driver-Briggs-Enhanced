פֶּתַע substantive suddenness ( √ unknown: compare Assyrian ina pitti, ina pittimma , in suddenness, instantly, DlHWB 553): — Num 35:22 . . . הֲדָפוֺ וְאִםבְּֿפֶתַע if he have thrust him in suddenness, before he is aware what he has done (i.e. accidentally); + פִּתְאֹם Num 6:9 וכי ימות מת עליו בפתע פתאם i.e. very suddenly; לְפֶתַע according to (לְ, p. 5 I 6 b) suddenness, Isa 29:5 והיה לפתע פתאם = at an instant, suddenly, Isa 30:13 שִׁבְרָהּ אשׁר פתאם לפתע יבוא. As adverb accusative, suddenly, Prov 6:15 (= Prov 29:1) פֶּתַע יִשָּׁבֵר, Hab 2:7.
