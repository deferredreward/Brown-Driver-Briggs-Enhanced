BDB9654 [H4223 H4229]

Biblical Aramaic

[מְחָא] verb smite (see Biblical Hebrew II. מָחָה ); —

Pe`al Perfect 3 feminine singular מְחַת, ל of thing, Dan 2:34; Dan 2:35 (+ עַל).
Pa`el Imperfect 3 masculine singular ימַחֵא בִידֵהּ Dan 4:32, i.e. hinder him (compare Eccl 8:4 ᵑ7 ; DAramaic Dialektproben. p. 5; Talmud: LevyNHWB under the word ).
Hithpa`al Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִתְמְחֵא Ezra 6:11 let him be smitten (nailed) עֲלֹ֑הִי.

[מַחְלְקָה] see חלק .
