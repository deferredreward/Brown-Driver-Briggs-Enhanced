יָאִיר proper name, masculine (he enlightens, or one giving light).

1. son of Manasseh Num 32:41 (twice in verse); Deut 3:14 (twice in verse); Josh 13:30; 1Kgs 4:13; 1Chr 2:22; 1Chr 2:23.
2. a judge in Gilead Judg 10:3; Judg 10:4; Judg 10:5.
3. father of Mordecai Esth 2:5.
