II. [חָפֵר] verb be abashed, ashamed ( ᵑ7 חֲפַר Prov 13:5, מַחְפְּרָנָא one causing shame Prov 19:26 Syriac , especially Aph`el be ashamed, put to shame; Arabic be bashful, shy, bashful; Ethiopic be ashamed, blush) —
