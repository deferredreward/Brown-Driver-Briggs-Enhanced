BDB8308 [H8299]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׂרִיג] noun masculine Gen 40:10 tendril, twig (from interlacing; compare Arabic palm-leaf braid, etc.; Ethiopic net (PräBASi. 371); Aramaic סְרִיגָא, network, lattice); — of vine, plural שָׂרִיגִם Gen 40:10, שָׂרִגִים Gen 40:12 of fig-tree, suffix שָׂרִיגֶיהָ Joel 1:7.
