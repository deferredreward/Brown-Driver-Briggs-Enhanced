BDB2425 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2427

מִזְעָר noun [masculine] a little, a trifle, a few.

1. of time עוֺד מְעַט מִזְעָ֑ר Isa 10:25; Isa 29:17 yet a trifle, a little = a very little while.
2. of number מְעַט מִזְעָר שְׁאָר Isa 16:14 a remnant, a very few (opposed to לוֺא כַּ בִּיר). In Isa3 without מעט: — מִזְעָֽר ׃ונשׁאר אנושׁ Isa 24:6.
