שְׁמוּעָה noun feminine report; — absolute ׳ שׁ 1Sam 4:19 +, שְׁמֻעָה 1Sam 2:24 +; construct שְׁמֻעַת 2Sam 4:4 suffix שְׁמֻעָתֵ֫נוּ Isa 53:1 plural absolute שְׁמֻעוֺת Dan 11:44 —

1. report, sometimes after שָׁמַע 1Sam 2:24; 1Sam 4:19 (= news), 1Kgs 10:7 = 2Chr 9:6; 2Kgs 19:7= Isa 37:7; Jer 51:46 רָעָה ׳ שׁ Jer 49:23, compare Ps 112:7 , and טוֺבָח ׳ שׁ Prov 15:30 ; Prov 25:25 tidings about Saul 2Sam 4:4 with בָּאָה 2Sam 13:30 (+ לֵאמֹר), 1Kgs 2:28; Ezek 21:12; Jer 10:22, compare Jer 51:46 + (בָּאָה omitted) Jer 51:46 ׳ אֶלשֿׁ ׳ תָּבוֺא שׁ Ezek 7:28 יְבַהֲלֻהוּ ׳ שׁ Dan 11:44 שְׁמוּעָתֵנוּ Isa 53:1 the report that reached us compare also Isa 28:9; Isa 28:19; Jer 49:14; Obad 1:1 (De Du and others specifically of prophetic message but see GieBeiträge 155 f. We Now Obad 1:1 ).
2. = mention בְּפִיךְ ׳ לאֹ הָֽיְתָח סְדֹם לִשׁ Ezek 16:56.
