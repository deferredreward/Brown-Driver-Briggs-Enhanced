מָזוֺן noun masculine food, sustenance (so Talmud, Biblical Aramaic, Syriac) — בָּר וָלֶחֶם וּמָזוֺן Gen 45:23 ( Di '׳ מ rather an Aramaic word; perhaps later gloss for original צֵדָה Gen 45:21; Gen 42:25'); הַמָּזוֺן לָרֹב 2Chr 11:23.
