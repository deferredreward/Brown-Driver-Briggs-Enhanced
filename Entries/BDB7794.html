BDB7794 [H7302]

Biblical Hebrew

רָוֶה adjective watered; — masculine singular ׳ ר, of garden גַּן (in simile) Jer 31:12; Isa 58:11 feminine singular הָרָוָה Deut 29:18 (opposed to הַצְּמֵאָה; apparently of herbage, in proverb, expression for everything ); perhaps also construct רְוֵה Job 10:15 = sated with afflication (for רְאֵה), see [ רָאֶה ].
