BDB8908 [H8168]

Biblical Hebrew

[שֹׁ֫עַל] noun [masculine] hollow hand, handful; —

1. hollow hand: suffix בְּשָׁעֳלוֺ Isa 40:12 (figurative of ׳ י holding waters).
2. handful: plural שְׁעָלִים 1Kgs 20:10 (dust, in hyperbole); construct שַׁעֲלֵי שְׂעֹרִים (as if from [שַׁ֫עַל] Kö ii. l. 35) Ezek 13:19 handfuls of barley.
