פִּתּוּח noun masculine engraving; — absolute ׳ פ 2Chr 2:13 suffix פִּתֻּחָהּ Zech 3:9 elsewhere plural פִּתּוּחִים 2Chr 2:6, etc.; — on (wood overlaid with) metal, פִּתּוּחֵי מִקְלְעוֺת כְּרוּבִים 1Kgs 6:29 (see v 1Kgs 6:21 1Kgs 6:22), in temple, so Ps 74:6 on stone Zech 3:9, especially פִּתּוּחֵי חֹתָם Exod 28:11; Exod 28:21; Exod 28:36; Exod 39:6; Exod 39:14; Exod 39:30 in general 2Chr 2:6; 2Chr 2:13.
