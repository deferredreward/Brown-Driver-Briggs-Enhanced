Biblical Hebrew

IV. אִי adverb not (frequently in Rabb , as אִי אֶפְשַׁר impossible; and in Ethiopic the ordinary negative; compare Phoenician אי CISi, 3, 5, and in איבל165; 18; 167; 11; Assyrian ai ) Job 22:30 אִינָֿקִי the non-innocent.

Job 22:30: read perhaps אֵל (Me), or אֱלֹהַּ (Ley), with וְתִמָּלֵט in verse b.
