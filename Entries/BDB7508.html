BDB7508 [H7087]

Biblical Hebrew

[קָפָא] verb thicken, condense, congeal (compare Syriac heap up, collect); —

Qal Perfect 3 plural קָֽפְאוּ תְהֹמֹת Exod 15:8 (song) the deeps were condensed, became firm walls; Imperfect יקפאון Zech 14:6 Kt (i.e. יִקְפָּא֑וּן [possible is also יִקָּפֵא֑וּן Niph`al]) Thes and others glorious(?) ones [stars] shall contract (dwindle), but read Qr וְפָּאוֺן see following Participle הַקֹּפְאִים עַלאשִׁמְרַיהֶם Zeph 1:12 the men who are thickening on their lees (easy-going men, under figure of undisturbed wine).
Hiph`il Imperfect 2 masculine singular suffix תַּקְפִּיאֵנִי Job 10:10 didst thou not curdle me like cheese (of formation of foetus) ?
