I. יֶ֫רַח noun masculineZech 11:8 month (Aramaic יַרְחָא, new moon, month; Palmyrene ירח VogNo. 1; Assyrian ar— u COTGlossary Muss-ArnJBL xi. 1892, 73, 163; compare Phoenician Ethiopic below יָרֵחַ; Sabean ורחֿ month, DHMZMG 1876, 603; 1883, 369 HalJAS vii, 1, 516) — absolute יֶ֫רַח 1Kgs 6:37 + 3 t.; construct id. Deut 21:13; 2Kgs 15:13 plural יְרָחִים Exod 2:2 + 3 t.; construct יַרְחֵי Job 7:3; Job 29:2 —

1. month, as measure of time, during, or in which Exod 2:2 (E), Zech 11:8; Job 39:2 יַרְחֵי שָׁ֑וְא Job 7:3 יַרְחֵי קֶדֶם Job 29:2 pleonastic יֶרַח יָמִים a month (of) days = a month of time Deut 21:13; 2Kgs 15:13.
2. calendar month, with name יֶרַח זִו 1Kgs 6:37 (2nd mo., compare v1Kgs 6:1); יֶרַח בּוּל v1Kgs 6:38 (8th mo.); יֶרַךְ הָאֵתָנִים 1Kgs 8:2 (7th mo.); compare בְּמִסְפַּר יְרָחִים אַליָֿבאֹ Job 3:6 גֶּרֶשׁ יְרָתִים Deut 33:4 produce of months, i.e. of various seasons of year. — compare synonym חֹדֶשׁ.
