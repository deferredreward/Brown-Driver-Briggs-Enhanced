BDB8483 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8485

שְׁדֵפָה noun feminine blighted or blasted thing; — לִפְנֵי קָמָה ׳וּשׁ 2Kgs 19:26 a blasted thing before (the standing grain, i.e. before maturity ( > שְׁדֵמָה field, as || Isa 37:27, compare Di), but improbable; Th proposes וָדִים ׳ל ׳וּשׁ; Kit שְׁדֻף הַקָּדִים; We (and most since) ׳וּשְׁדֵפָֽה ָ לְפָנַי קֻמְךָ וגו (Che וּשְׁפָיִים, for וּשְׁדֵפָה).
