שֶׁ֫טֶף, שֶׁ֫צֶף noun masculineNah 1:8 flood; — absolute שֶׁ֫טֶף Nah 1:8 + 3 t.; construct ׳שֶׁ Prov 27:4, ׳שֵׁ Ps 32:6 — flood, figurative of ׳י's judgement, עֹבֵר ׳שׁ Nah 1:8, compare Dan 9:26, זְרֹעוֺת ׳הַשּׁ Dan 11:22 (compare Dr); of calamity, מַיִם רַבִּים ׳שׁ Ps 32:6 אָף ׳שׁ Prov 27:4 a flood of anger; so שֶׁצֶף קֶצֶף Isa 54:8 (צ for ט before following צ Ew Di; > Du Che Marti strike out ׳שׁ as dittograph); שֶׁטֶף literally of rainflood, Job 38:25.
