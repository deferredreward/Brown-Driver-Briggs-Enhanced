[רָשַׁם] verb inscribe, note (Late Hebrew id..; so Aramaic רְשַׁם, ; Arabic is loan-word NöZMG xxix (1875), 327 Frä137. 250); —

Qal Passive participle with art, as substantive הָרָשׁוּם בִּכְתָב אֱמֶת Dan 10:21 that which is inscribed in the writing of truth.
