BDB8968 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8970

שְׁפֵלָה noun feminine lowland; — always absolute ׳ שׁ with article, except suffix שְׁפֵלָתֹה Josh 11:16 — lowland:

1. usually technical term of strip west of Judaean mountains (on exact limits see GASmGeogr. 201 ff.; Expos. Dec. 1896, 404 f. BuhlGeogr. 104 DrHast. DB iii. 892 f.): almost always distinguished from הָהָר and הַנֶּגֶב, sometimes from הָעֲרָבָה, in Deut 1:7; Josh 9:1 ( RD ), also from חוֺף הַיָּם, compare Josh 10:40; Josh 12:8 (both R D ), Judg 1:9 (perhaps RD , compare Bu GFM), Josh 15:33 ( P ), Jer 17:26; Jer 32:44; Jer 33:13; Obad 1:19; Zech 7:7 ; 2Chr 26:10 (distinguished from הַמִּישׁוֺר, table-land east of Jordan); region of sycomores 1Kgs 10:27 = 2Chr 1:15; 2Chr 9:27; 1Chr 27:28.
2. of lowland (near coast) north of Carmel Josh 11:2 west of mountains of Ephraim Josh 11:16 (both R D ).
