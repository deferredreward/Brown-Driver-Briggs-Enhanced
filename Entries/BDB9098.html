BDB9098 [H8388]

Biblical Hebrew

תָּאַר verb apparently incline (perhaps akin to תּוּר Thes; > Buhl denominative from תֹּאַר , q. v. ); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ ת, subject boundary: Josh 18:14, with ה locative Josh 15:9 b. 11; with מִן ֗֗֗ עַל Josh 15:9 a; with מִן only, Josh 18:17 probably also with accusative of location Josh 19:13 (read רִמּ֫וֺנָה וְתָאַר הַנֵּעָה to Rimmon, and it inclined to Ne`;a Di Steuern Benn and others).
