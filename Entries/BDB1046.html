BDB1045 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1047

בַּ֫יִת with article הַבַּיִת Isa 15:2 according to Ew Brd Di and others proper name, of a location but abbreviated; perhaps for בֵּית דִּבְלָתַיִם Jer 48:22 (so Ew and others) or בֵּית בָּמוֺת compare MI27 (compare De Di); others (Ges De Che Or) take ׳הַבּ here = the house, i.e. temple or shrine.
