BDB4184 [H3739]

Biblical Hebrew

III. [כָּרָה] verb give a feast (literally perhaps bring (guests), invite ; compare Assyrian karû , bring, kirêtu , feast, kireti iškun , he gave a feast, Dl :HWB 352); — only

Qal Imperfect. with accusative of cognate meaning with verb, לָהֶם כֵּרָה גְדוֺלָהוַיִּכְרֶה 2Kgs 6:23 and he gave a great feast for them (the context requires this sense, but text dubious, NöZMG 1886, 724, compare Klo ).
