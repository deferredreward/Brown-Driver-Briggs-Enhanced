מִכְתָּב noun masculine2Chr 21:12 writing; — absolute ׳מ Deut 10:4 + 5t.; construct מִכְתַּב Exod 32:16 + 2 t.; —

1. = handwriting וְהַמִּכְתָּב מִכְתַּב אֱלֹהִים הוּא Exod 32:16.
2. = thing written, Exod 39:30 (accusative of congnate meaning with verb with כָּתַב), Deut 10:4 specifically a royal enactment or edict (= כְּתָב כתם
4.), 2Chr 35:4 || כְּתָב, 2Chr 36:22 = Ezra 1:1 a prophetic writing 2Chr 21:12.
3. in a title, לְחִזְקִיָּהוּ ׳מ Isa 38:9 Writing of Hezekiah.
