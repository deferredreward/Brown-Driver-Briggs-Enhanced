[עַלִּיז] adjective exultant, jubilant (on form compare LagBN 110 ); — feminine singular עַלִּיזָה jubilant city Isa 22:2 (|| עִיר הֹמִיָּה), Isa 32:13, compare Zeph 2:15 and (city omitted) Isa 23:7 of person, plural שְׁאוֺן עַלִּיזִים Isa 24:8 construct עַלִּיזֵי גַּאֲוָתִי Isa 13:3, compare Zeph 3:11, see גַּאֲוָה .
