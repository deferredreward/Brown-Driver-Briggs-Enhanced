[הָלַם] verb smite, hammer, strike down (compare Phoenician מהלם below below מַהֲלֻמוֺת); —

Qal Perfect 3 feminine singular וְהָֽלְמָה consecutive Judg 5:26 3 plural הָֽלְמוּ Judg 5:22; Isa 16:8 suffix הֲלָמוּנִי Prov 23:25 Imperfect 3 masculine singular suffix יֶהֶלְמֵנִי Ps 141:5 3 masculine plural יַהֲלֹמ֑וּן Ps 74:6 Participle active ה֫וֺלָם פָּ֑עַם Isa 41:7 (compare Ges«GKC:29»§ 29, 3 b); passive construct הֲלוּמֵי Isa 28:1 — smite (with hammer), followed by accusative of person Judg 5:26 (fatally; || מָחַק, מָחַץ); also figurative of correction, discipline Ps 141:5 (|| הוֺכִּיחַ); of intoxicating, wine גֶּפֶן שִׂבְמָה בַּעֲלֵי גוֺיִם הָֽלְמוּ שְׂרוּקֶּיהָ Isa 16:8 the vine of Sibma, its choice branches smote down lords of nations (so Hi De Che Brd Di and others, RVm ; lords subject according to Ges Ew and others AV RV ); also הֲלוּמֵי יַיִן smitten down by wine Isa 28:1 compare further Prov 23:35 followed by accusative of thing ה֫וֺלָםפָּֿ֑עְם Isa 41:7 one striking an anvil; smite down, or strike off Ps 74:6 (object פִתּוּחִים carved work); also absolute of horse' hoofs Judg 5:22. — On וַהֲלֹם 1Sam 14:16 see הֲלֹם .
