BDB4152 [H3712]

BDB4151 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4153

כִּפָּה noun feminine branch frond (probably of palm tree, compare כַּף 3 d); — ׳כ Isa 9:13; Isa 19:15 figurative of nobles, rulers (both opposed to אַגְמוֺן rush, i.e. high and low; || רֹאשׁ וְזָנָב); with suffix כִּפָּתוֺ Job 15:32 (of wicked under figure of palm-tree).
