BDB9741 [H5622]

Biblical Aramaic

[סַרְבָּל] noun [masculine] probably mantle (see especially SACJPhil. xxvi (1899), 307 f., compare AndrM 74*, with conjecture as to original Persian form; > trousers; Late Hebrew; Jewish-Aramaic id., with both meanings, also shoes; Arabic mantle is loan-word Frä47; Egyptian Arabic shoe is Greek loan-word according to VollersZMG li (1897), 298, compare Kraussii. 412); — plural suffix סַרְבָּלֵיהוֺן Dan 3:21; Dan 3:27.
