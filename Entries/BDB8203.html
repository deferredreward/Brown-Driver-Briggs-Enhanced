BDB8203 [H7847]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׂטָה] verb turn aside (Aramaic סְטָא, id.; שְׂטָא stray; Ethiopic III. be seduced, etc.; cf DrJPhil. xi (1882), 205); —

Qal Perfect 2 feminine singular שָׂטִית Num 5:19; Num 5:20 Imperfect 3 feminine singular תִּשְׂטֶה Num 5:12 Num 5:29, jussive 3 masculine singular יֵשְׂטְ Prov 7:25 Imperative masculine singular שְׂטֵה Prov 4:15 — turn aside, of unfaithful wife Num 5:12 (absolute), + תַּחַת אִישׁ, i.e. while married, Num 5:19 (+ טֻמְאָה adverb accusative), Num 5:20 Num 5:29 (all P ); of youth, from (מֵעַל) way of evil woman Prov 4:15, toward (אֶל) her ways Prov 7:25.
