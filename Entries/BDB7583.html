BDB7582 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7584

[קֶ֫רֶת] noun feminine town, city (late poetry) (compare Phoenician קרת חדשת proper name, of a location in Cyprus, also = Carthage, and perhaps Cirta, Tigranocerta; compare Thes); — always קָ֑רֶת; — of Job's city, Job 29:7 indefinite Prov 8:3; Prov 9:3; Prov 9:14 city as a corporate body of men Prov 11:11.
