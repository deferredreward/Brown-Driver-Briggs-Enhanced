BDB1674 [H1573]

Biblical Hebrew

גֹּ֫מֶא noun masculine Job 8:11 rush, reed, papyrus (compare Löwp. 55) (Late Hebrew גּוֺמֶא, Ethiopic loan-word, according to Di; — name from swallowing, sucking up, water? so Thes, compare Che Isa 18:2 and elsewhere; = Coptic gome, see Di Exod 2:3 whence others derive as loan-word) — גֹּ֫מֶא Exod 2:3 + 3 t.; — rush, paper-reed, (Egyptian) Job 8:11 (|| אָחוּ); collective Isa 35:7 (|| קָנֶה ); as material, ׳ תֵּבַת ג Exod 2:3 chest of paper-reed; ׳ כְּלֵיגֿ Isa 18:2 vessels of paper-reed.
