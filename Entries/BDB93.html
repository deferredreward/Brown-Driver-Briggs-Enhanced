BDB93 [H101]

Biblical Hebrew

[אַגָּן] noun [masculine] bowl, basin (Talmud אוֺגָן, Aramaic אַגָּנָא, ; Arabic , vessel in which clothes are washed; Assyrian (plural) aganât¢ DlW).

1. basins used in ritual אֲגָנֹת Exod 24:6 ( E ).
2. אַגַּן הַסַּהַר, simile of curves of body Song 7:3.
3. metaphor of family of Eliakim. כְּלֵי הָאַגָּנוֺת = basin-vessels Isa 22:24 (= bowl-shaped vessels Che) opposed to כְּלֵי הַנְּכָלִים; both || כְּלֵי הַקָּטָן.
