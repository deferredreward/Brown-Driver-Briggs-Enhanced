יִרְאָה noun feminine fear; — ׳ יר Ezek 30:13 + 6 t.; construct יִרְאַת Gen 20:11 + 27 t.; suffix יִרְאָתִי Jer 32:40 יִרְאָתוֺ Exod 20:20 יִרְאָֽתְךָ Job 4:6 + 3 t.; יִרְאָתֶ֑ךָ Ps 5:8 + 2 t.; —

1. fear, terror Isa 7:25; Ezek 30:13 גְדוֺלָה ׳ י great fear Jonah 1:10; Jonah 1:16 || פַּחַד Deut 2:25 || רעד(הׅ Ps 2:11; Ps 55:6.
2. a terror = object of terror, || גֹּבַהּ Ezek 1:18 (strike out Co).
3. fear of God, reverence, piety יִרְאַת אֱלֹהִים Gen 20:11 ( E ), 2Sam 23:3; Neh 5:15 שַׁדַּי ׳ יר Job 6:14 אֱלֹהֵינוּ ׳ יר Neh 5:9 יהוה ׳ יר 2Chr 19:9; Isa 11:3; Isa 33:6; Ps 34:12; Prov 10:27 ; Prov 14:26; Prov 14:27; Prov 15:16; Prov 19:23; Prov 22:4; Prov 23:17 || the knowledge (of God) Prov 1:29 ; Prov 2:5; Isa 11:2 is the beginning of wisdom Ps 111:10; Prov 9:10, and knowledge Prov 1:7 the instruction of wisdom Prov 15:33 , is to hate evil Prov 8:13, and it involves departing from evil Prov 16:6 אֲדֹנָי ׳ י is wisdom Job 28:28 יִרְאָה is defined by context Job 15:4 with suffixes these are usually object, fear of him Exod 20:20 ( E ), of thee Ps 5:8; Ps 90:11; Ps 119:38 ; Isa 63:17, of me Jer 32:40, but subject, thy fear (i.e. thy religion) Job 4:6; Job 22:4.
4. יִרְאַת יהוֺה Ps 19:10 the fear of ׳ י = the law as revered.
