עֵצָה noun feminine counsel, advice (= (יְ)עֵצָה) — ׳ע Judg 20:7 + 28 t.; construct עֲצַת Isa 5:19 + 33 t.; suffix עֲצָתִי Prov 1:25 + 5 t. (+ Isa 46:11 Qr; Kt עצתו better); עֲצָֽתְךָ Ps 20:5; Ps 73:24 עֲצָתוֺ Hos 10:6 + 7 t. (including Isa 46:10 Kt see supr); עֲצָתָם Jer 18:23 + 4 t.; plural עֵצוֺת Deut 32:28 + 2 t.; עֲצָתָ֑יִךְ Isa 47:13 — counsel, advice 2Sam 15:31; 2Sam 15:34; 2Sam 16:23 (twice in verse); 2Sam 17:14 (3 t. in verse); 2Sam 17:23; 1Kgs 12:8; 1Kgs 12:13; 1Kgs 12:14 = 2Chr 10:8; 2Chr 10:13; 2Chr 25:16; Ezra 10:3 (where read אֲדֹנִי ׳ע Reuss Ry), vEzra 10:8; Job 29:21 political consultation Isa 47:13 as accusative of congnate meaning with verb 2Sam 17:7; 1Kgs 1:12; Isa 8:10; Ezek 11:2 (עצתרָֿע, || אָוֶן); with הָב֫וּ give counsel Judg 20:7; 2Sam 16:20 ׳חֵבִיא ע Isa 16:3 ׳עשׂה ע Isa 30:1 אָשִׁית עֵצוֺת בְּנַפְשִׁי Ps 13:3 = design, purpose Ezra 4:5; Neh 4:9; 1Chr 12:20 (Baer), Ps 14:6; Ps 20:5; Jer 18:23 (followed by עַל = against), Jer 49:30 (|| מַחֲשָׁבָה); עֲצַתגּֿוֺיִם Ps 33:10 suitable counsel for war (= generalship) 2Kgs 18:20 = Isa 36:5 (compare Prov 20:18 below); also עֲצַת שָׁלוֺם Zech 6:13 counsel of peace; practical wisdom, sagacity Isa 19:3, compare vIsa 19:11, Jer 19:7; Deut 32:28 (|| תְּבוּנָה), Hos 10:6. Especially in Wisdom Literature and prophets; counsel = good counsle, wisdom Job 38:2; Job 42:3; Prov 12:15; Prov 20:5 (its seat בְּלֶבאִֿישׁ); || תּוּשִׁיָּה Prov 8:14; Isa 28:29 (of ׳י); || חָכְמָה Jer 49:7 || חָכְמָה and תְּבוּנָה Job 12:13; Prov 21:30 || מוּסָר Prov 19:20 || תַּחְבֻּלוֺת Prov 20:18 (compare Isa 36:5 above); נִפְתָּלִים ׳ע Job 5:13 וּגְבוּרָה ׳רוּחַ ע Isa 11:2 (|| חָכְמָה וּבָינָה ׳ר); hearty counsel נֶפֶשׁ ׳ע Prov 27:9 counsel of wisdom, when wisdom is personified Prov 1:25; Prov 1:30 (|| תּוֺכַחַת); in bad sense רשׁעים ׳ע Job 10:3; Job 21:16; Job 22:18; Ps 1:1, compare Job 18:7; Ps 106:43; Isa 29:15 of plan of ׳י; ׳י ׳ע Ps 33:11; Prov 19:21; Isa 19:17; Isa 14:26 (הַיִעוּצָה ׳הע), Isa 25:1; Isa 46:10; Isa 46:11 קדושׁ ישׂראל ׳ע Isa 5:19 also Mic 4:12; Jer 32:19 (גְּדֹל הָעֵצָה), Jer 49:20; Jer 50:45; Ps 106:13 עֶלְיוֺן ׳ע Ps 107:11 instruction, guiding wisdom of ׳י Ps 73:24 אִישׁעֲֿצָתוֺ i.e. counsellor of ׳י Isa 40:13 in figure אַנְשֵׁי עֲצָתִי Ps 119:24 = my counsellors, said of testimonies of God; = prophecy, מלאכיו ׳ע Isa 44:26 (|| דְּבַר עַבְדּוֺ); מִזְּקֵנִים ׳תִּאֹבַד ע Ezek 7:26 (compare 1Kgs 12:8 || חָזוֺן, תּוֺרָה), מֵחָכָם Jer 18:18 (|| דָּבָר, תּוֺרָה).
