הָמוֺן noun masculine 1Sam 4:14 (feminine Job 31:34) sound, murmur, roar, crowd, abundance; — absolute ׳ ה 1Sam 4:14 + 27 t. ( + 2Kgs 25:11 = אָמוֺן in || Jer 52:15, see II. אָמוֺן above); construct הֲמוֺן Gen 17:14 + 30 t. + Ezek 39:11 ; Ezek 39:15 (גֵּיא הֲמוֺן גּוֺג); suffix הֲמוֺנוֺ Isa 5:13 + 2 t. + Ezek 31:18 Qr + 3 t. ( Kt המונה); so also Co for proper name (?) הֲמוֺנָה (q. v.) Ezek 39:16 הַמוֺנָהּ Isa 5:14 + 6 t. + Ezek 7:12; Ezek 7:13; Ezek 30:4 (all strike out B Co; in Ezek 7:13 also Da), הֲמֹנָהּ Ezek 29:19 הֲמוֺנָם Isa 31:4; Ezek 7:11 plural הֲמֹנִים Joel 4:14 (twice in verse); suffix הֲמוֺנֶיהָ Ezek 32:30 — הֲמָנְכֶם Ezek 5:7 see below המן below; —

1. sound, murmur, rush, roar, especially sound made by a crowd of people, murmur, roar Ps 65:8 לְאֻמִּים ׳ ה (|| שְׁאוֺן יַמִּים etc.); compare ׳ קוֺל ה Isa 13:4 sound of a roaring (|| קוֺל שְׁאוֺן), Isa 33:3; Dan 10:6 הֲמוֺן עַמִּים Isa 17:12 (|| שְׁאוֺן לְאֻמִּים) where again compared with הֲמוֺת יַמִּים; also Isa 31:4 of throng of shepherds (|| קוֺל); of a city ׳ ה קִרְיָה Job 39:7 (|| תְּשֻׁאוֺת נֹגֵשׂ); compare עִיר ׳ ה = noisy city Isa 32:14, הֲמוֺנָהּ Isa 5:14 of sound of songs שִׁירַיִךְ ׳ ה Ezek 26:13 (|| קוֺל כִנּוֺרַיִךְ), compare Amos 5:23 (|| זִמְרַת נְבָלֶיךָ); of crying ׳ קוֺל ה 1Sam 4:14 (|| קוֺל) צְעָקָה; see further הֲמוֺן מֵעִים figurative of sympathy Isa 63:15 (|| רַחֲמִים), compare הָמָה 2; of rain הַגֶּשֶׁם ׳ קוֺל ה 1Kgs 18:41 , compare Jer 10:13 = Jer 51:16 — of rumbling of chariot-wheels גַּלְגִּלָּיו ׳ ה Jer 47:3 (|| קוֺל שַׁעֲטַת פַּרְסוֺת & רַעַשׁ לְרִכְבּוֺ).
2. tumult, confusion (as occasioning a roar) 1Sam 14:19; 2Sam 18:29.
3. crowd, multitude (especially frequently in Ezra, Chronicles): —
a. frequently of great army Judg 4:7; 1Sam 14:16; 1Kgs 20:13; 1Kgs 20:28; Isa 29:5 (twice in verse); 2Chr 13:8; 2Chr 14:10 ; 2Chr 20:2; 2Chr 20:12 ; 2Chr 20:15; 2Chr 20:24; 2Chr 32:7 compare Ezek 39:11 also Ezek 7:12; Ezek 7:13; Ezek 30:4 (compare emendation above); on Jer 3:23 compare VB ; see further גֵּיא הֲמוֺן גּוֺג Ezek 39:11 ; Ezek 39:15; Dan 11:10 (חֲיָלִים רַבִּים ׳ ה) Dan 11:11 Dan 11:11 Dan 11:12 Dan 11:13 (|| חַיִל גָּדוֺל); also plural Joel Dan 4:14 (twice in verse).
b. of a whole people 2Sam 6:19; Isa 5:13; Isa 16:14; Ezek 7:11; Ezek 7:14; Ezek 29:19 ; Ezek 30:10; Ezek 30:15; Ezek 31:2; Ezek 31:18; Ezek 32:12 (twice in verse); Ezek 32:16 ; Ezek 32:18 ; Ezek 32:20; Ezek 32:24; Ezek 32:25; Ezek 32:26; Ezek 32:31; Ezek 32:32
c. of any great throng 2Kgs 7:13 (twice in verse) (so ᵑ0 2Kgs 25:11 , but || Jer 52:15 הָאָמוֺן) Ps 42:5; Job 31:34 (רַבָּה ׳ ה) Ezek 23:42 גּוֺיִם ׳ ה Gen 17:4; Gen 17:5 ( P ) compare Isa 29:7; Isa 29:8 so, under figure of overwhelming mass of waves Jer 51:42.
4. great number, abundance (late) נָשִׁים ׳ ה 2Chr 11:23
for וַיִּשְׁאַל הֲמַוֺן נָשִׁים PerlesAnal. 47 proposes ׳ וִיִּשְׂאוּ לָהֶם נ (compare נשׂא 3.d.); of cattle Jer 49:32 of things: materials for temple-building 1Chr 29:16 tithes & gifts 2Chr 31:10. Hence
5. abundance, wealth, Ps 37:16 compare Eccl 5:9 (|| כֶּסֶף), Isa 60:5 (יָם ׳ ה; || חֵיל גּוֺיִם).
