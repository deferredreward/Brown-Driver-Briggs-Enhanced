BDB6719 [H6472]

Biblical Hebrew

פַּעֲמֹן noun [masculine] bell, on high-priest's robe (from its stroke, beat); — ׳ פ absolute Exod 39:26 (twice in verse); construct זָחָב ׳ פ Exod 28:34 (twice in verse); plural הַפַּעֲמֹנִים Exod 39:25, construct פַּעֲמֹנֵי זָהָב Exod 28:33; Exod 39:25. פַּעֲנֵחַ see ׳ צָֽפְנַת פ.
