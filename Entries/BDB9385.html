[בְּקַר] verb

Pa`el inquire, seek (only Ezra) ( ᵑ7 Syriac; see Biblical Hebrew); — Perfect 3 masculine plural בַּקַּ֫רוּ Dan 4:19; Dan 6:1 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְבַקַּר Dan 4:15 Infinitive לְבַקָּרָה Dan 7:14 —
1. inquire, with עַל (of thing) concerning Dan 7:14 with subject indefinite, = passive, Dan 4:19 search was made, so Dan 6:1; Dan 4:15.
Hithpa`al Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִתְבַּקַּר Dan 5:17, impersonal, let search be made.
