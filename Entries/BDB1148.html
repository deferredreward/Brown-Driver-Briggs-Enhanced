BDB1148 [H1105]

Biblical Hebrew

I. בֶּ֫לַע noun [masculine] swallowing, devouring, thing swallowed.

1. swallowing = destruction, דִּבְרֵיבָֿ֑לַע Ps 52:6 devouring words (|| לְשׁוֺן מִרְמָה).
2. thing swallowed בִּלְעוֺ, Jer 51:44 ׳ וְהֹצֵאתִי אתבֿ מִפִּיו and I will bring forth that which he hath swallowed out of his mouth.
