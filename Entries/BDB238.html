Biblical Hebrew

I. אוֺרָה noun feminine light (late, Mishna id., compare Aramaic אוּרְתָּא evening-light, moon-light, star-light, etc.)

1. light (opposed to חֲשֵׁיכָה) Ps 139:12.
2. light of joy & happiness Esth 8:16 plural intensive אוֺרוֺת light of life Isa 26:19 (light that quickens dead bodies as dew the plants Ew Hi De Che Di RVm ; see BrMP 303 compare אוֺר חַיִּים ; but Ki Ges MV Bö RV translate herbs).
