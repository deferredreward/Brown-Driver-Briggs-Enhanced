שָׂבְעָה, [שִׂבְעָה] noun feminine satiety; — absolute ׳ שׂ Isa 56:11 +, construct שִׂבְעַתֿ Ezek 16:49, sf, שָׂבְעָתַךְ vEzek 16:28 —

1. as to food, especially ׳ אָכַל לְשׂ eat to satiety, one's fill, Isa 23:18; Ezek 39:19 ׳ לְשׂ also Isa 55:2 (figurative); ׳ שׂ of dogs Isa 56:11 (figurative); לֶֿחֶםָ ׳ שׂ leading to arrogance, as sin of Sodom Ezek 16:49 (+ גָּאוֺם, etc.).
2. to carnal desire Ezek 16:28 (figurative).
