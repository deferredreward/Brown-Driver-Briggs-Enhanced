BDB1846 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1848

[דָּגַר] verb gather together as a brood (compare Aramaic דְּגַר heap together; so Nasar.) —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular דָּ֨גָר Jer 17:11 3 feminine singular דָּֽגְרָה Isa 34:15 — gather together into its shadow Isa 34:15 (of קִפֹּז, q. v.), דָּגָר וְלֹא יָלָד֑ Jer 17:11 (of partridge; simile of one getting riches unjustly).
