חֲטָאָה noun feminine sin, sin-offering (?) —

1. sin, chiefly in the phrase גְדֹלָה ׳ ח great sin Gen 20:9 ( E ) Exod 32:21; Exod 32:30; Exod 32:31 ( J E ), 2Kgs 17:21 ׳ כְּסוּי ח Ps 32:1 he whose sin is covered is blessed; but the prayer of the wicked becomes sin when he is judged Ps 109:7.
2. sin-offering (according to most) עולה וחטאה Ps 40:7 whole burnt-offering and sin-offering (so Vrss Thes MV SS and others, but < whole burnt-offering with sin see Br MP 329).
