BDB990 [H961]

Biblical Hebrew

בִּזָּה noun feminine spoil, booty (late) — בִּזָּה 2Chr 14:13 + 9 t.; — spoil, prey, of things 2Chr 28:14; Esth 9:10; Esth 9:15; Esth 9:16, compare 2Chr 14:13; 2Chr 25:13; (|| שָׁלָל & רכושׁ); verbal noun, spoiling, ׳ בַּב Ezra 9:7; Dan 11:33, compare also ׳ לְב Neh 3:36.

בִּזָּיוֺן see below בזה.
