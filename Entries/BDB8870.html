BDB8869 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8871

I. שָׁמִיר noun masculine: Ezek 3:9 thorn(s), adamant, flint; — absolute ׳ שׁ Isa 5:6 +; suffix שְׁמִירוֺ Isa 10:17 —

1. collective thorns, thorn-bushes, token of waste-land, only Isaiah, + שַׁיִת Isa 5:6; Isa 7:23; Isa 7:24; Isa 7:25; Isa 27:4 figurative Isa 9:17 ; Isa 10:17 ׳ קוֺץ שׁ Isa 32:13.
2. adamant, as sharp, כְּתוּבָה ֗֗֗ בְּצִפֹּרֶן ׳ שׁ Jer 17:1 (|| בְּעֵט בַּדְוֶל); as hard, figurative of prophet's firmness Ezek 3:9; Zech 7:12 figurative of hard heart.
