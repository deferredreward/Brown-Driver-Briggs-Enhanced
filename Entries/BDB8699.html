שַׁלְוָה noun feminine quietness, ease; — absolute ׳שׁ Ps 122:7 +; construct שַׁלְוִת Ezek 16:49; Prov 1:32 plural suffix שַׁלְוֺתַ֫יִךְ Jer 22:21(שַׁלְוָתֵךְ Vrss Gie); — quietnees, peace, Prov 17:1 (opposed to רִיב), Ps 122:7, (|| שָׁלוֺם); ease, careless security, Ezek 16:49 (שַׁלְוַת הַשְׁקֵט), Prov 1:32 prosperity, Jer 22:21; Dan 8:25; Dan 11:21; Dan 11:24 ׳בְּ in (time of) security (of sudden attack), if not unawares (Thes Ew Hi Ke and others); compare literally out of quiet, i.e. suddenly, unawares (e.g. 1 Macc 1:30 = ἐξάπινα), מִן שִׁלְיָא ᵑ7 Prov 3:25; Prov 6:15 ( = פִּתְאֹם), Ps 30:7 see [ שָׁ֫לוּ].
