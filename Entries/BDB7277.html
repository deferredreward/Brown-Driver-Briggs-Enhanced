קַב noun [masculine] kab (Late Hebrew id.; Syriac ; Talmud קַבָא); — a measure of capacity, Biblical Hebrew only dry measure, רֹבַע הַקַּב 2Kgs 6:25 1/4 kab; on size of קַב = 4 לֹג = 1/6 סְאָה = 1/6 הִין = approximately 2 litres see NowArchaeology i. 202 f. BenzArchaeology 182.
