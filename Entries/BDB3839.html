יָתֵד noun feminine Deut 23:14 peg, pin (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic ) — יָתֵד Deut 23:14 + 9 t.; construct יְתַד Judg 4:21; Judg 16:14 (where with article but see Ges«GKC:127»§ 127, R. 4 a); plural יְתֵדֹת Exod 38:20 construct יִתְדֹת Exod 27:19 + 4 t.; suffix וִיתֵידֹתַיִךְ Isa 54:2 יְתֵדֹתָיו Exod 27:19; Isa 33:20 וִיתֵדֹתֶיהָ Exod 39:40 וִיתֵדֹתָם Num 3:37; Num 4:32 — pin, peg, especially

a. tent-pin Judg 5:26; Judg 4:21 יְתַד הָאֹהֶל vJudg 4:22 read perhaps also Job 4:21 (figurative; compare II. יֶתֶר); often of tabernacle Exod 27:19 (twice in verse); Exod 35:18 (twice in verse); Exod 38:20; Exod 38:31; Exod 38:21 || מֵיתָרָיו Exod 39:40, compare Num 3:37 ; Num 4:32 (all P ); of Zion under figure of tent Isa 33:20; Isa 54:2 figurative of ruler as support of state Zech 10:4.
b. a peg for hanging Ezek 15:3 so figurative of Eliakim Isa 22:23; Isa 22:24 of secure position Ezra 9:8 (compare Arabic, see Thes Hi Hariri361 Der., of a king).
c. for digging Deut 23:14 (= spade).
d. the pin or stick used in beating up the woof in the loom Judg 16:14 a, see GFMPAOS, Oct. 1889, clxxvi. ff.; in היתד הּארג vb, del היתד (Idib and in comm. on the passage)
