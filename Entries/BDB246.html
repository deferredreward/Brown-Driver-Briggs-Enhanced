BDB245 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB247

אוּרִיָּה proper name, masculine (flame of Yah or my light is Yah see יָהּ).

1. Hittite husband of Bathsheba 2Sam 11:3f; 2Sam 23:39.
2. priest in reign of Ahaz Isa 8:2; 2Kgs 16:10f.
3. priest in time of Nehemiah Ezra 8:33; Neh 3:4; Neh 3:21; Neh 8:4.
