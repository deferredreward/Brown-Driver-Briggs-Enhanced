BDB7559 [H7131]

Biblical Hebrew

קָרֵב verbal adjective approaching; — ׳ ק 1Sam 17:41 +; plural קְרִבִים Ezek 40:46; Ezek 45:4 — approaching:

1. as predicate, אֶלדָּֿוִד ׳ הֹלֵח וְק 1Sam 17:14, אַתֶּס קְרֵבִים לַמִּלְחָמָה Deut 20:8 abs ׳ הָלוֺח וְק 2Sam 18:25 c, article as substantive, 1Kgs 5:7 (+ אֶלוֺשֻׁלְחָן), Num 17:28 (twice in verse) (+ אֶלֿ ׳ מִשְׁכַּן י).
2. with article approaching ׳ י temple or tabernacle, for service, with לְשָׁרֵת Exod 40:46 (+ ׳ אֶליֿ), Exod 45:4 absolute ׳ הַוָּר הַקּ Num 1:51; Num 3:10; Num 3:38; Num 18:7.
