BDB206 [H362]

BDB205 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB207

[אֵילִים], always אֵילִם proper name [of a location] (= place of terebinths or other great trees, see Di Gen 14:6 & Exod 15:27 (12 fountains & 70 palms)) 2nd station of Israel in desert after passing sea Exod 16:1 (twice in verse); Num 33:9; Num 33:10 אֵי֫לִמָה Exod 15:27; Num 33:9 probably = Wady Gharandel compare RobBR i. 100, 105.
