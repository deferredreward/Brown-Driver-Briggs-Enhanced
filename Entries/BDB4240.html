BDB4240 [H3791]

BDB4239 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4241

כְּתָב noun masculine Ezra 4:7 writing (late Aramaic; Biblical Aramaic כְּתָב Syriac ); — ׳ כ absolute 1Chr 28:19 + 4 t.; construct Ezek 13:9 + 4 t.; suffix כְּתָבָהּ Esth 1:22 + 2 t.; כְּתָבָם Ezra 2:62 + 3 t.; — writing:

1. register, enrolement, Ezek 13:9; Ezra 2:62 = Neh 7:64.
2. = mode of writing, character, letter, הִנִּשְׁתְּוָן כָּתוּב אֲרָמִית ׳ וּכ Ezra 4:7 and the writing of the letter was written in Aramaic (characters; so probably, see BeRy Ryle, compare Late Hebrew HoffmZAW i.(1881), 334 ff.); compare Esth 1:22; Esth 3:12; Esth 8:9 (twice in verse).
3. = letter, 2Chr 2:10; Esth 9:27.
4. of a royal enactment, edict 2Chr 35:4 (|| מִכְתָּב), Esth 3:14; Esth 8:8; Esth 8:13 more fully כְּתָבהַֿדָּת Esth 4:8.
5. of a writing with divine authority 1Chr 28:19 אֱמֶת ׳ כ Dan 10:21, i.e. a book of truth.
