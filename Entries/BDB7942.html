רְכוּשׁ, רְכֻשׁ noun masculine Gen 13:6 property, goods; — absolute רְכוּשׁ Num 16:32 +, רְכֻשׁ Gen 14:16; Gen 14:21 construct רְכוּשׁ 2Chr 35:7, רְכֻשׁ Gen 14:11 suffix רְכֻשׁוֺ Gen 14:12 +, רְכוּשָׁם Gen 12:5 +, etc.; — property, goods, Proverbs, Chronicles, Daniel, Gen 14:

1. general term for movable possessions of all kinds (often specifically including cattle), Gen 12:5; Gen 15:4; Num 16:32; 1Chr 27:31; 1Chr 28:1; 2Chr 31:3; 2Chr 32:29; Ezra 8:21; Ezra 10:8.
2. used specifically of cattle, flocks, etc. Gen 13:6; Gen 31:18; Gen 36:7; Num 35:3; 2Chr 35:7.
3. of stores, utensils, etc. Gen 46:6; Ezra 1:4; Ezra 1:6, as camp-baggage Dan 11:13, especially as booty Gen 14:11; Gen 14:12; Gen 14:16 (twice in verse); Gen 14:21; 2Chr 20:25; 2Chr 21:14; 2Chr 21:17; Dan 11:24; Dan 11:28.

רָם, רֻם see דום . רֵם see רְאֵם .
