BDB2960 [H2611]

Biblical Hebrew

חָנֵף adjective profane, irreligious (Syriac profane, hence often Healthen, apostate; Arabic inclining to a right state, especially the true religion a Muslim) — absolute ׳ ח Job 8:13 + 10 t.; plural חֲנֵפִים Isa 33:14 חַנְפֵי Job 36:13; Ps 35:16 — profane, godless: of persons, ׳ אָדָם ח Job 34:30 ׳ גּוֺי ח Isa 10:6 as substantive godless man Isa 9:16 (|| מרע) Job 8:13; Job 13:16; Job 17:8; Job 20:5 ; Job 27:8; Prov 11:9 collective ׳ עֲדַת ח Job 15:34 plural Isa 33:14 חַנְפֵילֵֿב Job 36:13 בְּחַנְפֵי לַעֲגֵי מָעוֺג Ps 35:16 as profane men, mockers for cake (i.e. table-jesters; on construct see Ges § 130. 5), but text dubious (see Checritical note Bae).
