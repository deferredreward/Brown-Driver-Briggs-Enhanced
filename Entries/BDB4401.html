BDB4400 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4402

[לָחַשׁ] verb whisper, charm (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic לְחַשׁ ; Ethiopic ; whisper, see RSJPh xiv. 1885, 122, possibly originally serpent-charming); —

Pi`el Participle plural קוֺל מְלַחֲשִׁים Ps 58:6 voice of whisperers (i.e. serpent-charmers; || חוֺבֵר תֲבָרִים). Hithpa`el whisper together, Imperfect יִתְלַחֲשׁוּ with עַל Ps 41:8 Participle plural מִתְלַחֲשִׁים 2Sam 12:19.
