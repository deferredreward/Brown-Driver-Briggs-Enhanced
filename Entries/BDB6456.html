ע֫שֶׁק noun masculine Eccl 7:7 oppression, extortion; — ׳ ע absolute Jer 6:6 +, construct Ps 119:134; Eccl 5:7 —

1. Jer 6:6; Jer 22:17; Ezek 22:7; Ezek 22:12; Ps 73:8 with genitive subject Ps 119:134, object Eccl 5:7 as accusative of cognate meaning with verb with עָשַׁק (q. v.) Ezek 18:18; Ezek 22:29.
2. national oppression, Isa 54:14.
3. concrete, gain of extortion. Ps 62:11, so perhaps Eccl 7:7 as accusative of cognate meaning with verb Lev 5:23 (|| גָּזֵל). — Isa 30:12; Isa 59:13 read עִקֵּשׁ Lag Gr and others
