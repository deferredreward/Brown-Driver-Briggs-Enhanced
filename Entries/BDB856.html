BDB855 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB857

אִתַּי proper name, masculine (perhaps from אֵת with = companionable) —

1. one of David's captains, a native of Gath 2Sam 15:19; 2Sam 15:21; 2Sam 15:22 (twice in verse); 2Sam 18:2; 2Sam 18:5; 2Sam 18:12.
2. one of David's 30 mighty men, a Benjamite 2Sam 23:29 (in 1Chr 11:31 אִתַי).
