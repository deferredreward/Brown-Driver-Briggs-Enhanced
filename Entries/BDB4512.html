BDB4511 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4513

I. מִדָּה noun feminine measure, measurement, stature, size, garment (mostly late) — ׳ מ Exod 26:2 + 35 t.; construct מִדַּת Ezek 40:21 + 2 t.; plural מִדּוֺת Ezek 41:17 + 12 t.; מִדּוֺתָיו Ps 133:2 מִדּוֺתֶיהָ Ezek 48:16 —

1. measure, act of measurement, i.e. long measure Ezek 41:17 (strike out Co), Ezek 42:15; Ezek 48:30; Ezek 48:33 compare Josh 3:4 ( P ), 1Chr 23:29, see also Lev 19:35 ( H ); once of liquid measure, figurative, Job 28:25 ׳ קַו֯ה הַמּ Jer 31:39 measuring-line; ׳ חֶבֶל מ Zech 2:5 ( id.); ׳ קְנֵה הַמּ measuring-reed Ezek 40:3; Ezek 40:5; Ezek 42:16 (strike out Co), Ezek 42:16 Ezek 42:17 Ezek 42:18 Ezek 42:19 .
2. measurement, size of curtains in tabernacle Exod 26:2; Exod 26:8; Exod 36:9; Exod 36:15 (all P ); compare 1Kgs 6:25; 1Kgs 7:37 (both || קֶצֶב), 1Kgs 7:9 1Kgs 7:11; 2Chr 3:3; Ezek 40:10 (twice in verse); Ezek 40:21; Ezek 40:22; Ezek 40:24; Ezek 40:28; Ezek 40:29; Ezek 40:32; Ezek 40:33; Ezek 40:35 ; Ezek 43:13 ; Ezek 46:22; Ezek 48:16 once of time, מִדַּת יָמַי Ps 39:5 probably also Job 11:9 (figurative of God's wisdom) where מִדָּהּ = מִדָּתָהּ, compare Sta§ 347, approximately 2; בֵּית מִדּוֺת = house of size Jer 22:14 אִישׁ מִדָּה = man of size, stature, 1Chr 11:23; 1Chr 20:6 so also probably || 2Sam 21:20 (for HCT מדין, Qr מָדוֺן, see [ מַד ]), We Dr Kit Bu; plural Num 13:32; Isa 45:14.
3. measured portion, extent, stretch (of wall) Neh 3:11; Neh 3:19; Neh 3:20; Neh 3:21; Neh 3:24; Neh 3:27; Neh 3:30, compare Ezek 45:3.
4. garment (plural poetic) Ps 133:2.
