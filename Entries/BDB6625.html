BDB6625 [H6388]

Biblical Hebrew

I. פֶּ֫לֶג noun masculine Ps 65:10 (cleft) channel, (artificial) canal; — usually plural פְּלָנִים Isa 30:25 ( + יִבְלֵימָֿ֑יִם); construct פַּלְגֵימַֿיִם Ps 1:3, simile Isa 32:2; Prov 21:1, metaphor Prov 5:16 , of tears Lam 3:48; Ps 119:136 פְּלָגָיו Ps 46:5 (i.e. irrigating canals from river, metaphor); פַּלְגֵישָֿׁ֑מֶן Job 29:6 (figurative of prosperity); singular פֶּלֶג אֱלֹהִים Ps 65:10, poetic of channel for rain (compare Job 38:25).
