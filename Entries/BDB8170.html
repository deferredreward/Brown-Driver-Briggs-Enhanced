BDB8169 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8171

שָׂדַי noun masculine Ps 96:12 field, land (rare original form of שָׂדֶה (q. v. below), only in poetry); — absolute ׳ שׂ Jer 4:17, שָׂדָ֑י Hos 10:4 +; —

1. cultivated field Hos 12:12, also Hos 10:4 (si vera lectio, but see Now), yielding food Deut 32:13; Lam 4:9 ׳ שֹׁמְרֵי שׂ Jer 4:17 keepers, watchmen of a field.
2. home of wild beasts: ׳ בְּהֵמוֺת שׂ Ps 8:8; Joel 2:22, ׳ חַיְתוֺ שׂ Isa 56:9; Ps 104:11, ׳ זִזִ שׂ Ps 50:11; Ps 80:14.
3. plain, opposite mountain, Jer 18:14 (but dubious, Co Du שִׂרְיֹן).
4. land, opposed to sea, Ps 96:12 (compare שָׂדֶה 3; || תֵּבֵל Ps 98:7).
