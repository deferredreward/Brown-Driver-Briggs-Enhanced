[זָחַח] verb remove, displace (Arabic push, thrust away; compare also Aramaic זוּחַ, , move, move away (intransitive)) —

Niph`al Imperfect וְלֹא יִזַּח הַחשֶׁן מֵעַל הָאֵפוֺד Exod 28:28 and that the breast-piece be not displaced etc., Exod 39:21 (both P).
