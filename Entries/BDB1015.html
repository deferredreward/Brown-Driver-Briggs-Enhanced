BDB1015 [H981]

BDB1014 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1016

[בָּטָה, בָּטָא] verb speak rashly, thoughtlessly (Late Hebrew id., בטי) —

Qal Participle בּוֺטֶה Prov 12:18 one that babbleth (opposed to לְשׁוֺן חֲכָמִים). Pi`el Imperfect יְבַטֵּא בִשְׁבוּעָה Lev 5:4 speak rashly, unadvisedly; so וַיְבַטֵּא בִשְׂפָתָיו (of Moses) Ps 106:33 Infinitive לְבַטֵּא בִשְׂפָתַיִם Lev 5:4 (P).
