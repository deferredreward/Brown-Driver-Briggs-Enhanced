BDB4138 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4140

כַּ֫עַס noun masculine vexation, anger; — ׳כ Deut 32:27 + 13 t.; כָּ֑עַס Prov 21:19; Eccl 1:18 suffix כַּעְסִי 1Sam 1:16, כַּעַסְךָ Ps 85:5, כַּעְסוֺ Prov 12:16; 1Kgs 15:30 plural כְּעָסִים 2Kgs 23:26. — vexation:

1. of men, especially caused by unmerited treatment, 1Sam 1:6; 1Sam 1:16; Prov 12:16; Prov 17:25; Prov 21:19; Prov 27:3; Eccl 7:9.
2. vexation, anger of Yahweh (caused especially by worship of other gods) 1Kgs 15:30; 1Kgs 21:22; 2Kgs 23:26 עִמָּנוּ ׳כ Ps 85:5 anger (of Yahweh) with us; בָּנָיו ׳כ Deut 32:19 anger against his sons, אוֺיֵב ׳כ vDeut 32:27 anger against the enemy (both object genitive see Di; RV provocation of, compare Dr); קָרְבָּנָם ׳כ Ezek 20:28 provocation of their offering (so RV but Co strike out, rightly).
3. vexation, grief Ps 6:8; Ps 10:14; Ps 31:10; Eccl 1:18; Eccl 2:23; Eccl 7:3; Eccl 11:10, also כעס Eccl 5:16 accusative Ew aI.
