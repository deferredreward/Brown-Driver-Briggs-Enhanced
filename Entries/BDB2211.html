הָפַךְ verb turn, overturn (Late Hebrew id., Aramaic הֲפַךְ, , Arabic WSG 47, Assyrian abâku, apâku DlW) —

Isa 29:16, see הֶפֶךְ ; Participle active הֹפֵךְ Amos 5:8 הַהֹפְכִי Ps 114:8 plural הַהֹפְכִים Amos 5:7 passive הֲפוּכָה Hos 7:8; Lam 4:6. —
1. transitive
a. turn, turn about, turn over, with accusative: e.g. turn the back (עֹרֶף) Josh 7:8 ( J E ); a dish (צַלַּחַת) עַלפָּֿנֶיהָ 2Kgs 21:13 the hand, & so the horses of a chariot 1Kgs 22:34 = 2Chr 18:33; 2Kgs 9:23 but also (followed by בְּ ) of hostility Lam 3:3 turn a cake (passive) Hos 7:8 of ׳ י, שָׂפָה בְּרוּרָה ׳ ה Zeph 3:9 i.e. restore speech of a pure kind, followed by אֶלעַֿמִּים; לֵב אַחֵר 1Sam 10:9 another heart, followed by לוֺ; turn a wind (רוּחַ; i.e. bring from different quarter) Exod 10:19 ( J ).
b. overturn, overthrow, followed by accusative Sodom, etc., Gen 19:21; Gen 19:25 ; Gen 19:29 ( J ), Deut 29:22; Jer 20:16; Lam 4:6 (compare ׳ מַהְפֵּכַת וגו Amos 4:11 see below; also הֲפֵכָה, [מַהְפֵּכָה]); city of Ammon 2Sam 10:3 = 1Chr 19:3 (object omitted); mountains Job 9:5; Job 28:9 throne Hag 2:22 (|| הִשְׁמִיד ), chariots Hag 2:22 tent (אֹהֶל), לְמַ֫עְלָה ׳ ה Judg 7:3 object earth (׳ ה = devastate) Job 12:15 wicked men Amos 4:11 (יֲמֹרָה הָפַכְּתִּי בָּכֶם כְּמַהְפֵּכַת אֱלֹהִים אֶתסְֿדֹם וְאֶתֿ) Prov 12:7; Job 34:25 (object omitted).
c. turn = change, transform (1) followed by accusative = alter; a colour (עֵינוֺ) Lev 13:55 ( P ); skin Jer 13:33 bed (מִשְׁכָּבוֺ see ׳ מ; Che his lying down) Ps 41:4 i.e. restore to health; (2) pervert דִבְרֵי אֱלֹהִים Jer 23:36 (3) followed by accusative with Infinitive Ps 105:25 he turned their heart to hate his people; (4) turn to, into , followed by two accusative Lev 13:10; Ps 114:8 rock into pool of water; usually followed by accusative + לְ, Ps 66:6 sea into dry land, Ps 78:44 rivers into blood, compare Ps 105:29; Jer 31:13 mourning into joy, so Ps 30:12; Amos 5:7 justice into wormwood, compare Amos 6:12; Amos 8:10 feasts into mourning; Deut 23:6 = Neh 13:2 curse into blessing; Amos 5:8 death-shade into morning.
2. intransitive
a. turn, turn back, Judg 20:39; Judg 20:41; 2Kgs 5:26; Ps 78:9 ; 2Chr 9:12 followed by לדרך 1Sam 25:12
b. turn = change, change into, followed by predicate adjective לָבָן ׳ ה turn white Lev 13:3; Lev 13:4; Lev 13:13; Lev 13:20 (all P ).
Niph`al Perfect נֶהְפַּךְ Exod 7:15 + 10 t.; נֶהְפָּ֑ךְ Job 20:14 וְנֶהְפַּךְ consecutive Lev 13:16 נֶהֶפְכָה Lam 5:2 3 masculine plural נֶהְפְּכוּ Job 19:19 + 3 t., נֶהֶפְכוּ 1Sam 4:19 ; Dan 10:16, וְנֶהֶפְכוּ consecutive Exod 7:17 ; Isa 34:9, etc.; Imperfect יֵהָפֵךְ Isa 60:5 ; Joel 3:4 וַיֵּהָפֵךְ Exod 14:5 ; Isa 63:10 2 masculine singular תֵּהָפֵךְ Job 30:21; Ezek 4:8 וַיֵּהָֽפְכוּ Exod 7:20 Infinitive absolute נַהֲפוֺךְ Esth 9:1 Participle נֶהְפָּךְ Prov 17:20 נֶהְפָּ֑כֶת Jonah 3:4 —
1. reflexive & intransitive
a. turn oneself, turn, turn back (compare Qal 2.a.) Josh 8:20 ( J E ); turn aside Ps 78:57 turn from side to side Ezek 4:8 pains turned upon (עַל) her 1Sam 4:19 (i.e. came suddenly upon her) compare Dan 10:16 turn against followed by בְּ Job 19:19 turn to (ward), followed by עַל , Isa 60:5 (|| בא לְ).
b. turn = change (oneself) followed by predicate adjective Lev 13:25 ( P ); followed by predicate noun (?), vine turns into degenerate plant Jer 2:21 no predicate expressed Job 20:14 (compare vb); of heart turned in compassion Hos 11:8 (|| נִכְמְרוּ נִחוּמָ֑י), in sorrow, distress, Lam 1:20 of heart changed, so as to be favourably disposed toward (followed by אֶל) Exod 14:5 ( J ) = change of mind; followed by לְ, of plague spots changing colour Lev 13:16; Lev 13:17 ( P ); rod changing to serpent Exod 7:15 ( P ); faces to paleness Jer 30:6 dance to mourning Lam 5:15 comeliness to corruption Dan 10:8 וַיֵּהָפֵךְ לָהֶם לְאוֺיֵב Isa 63:10; Job 30:21.
c. be perverse, only participle used substantive נֶהְפָּךְ בִּלְשׁוֺנוֺ Prov 17:20 he that is perverse with his tongue.
2. passive,
a. be turned, turned over to followed by לְ, an inheritance to strangers Lam 5:2.
b. be reversed Esth 9:1.
c. be turned, changed, followed by לְ, waters into blood Exod 7:17; Exod 7:20 ( E ); Saul into another man 1Sam 10:6 streams into pitch Isa 34:9 sun to darkness Joel 3:4 stones to chaff Job 41:20 month changed from sorrow to gladness Esth 9:22 compare Ps 32:4 where complement omitted

e. be upturned, of subterran. work of miners Job 28:5 = there is an upturning.
