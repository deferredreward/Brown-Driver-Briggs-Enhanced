BDB1207 [H1158]

Biblical Hebrew

[בָּעָה] verb inquire, cause to swell or boil up (Late Hebrew id; Arabic seek, suppurate, swell; Aramaic בְּעָא, seek) —

Qal Imperfect 3 feminine singular תִּבְעֶה Isa 64:1 2 masculine plural תִּבְעָיוּן Isa 21:12 Imperative בְּעָיוּ Isa 21:12 —
1. of rising desire, seek, inquire, absolute, of inquiring of prophet Isa 21:12 (twice in verse).
2. cause to boil up, אֵֿשׁ ׳ מַיִם תִּב Isa 64:1 (but gloss Che).
Niph`al Perfect נִבְעוּ Obad 1:6 searched out (|| נֶחְפְּשׂוּ); Participle נִבְעֶה Isa 30:13 swelling, swelling out (of decaying wall; Di swelling, enlarging, of crack in wall).
