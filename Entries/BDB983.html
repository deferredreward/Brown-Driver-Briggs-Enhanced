בּ֫שֶׁת noun feminine shame Job 8:22 + 20 t.; suffix בָּשְׁתִּי Ps 69:20 + 7 t.

1. shame 1Sam 20:30 (twice in verse); Ps 40:16; Ps 69:20; Ps 70:4; Isa 30:3; Isa 30:5; Isa 61:7; Jer 2:26; Jer 3:25; Jer 20:18; Hab 2:10; Zeph 3:5; Zeph 3:19 לָבַשׁ בּשֶׁת clothe with shame Job 8:22; Ps 35:26; Ps 132:18 || עטה בשׁת Ps 109:29 בּשֶׁת (ה)פָּנִים shame (or confusion) of face 2Chr 32:21; Ezra 9:7; Dan 9:7; Dan 9:8, compare Ps 44:16; Jer 7:19 בּשֶׁת עֲלוּמֶיךָ shame of thy youth Isa 54:4 עֶרְיָהבֿשֶׁת nakedness (that is) shame Mic 1:11 compare Ps 45:5 בּשֶׁת עֶרְוַת אמך 1Sam 20:30.
2. shameful thing, substituted for בַּעַל (q. v.) by later editors, Hos 9:10; Jer 3:24 מִזְבְּחוֺת לַבּשֶׁת Jer 11:13, compare יְרֻבֶּשֶׁת 2Sam 11:21 = יְרֻבַּעַל Judg 6:32 אִישׁבּֿשֶׁת 2Sam 2:8 = אֶשְׁבַּעַל 1Chr 8:33.
