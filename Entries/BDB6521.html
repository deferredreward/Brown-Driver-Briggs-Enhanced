[פָּגַר] verb Pi`el be exhausted, faint (ᵑ7 פְּגַר derived species tear down, destroy; Assyrian pagru, body, corpse; Late Hebrew פֶּגֶר = Biblical Hebrew; Aramaic פַּגְרָא, ; Palmyrene פגר; Mandean פאגרא NöM § 89); — Perfect 3 plural פִּגְּרוּ מִן they were too faint to go, etc. 1Sam 30:10; 1Sam 30:21.
