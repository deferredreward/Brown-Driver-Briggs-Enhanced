[פָּלָה] verb

Niph`al be separated, distinct (|| form of פלא q. v.; compare Arabic , separate from sucking, wean; Ethiopic separate, distinguish); —
1. Perfect 1 plural consecutive וְנִפְלִינוּ Exod 33:16 and we shall be distinct, with מִן person
2. 1 singular נִפְלֵיתִי Ps 139:14 I am wonderful (namely in bodily stature etc.); < Vrss Hupfeld Now Bae We תָ- thou [׳ י] art wonderful (פלא 3).
Hiph`il Perfect 3 masculine singular הִפְלָה Ps 4:4, ׳ וְה consecutive Exod 9:4 1 singular וְהִפְלֵיתִי consecutive Exod 8:18 Imperfect 3 masculine singular יַפְלֶה Exod 11:7 Imperative masculine singular הַפְלֵה Ps 17:7 — of ׳ י, make separate, with accusative Exod 8:18 (by treating differently); set apart Ps 4:4, but perhaps read חֶסֶד לִי (for Mt חָסִיד לוֺ), hath made wonderful (his) kindness to me (Dy Gr Che We Du [Bae 'perhaps']), so Ps 17:7 (compare Ps 31:22); distinguish between, בֵּין thing, Exod 9:4, בֵּין person Exod 11:7.
