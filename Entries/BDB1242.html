BDB1242 [H1198]

BDB1241 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1243

בַּ֫עַר noun masculine brutishness (only in poetry) — absolute ׳ ב Ps 49:11 + 3 t., בָּ֑עַר Prov 12:1 — combination, ׳ אִישׁבֿ brutish man Ps 92:7 (|| כסיל); elsewhere ׳ ב alone in same sense (concrete) Ps 49:11 (|| id.), & as predicate = adjective, Ps 73:22 ; Prov 12:1 ; Prov 30:2.
