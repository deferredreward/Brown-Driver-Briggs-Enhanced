קַל adjective light, swift, fleet; — masculine singular ׳ק Amos 2:15 +, קָ֑ל vAmos 2:14 feminine singular קַלָּה Jer 2:23 plural קַלִּים Isa 18:2+; — קַל בְּרַגְלָיו light with his feet Amos 2:15; 2Sam 2:18 ׳ק, alone, swift, messengers Isa 18:2, pursuers Lam 4:19 (מִן compare), camel Jer 2:23, cloud Isa 19:1 of swift vanishing of wicked Job 24:18 (Bu קַלּוּ for קַל הוּא); as substantive Amos 2:14; Jer 46:6; Eccl 9:11, = swift (horse) Isa 30:16 קַל as adverb, swiftly (+ מְהֵרָה) Isa 5:26; Joel 4:4.
