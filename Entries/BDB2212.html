Biblical Hebrew

הֶ֫פֶךְ noun masculine the contrary, contrariness, perversity; — absolute ׳ ה Ezek 16:34 הֶ֑פֶךְ Ezek 16:34 suffix הַפְכְּכֶם Isa 29:16 (see Baer); —

1. the contrary, opposite thing וַיְהִיבָֿךְ הֶפֶךְ מִןהַֿנָּשִׁים Ezek 16:34 & there hath occurred in thee the contrary from other women; Ezek 16:34 וַתְּהִי לְהֶ֑פֶךְ so thou hast become the contrary.
2. הַפְכְּכֶם Isa 29:16 Oh, your perversity!
