פָּגַע verb meet, encounter, reach (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic פְּגַע, ; possibly akin to Arabic , happen to, light upon; is pain, afflict); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ פ 1Kgs 2:32 +, suffix וּפְגָעוֺ consecutive Amos 5:19, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּפְגַּע Gen 28:11+; 1 plural נְפְגַּע Job 21:15, etc.; Imperative masculine singular פְּגַע Judg 8:21+; masculine plural פִּגְעוּ Gen 23:8 Infinitive construct לִפְגֹּע 1Sam 22:17, suffix פִּגְעוֺ Num 35:19; Num 35:21 —
1. meet, light upon, with accusative of person 1Sam 10:5; Exod 5:20 ( J ), with suffix person Amos 5:19 שׁוֺר ׳ פ Exod 23:4 with ב person Gen 32:2 ( E ), Num 35:19; Num 35:21 ( P ); with ב location Gen 28:11 ( E ).
2. meet, with kindness, with accusative of person Isa 64:5 (subject ׳ י), so Isa 47:3 according to Ew De Di and others; CheHpt אֶפְרַע (reading, with Du, אמר for אָדָם).
3. encounter with hostility, fall upon with ב person Josh 2:16 ( J E ), Judg 8:21; Judg 15:12; Judg 18:25; 1Sam 22:17; 1Sam 22:18 (twice in verse); 2Sam 1:15; 1Kgs 2:25 ; 1Kgs 2:29 ; 1Kgs 2:31; 1Kgs 2:32; 1Kgs 2:34; 1Kgs 2:46; Ruth 2:22 of God, with accusative (suffix), + ב instrumental Exod 5:3.
4. encounter with request, entreat, with ב person Jer 7:16; Job 21:15 , followed by clause Jer 27:18; Ruth 1:16, + ל person on whose behalf Gen 23:8
5. strike, touch, of boundary, with ב location Josh 16:7; Josh 19:11; Josh 19:22 ; Josh 19:26; Josh 19:27; Josh 19:34 (twice in verse), compare Josh 17:10 with אֶל location Josh 19:11.
