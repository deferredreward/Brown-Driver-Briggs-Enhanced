עָנִי adj poor, afflicted, humble; — ׳ע Deut 24:12 + 51 t.; plural עֲיִּים Isa 3:15 + 6 t.; construct עֲנִיֵּי Isa 10:2 + 4 t. (see also Kt and Qr below עָנָו above); suffix עֲנִיֶּיךָ Ps 72:2; Ps 74:19, עֲנִיֶּ֑ךָ Deut 15:11, עֲנִיָּו Isa 49:13 feminine singular עֲנִיָּה Isa 51:21; Isa 54:11 ( + Isa 10:30 ᵑ0, but read עֲנִיהָ, see I. ענה); —

1. poor, needy, || אביון Deut 15:11; Deut 24:14. 15 Prov 31:20, || יתום Job 24:9; Exod 22:24 (E), Deut 24:12 having right to gleanings Lev 19:10; Lev 23:22 (H); ׳יְמֵי ע Prov 15:15, compare Prov 14:21 (Kt).
2. poor and weak, oppressed by the rich and powerful Isa 3:14; Isa 3:15; Isa 32:7 (Qr), Isa 58:7; Isa 29:12; Isa 36:6; Isa 36:15; Prov 30:14; Eccl 6:8; Ezek 18:17; Zech 7:10; Job 24:4, also Amos 8:4 (Qr); || דל Job 34:28; Prov 22:22 עֲנִיֵּי עַמִּי Isa 10:2 עני ואביוך Job 24:14; Prov 31:9; Jer 22:16; Ezek 16:49; Ezek 18:12; Ezek 22:29.
3. poor, weak, afflicted Israel, or pious in Israel afflicted by wicked nations or the wicked in Israel itself Ps 10:2; Ps 10:9 (twice in verse); Ps 14:6 (dubious, compare Ps 53:6) Ps 102:1 (or proper name), Isa 14:32; Hab 3:14 of Zion, עֲנִיָּה Isa 51:21; Isa 54:11 ׳שׁד ע Ps 12:6 ׳יחיד וע Ps 25:16 עני ואביון Ps 35:10; Ps 37:14; Ps 40:18 = Ps 70:6; Ps 74:21; Ps 86:1; Ps 109:16; Ps 109:22, plural Isa 41:17 עני וְכוֺאֵב Ps 69:30 וָרָשׁ ׳ע Ps 82:3 ׳עֱנוּת ע Ps 22:25 וְגֹוֵעַ ׳ע Ps 88:16 || דל Isa 26:6; Zeph 3:12 וּנְכֵהרֿוּחַ ׳ע Isa 66:2 — Zech 11:7; Zech 11:11 see II. כְּנַעֲנִי; — God does not forget them Ps 9:13 (Kt); Ps 9:19 (Qr); Ps 10:12 (Kt) Ps 74:19, but has compassion on them Isa 49:13, saves Ps 34:7, delivers Ps 35:10, and bestows various favours Ps 68:11; Ps 140:13, the king also judges Ps 72:2; Ps 72:4, and delivers Ps 72:12.
4. humble, lowly, Zech 9:9 (victorious king); opposed to לֵצִים Prov 3:34 (Kt); opposed to גֵּאִים Prov 16:19 (Kt); עם עני opposed to רמות עינים Ps 18:28 = 2Sam 22:28.
