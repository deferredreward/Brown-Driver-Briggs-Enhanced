I. פַּ֫חַד49 noun masculine1Sam 11:7 dread; — ׳פ absolute Gen 31:42 +, construct 1Sam 11:7 +, suffix פַּחְדְּךָ Deut 2:25; Ps 119:120, פַּחְדּוֺ Job 13:11; 1Chr 14:17, etc.; plural פְּחָדִים Job 15:21: —

1. dread, ׳י׳פ, i.e. before ׳י, often (not always) terrifying, unfitting for action, Isa 2:10; Isa 2:19; Isa 2:21; 1Sam 11:7; 2Chr 14:13; 2Chr 17:10, so אֱהִֹים ׳פ 2Chr 20:29, with suffix Job 13:11; Ps 119:120 trembleth for dread of thee; with genitive object Israel, Deut 2:25 (|| יִרְאָה), Deut 11:25 (|| מוֺרָא), Ps 105:38; Esth 8:17; Esth 9:2, אוֺיֵב ׳פ Ps 64:2, of individual 1Chr 14:17; Esth 9:3 with genitive object of thing Prov 1:33 absolute, in general, Exod 15:16 (song; || אֵימָ֫תָה), Job 4:14 (|| רְעָדָה), Jer 30:5 (|| קוֺל חֲרָדָה) +; as accusative of congnate meaning with verb with פָּחַד Ps 14:5 + (see פָּחַד); c. genitive subject Prov 1:26; Prov 1:27 (both || אֵיד) Deut 28:67.
2. = object of dread Ps 31:12; Ps 36:2, אֵלַי ׳פ Job 31:23 פַּחַד פָּחַדְתִּי Job 3:25 מִקּוֺל הַפַּחַד Isa 24:18 sound of the disaster, for ׳מִפְּנֵי הַפּ of original Jer 48:44, but קוֺל פְּחָדִים Job 15:21 = a sound of terrors; ׳פ פִּתְאֹם Job 22:10; Prov 3:25.
3. as designation of deity יִצְחָק ׳פ Gen 31:42 (|| אֱהֵֹי אַבְרָהָם), so vGen 31:53 (|| id.; both E).
