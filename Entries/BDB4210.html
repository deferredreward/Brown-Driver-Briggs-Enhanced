BDB4210 [H3753]

Biblical Hebrew

[כִּרְכָּרָה] noun feminine dromedary (according to Ki Thes; perhaps from constantly repeated undulating movement); — only plural absolute וּבַכִּרְכָּרוֺת Isa 66:20 (+ בַּסּוּסִים וּבָרֶכֶב וּבַצַּבִּים וּבַפְּרָדִים; the whole list is a gloss according to Du CheIntr Is.).
