BDB3532 [H4315]

Biblical Hebrew

(מֵיטָב) noun [masculine] the best, only construct מֵיטַב and as superlative; the best of a thing: ׳ שָׂדֵהוּ וּמ ׳ מ כָּרְמוֺ Exod 22:4 ( J E ) the best of his field and the best of his vineyard (choicest fruit); הַצּאֹן ׳ מ the best of the sheep 1Sam 15:9; 1Sam 15:15 הָאָרֶץ ׳ בְּמ in the best of the land Gen 47:6; Gen 47:11 ( P ).

יֻטָּה, יוּטָה see below נטה.

יְטוּר see below טוּר.
