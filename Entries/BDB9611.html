BDB9610 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9612

[כְּלַל] verb complete (see Biblical Hebrew I. [כָּלַל]; but ׳ שַׁכ perhaps loan-word from Assyrian šuklulu , ušaklil, uštaklil, DIWB 331, compare שֵׁיוֵב, שֵׁיצֵא and Buhl14); —

Shaph`el of building: Perfect 3 masculine singular suffix שַׁכְלְלֵהּ Ezra 5:11 he finished it; 3 masculine plural שַׁכְלִלוּ Ezra 6:14, also (with accusative of thing) Ezra 4:12 ( Kt שׁודי אשׁכללו, read Qr ) ׳ שׁוּרַיָא שׁ; Infinitive לְהַשְׁכְּלָלָה, accusative of thing Ezra 5:3; Ezra 5:9.
Ishtaph. be completed, of walls; Imperfect 3 masculine plural יִשְׁתַּכְלְלוּן Ezra 4:13; Ezra 4:16.
