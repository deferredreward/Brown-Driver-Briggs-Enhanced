תּוֺכַ֫חַת noun feminine argument, reproof; — ׳ת Prov 10:17 + 9 t.; suffix תּוֺכַחְתִּי Hab 2:1 + 5 t.; תּוֺכַחְתּוֺ Prov 3:11 plural תּוֺכָחוֺת Ps 38:15 + 3 t.; construct תּוֺכְחוֺת Prov 6:23; Ezek 25:17 תֹּכְחוֺת Ezek 5:15 (but latter strike out Co; given by SS under תּוֺכֵחָה); —

1. argument, impeachment, spoken by lips and mouth Ps 38:15; Job 13:6; Job 23:4; Hab 2:1.
2. reproof, chiding Prov 1:23; Prov 27:5 || עצה Prov 1:25; Prov 1:30 || מוסר Prov 3:11; Prov 5:12; Prov 10:17; Prov 12:1; Prov 13:18; Prov 15:5; Prov 15:10; Prov 15:32 מוסר ׳ת Prov 6:23 reporoofs for discipline; חיים ׳ת Prov 15:31 reproof that giveth life; אישׁ תוכחוֺת Prov 29:1 man of reproofs (who deserves them).
3. correction, rebuke Ps 39:12; Ps 73:14 שֵׁבֶט ׳ות Prov 29:15 חֵמָה ׳תּ Ezek 5:15; Ezek 25:17.
