BDB9067 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB9069

שֵׁשַׁךְ proper name [of a territory] = בָּבֶל (Jerome Rabb and modern; by Atbaš (׳ בש ׳ את), i.e. disguising name by substituting last letter of alphabet for first, next last for second, etc., compare Thes); — Jer 51:41 and hence (perhaps gloss) Jer 25:26 (see Gie).
