I. חָרַץ verb cut, sharpen, decide (Late Hebrew id., cut in, decide, Aramaic in derivatives; Assyrian —arâƒu , dig, decide, —ariƒu , trench, ZehnpfBAS i. 502 ; Phoenician חרץ decision, HoffmAGG xxxvi, May 1889, 11) —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ ח Josh 10:21 חָרָ֑צְתָּ 1Kgs 20:40 Imperfect יֶחֱרַץֿ Exod 11:7 2 masculine singular תֶּחֱרָ֑ץ 2Sam 5:24 Passive participle חָרוּץ Isa 10:22; Lev 22:22 plural חֲרוּצִים Job 14:5 (see also חָרוּץ below); —
1. cut, mutilate Lev 22:22 ( עַוֶּרֶת אוֺ שָׁבוּר אוֺחָֿרוּץ אוֺ יַבֶּלֶת).
2. sharpen, figurative the tongue, כֶּֿלֶב לְשֹׁנוֺ לְ ׳ לֹא יֶח Exod 11:7 ( J ), i.e. utter no sound against Israel, Josh 10:21.
3. decide 1Kgs 20:40 (absolute); so participle pass חרוצים ימיו Job 14:5 his days are determined, fixed (|| מִסְפַּר חדשׁיו), Isa 10:22 ׳ כִּלָּיוֺן ח; act with decision 2Sam 5:24.
Niph`al Participle נֶחֱרָצָה Isa 10:23 + 3 t.; נֶחֱרֶצֶת Dan 9:26 (both these forms infinitive construct according to BaNB 90 ); decisive ׳ כָּלָה וְנֶח Isa 10:23 ; Isa 28:22; Dan 9:27 a consumption and strict decision (i.e. that which is strictly determined), נֶעֱשָׂ֑תָה ׳ כי נ Dan 11:36 שֹׁמֵמוֺת ׳ נח Dan 9:26 strict determining of desolation.
