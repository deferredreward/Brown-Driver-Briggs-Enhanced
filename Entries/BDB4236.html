BDB4235 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4237

כִּשְׁרוֺן noun [masculine] skill, success; —

1. skill, בְּחָכְמָה וּבְדַעַת וּבְכִשְׁר֑וֺן Eccl 2:21 construct כָּלכִּֿשְׁרוֺן הַמַּעֲשֶׂה Eccl 4:4 all skill of work = skilful work.
2. success, profit, וּמַהכִּֿשְׁרוֺן לִבְעָלָיהָ Eccl 5:10 and what profit have the owners of it ?
