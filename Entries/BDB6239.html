Biblical Hebrew

[עָנַק] verb denominative serve as necklace; —

Qal 3 feminine singular suffix עֲנָקַתְמוֺ Ps 73:6 pride is necklace for them.
Hiph`il Imperfect and Infinitive absolute הַעֲנֵיק תַּעֲנִיק לוֺ Deut 15:14 thou shalt make a rich necklace for him from thy flock, etc., figurative for richly load him.
