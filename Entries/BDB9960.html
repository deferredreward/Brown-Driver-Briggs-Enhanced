BDB9960 [H7990]

Biblical Aramaic

שַׁלִּיט adjective having mastery, ruling; — absolute ׳ שׁ Ezra 7:24 +, emphatic אָ֯ Dan 2:15 masculine plural שְׁלִיטִין Ezra 4:20, מִן- Dan 4:23 —

1. having, exercising, mastery:
a. of God, followed by ב, Dan 4:14; Dan 4:22; Dan 4:29; Dan 5:21 absolute, of heavens Dan 4:23
b. of kings Ezra 4:20 (ב).
2. as noun = ruler Dan 2:10, captain Dan 2:15.
3. having authority to do a thing (Infinitive), hence impersonal = it is authorized , Ezra 7:24 (so often in Syriac; compare S-CA 11 + אנת שליט למבנה).
