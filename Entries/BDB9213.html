אֶתְנַן noun masculineEzek 16:34 hire of harlot ( = אֶתְנָה); — absolute ׳א Ezek 16:34; Ezek 16:41, אֶתְנָ֑ן Hos 9:1 +; construct אֶתְנַן Deut 23:19 +; suffix אֶתְנַנָּהּ Isa 23:18, נַָּ֯הֿ vIsa 23:17 plural suffix אֶתְנַנֶּיהָ Mic 1:7a (We proposes אֲשֵׁרֶיהָ, compare Now Marti); — harlot's hire, Deut 23:19 (זוֺנָה ׳א); elsewhere in figure: of idolatrous Israel Hos 9:1, Jerusalem Ezek 16:31; Ezek 16:34 (twice in verse); Ezek 16:41, Tyre Isa 23:17; Isa 23:18 of costly idols of Samaria Mic 1:7 b. 7 c, compare vMic 1:7 a (see above).
