BDB2942 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2944

חָנָן proper name, masculine (gracious; compare Sabean חנן, Phoenician חנא) —

1. one of the warriors of David 1Chr 11:43.
2. one of the heads of families of Nethinim Ezra 2:46 = Neh 7:49.
3. names of Levites Neh 8:7; Neh 10:11; Neh 13:3.
4. chiefs of the people:
\ \ a. Neh 10:23.
\ \ b. Neh 10:27.
5. Benjamite name 1Chr 8:23; 1Chr 8:38; 1Chr 9:44 (compare proper name, of a location בֵּית חָנָן, Mishna כְּפַר חָנָן).
6. אִישׁ הָאֱלֹהִים head of a prophetic guild, בְּנֵי חָנָן Jer 35:4.
