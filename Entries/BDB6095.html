Biblical Hebrew

[עָלַל] verb denominative

Po`el. glean (grapes or olives; לִפֵּט of grain); — Imperfect 2 masculine singular תְּעוֺלֵל Lev 19:10 (object כֶּרֶם; || תְּלַפֵּט), Deut 24:21 (absolute, + אַחֲרֶיךָ); 3 masculine plural figurative (+ Infinitive absolute) עוֺלֵל יְעוֺלְלוּ כַגֶּפֶן שְׁאֵרִית יִשְׂרָאֵל Jer 6:9 וַיְעֹלְלֻהוּ Judg 20:45 they made a gleaning of them (i. e. smote the fugitives).
