BDB3000 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3002

חוֺף noun [masculine] shore, coast (as surrounding, enclosing) — ׳ ח only construct Gen 49:13 (twice in verse) + 5 t.: — shore of sea הַיָּם ׳ ח Deut 1:7; Josh 9:1 (both D ), Judg 5:17 ; Jer 47:7; Ezek 25:16 יַמִּים ׳ ח Gen 49:13 (poem); ׳ ח אֳנִיּוֺת Gen 49:13 the shore of ships, i.e. to which ships come.
