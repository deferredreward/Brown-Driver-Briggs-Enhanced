[אַוָּה] noun feminine desire — construct אַוַּת Deut 12:15 + 5 t.; suffix אַוָּתִי Hos 10:10 — desire, will, usually followed by נֶפֶשׁ; of natural human desire (morally indifferent), for meat Deut 12:15; Deut 12:20; Deut 12:21 of longing for sanctuary Deut 18:6 of royal good pleasure 1Sam 23:20 without נֶפֶשׁ , of wild-ass Jer 2:24 of divine will Hos 10:10.
