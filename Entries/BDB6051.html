BDB6051 [H5908]

BDB6050 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6052

עַכָּבִישׁ noun masculine spider ( ᵑ7 עַכּוּבִיתָא, עַכָּבִיתָא; whence Arabic as loan-word; Late Hebrew עַכְשׁוּב probably tarantula, according to LevyNHWB iii.648); — ׳ בֵּית ע Job 8:14 i.e. web, so probably Job 27:18 (reading ׳ ע for ᵑ0 עָשׁ); compare ׳ קוּרֵי ע Isa 59:5 filaments of a spider.
