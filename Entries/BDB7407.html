Hiph`iln Perfect 3 masculine singular הִקְטִיר Lev 9:10 +; 2 masculine singular הִקְטַרְתָּ Exod 29:13; Exod 29:18; Exod 29:25, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יַקְטִיר Lev 4:26 ; Lev 16:25; Exod 40:27 +, etc.: Imperative masculine singular הַקְטֵר 2Kgs 16:15 Infinitive absolute הַקְטֵיר 1Kgs 9:25 construct לְהַקְטִיר Exod 30:20 +; Participle מַקְטִיר Jer 33:18 +, etc.
1. make sacrifices smoke, usually
a. in worship of ׳ י: absolute 1Kgs 12:33; 1Kgs 13:1; 2Chr 29:11 with accusative חלב Lev 17:6 ; Num 18:17; 1Sam 2:15; 1Sam 2:16, עלת 2Kgs 16:13; 2Kgs 16:15; 2Chr 13:11, אִשֶּׁה Exod 30:20; Lev 2:11; Num 18:17, מנחה Jer 33:18 ראֹשׁ, etc., Lev 8:20 אַזְכָּרָה Lev 2:16 , חלבי השׁלמים Lev 6:5 קטרת 1Sam 2:28 on 1Kgs 9:25 see Klo Benz; place is על מזבח Lev 4:10 + 5 t., הַמִוְבֵּ֫חָה Exod 29:13; Exod 29:18; Exod 29:25 + 23 t. Leviticus, Num 5:26 (all P ); with הַמִּזְבֵּחַ (accusative) Lev 6:8 בַּבָּמוֺת 1Kgs 3:3 בגיא בן הִנֹּם 2Chr 28:3.
b. less often in worship of other gods, לאלהין (gods of Solomon's wives) 1Kgs 11:8, לאלהיו (of Moab) Jer 48:35, להם (Baalim) Hos 2:15 — 2Chr 34:25 read Qr Qal.
2. cause incense to smoke, offer incense absolute 2Chr 2:5; 2Chr 26:18 (twice in verse); 2Chr 26:19 with עַל of altar 2Chr 26:16; Exod 30:7 ; Exod 40:27 with accusative יַקְטִירֶנָּה Exod 30:7 ; Exod 30:8, קטרת סמים Exod 30:7 ; Exod 40:27; 2Chr 2:3, קטרת תמיד Exod 30:8 , קטרת ׳ הק Num 17:5 ; 2Chr 29:7 .
3. make smoke upon (על) both altars (of burnt-offering and of incense) 1Chr 6:34, compare also 1Chr 23:13.
