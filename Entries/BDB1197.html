BDB1196 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1198

תַּבְנִית noun feminine construction, pattern, figure ֯ ׳ תּ absolute 1Chr 28:19 construct Exod 25:9 (twice in verse) + 14 t. + Ezek 8:10 (strike out Co); תַּבְנִיתוֺ 2Kgs 16:10, תַבְנִיתָם Exod 25:40 —

1. apparently originally construction, structure, yet only P & late: ׳ תַּבְנִית מִזְבַּה י Josh 22:28 compare Ps 144:12 in simile הֵיכָל ׳ בְּנוֺתֵינוּ כְזָוִיּוֺת מְחֻטָּבִים תּ carved according to the construction of a palace, palace-fashion.
2. pattern, according to which anything is to be constructed ( P & late), of tabernacle Exod 25:9, utensils of tabernacle Exod 25:9 Exod 25:40 an altar 2Kgs 16:10 תַּבְנִיתוֺ לְכָלֿ מַעֲשֵׂהוּ (distinguished from דְּמוּת); temple 1Chr 28:11 compare 1Chr 28:12 chariot, i.e. cherubim 1Chr 28:18 ׳ מַלְאֲכוֺת הַתּ 1Chr 28:19 i.e. objects of which the patters is given.
3. figure, image, Hexateuch only D , of idols in form of animals Deut 4:16; Deut 4:17 (twice in verse); Deut 4:18 (twice in verse); elsewhere late Isa 44:13; Ezek 8:10 (strike out B Co) Ps 106:20 compare יָד ׳ תּ Ezek 8:3; Ezek 10:8 i.e. something like a hand.
