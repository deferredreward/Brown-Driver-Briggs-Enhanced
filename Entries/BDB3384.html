י

י, Yôdh, tenth letter; used as numeral 10 in post-Biblical Hebrew; יא or א ׳ ׳ י = 11; יב, ב ׳ ׳ י = 12, etc.; 14 and 15, however, are not ה ׳ ׳ י, ו ׳ ׳ י, which might stand for abbreviation of יהוה, but ו ׳ ׳ ט, ז ׳ ׳ ט, see ט , and Ges«GKC:5»§ 5. 4. R. 3 a.
