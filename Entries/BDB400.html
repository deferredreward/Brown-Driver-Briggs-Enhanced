BDB400 [H402]

Biblical Hebrew

אָכְלָה17 noun feminine food, eating (with some verbal force, compare DrJPh xi. 217) only P , & Ezekiel; always לְאָכְלָה,

1. especially in phrases like ׳ לָכֶם יִהְיֶה לְאָ Gen 1:29; Gen 6:21; Gen 9:3; Lev 11:39 so Lev 25:6 וְהָיְתָה שַׁבַּת הָאָרֶץ לָכֶם לְאָבְלָה compare ׳ נָתַן לְא Gen 1:30; Exod 16:15.
2. devouring, by wild beasts, only figurative of ravaged people Ezek 29:5; Ezek 34:5; Ezek 34:8; Ezek 34:10 ; Ezek 39:4 , compare Ezek 35:12.
3. consumimg, in fire Ezek 15:4; Ezek 15:6, of fire-sacrifice of children Ezek 23:37 figurative of judgment of ׳ י Ezek 21:37 (compare also infinitive of אָכַל).
