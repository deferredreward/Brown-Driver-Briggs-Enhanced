BDB2072 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2074

הֲדֹם noun masculine stool, footstool, always joined with רַגְלַיִם; only in poetry and late writings, (properly something cast down, low) — ׳ ה absolute Ps 110:1 (or construct before לְ, compare Ges«GKC:130»§ 130, 1); construct Lam 2:1+ 4t. — footstool , never literal, usually of ׳ י Isa 66:1 רַגְלַי ׳ הַשָּׁמַיִם כִּסְאִי וְהָאָרֶץ ה; elsewhere with reference to sanctuary רַגְלָיו ׳ ה (compare מְקוֺם רַגְלַי Isa 60:13) Lam 2:1, or perhaps of ark (as place on which God rests) Ps 99:5; Ps 132:7, רַגְלֵי ׳ וְלַה ׳ בֵּית מְנוּחָה לַאֲרוֺן בְּרִיתיֿ אֱלֹהֵינוּ 1Chr 28:2 of conquest of enemies of Messianic king by Yahweh's agency אָשִׁית אֹיְבֶיךָ הֲדֹם לְרַגְלֶיךָ Ps 110:1.
