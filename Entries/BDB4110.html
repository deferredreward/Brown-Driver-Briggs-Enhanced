BDB4109 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4111

כְּסוּת noun feminine covering — ׳כ Gen 20:16 + 3 t.; suffix כְּסוּתְךָ Deut 22:12 + 3 t. suffixes; —

1. covering, clothing Exod 21:10; Exod 22:26 (Covt. code), Deut 22:12; Job 24:7; Job 31:19 of שַׂק as clothing of heavens Isa 50:3 in figure
2. covering for concealment, of Abaddon, the subterranean abode of the dead Job 26:6 עֵינַיִם ׳כ, covering of the eyes Gen 20:16 (so that they cannot see the wrong, figurative of a present offered in compensation for it; E).
