BDB8405 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8407

I. [שָׁבַח] verb Pi`el soothe, still (Arabic be free from care, etc., JenZA 1 (1886),188 ;iv (1889),269, compare Assyrian pašâ—u , grow calm, so BaES9 SchulthLex.); —

Pi`el Imperfect 3 masculine singular sf יְשַׁבְּחֶ֑נָּה Prov 29:11 he stilleth it, i.e. רוּחוֺ his temper (Bi Toy read חָשַׁךְ אַפּוֺ ); 2 masculine singular sf תְּשַׁבְּחֵם Ps 89:10 thou stillest them, i.e. waves (|| גֵּאוּת הַיָּם).
Hiph`il Participle (probably Masoretic error for Pi`el מְשַׁבֵּחַ) מַשְׁבִּיחַ שְׁאוֺן יַמִּים Ps 65:8 stilling the roar of the seas.
