BDB270 [H2970 H3153]

Biblical Hebrew

יַאֲזַנְיָ֫הוּ proper name, masculine (Yahweh heareth) — so 2Kgs 25:23; Ezek 8:11 יַאֲזַנְיָה Jer 35:3; Ezek 11:1 contracted (וִ)יזַנְיָהוּ Jer 40:8, (וִ)יזַנְיָה Jer 42:1 —

1. a JudÊan 2Kgs 25:33; Jer 40:8.
2. an elder of Israel, son of Shaphan Ezek 8:11.
3. son of Jeremiah Jer 35:3.
4. a leading JudÊan, son of Azur Ezek 11:1.
5. a leading JudÊan Jer 42:1 (= עֲזַרְיָה Jer 43:2).
