BDB7950 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7952

II. רְמִיָּה noun feminine laxness, slackness; — always absolute ׳ ר; — ׳ כַּף ר Prov 10:4 slack (negligent, idle) hand (opposed to יַד חָרוּצִים ); ׳ ר alone = slackness (abstract for concrete = one who is slack) Prov 12:24 (|| id.), vProv 12:27 (see חרך); ׳ נֶפֶשׁ ר Prov 19:15 (|| עַצְלָה); as adverb עשֶֹׁה רְמִיָּה ׳ מְלֶאכֶת י Jer 48:10.
