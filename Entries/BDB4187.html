BDB4187 [H3746]

BDB4186 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4188

כָּרִי adjective [of a people] probably = Carian, only with article as substantive collective Carians, name given to foreign body-guard of king (compare RSOTJC 249, 2d ed.262) לַכָּרִי וְלָרָצִים 2Kgs 11:4 compare 2Kgs 11:19 הכרי 2Sam 20:23 Kt (< Qr הַכְּרֵתִי which We Dr Bu prefer).
