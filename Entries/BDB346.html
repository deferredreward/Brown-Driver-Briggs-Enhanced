אֲחַשְׁדַּרְפְּנִים noun masculine plural satraps (Persian Khshatrapâvan, protectors of the realm, see SpiegAPK 215 = ἐξατράπης, σατράπης, compare LagG. Abh. 68, 14: Semitic i. 42 f, who reads אַחְשַׁדְרְפָן) — ׳א Esth 8:9; Esth 9:3 construct אֲחַשְׁדַּרְפְּנֵי Esth 3:11; Ezra 8:36.
