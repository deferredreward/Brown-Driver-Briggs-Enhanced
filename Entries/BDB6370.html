BDB6370 [H6170]

BDB6369 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6371

[עֲרוּגָה] noun feminine garden terrace or bed; — construct עֲרוּגַת הַבּשֶֹׁם Song 5:13 bed of balsam (simile); plural construct ׳עֲרֻגוֺת הַבּ Song 6:2, מַטָּעָהּ ׳ע Ezek 17:7 i.e. where the vine was planted, עֲרֻגֹת צִמְחָהּ vEzek 17:10 (all figurative).
