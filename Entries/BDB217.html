Biblical Hebrew

אַיָּלוֺן proper name [of a location] (Deer-field) Aijalon — ׳ א Josh 10:12 + 8 t.; אַיָּ֫לֹנָה 1Sam 14:31 —

1. city in Dan Josh 19:42; Josh 21:24 (Levitical city) Judg 1:35; 1Sam 14:31 doubtless same; so 1Chr 6:54 (where Dan omitted); apparently later in Benjamin 1Chr 8:13; 1Chr 2:10; 1Chr 28:18 (see Be); עֵמֶק אַיָּלוֺן Josh 10:12 almost certainly named from same; = Epiph. Ἰαλω modern Jâlo RobBR ii. 253, Surveyiii. 19.
2. city in Zebulun Judg 12:12.
