טְרֵפָה noun feminine animal torn (by wild beasts); torn flesh; — absolute ׳ ט Gen 31:39 + 8 t.

1. animal torn (by wild beasts), of sheep and goats Gen 31:39 ( E ); ox, ass or sheep Exod 22:12 ( J E ; strike out WeKlPr on Amos 3:12, as gloss from Gen 31:39); indefinite Gen 31:31 ( J E ; forbidden as food); commonly with נְבֵלָה (i.e. what dies naturally) as forbidden food, Lev 7:24 ( P ), Lev 17:15; Lev 22:8 (both H ), Ezek 4:14; Ezek 44:31 torn flesh, in metaphor of Nineveh's king as lion Nah 2:13 (|| טֶ֫רֶ ף).
