BDB8030 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8032

רֹעַ noun [masculine] badness, evil; — ׳ ר absolute Gen 41:19 + 4 t. Jeremiah; construct Hos 9:15 + 13 t.; —

1. badness, bad quality, of cattle Gen 41:19 ( E ), figs Jer 24:2; Jer 24:3; Jer 24:8; Jer 29:17.
2. wilfulness, רֹעַ לבב 1Sam 17:28 (Gerber161 makes 2 and 4 [infinitive of] verb).
3. ethical, evil, badness, only in phrase: רֹעַ מעללים evil of doings Deut 28:20; Isa 1:16; Jer 4:4; Jer 21:12; Jer 23:2; Jer 23:22; Jer 25:5; Jer 26:3; Jer 44:22; Hos 9:15; Ps 28:4 (compare רַע מעללים 1Sam 25:3).
4. sadness, פָּנִים ׳ ר Eccl 7:3, לֵב ׳ ר Neh 2:3 (compare [ רָעַע] 2).
