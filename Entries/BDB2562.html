BDB2561 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2563

I. [חָדָה] verb be or grow sharp; —

Qal grow sharp, Imperfect with apocope = jussive יָ֑חַד Prov 27:17 a;
Hiph`il sharpen (figurative), Imperfect apoc. יַ֫חַד Prov 27:17 b; בַּרְזֶל בְּבֶרְזֶל יָ֑חַד וְאִישׁ יַחְד פְּנֵירֵֿעֵהוּ let iron by means of iron grow sharp, and let a man sharpen the countenance (presence, bearing) of his friend; — so KiMichl. 126 a De Now KöLgb i, 373 f., compare Str . (who however makes both forms Hiph`il)
