שׁוֺלָל adjective indeclinable (Ges«GKC:118o»§ 118o) barefoot (apparently stripped as to walking; always with הלן); — אֵילְכָה שׁוֺלָל וְעָרוֺם Mic 1:8 Qr I will go barefoot and naked (Kt שילל); ׳טוֺלִיךְ יוֺעֲצִם שׁ Job 12:17, compare vJob 12:19.
