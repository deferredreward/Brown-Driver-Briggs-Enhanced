BDB1734 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1736

גָּרוֺן noun masculine Ps 69:4 neck, throat (connection with above √ dubious; Thes Rob-Ges (compare MV) from גרה in (assumed) sense of גרר of a dragging, scraping sound; compare מְגֵרָה; yet √ perhaps גרן q. v.; Arabic (sub Frey Lane) front part of neck of camel, etc.) — ׳ ג Isa 3:16; Isa 58:1 suffix גְּרוֺנִי Ps 69:4 גְּרוֺנֵךְ Ezek 16:11 + Jer 2:25 Qr ( Kt גורנך); גְּרוֺנָם Ps 115:7; Ps 149:6, גְּרֹנָם Ps 5:10 — throat;

1. of the visible exterior (front) of throat, neck ׳ נְטֻו֯וֺת ג Isa 3:16 outstretched of neck (= with outstretched neck); וְרָבִיד עַלגְּֿרוֺנֵךְ Ezek 41:19 and a chain upon thy neck.
2. throat, as capable of thirst Jer 2:25 as organ of speech קְרָא בְגָרוֺן Isa 58:1 (|| הָרֵם קוֺלֶ֑ךָ), ׳ לֹא יֶהְגּוּ בִּג Ps 115:7 also רוֺמֲמוֺת אֵל בִּגְרוֺנָם Ps 149:6 high songs to God (be) in their throat; compare נִחַר גְּרוֺנִי Ps 69:4 my throat is parched (|| יָגַעְתִּי בְקָרְאִי); so also קֶבֶרפָּֿתוּחַ גְּרֹנָם Ps 5:10 their throat is an opened sepulchre.
