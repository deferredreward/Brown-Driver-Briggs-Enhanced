Biblical Hebrew

I. חָמֵץ verb be sour, leavened (Late Hebrew id., Pi`el Hiph`il make sour, leaven; Arabic be sour; Aramaic חֲמַע be sour, leavened, be leavened) —

Qal Perfect חָמֵץ Exod 12:39 Imperfect יֶחְמָ֑ץ Exod 12:34 Infinitive suffix חֻמְצָתוֺ Hos 7:4 +; — be leavened, of dough (בָּצֵק), Exod 12:34; Exod 12:39 ( E ), compare Hos 7:4 .
Hithp. be soured, embittered, Ps 73:21 (|| כִּלְיוֺתַי אֶשְׁתּוֺנָן).
