BDB8235 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8237

IV. [שָׂכַךְ] Pilpel prick or spur on (compare Arabic pierce (perhaps denominative from) thorn, point of spear, etc.; Ethiopic thorn); — prick or spur on, accusative of person: Perfect 1 singular וְסִכְסַכְתִּ֫י Isa 19:2 (+ ב against); Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְסַכְסֵךְ Isa 9:10.
