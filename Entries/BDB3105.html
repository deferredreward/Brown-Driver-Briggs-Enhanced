BDB3105 [H2731]

Biblical Hebrew

I. חֲרָדָה noun feminine trembling, fear, anxiety — חֲרָדָה Gen 27:33 + 5 t. + Ezek 38:21 (read for הָרַי, B Co compare Dr 1Sam 14:15); construct חֶרְדַּת 1Sam 14:15; Prov 29:25 (see LagBN 113); plural חֲרָדוֺת Ezek 26:16 —

1. trembling, quaking (of terror ascribed to supernatural cause) 1Sam 14:15 (twice in verse) (hence אלהים ׳ ח see Dr; || וַתִּרְגַּזהֿארץ) compare Ezek 38:21 (see above); Dan 10:7 ׳ קול ח Jer 30:5 voice of trembling; followed by genitive object אָדָם ׳ ח Prov 29:25 trembling before man; opposed to pleasure Isa 21:4 tremblings (plural) Ezek 26:16 under figure of garment (ילבשׁו ׳ ח), of effect on coast-princes of fall of Tyre.
2. anxious care 2Kgs 4:13, accusative of cognate meaning with verb with חָרַד.
