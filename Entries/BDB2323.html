BDB2322 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2324

זְוָעָה and, by transposition, זַעֲוָה (Di Deut 28:25 compare Sta§ 192 a) noun feminine a trembling, i.e. object of trembling, terror: זְוָעָה Isa 28:19, also Kt Jer 15:4 + 4 t.; זַעֲוָה Deut 28:25; Ezek 23:46 (also Qr Jer 15:4 + 4 t.); — object of trembling, terror, fright; זְהָיִיתָ לְזַעֲוָה Deut 28:25 and thou shalt become a terror to all (לְכֹל) the kingdoms of the earth; ׳ וגו ׳ וּנְתַתִּים לְז) Jer 15:4; Jer 29:18; Jer 34:17 (|| וְלִשְׁרֵקָה וּלְחֶרְפָּה לְאָלָה וּלְשַׁמָּה) also ׳ וגו ׳ וּנְתַתִּים לְז Jer 24:9 (|| וּלְמָשָׁל לִשְׁנִינָה וְלִקְלָלָה לְחֶרְפָּה), Ezek 23:46; 2Chr 29:8 .
