יְבֶרֶכְיָ֫הוּ proper name, masculine (= יְבָרֶכְיָהוּ s. Yah blesseth Ges«GKC:27»§ 27, 3) father of a Zechariah in Isaiah's time Isa 8:2 usually in abbreviated form as following: בֶּרֶכְיָה

1. son of Zerubbabel 1Chr 3:20.
2. a Levite gruard of the ark 1Chr 9:16; 1Chr 15:23.
3. father of Meshullam, one of Nehemian's chiefs Neh 3:4; Neh 3:30; Neh 6:18.
4. father of the prophet Zechariah Zech 1:1 = בֶּרֶכְיָהוּ Zech 1:7 בֶּרֶכְיָהוּ also
5. father of Asaph 1Chr 6:24; 1Chr 15:17.
6. Ephraimite chief 2Chr 28:12.

בֶּרֶכְיָה, בֶּרֶכְיָהוּ see יְבֶרֶכְיָהוּ above.
