BDB4149 [H3714]

BDB4148 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4150

כָּפִיס noun masculine apparently (si vera lectio) technical term for some beam in a house, perhaps rafter, or girder (Late Hebrew id., Levy, Jastrow, but according to HoffmZAW ii. 1881, 71 it is changed from oblong block of wood (O.T.) to a building-stone , or brick in Mishna); — only Hab 2:11 for a stone out of the wall crieth out, מֵעֵץ יַעֲנֶנָּה ׳ וְכ and a rafter out of the timber-work answereth it.
