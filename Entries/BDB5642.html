סַנְבַּלַּט (so Baer; van d. H. Ginsb סַנְבַלַּט) proper name, masculine leader of Samaritan opponents of Nehemiah: Neh 2:10; Neh 2:19; Neh 3:33; Neh 4:1; Neh 6:1; Neh 6:2; Neh 6:5; Neh 6:12; Neh 6:14; Neh 13:28 (Babylonian, = Sin-uballi‰, Sin gave life SchrCOT Neh 2:10).
