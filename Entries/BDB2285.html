BDB2284 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2286

זְבוּלֻן, זְלֻבוּן proper name, masculine Zebulun (on explanation of name given in E and J compare זבד, זבל); — זְבוּלֻן Gen 49:13 + 25 t.; זְבֻלוּן Judg 5:18 + 17 t.; זְבוּלוּן Judg 1:30 —

1. sixth son of Jacob and Leah Gen 30:20; Gen 35:23; Gen 46:14; Gen 49:13; Exod 1:3; 1Chr 2:1.
2. the tribe of Zebulun Num 1:9; Deut 27:13; Deut 33:18 (twice in verse); Judg 1:30; Judg 4:10; Judg 5:14; Judg 5:18; Ezek 48:26; 1Chr 12:34; 1Chr 12:41; 1Chr 27:19; 2Chr 30:11; 2Chr 30:18 compare ׳ שָׂרֵי ז Ps 68:28 ׳ שַׁעַר ז Ezek 48:33 ׳ בְּנֵי ז Num 1:30; Num 2:7; Num 7:24; Num 26:26; Josh 19:10; Josh 19:16; Judg 4:6 ׳ מַטֵּה ז Num 1:31; Num 2:7; Num 13:10 ׳ מַטֵּה בְּנֵי ז Num 10:16; Num 34:25.
3. territory of the tribe ׳ אֶרֶץ ז Judg 12:12, ׳ אַרְצָה ז Isa 8:23, ׳ גְּבוּל ז Ezek 48:27 also ׳ ז alone Josh 19:27; Josh 19:34; 2Chr 30:10, ׳ מַטֵּה ז Josh 21:7; Josh 21:34; 1Chr 6:48; 1Chr 6:62.
