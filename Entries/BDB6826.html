I. [פָּרַשׁ] verb make distinct, declare (Late Hebrew seperate oneself,

Pi`el separate, explain, so Aramaic פְּרַשׁ especially Pa`el; Syriac separate, distinguish, explain, compare Mandean, NöM 221); —
Qal Infinitive construct ׳ לִפְרשׁ לָהֶם עַלפִּֿי י Lev 24:12 ( P ) to declare distinctly to them.
Niph`al Participle נִפְרָשׁוֺת Ezek 34:12 read probably שׂוֺת-, see פרשׂ .
Pu`al Perfect 3 masculine singular פֹּרַשׁ Num 15:34 ( P ) what should be done to him had not been distinctly declared; Participle מְפֹרָשׁ Neh 8:8 made distinct (compare B Aram Ezra 4:18), see BeRy KöEinl. 99, > interpreted, Ke and others, BerlinerT. Onk ii.74.
