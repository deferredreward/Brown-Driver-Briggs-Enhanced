Biblical Hebrew

I. עֶ֫צֶם125 noun feminine Ps 35:10 (with pronoun masculine Ezek 37:5; Ezek 37:6 etc., compare AlbrZAW xvi (1896). 73) bone, substance, self; — absolute ׳ י Gen 2:23 +; עָ֑צֶם; Prov 15:30; Prov 16:24 construct עֶצֶם Exod 24:10 +; suffix עַצְמִי Job 19:20 +, etc.; plural עֲצָמוֺת 2Kgs 23:16 +; construct עַצְמוֺת Josh 24:22 +; suffix עַצְמֹתַי Job 4:14 +, etc.; also עֲצָמִים Ezek 24:4 +, suffix עֲצָמַי Gen 2:23 +, etc.; —

1. bone:
a. of living person Mic 3:2; Mic 3:3 (hyperbole), Job 10:11; Job 19:20; Lam 4:8; Ps 102:6; Eccl 11:5 +; figurative of close relationship עֶצָם מֵעֲצָמַי Gen 2:23, compare Gen 29:14 (both J ), Judg 9:2 ; 2Sam 5:1 = 1Chr 11:1; 2Sam 19:13 ; 2Sam 19:14 (all + בָּשָׂר).
b. ׳ ע + בָּשָׂר = body, Job 2:5, plural = bodily frame Jer 20:9 (simile), limbs, members, לַעֲצָמֶיהָ Judg 9:29 i.e. limb by limb (GFM); as seat of vigour Job 20:11, etc.
c. (poetry) as seat of disease and pain Job 30:17; Job 30:30 and (especially of personified Israel) Ps 22:15; Ps 31:11; Ps 102:4; Lam 1:13; Hab 3:16 +.
d. plural as representing entire person = one's whole being, (especially of personified Israel) Ps 6:3 (|| נֶפֶשׁ Ps 6:4), Ps 35:10 (|| נֶפֶשׁ Ps 35:9), Isa 66:14 of individual (including physical and moral) Prov 3:8; Prov 14:30 ; Prov 15:30 ; Prov 16:24.
e. singular = external body Lam 4:7 Ew Th and others, but dubious; Bi Bu bones (= branches) of coral (פְּנִינִים); Löhr עוֺרָם their skin. feminine especially of dead Num 19:16; Num 19:18 ( P ), Ezek 37:1 +; often (plural) = remains Amos 6:10; Gen 50:25; Exod 13:19 (twice in verse); Josh 24:32 (all E ) +.
2. bone of animal Exod 12:46; Num 9:12 (both P ); Ezek 24:4; Ezek 24:5 (symbolic); of (living) hippopotamus Job 40:18.
3. singular substance, self (compare גֶּרֶם 3): הַשָּׁמַיִם ׳ כְּע Exod 24:10 ( J E ), like the substance of the sky = the sky itself; תֻּמּוֺ ׳ בְּע Job 21:23 i.e. full prosperity; especially ׳ אֶתעֿ הַיּוֺם הַזֶּה Ezek 24:2 this selfsame day, ׳ וגו ׳ בְּע Ezek 24:2; Ezek 40:1; Gen 7:13; Gen 17:23; Gen 17:26; Exod 12:17; Exod 12:41; Exod 12:51; Lev 23:21; Lev 23:28; Lev 23:29; Lev 23:30; Deut 32:48; Josh 5:11 (all P ), ׳ וגו ׳ עַדעֿ Ezek 2:3; Lev 23:14 ( P ), Josh 10:27 ( R ). — Ezek 24:5 a read הָעֵצִים (Bö Ew and others); Ezek 24:10 strike out הָעֲצָמוֺת (Co Toy); Job 7:15 read מֵעַצְּבוֺתָ֑י (Reiske Me Bu Du and others); Ps 53:6 read perhaps מֵעֲצַת (compare || Ps 14:6 Bae; see Hup Che and others); Ps 69:5 read מֵעַצְמוֺתַי (for ᵑ0 מַצְמִיתַי), ᵑ6 Ol Che Bae and others
