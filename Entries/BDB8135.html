BDB8135 [H7571]

BDB8134 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8136

[רֶ֫תַח] noun [masculine] boiling; — plural suffix רְתָחֶיהָ Ezek 24:5, as accusative of congnate meaning with verb cause its boilings to boil, make it boil vigorously; but read נְתָחֶיהָ Hi-Sm Co Da (possibly) and all modern (compare vEzek 24:4 vEzek 24:6 || עצמים).
