BDB3125 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3127

חָרוּל noun [masculine] a kind of weed, perhaps chickpea (cicercula), see Löwp. 153 (Syriac fodder for horses; ᵑ7 Prov 24:31 gives חוּרְלָא) — absolute ׳ח Job 30:6; Zeph 2:9 plural חֲרֻלִים Prov 24:31 — as growing in devastated land, collective Zeph 2:9 in vineyard of slothful, plural ׳כִּסּוּ פניו ח Prov 24:31 (|| קִמְּשׂגִים); as sole shelter of certain outcast peoples Job 30:7 (שִׂיחִים).
