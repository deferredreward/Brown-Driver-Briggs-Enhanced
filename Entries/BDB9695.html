נִדְנֵ֑ה (Masora Baer) noun [masculine] sheath ( ᵑ7 נְדָן, לְדָן; see Biblical Hebrew (late) נָדָן, Persian loan-word); — ׳ בְּ֗֗֗נ Dan 7:15 my spirit in (its) sheath, i.e. my body; < נִדְנָה emphatic, or suffix נִדְנַהּ (K§ 54, 3. β)); or (NöGGA. 1884, 1022 Bev) נְדָנַהּ; but expression at best strange; read probably בְּגִין דְּנָה on account of this (בְּגִין as ᵑ7 j ; D§ 47. 3 (2nd ed. 47. 10)), M72* compare Buhl Dr.
