גֵּר noun masculine Exod 12:48 sojourner (Arabic , Ethiopic Aramaic , גִּיּוֺר, prostelyte, גַּיֵּר proselytize, Phoenician גר in proper name, & plural גרם ) — גֵּר Gen 15:16 + 74 t.; suffix גֵּרְךָ Exod 20:10 + 4 t., גֵּרוֺ Deut 1:16 plural גֵּרִים Exod 22:20 + 9 t., גֵּירִים 2Chr 2:16 —

1. sojourner, temporary dweller, new-comer (no inherited rights), compare Exod 12:19; Lev 24:16; Num 15:30; Josh 8:33 (opposed to homeborn); of Abraham at Hebron Gen 23:4 ( P ; || תּוֺשָׁב); Moses in desert Exod 2:22 ( J ) Exod 18:3 ( E ; here explanation of name Gershom, Moses' son); as claiming hospitality Job 31:32 perhaps in above cases, and certainly in general, with technical sense; figurative of Yahweh Jer 14:8 of Israel in Egypt Gen 15:13; Exod 22:20; Exod 23:9 (all J E ) Lev 19:34 ( H ) Deut 10:19; Deut 23:8 גֵּרִים with Yahweh Lev 25:23 ( H ) 1Chr 29:15; Ps 39:13 (in all || תּוֺשָׁב) compare Ps 119:19.
2. usually of גֵּרים in Israel 2Sam 1:13 (Amalekite) compare Josh 8:33; Josh 8:35 ( E ) Josh 20:9 ( P ) Isa 14:1 dwellers in Israel with certain conceded, not inherited right (compare RSOTJC 434; 2nd ed. 342. n.; K 42; Sem 75 f. StaGeschichte.i.400). Lev 19:34 (all H ); Deut 10:18; Deut 10:19; Deut 14:29; Deut 24:19; Deut 24:20; Deut 24:21; Deut 26:12; Deut 26:13 (all || יָתוֺם וְאַלְמָנָה); oppression prohibited Lev 19:33 ( H ) Deut 24:14 ; Exod 22:20 ; Exod 23:9 (twice in verse) ( J E ) Deut 24:17 ; Deut 27:19 ; Jer 7:6; Jer 22:3; Zech 7:10 (these eight || יָתוֺם וְאַלְמָנָה); object of care to ׳ י Ps 146:9 (|| id.); charge that גֵּר has been oppressed Ezek 22:7; Mal 3:5 (both || id. ); also Ezek 22:29 (|| עָנִי וְאֶבְיוֺן), Ps 94:6 compare also command that a poor brother be treated like גֵּר i.e. kindly, Lev 25:35 ( H ).
