BDB6724 [H6476]

BDB6723 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6725

פָּצַח verb cause to break or burst forth, break forth with (compare Arabic break, crush; Ethiopic shatter; Syriac is be joyous, hilarious); —

Qal Perfect 3 plural פָּֽצְחוּ רִנָּה Isa 14:7 they have broken forth with joyous shout; Imperfect יִפְצְחוּ רִנָּה Isa 55:12 (of mountains); Imperative feminine singular פִּצְחִי רִנָּה Isa 54:1 (of Zion); masculine plural ׳ פִּצְחוּ ר Isa 44:23; Isa 49:13 Qr (> Kt יפצחו; of mountains); absolute פִּצְחוּ Isa 52:9; Ps 98:4 (both + רַנְּנוּ).
Pi`el break bones in pieces: Perfect 3 plural פִּצֵּ֑חוּ Mic 3:3.
