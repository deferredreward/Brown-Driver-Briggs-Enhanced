כְּלוּב

proper name, masculine 1. a name in Judah 1Chr 4:11, ᵐ5 Ξαλεβ (according to WeGentib. 20, Hist. 218 = כָּלֵב בֶּןֿ חֶזְרוֺן; read also for כַּרְמִי v1Chr 4:1).
2. father of one of David's officers, according to 1Chr 27:26 (ᵐ5 Ξοβουδ, A Ξαλουβ, ᵐ5L Ξαλουβ).
