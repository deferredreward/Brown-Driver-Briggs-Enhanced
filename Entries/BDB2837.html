BDB2836 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2838

מַחֲמָאֹת noun feminine plural curd-like things = smooth, hypocritical, words, only construct פִּיו ׳ חָֽלְקוּ מ Ps 55:22 smooth are the butter-words of his mouth (EW De Hup), but read probably (חלק(ו) מֵחֲמָאֹת פיו (פניוׅ) his mouth (face) is smoother than curds (Vrss Ki Thes Ol Che Bae We Du [מֵחֶמְאָה]).
