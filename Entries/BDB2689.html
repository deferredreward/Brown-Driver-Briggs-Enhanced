יְחֶזְקֵאל proper name, masculine (God strengtheneth, compare foregoing; see LagBN 134) —

1. the well-known priest and prophet, son of Buzi, ᵐ5 Ιεξεκιηλ, Ezek 1:3; Ezek 24:24.
2. priest of 19th course (assigned to David's time) 1Chr 24:16, ᵐ5 Εξεκηλ, ᵐ5L (20th course, compare A) Ιεξεκιηλ.
