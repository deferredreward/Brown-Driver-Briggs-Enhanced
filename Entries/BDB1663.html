BDB1663 [H1538]

Biblical Hebrew

גֻּלְגֹּ֫לֶת noun feminine skull, head, poll (person) (on formation compare BaNB 205; Aramaic גּוּלְגַּלְתָּא) — absolute ׳ ג 2Kgs 9:35 + 3 t.; suffix גֻּלְגָּלְתּוֺ Judg 9:53; 1Chr 10:10 plural suffix גֻּלְגְּלֹתָם Num 1:2 + 5 t.; —

1. skull, as broken by a stone Judg 9:53 as severed from body 2Kgs 9:35; 1Chr 10:10.
2. head, poll (in counting, taxing, etc.; only P and late) ׳ עֹמֶר לַגּ Exod 16:16 an omer for every man; compare Exod 38:26; Num 1:2; Num 1:18; Num 1:20; Num 1:22; Num 3:47; 1Chr 23:3; 1Chr 23:24.
