BDB3351 [H2934]

BDB3350 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3352

טָמַן verb hide, conceal, especially in earth (Late Hebrew id. ; whence perhaps Aramaic טְמֵן keep, preserve; compare Arabic bury, hide (loan-word Frä137); Aramaic , טְמַר id.); —

Qal Perfect טְמֵן Ps 35:8 + 2 t. etc; Imperfect וַיִּטְמֹן Gen 35:4, etc.; Imperative suffix טָמְנֵהוּ Jer 13:4 טָמְנֵם Job 40:13 Infinitive construct לִטְמוֺן Job 31:33; Ps 64:6 Passive participle טָמוּן Job 3:16 + 3 t.; טְמוּנָה Josh 7:22 plural טְמֻנִים Josh 7:21 construct טְמוּנֵי Deut 33:19 —
1. hide, with accusative Gen 35:4 ( E ), Exod 2:12 ( E ; dead body in sand), Josh 2:6 ( J E ; spies under flax-stalks), Josh 7:21; Josh 7:22 ( J E ; Achan's theft), Jer 13:4; Jer 13:5; Jer 13:6; Jer 13:7 (Jeremiah's girdle), Jer 43:9; Jer 43:10 (stones at Tahpanhes); hide, bury hand in dish (צַלָּ֑חַת) Prov 19:24; Prov 26:15 (of עָצֵל the sluggard); נֵפֶל טָמוּן Job 3:16 hidden abortion, i.e. a lifeless child at once buried; שְׂפֻנֵי טְמוּנֵי חול Deut 33:19 (poem); of hiding iniquity עון Job 31:33 hiding the wicked in the dust עפר Job 40:13.
2. often of hiding, concealing, secretly laying a snare רֶשֶׁת Ps 9:16; Ps 35:8, followed by לִי Ps 31:5 שַׁחַת רִשְׁתָּם Ps 35:7 פַּח followed by לִי Ps 140:6; Ps 142:4 followed by לרגלי Jer 18:22 חבל Job 18:10 מוֺקשׁים Ps 64:5.
3. טָמוּן that which is darkened = darkness, Job 40:13 nearly = ׃שְׁאוֺל ׳ פניהם חֲבשׁ בט bind their faces in darkness (i.e. the wicked); hide = reserve Job 20:26 all darkness is held in reserve for his treasures, i.e. all calamities are stored up for them.
Niph`al Imperative חִטָּמֵן בֶּעָפָר ׳ מִפְּנֵי פַּחַד י Isa 2:10 hide thyself in the dust, from before the terror of ׳ י.
Hiph`il (or Qal? BaZMG 1889, 180) Imperfect וַיֵּלִכוּ וַיַּטְמִנוּ 2Kgs 7:8 (twice in verse) and they went and hid (it), i.e. plunder from Aramaic camp.
