שְׁבוּל, שְׁבִיל noun [masculine] way, path (properly as flowing along, stretching out ? compare Late Hebrew שְׁבִיל, Arabic ); Aramaic שְׁבִילָא); — plural suffix רַבִּים וּשְׁבִילְיךָ֯ בְּמַיִם [ Kt שְׁבִילְךָ Ps 77:20 (of ׳ י; || דַּרְכֶּ֑ךָ); construct (figurative of course of life) שְׁבוּלֵי עוֺלָם Jer 18:15 ( Kt ; Qr שְׁבִילֵי; || דְּרָכִים).
