BDB7097 [H6761]

Biblical Hebrew

צֶ֫לַע noun [masculine] limping, stumbling; — figurative of calamity, absolute ׳ לְצ Ps 38:18 I am ready (נָכוֺן) for stumbling (and falling); suffix בְּצַלְעִי Ps 35:15 at my stumbliing; ׳ שֹׁמְרֵי צ Jer 20:10 watchers of my stumbling; אֵיד נָכוֺן לְצַלְעוֺ Job 18:12 ruin is ready for his stumbling. - See also צֵלָע construct
