דְּבֵלָה noun feminine lump of pressed figs, pressed (fig-) cake (Late Hebrew דְּבֵילָה, Aram id., , = Greek παλάθη; Arabic lump, large gobbet or mouthful; compare Assyrian dublu , foundation, & Hebrew synonym אֲשִׁישָׁה DlHA 58) — דְּבֵלָה 1Sam 30:12 construct דְּבֶלֶת 2Kgs 20:7 = Isa 38:21 plural דְּבֵלִים 1Sam 25:18; 1Chr 12:40 — used as food 1Sam 25:18; 1Sam 30:12; 1Chr 12:40 דְּבֶלֶת תְּאֵנִים, as application to boil, or eruption 2Kgs 20:7 = Isa 38:21.
