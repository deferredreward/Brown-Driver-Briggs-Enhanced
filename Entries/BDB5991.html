BDB5991 [H5848]

Biblical Hebrew

I. [עָטַף] verb turn aside (Syriac turn, return; Arabic incline, bend); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular יַעֲטֹף turn aside (so Ew Di De BaeKau and others), Job 23:9 he turneth aside to the right (יָמִין; || שְׂמאֹול בַּעֲשׂתוֺ ), read probably 1 singular אֶעֱטֹף Me Bu Du, I turn… and do not see him ( and || בִּקַּשְׁתִּו ׳ שׂ).
