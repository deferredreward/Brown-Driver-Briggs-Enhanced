I. רֶ֫סֶן noun masculine Isa 30:28 :

1. halter;
2. jaw (?);
—
1. absolute ׳ ר Ps 32:9 restraining mouth of horse or ass (+מֶתֶג); figurative of ׳ שֵׁם י, עַללְֿחָיֵי עַמִּים ׳ ר Isa 30:28 מִפָּנַי שִׁלֵּ֑חוּ ׳ ר Job 30:11, i.e. threw off restraint.
2. suffix כֶּפֶל רִסְנוֺ Job 41:5 the double of his jaw, his double jaws (of crocodile), si vera 1.; connection of meanings strange, || פְּנֵי לְבוּשׁוֺ, whence GHBW right Du conjecture סִיֹנוֺ.
