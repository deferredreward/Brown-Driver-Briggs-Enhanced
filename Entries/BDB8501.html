BDB8500 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8502

[תְּשֻׁאָה] noun feminine noise (properly of devastation, storm); — ᵑ0 only plural תְּשֻׁאוֺת absolute city noises Isa 22:2 (of עִיר הוֺמִיָּה), adverb accusative = (with) shoutings Zech 4:7 so construct נוֺגֵשׂ לֹא יִשְׁמָ֑ע ׳ ת Job 39:7 (subject wild ass); סֻכָּתוֺ ׳ ת Job 36:29 (of thunder); read perhaps singular תְּשֻׁוָה (= תְּישׁאָה) Job 30:22 Kt , dissolvest me in (or, into) the roar of the storm (|| make me ride on wind), so Ew Ol De Di Du Bu and others; > תֻּשִׁיָּה Qr AV my substance (?), Du ׳ מִתֻּשׁ 'ohne Halt' (?).
