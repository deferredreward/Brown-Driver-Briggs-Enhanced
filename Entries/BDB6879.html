BDB6878 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6880

[פָּתַל] verb twist (Late Hebrew Pi`el and derivatives; Arabic ; Ethiopic ; Aramaic פְּתַל, chiefly derivatives, in figure senses; Syriac ); —

Niph`al Perfect 1 singular נִפְּתַּלְתִּי עִם Gen 30:8 I have wrestled (literally twisted myself) with; Participle עֲצַת נִפְתָּלִים Job 5:13 the plan of the tortuous; נִפְתָּל וְעִקֵּשׁ Prov 8:8 anything tortuous and twisted. Hithpa`el Imperfect 2 masculine singular עִםֿ עִקֵּשׁ תִּתְפַּתָּ֑ל Ps 18:27 with the twisted thou dost deal tortuously= 2Sam 22:27 (so read for impossible תִּתַּפָּ֑ל).
