[יָצַת] verb kindle, burn (intransitive) (Late Hebrew id. rare)) —

Qal Imperfect 3 feminine singular וַתִּצַּת Isa 9:17 יִצַּ֑תּוּ Isa 33:12; Jer 51:58 (Köi. 431); 3 feminine plural תִּצַּתְנָה Jer 49:2 — kindle, intransitive, figurative of wickedness, Isa 9:17 followed by בָּאֵשׁ be kindled with fire ( = set on fire), of thorns (in simile) Isa 33:12, Jer 49:2 (of dependent towns and villages of Rabbah, under figure of daughters), Jer 51:58 (of gates of Babylon).
