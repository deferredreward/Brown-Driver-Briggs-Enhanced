סְעָרָה noun feminine tempest, storm-wind; — absolute סְעָרָה Isa 29:6 + (so also 2Kgs 2:1; 2Kgs 2:11 Ginsb; Baer בַּסֳּעָרָה); construct סַעֲרַת Jer 23:19; Jer 30:23 plural סְעָרוֺת Ezek 13:11; Ezek 13:13 construct סַעֲרוֺת Zech 9:14 — tempest, storm-wind, as instrument of ׳י's wrath, Isa 29:6 (+ סוּפָה), compare Isa 40:24; Isa 41:16, also רוּחַ סְעָרוֺת Ezek 13:11; Ezek 13:13 (against nation under figure of wall); storm-wind of Elijah's translation 2Kgs 2:1; 2Kgs 2:11 of theophany Ezek 1:4 (רוּחַ סְעָרָה), Job 38:1; Job 40:6, and סַעֲרוֺת תֵּימָן Zech 9:14 ׳סַעֲרַת י as figurative of ׳י's wrath Jer 23:19; Jer 30:23 of ordinary tempests only in late Psalms: רוּחַ סְעָרָה Jer 107:25; Jer 148:8, ׳ס opposed to דְּמָמָה Jer 107:29.
