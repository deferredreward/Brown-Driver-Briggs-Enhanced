BDB1648 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1650

גַּלְעֵד proper name [of a location] (witness-pile) name of pile of stones erected by Jacob and his company (KitGeschichte.129; by Laban We Di) Gen 31:47; Gen 31:48 (see גַּל 1.c.) apparently intended to explain גִּלְעָד q. v.; Di conjecture also (as name of altar) Josh 22:34 (compare ᵑ6 ).
