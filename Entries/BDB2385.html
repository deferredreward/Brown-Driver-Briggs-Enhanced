BDB2384 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2386

[זָמַן] verb (Aramaic and late) only

Pu`al Participle be fixed, appointed, of time (Arabic continue, Aramaic Pa`el זַמֵּן, summon to fixed time or place, invite, appoint; compare זִמָ֑ן below) — עִתִּים מְזֻמָּנִים Ezra 10:14; Neh 10:35 מְזֻמָּנוֺת ׳ ע Neh 13:31.
