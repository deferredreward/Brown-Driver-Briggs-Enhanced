BDB2661 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2663

חָזוֺן noun masculine Dan 8:1 vision (on formation compare LgBN 204) — absolute ׳ ח Hos 12:11 + 25 t.; construct חֲזוֺן Isa 1:1 + 8 t.

1. vision, as seen in the ecstatic state || קסם Mic 3:6 || מקסם Ezek 12:24 || חלמות Dan 1:17 || נביא Dan 9:24 ׳ חזה ח Ezek 12:27 ; Ezek 13:16 ׳ ראה ח Dan 8:15 ׳ ראה בח Dan 8:2 (twice in verse); Dan 9:21 ׳ דבּר בח Ps 89:20 חזון נראה אל Dan 8:1 vision appeared unto; ׳ מצא ח מיהוה Lam 2:9 find a vision from Yahweh; also Ezek 12:22; Ezek 12:23; Dan 8:13; Dan 8:17; Dan 8:26; Dan 10:14; Dan 11:14.
2. vision, in the night חֲזוֺן לַיְלָה Isa 29:7.
3. divine communication in a vision, oracle, prophecy ׳ בקשׁ ח מנביא seek a vision (prophecy) from prophet Ezek 7:26 || דבר (יםׅ 1Sam 3:1; 1Chr 17:15 חֲזוֺן לִבָּם יְדַבֵּ֑רוּ Jer 23:16 a prophecy of their own hearts (minds) they speak; חֲזוֺן שֶׁקֶר וְקֶסֶם אֱלִיל וְתַרְמִית לִבָּם הֵמָּה מִתְנַבְּאִים Jer 14:14 false vision and (see אֱלִיל ) worthless divination & deceit of their own hearts they are prophesying; ׳ כתב ח write the vision Hab 2:2 also Hos 12:11; Hab 2:3; Prov 29:18 ; Ezek 7:13 (strike out Co).
4. vision, as title of book of prophecy, ספר חזון נחוּם Nah 1:1 ישׁעיהו ׳ ח Isa 1:1 עבדיה ׳ ח Obad 1:1 of other writings of prophets 2Chr 32:32.
