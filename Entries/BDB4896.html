אַמְתַּ֫חַת noun feminine sack, only Gen 42-44; — ׳ א construction Gen 44:2; Gen 44:12 suffix אַמְתַּחְתִּי Gen 42:28, אַמְתַּחְתּוֺ Gen 42:27 + 2 t.; plural construct אַמְתְּחֹת Gen 44:1 suffix אַמְתְּחֹתֵינוּ Gen 43:18 + 3 t.; אַמְתְּחֹתֵיכֶם Gen 43:12; Gen 43:23 — sack (s) in which brethren of Joseph carried corn from Egypt; in phrase ׳ בְּטִי אַמ Gen 42:27 in the mouth of the sack, so Gen 43:12; Gen 43:21; Gen 44:1; Gen 44:2; Gen 44:8 elsewhere Gen 42:28; Gen 43:18; Gen 43:21; Gen 43:22; Gen 43:23; Gen 44:1; Gen 44:11; Gen 44:11; Gen 44:12 (all J ).
