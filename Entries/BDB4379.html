BDB4378 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4380

[לוּעַ, or לָעַע] verb swallow, swallow down (Syriac , lap or lick up Judg 7:5-7); —

Qal Perfect 3 plural וְלָע֫וּ consecutive Obad 1:16 (Ges «GKC:67»§ 67 R. 12); — absolute, figurative of nations drinking (cup of judgment). — Hi De talk wildly, see II. לוע; We Now נָעוּ reel, totter (compare Isa 24:20; Isa 29:9).
