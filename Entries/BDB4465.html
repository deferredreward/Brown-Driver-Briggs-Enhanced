Biblical Hebrew

[לָקַשׁ] verb only

Pi`el 3 masculine plural, probably denominative from לֶקֶשׁ = take the aftermath, i.e. take everything (Wetzst in DeHiob 26, 6) (> Buhl II. לקשׁ, compare Arabic schnell zusammenraffen [compare Lane2668 ]); — despoil; וְכֶרֶם רָשָׁע יְלֵַקּשׁוּ ׃ Job 24:6 and the vineyard of the wicked (but read probably עָשִׁיר the rich Bu Du) they despoil (|| קָצַר).
