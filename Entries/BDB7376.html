Biblical Hebrew

קוֺעַ proper name [of a people] named with Babylonian, Chaldean, Assyrian, ׳ (פְּקוֺד וְ)שׁוֺעַ וְק Ezek 23:23 Šô±a and †ô±a ; identification by DlPa 235 with Assyrian Sutû, Kutû (abbreviated Su (? see שׁוֺעַ) and [by inference] Ku ), east of Tigris, on border of Elam and Media; compare COT Ezek 23:23 DrHast. iii, KOA; but קוֺעַ = Kutû now doubted by WklAltor, Forsch. ii. 2 (1899), 54.
