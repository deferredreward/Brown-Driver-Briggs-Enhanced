BDB7356 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7358

[קוֺט] dubious verb intransitive, assumed meaning break, snap (supposed to be akin to Arabic cut, cut off, pare, trim [whence Buhl assumes √ קטט]), to account for יְקוֺט Job 8:14, either as

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular whose hope snappeth; or as noun [masculine] = fragile thing (|| בֵּית עַכָּבִישׁ; compare Di Bu); probably corrupt; Du Beer read קוּרִים Bu conjecture קֻרֵי קַיִט [Aramaism for קַיִץ ׳ ק].
