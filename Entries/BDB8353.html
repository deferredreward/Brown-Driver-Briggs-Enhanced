BDB8353 [H5640]

Biblical Hebrew

שָׂתַם verb usually stop up, in sense of shut out, shut ears against (late || form of סָתַם; yet awkward; BaES 9 compare Arabic frustrate, disappoint); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular תְּפִלָּתִי ׳ שׂ Lam 3:8 (> van d. H. שָׂתָם) he (׳ י) hath shut out my prayer (Bu ׳ מתּ ׳ שׂ shut up [himself] from).
