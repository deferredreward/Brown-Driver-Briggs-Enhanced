BDB5498 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5500

סְגוֺר noun [masculine] enclosure, encasement; — construct סְגוֺר לִבָּם Hos 13:8 the encasement of their heart (pericardium; i.e. their vitals). — סְגוֺר Job 28:15 must = fine gold, si vera lectio, but see סגר , Qal. near the end; סְגֹר Ps 35:3 see id., Qal 2.b.
