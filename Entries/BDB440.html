Biblical Hebrew

אֱלִיהוּ Job 32:4 + 3 t.; אֱלִיהוּא Job 32:2 + 6 t.; proper name, masculine (He is (my) God)

a. the young friend of Job Job 32:2; Job 32:4; Job 32:5; Job 32:6; Job 34:1; Job 35:1; Job 36:1.
b. an Ephraimite, Samuel's great-grandfather 1Sam 1:1 compare אֱלִיאָב 1Chr 6:12, אֱלִיאֵל v.1Chr 6:19.
c. chief of Manasseh 1Chr 12:20.
d. a Korahite 1Chr 26:7 .
e. one of the brethren of David 1Chr 27:18 (compare אֱלִיאָב 1Sam 16:6).
