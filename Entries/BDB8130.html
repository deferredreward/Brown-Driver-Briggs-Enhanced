BDB8129 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8131

I. רֶ֫שֶׁף noun masculine Hab 3:5 flame, fire-bolt; — absolute ׳ ר Deut 32:24 +; plural רְשָׁפִים Ps 78:48, construct ׳ רִשְׁפֵ Ps 76:4, רִשְׁפֵּי Song 8:6 suffix רְשֶׁפֶיהָ Song 8:6 —

1. flame: אֵשׁ ׳ ר ׳ ר Song 8:6 its flames (that is of אַהֲבָה, קִנְאָה) are flames of fire; ׳ בְּנֵי ר Job 5:7 = sparks; ׳ ר = pointed flame of lighting Ps 78:48 (|| בָּרָד); קָ֑שֶׁת ׳ ר Ps 76:4 Sharp flames of the bow, figurative for arrows.
2. fire-bolt of ׳ יְ brining pestilence and death, Deut 32:24 (compare Dr; || קֶטֶב מְרִירִי), Hab 3:5 (|| דֶּבֶר).
