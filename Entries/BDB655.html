אֲפֻדָּה noun feminine ephod (= אֵפוֺד, for which it supplies construct & suffix forms).

1. of high-priest's ephod, compare אֵפוֺד 1 b, only חֵשֶׁב אֲפֻדָּתוֺExod 28:8; Exod 39:5 the cunningly-wrought band of his ephod.
2. of sheathing of idol-images, אֲפֻדַּת מַסֵּכַתוְהָכֶ֑ךָIsa 30:22 (|| צִפוּי פְסִילֵי כַסְפֶּ ךָ) compare אֵפוֺר 3 a.
