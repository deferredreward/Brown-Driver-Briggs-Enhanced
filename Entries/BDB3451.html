Biblical Hebrew

יְדוּתוּן, יְדֻתוּן proper name, masculine usually יְדוּתוּן; יְדֻתוּן 2Chr 5:12; 2Chr 35:15 יְדיּ֯תוּן Ps 39:1; Ps 77:1; Neh 11:37; 1Chr 16:38 chief of one of the three choirs of the temple (only Chronicles & Psalm-titles) 1Chr 9:16; 1Chr 16:38; 1Chr 16:41; 1Chr 16:42; 1Chr 25:3 (twice in verse); 1Chr 25:6 ; 2Chr 5:12 the king's seer 2Chr 35:15 his descendants formed one of the perpetual temple choirs בְּנֵי יְדוּתוּן 1Chr 16:42; 1Chr 25:1; 1Chr 25:3; 2Chr 29:14 בֶּןיְֿדיּ֯תוּן Neh 11:17. In Psalm-titles על ידותון Ps 62:1; Ps 77:1 ( Qr ); לִידיּתוּן Ps 39:1 (לְ error for עלֿ), all = after the manner of (the choir of) Yeduthun (musical term according to RSOTJC 422, 2nd ed. 143). — see also LagOr ii. 16 ff. and proper name אָסָף, הֵימָן (sub אמן), אֵיתָן (sub יתן).
