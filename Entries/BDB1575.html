מָגוֺר noun masculine fear, terror — absolute ׳מ Ps 31:14 + 6 t. + Jer 20:3 compare below; plural suffix מְגוּרַי Lam 2:22 — fear, terror Isa 31:9 = cause of terror Jer 20:4 elsewhere in phrase, coined by Jeremiah, מִסָּבִיב ׳מ terror on every side Jer 6:25; Jer 20:3 (where as proper name), vJer 20:10; Jer 46:5; Jer 49:29; Ps 31:14; Lam 2:22 (׳מְגוּרַי מִסּ).
