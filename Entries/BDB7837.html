I. רָזוֺן noun [masculine] leanness, wasting, scantness; — absolute ׳ ר:

1. leanness Isa 10:16 (figurative; opposed to מִשְׁמַנָּיו).
2. wasting, i.e. wasting disease, Ps 106:15 (compare Bae).
