[פָּשַׂק ] verb part, open wide (Late Hebrew פָּסַק, Aramaic פְּסַק, , all cut, sever, cleave); —

Qal Participle שֲֵֹׁק שְׂפָתָיו Prov 13:3 i.e. one talkative.
Pi`el Imperfect 2 feminine singular וַתְּפַשְּׂקִי אֶתרַֿגְלַיִךְ Ezek 16:23 (with ל person; sensu obsc.).
