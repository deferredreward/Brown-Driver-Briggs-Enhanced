Biblical Hebrew

מַסַּע noun [masculine] pulling up, breaking camp, setting out, journey; — absolute ׳ מ Num 10:2; Deut 10:11 plural construct מַסְעֵי Num 10:28; Num 33:1 suffix מַסָּעָיו Gen 13:3, מַסְעֵיהֶם Exod 17:1 + 6 t.; —

1.
a. pulling up, breaking camp (with accusative מַחֲנֶה; Ges«GKC:45e»§ 45e, «GKC:115d»115d) Num 10:2.
b. setting out Num 10:6, compare Num 10:23.
2. station, stage, journey (by stages), ׳ וַיֵּלֶךְ לְמ Gen 13:3 and he went by (according to) his journeyings (compare הלך , p. 231 a above); לְמַסְעֵיהֶם Exod 17:1 compare Exod 40:36; Exod 40:38; Num 10:12 (all P ; all with נָסַע), Num 33:1; Num 33:2 (|| מוֺצָאֵיהֶם), Num 33:2 (|| id. ); קוּם לֵךְ לְמַסַּע Deut 10:11 for journeying (see Dr).
