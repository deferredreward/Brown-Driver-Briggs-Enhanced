Hiph`il Perfect 3 masculine singular הֵנִיף Josh 8:31 +, 2 masculine singular הֵנַ֫פְתָּ Exod 20:25 (Ges«GKC:72k»§ 72k), וְהֵנַפְתָּ֫ Exod 29:24 + 3 t.; 1 singular הֲנִיפֹ֫תִי Job 31:21 Imperfect וַיָּ֫נֶף Lev 8:27; Num 8:21, suffix וַיְנִיפֵ֫הוּ Lev 8:29 2 masculine singular תָּנִיף Deut 23:26 + 2 t., etc.; Imperative masculine plural הָנִ֫יפוּ Isa 13:2 Infinitive construct הָנִיף Isa 10:15 + 2 t.; לַהֲנָפָה Isa 30:28 (Ges«GKC:72z»§ 72z BaNB 90, see also הֲנָחָה ), etc.; Participle מֵנִיף Isa 19:16; Zech 2:13, etc.; — swing, wield, wave: —
1. wield, move tool to and fro in using it, with עַלֿ of material: כִּי חֵרְבְּךָ הֵנַפְתָּ עָלֶיהָ Exod 20:25 ( E ), i.e. over stone, so Deut 27:5; Josh 8:31 ( D ), and Deut 23:26 a sickle thou shalt not wield over the standing grain of thy neighbour; absolute מְנִיפוֺ Isa 10:15 against him that wieldeth it (i.e. a saw), and כְּהָנִיף שֵׁבֶט וְאֶתמְֿרִימוֺ Isa 10:15 (read אֶתֿ) like a rod's wielding him that lifteth it.
2. Shake or wave the hand;
a. wave hand אֶלהַֿמָּקוֺם 2Kgs 5:11 , in healing ceremony (i.e. probably toward sanctuary KmpKau; > toward the spot [where leprosy appears], so most).
b. with עַלֿ, shake or brandish against, Isa 11:15; Isa 19:16; Zech 2:13 (all of ׳ י) Job 31:21 [compare Eccl 12:18 (without עַל) in mockery].
c. wave hand, as a signal, Isa 13:2.
3. swing to and fro בְּנָפַת,in a sieve, Isa 30:28 (figurative, of nations).
