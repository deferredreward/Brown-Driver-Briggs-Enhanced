שַׁאֲנָן adjective at ease, secure (BaNB § 143a Ges«GKC:84b»§ 84 b k); — absolute׳שׁ Isa 33:20 plural שַׁאֲנִּים Amos 6:1 +, שַׁאֲנַנּוֺת Isa 32:9; Isa 32:11; Isa 32:18 —

1. at ease, secure: ׳נָוֶה שׁ Isa 33:20 ,secure habitation (of Jerusalem), compare ׳מְנוּחוֺת שׁ Isa 32:18 (|| מִבְטָחִים).
2. as substantive, one at ease, free from misfortune, Job 12:5.
3. at ease, with collateral idea of careless, wanton, arrogant, Amos 6:1; Isa 32:9; Isa 32:11; Zech 1:15; Ps 123:4.
4. as substantive abstract = arrogance: suffix שַׁאֲנַנְךָ, 2Kgs 19:28 (|| הִתְרַגֶּזְךָ) = Isa 37:29, but < שְׁאוֺנְךָ thine uproar, BuZAW xii (1892), 36 Gr Che Marti Kit (perhaps), Bur. compare שׁלאנן.
