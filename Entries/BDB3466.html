Biblical Hebrew

מַדָּע noun masculine 2Chr 1:12 knowledge, thought (late); מַדָּע 2Chr 1:10 + 4 t.; מִדָּֽעֲךָ Eccl 10:20 —

1. knowledge (|| חָכְמָה) of Solomon, 2Chr 1:10; 2Chr 1:11; 2Chr 1:12; Dan 1:4 (|| חָכְמָה and דַּעַת), Dan 1:7 (|| הַשְׂכֵּל).
2. place of knowledge, mind, (in our idiom also) thought Eccl 10:20 .
