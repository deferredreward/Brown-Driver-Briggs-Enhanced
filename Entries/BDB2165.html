BDB2164 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2166

[הֲלִיכָה] noun feminine a going, way, travelling-company, suffix הֲלִיכָתָם Nah 2:6 Qr (הלוכתם Kt ); elsewhere only plural, construct הֲלִיכוֺת Ps 68:25, so Prov 31:27 Qr (הילכות Kt ), הֲלִכוֺת Hab 3:6, הֲלִיכֹת Job 6:19 suffix הֲלִיכוֺתֶיךָ Ps 68:25 —

1. going, walk,
a. Nah 2:6 they shall stumble in their going; elsewhere plural goings:
b. Ps 68:25 (twice in verse) of going, marching, progress of God into the sanctuary ( בַּקֹּדֶשׁ; so Hup Pe Che; Ew Hi De in holiness reference either to solemn processions of worshippers (e.g. De) or, perhaps better, to the theophanic progress of God himself (e.g. Che); also
c. of goings = doings צוֺפִיָּה בֵּיתָהּ ׳ ה Prov 31:27 watching the doings of her household; of God Hab 3:6 הֲלִכוֺת עוֺלָם לוֺ ways of eternity (al of old) are his.
2. travelling-company, caravan הֲלִיכֹת שְׁבָא Job 6:19 (|| אָרְהוֺת תֵּמָא).
