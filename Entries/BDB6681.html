פְּנִ֫ימָה adverb (ה locative)

1. towards the (in-)side.
2. within (literally faceward, from point of view of one entering by opposite door); — of a building, usually temple:
1. after verb of motion Lev 10:18; 2Chr 29:18, and (after הִגִּיד) 2Kgs 7:11, also ׳לִפ Ezek 41:3; 2Chr 29:16.
2. ׳פ 1Kgs 6:18, also ׳לִפ v1Kgs 6:30, so read also v1Kgs 6:29 (for ᵑ0 מִלִּפְנִים, see KmpKau Kit Benz), Ezek 40:16 (twice in verse), also Ps 45:14 ᵑ0, i.e. within the house, but Krochm Gr Che and others (plausibly) פְּנִינִים, q. v.; ׳מִפּ 1Kgs 6:19; 1Kgs 6:21.= 2Chr 3:4.
