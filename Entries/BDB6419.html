Biblical Hebrew

I. עֲרוֺעֵר noun [masculine] probably a tree or bush; juniper? (Arabic juniper (or cypress ? see Lane ), so TristrNHB 358 RobBR ii. 124; on formative see LagBN 162 BaNB 160); — עֲרוֺעֵר בַּמִּדְבָּר Jer 48:6, + Jer 17:6, where read probably עֲרֹעֵר בָּעֲרָבָה (for ᵑ0 עַרְעָר; > Gf and others conversely, read עַרְעָר Jer 48:6).
