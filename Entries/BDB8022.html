BDB8022 [H7482]

BDB8021 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8023

רַ֫עַם noun [masculine] thunder; — ascribed to ׳׃ י ׳ר absolute Isa 29:6, בְּסֵתֶר רַ֑עַם Ps 81:8 (Baer Gi; i.e. thunder-cloud); suffix קוֺל רַעַמְךָ Ps 77:19; Ps 104:7 construct רַעַם גבורתו Job 26:14 (figurative of ׳י's display of might; opposed to שֵׁמֶץ); figurative of captains, שָׂרִים ׳ר Job 39:25, i.e. thunderous shouting ( + תְּרוּעָה).
