אַרְבָּעִים136 plural noun indeclinable a forty; —

1. sine num. al.:
a. usually apposition before noun, especially שָׁנָה , יוֺם (singular): אַרְבָּעִים שָׁנָה Judg 3:11; 1Sam 4:18; Amos 2:10; Amos 5:25 + 29 t.; יוֺם ׳ א Gen 7:17; 1Kgs 19:8; Jonah 3:4 + 6 t., יוֺם ׳ א לַיְלָה ׳ וְא Gen 7:4; Gen 7:12; Exod 24:18 + 7 t.; also בַּת ׳ א 1Kgs 7:38, גָּמָל ׳ א 2Kgs 8:9, אַמָּה ׳ א Ezek 41:2 and ( אַמָּה omitted) Ezek 46:22, but בָּאַמָּה ׳ א 1Kgs 6:17 (v: אַמָּה); אֶלֶף ׳ א Num 1:33; Num 2:19; Num 26:18; Josh 4:13; Judg 5:8; 2Sam 10:18 ; 1Kgs 5:6; 1Chr 12:37 ( van d. H. v.1Chr 12:36), 1Chr 19:18 (see also below); rarely followed by plural noun, בָּנִים ׳ א Judg 12:14, אֲדָנִים ׳ א Exod 26:19; Exod 26:21; Exod 36:24; Exod 36:26 ( P ). — (As round number Gen 7:17; Judg 3:11; Judg 5:31; Judg 8:28; 1Sam 4:18 +).
b. after plural noun, ׳ פָּרוֺת א Gen 32:16 ( E ), ׳ שְׁקָלִים א Neh 5:15.
c. noun omitted Gen 18:29, ׳ הָא v Gen 18:29 יַכֶּנּוּ ׳ א Deut 25:3 forty (blows) may he smite him.
2. with other numbers:
a. before unit, + singular noun:, 40 + 1שָׁנָה 1Kgs 14:21 ; 1Kgs 15:10 ; 2Kgs 14:23 40 + 2 שָׁנָה 2Chr 22:20 , אִישׁ 2Kgs 10:14 , עִיר Num 35:6, + אֶלֶף Judg 12:6 40 + 5 שָׁנָה Josh 14:10 ( J E ), etc. (12 t.); + plural noun יְלָדִים 2Kgs 2:24 after plural noun הָעַמּוּדִים + 40 + 5 1Kgs 7:3 noun omitted 40 + 5 Gen 18:28, compare Ezra 2:24 = Neh 7:28.
b. after unit, 9 + 40 שָׁנָה Lev 25:8 ( H ); 1 + 40 אֶלֶף Num 1:41; Num 2:28, etc. (9 t. Numbers, P ).
c. with hundreds, 40 שָׁנָה + 800 שָׁנָה Gen 5:18 ( P ); 100 + 40 שָׁנָה Job 42:16 + units, 7 שָׁנִים + 40 + 100 שָׁנָה Gen 47:28 ( P ); but (Ezra Nehemiah, mostly predicate, without noun), 900 + 40 + 5 Ezra 2:8, compare vEzra 2:25; Neh 7:62, and (׳ א without וְ) 600, 40 + 2 Ezra 2:10, see also || Neh 7:15; Ezra 2:34 = Neh 7:36; Ezra 2:66 = Neh 7:68 (ver. omitted Baer Gi), Neh 7:13; Neh 7:29; Neh 7:44 1000, 200, 40 + 7 Ezra 2:38 = Neh 7:41 plural noun 200 + 40 + 5 Neh 7:67, plural noun 200, 40 + 2; Neh 11:13 also singular noun נֶפֶשׁ 700, 40 + 5 Jer 52:30.
3. as ordinal, שָׁנָה ׳ בְּא Deut 1:3 in the fortieth year; also ׳ בִּשְׁנַת הָא Num 33:38; 1Chr 26:31 וְאַחַת ׳ בִּשְׁנַת א 2Chr 16:13 in the forty-first year
