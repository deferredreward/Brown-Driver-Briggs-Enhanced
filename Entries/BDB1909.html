[דָּחַק] verb thrust, crowd, oppress (Late Hebrew id., Arabic drive away, remove, Aramaic דְּחַק, ) —

Qal Imperfect יִדְחָק֔וּן thrust, crowd, of locusts in swarm Joel 2:8 Participle active plural suffix לֹחֲצֵיהֶם וְדֹחֲקֵיהֶם Judg 2:18 (because of) them that maltreated and oppressed them.
