BDB2838 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2840

חָמַד verb desire, take pleasure in (Aramaic חֲמַד (not in Syriac); Arabic praise, eulogize, appove of; Sabean חמדם in gratitude [praise] there-for DHMZMG 1875, 595; 1876, 686) —

Qal Perfect ׳ח Ps 68:17; Prov 12:12 חָֽמְדוּ Prov 1:22 וְחָֽמְדוּ consecutive Mic 2:2 חֲמַדְתֶּם Isa 1:29 Imperfect יַחְמֹד Exod 34:24 2 masculine singular תַּחְמֹד Exod 20:16 (twice in verse) + 3 t. (including Prov 6:25 jussive); suffix וָאֶחְמְדֵם Josh 7:21 וְנֶחְמְדֵ֫הוּ Isa 53:2 Passive participle חֲמוּדוֺ Ps 39:12; Job 20:20, חֲמוּדֵיהֶם Isa 44:9 — desire:
\ \ a. in bad sense of inordinate, ungoverned, selfish desire, followed by accusative Exod 20:17 (twice in verse) (E) = Deut 5:18 (Deut 5:18 b has תִּתְאַוֶּה), Exod 34:24 (JE) Deut 7:25; Josh 7:21 (JE) Mic 2:2; Prov 12:12 of lustful desire Prov 6:25.
\ \ b. = take pleasure in, of idolatrous tendency Isa 1:29, so participle plural חֲמוּדֵיהֵם Isa 44:9 their delightful things (things in which they delight, see also vIsa 44:11 and BevJPhoenician xvii. 1888. p. 126); לֵצִים לָצוֺן חָֽמְדוּ Prov 1:22 how long do scorners delight in scoring.
\ \ c. less often in good sense, said of God לְשִׁבְתּוֺ ׳הָהָר חָמַד א, poetic Ps 68:17 object the suffering servant of ׳י, Isa 53:2 no beauty in him, that we should desire him (choose him, be drawn toward him); passive participle collective חֲמוּדוֺ Ps 39:12 his desired things, i. e. chosen, choice, desirable (see Niph`al); so probably Job 20:20. Niph`al Participle נֶחְמָד Gen 2:9 + 2 t.; הַנֶּחֱמָדִים Ps 19:11 — desirable: ׳עץ נ לְמַרְאֶה Gen 2:9, לְהַשְׂכִּיל ׳נ Gen 3:6, מִזָּהָב ׳הַנֶּח Ps 19:11 which are more desirable than gold; אוֺצָר נֶחְמָד Prov 21:20 desirable, i.e. costly treasure. Pi`el Perfect 1 singular delight greatly וְיָשַׁ֫בְתִּי ׳בְּצִלּוֺ ח Song 2:3 in his shadow I greatly delighted and sate, i.e. sate with great delight.
