BDB4735 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4737

מָסַךְ verb mix, produce by mixing (compare perhaps מזג, מֶזֶג BaES 33, 51); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ מ Isa 19:14 מָֽסְכָה Prov 9:2 מָסָ֑כְתִּי Prov 9:5; Ps 102:10 Infinitive construct מְסֹךְ Isa 5:22 —
1. pour, only figurative, בְּקִרְבָּהּ ׳ מ רוּחַ עִוְעִים Isa 19:14 (subject ׳ מ); mix, with accusative שֵׁכָר Isa 5:22 i.e. make a choice drink by mixing with spices, etc. (mixing with water came later, compare 2 Macc 15:39) Löwp. 419 Frä162 f, 171 f. NowArchaeology 1, 120 BenzArchaeology 95; object יַיִן figurative, subject wisdom Prov 9:2; Prov 9:5 שִׁקֻּוַי בִּבְכִי מָסָ֑כְתִּי Ps 102:10 my drink with weeping have I mixed (see Assyrian [akâl] ul âkul, bikîtum kurmati, mê ul aštî, dîmtu maštîtî ZimBP 34).
