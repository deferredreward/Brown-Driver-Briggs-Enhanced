BDB2051 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2053

הִגָּיוֺן noun masculine resounding music, meditation, musing; — ׳ ה absolute Ps 9:17; Ps 92:4 construct הֶגְיוֺן Ps 19:15 suffix הֶגְיוֺנָם Lam 3:62 —

1. resounding music; עֲלֵי חִגָּיוֺן בְּכִנּוֺר Ps 92:4 with sounding music upon the lyre (Che); compare סֶ֑לָה ׳ ה Ps 9:17 (a musical direction, see סלה ).
2. meditation, musing אִמְרֵיפִֿי וְהֶגְיוֺן לִבִּי Ps 19:15 the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart; also in bad sense = plotting שֹׂפְתֵי קָמַי וְהֶגְיוֺנָם עָלַי Lam 3:62 the lips of those rising against me, and their imagining against me (|| מַחְשְׁבֹתָם Lam 3:61 compare I. הָגָה 3.b.).
