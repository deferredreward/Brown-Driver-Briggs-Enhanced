צְרִיחַ noun [masculine] perhaps excavation, underground chamber; — ׳ צ absolute Judg 9:49, construct Judg 9:46 plural צְרִחִים 1Sam 13:6 — used as hiding-places 1Sam 13:6 (+ מְעָרוֺת, חֲוָחִים, סְלָעִים, בֹּרוֺת ); as refuge Judg 9:46 (where connected with shrine), Judg 9:49 ( Vrss stronghold; on uncertainty of meaning see GFM).
