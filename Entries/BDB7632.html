Biblical Hebrew

קֶ֫שֶׁב noun masculine attentiveness; — קָ֑שֶׁב as sign of life 2Kgs 4:31,

(so read) Baer has קָשֵׁ֑ב; of a living deity 1Kgs 18:29 as accusative of cognate meaning with verb וְהִקְשִׁיב קֶשֶׁב רַבקָֿ֑שֶׁב Isa 21:7 he shall attend an attentiveness (give close attention), — abundance of attentiveness compare Isa 63:7; Ps 145:7).
