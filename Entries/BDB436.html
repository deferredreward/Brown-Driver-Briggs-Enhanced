BDB435 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB437

אֱלִיאֵל proper name, masculine (El is God, or my God is God) only Chronicles

a. two or three of David's chiefs 1Chr 11:46; 1Chr 11:47; 1Chr 12:11.
b. chief of Manasseh 1Chr 5:24.
c. two chiefs of Benjamin 1Chr 8:20; 1Chr 8:22.
d. chief of the Hebronites 1Chr 15:9; 1Chr 15:11.
e. a chief Kohathite 1Chr 6:19 = אֱלִיאָב v. 1Chr 6:12 compare אֱלִיהוּ 1Sam 1:1.
f. a Levite 2Chr 31:13 .
