Biblical Hebrew

צֶ֫מֶר noun masculine Ezek 44:17 wool; — absolute ׳ צ Isa 1:18, צָ֑מֶר; Lev 13:48 construct צֶ֫מֶר Ezek 27:18 (see below); suffix צַמְרִי Hos 2:7; Hos 2:11 — wool (usually with ( ם) פִּשְׁתִּי flax, linen): in natural state ׳ גִּזַּת הַצּ Judg 6:37, ׳ אֵילִים צ 2Kgs 3:4 (but construction difficult, and ׳ צ perhaps gloss); Hos 2:7; Hos 2:11 (as gift of value); white Isa 1:18 (simile; || שֶׁלֶג), simile of snow Ps 147:6 (from white flakes); white (?) wool as merchandise Ezek 27:18 ( צָ֑חַר ׳ צ, but on text see צַ֫חַר ), Prov 31:12 prey of moth Isa 51:8 (simile); made up into garments Deut 22:11,׳ בֶּנֶד צ Lev 13:47; Lev 13:59 compare Lev 13:48 Lev 13:52 בֶּגֶד omitted Ezek 34:3; Ezek 44:17.
