BDB2695 [H2399]

Biblical Hebrew

חֵטְא noun masculine Deut 15:9 sin (on formation see Lag BN 142) — ׳ ח Lev 19:17 + 15 t.; suffix חָטְאוֺ Lev 24:15 + 5 t., חֲטָאָו֯ Lam 3:39, חֶטְאָם Lev 20:20 plural חֲטָאִים Eccl 10:4 construct חֲטָאֵי 2Kgs 10:29; Ezek 23:49 suffix חֲטָאַי Gen 41:9, חֲטָאָ֑י Isa 38:17; Ps 51:11, חֲטָאֵינוּ Dan 9:16; Ps 103:10, חֲטָאֵיכֶם Isa 1:18 —

1. sin:
a. against man Gen 41:9 ( E ) Eccl 10:4.
b. elsewhere ag. God, absolute Isa 31:7; Hos 12:9 חָטָא ׳ ח Lam 1:8 compare Deut 19:15; 2Kgs 10:29 משׁפט מות ׳ ח sin worthy of death Deut 21:22 = מות ׳ ח Deut 22:26 with בְּ, because of, Dan 9:16 God is entreated: hide thy face from my sins Ps 51:11 and it is said in faith, thou wilt cast behind thy back all my sins Isa 38:17.
2. guilt of sin: היה חטא ב sin (the guilt of sin) come on one Deut 15:9; Deut 21:22; Deut 23:22; Deut 23:23; Deut 24:15 בחטאו מת in his sin (guilt) die Num 27:3 ( P ); אישׁ בחטאו each for his own sin Deut 24:16 = 2Kgs 14:16 = 2Chr 25:4 בחטא יחמתני אמי Ps 51:7 in sin (condition of sin and guilt) my mother conceived me; אם חטאיכם כשׁנים Isa 1:18 though your sins be like scarlet (in guilt); לא כחטאינו עשׂה לנו Ps 103:10 not according to our sins (their guilt) hath he done to us.
3. punishment for sin: ׳ נשׂא ח bear sin (its punishment) Lev 20:20; Lev 24:15 ( H ), Num 9:13 ; Num 18:22 ( P ); with עַל, bear sin because of Lev 19:17; Lev 22:9 ( H ) Num 18:32 ( P ); bear sins of idols (in worshipping idols) Ezek 23:49 the ideal servant of Yahweh bore the sins of many Isa 53:12 without נשׂא, only Lam 3:39 גבר על חטאו (wherefore doth) a man (complain) for the punishment of his sin ?
