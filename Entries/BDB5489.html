[סִבְלָה] noun feminine burden; — plural construct סִבְלֹת מִצְרַיִם Exod 6:6 ( P ), of the heavy labours imposed on Israel by Egyptian, Exod 6:7 suffix סִבְלֹתֵיכֶם Exod 5:4 ( J ), סִבְלֹתָם Exod 1:11; Exod 5:5 (both J ), Exod 2:11 ( E ); (compare Ps 81:7, סֵבֶל).
