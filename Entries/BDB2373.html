II. [זָלַל] verb

1. be light, worthless,
2. make light of
> (Assyrian zalâlu , be in ruins, COTGloss (AssurnasirpalStandard Inscr. 15), Arabic = Aramaic (rare), easy; but זְלַל, , are more common (all intransitive)) —
