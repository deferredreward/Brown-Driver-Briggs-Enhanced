בַּרְזִלַּי proper name, masculine (man of iron)

**no adjective from names of metals; perhaps Aramaic, and בַּר = son NesZPV xv.257; AJSL Apr. 1897. 173. —
1. a Gileadite 2Sam 17:27; 2Sam 19:32; 2Sam 19:33; 2Sam 19:35; 2Sam 19:40; 1Kgs 2:7 בַּרְזִלָּי֑ 2Sam 19:34; Ezra 2:61 = Neh 7:63 בְּנֵי בַרְזִלַּי אֲשֶׁר לָקַח מִבְּנוֺת בַּרְזִלַּי הַגִּלְעָדִי וַיִּקָּרֵא עַלשְֿׁמָם, where 2nd Barzillai = above, & former is
2. a priest Ezra 2:61 = Neh 7:63, who adopted name בַּרְזִלַּי.
3. 2Sam 21:8 a Meholathite.
