II. [פָּרָשׁ] noun [masculine] horse, steed (less common synonym of סוּס ( > explained away by SchwZAW viii (1888). 191); Arabic horse, mare (often), so Ethiopic : compare Sabean פרס horse, MordtmHim. Inschr. 70); — Plural פָּרָשִׁים (erroneous for * פְּרָשִׁים Köii. 1, 89) Ezek 27:14 +, suffix פָּרָשָׁיו Isa 28:28; 1Sam 8:11 — steeds Ezek 27:14 ( + סוּסִים, פְּרָדִים), from Togarmah; simile of swiftness Joel 2:4 (|| סוּסִים ); probably also Isa 28:28; Jer 46:4 (|| סוּסִים, compare Gf Gie), and perhaps ׳ וְשָׂם לוֺ בְּמֶרְכַבְחוֺ וּבָעָרָשָׁיו וְרָצוּ וגו 1Sam 8:11 (king subject).
