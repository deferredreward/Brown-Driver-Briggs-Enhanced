BDB1502 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1504

גְּדַלְיָ֫הוּ proper name, masculine (Yah(u) is great) —

1. governor of Judea = גדליה2Kgs 25:22-25; Jer 39:14; Jer 40:6-16; Jer 41; Jer 43:6 (24 t.)
2. son of Pashur, one of the chiefs of Jerusalem in the time of Jeremiah Jer 38:1.
3. one of the sons of Jeduthun, in the time of David 1Chr 25:3; 1Chr 25:9.
