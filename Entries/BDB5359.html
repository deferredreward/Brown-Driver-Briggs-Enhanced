BDB5359 [H5350]

BDB5358 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5360

[נִקּוּד] noun [masculine] perhaps what is crumbled or easily crumbles, crumb (compare נְקֻדָּה); — only plural

1. crumbs: כֹּל לֶחֶם צֵידָם יָבֵ֖שׁ הָיָה נִקֻּדִים Josh 9:5 all the bread of their provision was dry (and) become crumbs (ᵐ5 βεβρωμένος [οι]; ᵑ7 in frusta comminuti), compare ׳יָבֵשׁ וְהָיָה נ vJosh 9:12 (see Di Benn).
2. apparently a kind of (hard) biscuit or cake 1Kgs 14:3 (brought as present; + לֶחֶם, etc.).
