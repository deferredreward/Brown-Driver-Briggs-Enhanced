[שַׁעֲשֻׁעִים], שׁוּעִים- noun [masculine] plural intensive delight (BaNB 206); — absolute שׁוּעִים- Jer 31:20; Prov 8:30 suffix שׁוּעָיו- Isa 5:7, usually שֻׁעַי- Prov 8:31; Ps 119:24, שֻׁעָ֑י- vPs 119:77 + 3 t.; — delight, in phrase ׳נְטַע שׁ Isa 5:7 the planting of his delight (in which he took delight), so ׳יֶלֶד שׁ Jer 31:20 (|| בֵּן יַקִּיר); אֶתֿ ׳שׁ Prov 8:31 my delight (was) with the sons of men; elsewhere = object of delight, of wisdom vProv 8:30 God's law, etc., Ps 119:24; Ps 119:77; Ps 119:92; Ps 119:143; Ps 119:174.
