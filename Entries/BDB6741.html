מִפְקָד noun [masculine] muster, appointment, appointed place; — absolute ׳ מ only in ׳ שַׁעַר הַמּ Neh 3:31, see שַׁעַר ; construct ׃מִפְקַד

1. muster of people 2Sam 24:9 = 1Chr 21:5.
2. appointment of (by) Hezekiah 2Chr 31:13.
3. appointed place of (in) temple Ezek 43:21 (Krae watch, guardhouse; Köii. 1, 93 conjecture מוֺקֵד).
