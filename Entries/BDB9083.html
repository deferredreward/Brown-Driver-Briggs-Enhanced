[שָׁתַק] verb be quiet (late) (Late Hebrew id., be silent; Aramaic שְׁתַק, שְׁתֵיק, be quiet, silent (compare also שׁקט)); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular, of sea יִשְׁתֹּק הַיָּם Jonah 1:11; Jonah 1:12 (followed by מֵעַל), of quarrel, strife Prov 26:30 3 masculine plural of persons at sea, יִשְׁתֹּ֑קוּ Ps 107:30.
