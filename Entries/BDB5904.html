BDB5903 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5905

[עוּץ] verb counsel, plan (Aramaic id.; || form of יעץ); — only Qal Imperative masculine plural עֻ֫צוּ Judg 19:30 (GFM reads עֵצָה, but see Bu), Isa 8:10 with accusative of congnate meaning with verb עֵצָה. (StaThLz 1894, Apr. 28, 235 derives עֻצוּ from יעץ).
