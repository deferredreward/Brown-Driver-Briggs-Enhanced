BDB5104 [H5144]

Biblical Hebrew

[נָזַר] verb denominative

Hiph`il be a Nazirite, live as Nazirite, followed by מִן (abstaining) from (so Late Hebrew); — only Num 6 in law of Naz.: Perfect ׳ וְהִזִּיר לי consecutive Lev 25:12 Imperfect ׳ יַזִּיר לי Lev 25:5 also מִיַּיִן ֗֗֗ יַזִּיר Lev 25:3 from wine. . . he shall abstain as a Nazirite; Infinitive ׳ לְהַזּיר לְי Lev 25:2, ׳ כָּלָיְֿמֵי הַזּירוֺ לי Lev 25:6.
