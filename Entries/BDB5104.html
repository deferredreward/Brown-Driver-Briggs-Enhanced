BDB5104 [H5144]

BDB5103 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5105

[נָזַר] verb denominative Hiph`il be a Nazirite, live as Nazirite, followed by מִן (abstaining) from (so Late Hebrew); — only Num 6 in law of Naz.: Perfect ׳וְהִזִּיר לי consecutive vLev 25:12 Imperfect ׳יַזִּיר לי vLev 25:5 also מִיַּיִן ֗֗֗ יַזִּיר vLev 25:3 from wine. . . he shall abstain as a Nazirite; Infinitive ׳לְהַזּיר לְי vLev 25:2, ׳כָּלָיְֿמֵי הַזּירוֺ לי vLev 25:6.
