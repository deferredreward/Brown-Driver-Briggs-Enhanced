BDB4227 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4229

מַכְשֵׁלָה

noun feminine 1. overthrown mass.
2. stumbling-block; —
1. figurative of kingdom Isa 3:6.
2. plural הַמַּכְשֵׁלוֺת Zeph 1:3 stumbling-blocks=idols (compare מִכְשׁוֺל 2 c), but rather dubious; We thinks a gloss. SchwZAW x. (1890), 169 reads וְכִשַּׁלְתִּי and I will cause the wicked to stumble.
