[נָסָה] verb Pi`el test, try (Late Hebrew id., Pi`el; ᵑ7 Pa`el נַסִּי; Syriac Pa`el ; compare Ethiopic temptation); —

Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine singular נִסָּה Deut 4:34 + 3 t.; suffix נִסָּהוּ Exod 15:25 3 feminine singular נִסְּתָה Deut 28:56 1 singular נִסִּיתִי Eccl 7:23, + 4 t. Perfect; Imperfect 3 masculine singular suffix יְנַסֵּם Dan 1:14 1 singular אֲנַסֶּה Judg 6:39 suffix אֲנַסְּכָה Eccl 2:2 3 masculine plural יְנַסּוּ Num 14:22 2 masculine plural תְּנַסּ֑וּן Exod 17:2, + 7 t. Imperfect; Imperative נַס Dan 1:12 suffix נַסֵּנִי Ps 26:2 Infinitive construct נַסּוֺת Exod 20:20, + 8 t. Infinitive; Participle מְנַסֶּה Deut 13:4 —
1. test, try (synonym בחן), absolute 1Sam 17:39 (a sword); with בְּ Judg 7:39; Eccl 2:1 with accusative Dan 1:12; Dan 1:14 accusative + בְּ 1Kgs 10:1 = 2Chr 9:1; Eccl 7:23 .
2. attempt, assay, try to do a thing, with Infinitive Deut 4:34; Deut 28:56 with accusative Job 4:2 ( venture a word).
3. test, try, prove, tempt [but not in modern sense of the word: see Dr Deut 6:16 ; Psalms 453, 483]
a. God tests or proves Abraham Gen 22:1 ( E ), Israel Exod 15:25; Exod 20:20 ( E ), Exod 16:4 ( J ), Deut 8:2 ; Deut 8:16 ; Deut 13:4 with בְּ Judg 2:22; Judg 3:1; Judg 3:4 tribe of Levi Deut 33:8 (poem); Hezekiah 2Chr 32:31 psalmist Ps 26:2.
b. Israel tests , or tries God: Exod 17:2; Exod 17:7; Num 14:22 ( J ), Deut 6:16; Ps 78:18; Ps 78:41; Ps 78:56; Ps 95:9; Ps 106:14 so Ahaz, Isa 7:12.
