BDB6505 [H6287]

Biblical Hebrew

פְּאֵר noun masculine Ezek 44:18 head-dress, turban (? originally ornament); — absolute ׳ פ of bridegroom Isa 61:10, sign of joy Isa 61:3 (opposed to mourning, and so) פְּאֵרְךָ Ezek 24:17, plural suffix פְּאֵרֵכֶם Ezek 24:23 (worn by men of position); of priests פַּאֲרֵי פִשְׁתִּים Ezek 44:18, ׳ הַמִּנְבָּעֹת שֵׁשׁפּ Exod 39:28 ( P ); plural absolute פְּאֵרִים Isa 3:20 (of luxurious women).
