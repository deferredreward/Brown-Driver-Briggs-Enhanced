BDB449 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB451

אֱלִיפֶ֫לֶט (פָֿ֑לֶט 2Sam 5:16 +) proper name, masculine (God is deliverance, compare פַּלְטִיאֵל)
\ \ a. son of David 2Sam 5:16; 1Chr 3:6; 1Chr 3:8; 1Chr 14:7 = אֶלְפָּ֑לֶט 1Chr 14:5.
\ \ b. one of David's heroes 2Sam 23:34 (compare also אֱלִיפָל 1Chr 11:35).
\ \ c. a Benjamite 1Chr 8:39.
\ \ d. one of the line of Adonikam Ezra 8:13.
\ \ e. of the line of Hashum Ezra 10:33.
