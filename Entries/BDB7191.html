צָפַן verb hide, treasure up (Late Hebrew id.; Tel Amarna ƒapânu ; set, of sun); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ צ Prov 27:16: 2 masculine singular צָפַנְתָּ Job 10:13 +, etc.; Imperfect יִצְפֹּן Job 21:19 + Prov 2:7 Qr ( Kt רצפן), suffix יִצְפְּנֵנִי Ps 27:5, etc.; Participle active plural suffix צֹפְנֶיהָ Prov 27:16 passive צָפוֺן Prov 13:22 +, Ps 17:14 Kt ; feminine צְפוּנָךְ Hos 13:12, etc.; —
1. transitive hide, with accusative of person Exod 2:2 ( E ), Josh 2:4 ( J E ), of hiding a quarrelsome woman, like hiding wind Prov 27:16 (twice in verse) (si vera 1.; compare Toy); of ׳ י's hiding his servants (from evil) Ps 27:5; Ps 31:21: = treasure up a thing, Prov 10:14, + אִתְּךָ with thyself, in thine own keeping Prov 2:1 = Prov 7:1, in (ב) the heart Job 10:13 (subject ׳ י; of secret purposes), Ps 119:11, compare Job 23:12 + ל person Prov 13:22 ; Song 7:14, subject ׳ י 21:19 reserve penalty; blessings Ps 31:20; Prov 2:7 + מִן Job 17:4 thou [God] hast treasured up their heart away from understanding, kept it therefrom; passive, of sin Hos 13:12 (absolute; || צָרוּר ); read possibly צָפוּן for צָפוּ Job 15:22 treasured up for (אֱלֵי) the sword (see I. צפה ); צָפוּן = treasured, cherished place (i.e.Jerusalem) Ezek 7:22 = treasure Ps 17:14 Qr ( Kt צפינך, see צפין ), Job 20:26 (on dubious text see Bu); plural of ׳ י's treasured ones, his saints Ps 83:4.
2. intransitive lie hid, lurk, absolute Ps 56:7, with ל person Prov 1:11 ; Prov 1:18 and (of eyes of wicked) Ps 10:8.
Niph`al Perfect 3 masculine singular נִצְפֵּן Jer 16:17 be hidden from before (מִנֶּנֶד) ׳ י's eyes (|| נִסְתְּרוּ); 3 plural נִצְפְּנוּ Job 24:1 + מִשַׁדַּי stored up on the part of shadday; stored up for (ל) one Job 15:20.
