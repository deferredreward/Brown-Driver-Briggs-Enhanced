BDB1716 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1718

גְּעָרָה noun feminine rebuke — Prov 13:1 + 2 t.; גַּעֲרַת Eccl 7:5 + 5 t.; suffix גַּעֲרָֽתְךָ Ps 18:16 + (suffixes 6 t.); —

1. of man Prov 13:1; Prov 13:8; Prov 17:10; Eccl 7:5; Isa 30:17 (twice in verse).
2. of God Job 26:11; Ps 18:16 (= 2Sam 22:16) Ps 76:7; Ps 80:17; Ps 104:7; Isa 50:2; Isa 51:20; Isa 66:15.
