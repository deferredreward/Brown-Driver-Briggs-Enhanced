Biblical Hebrew

[תָּאַר] verb

Pi`el denominative draw in outline, trace out; — Imperfect 3 masculine singular suffix יְתָֽאֲרֵ֫הוּ Isa 44:13 (twice in verse) (both with ב instrumental), of shaping idols; for ᵑ0 2 masculine plural תְּתָאוּ Num 34:7; Num 34:8 CheEncy. Bib. 2109 proposes תְּתָאֲרוּ ye shall trace out (the boundary; with וְתֵאַרְתֶּם for וְהִתְאַוִּיתֶם Num 34:10); see also תאה , תוה.

תַּאֲרֵעַ see תַּחֲרֵעַ below חרע p. 357.

תְּאַשּׁוּר see below [אָשַׁר] p. 81.
