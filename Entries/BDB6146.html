BDB6146 [H6005 H410 H5973]

Biblical Hebrew

עִמָּנוּאֵל proper name, masculine Immanuel (with us is God); — ׳ י Isa 7:14 van d. H. Baer; עִמָּנוּ אֵל Gi; — name of child, symbolizing presence of ׳ י to deliver his people (on interpret. see Commentaries) — עִמָּנוּ אֵל Isa 8:8; Isa 8:10 is declaration of trust and confidence, with us is God ! (compare Ps 46:8; Ps 46:12); see עִם .
