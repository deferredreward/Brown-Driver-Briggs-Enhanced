BDB7378 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7380

[תְּקוּפָה] noun feminine coming round, circuit; — construct תְּקוּפַת הַשָּׁנָה Exod 34:22 ( J E ), adverb, at the circuit (completion) of the year, so הַשָּׁנָה ׳ לִת 2Chr 24:33 = plural construct לִתְקֻפוֺת הַיָּמִים 1Sam 1:20 singular suffix of finished circuit of sun Ps 19:7 (opposed to מוֺצָאוֺ; compare of moon, בתקופתו Eccl 43:7).
