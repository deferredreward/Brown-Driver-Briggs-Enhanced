BDB6451 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6453

עָשַׁן verb denominative smoke, be wroth; —

Qal
1.
a. smoke, Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ ע of mountain Exod 19:18, so Imperfect 3 masculine plural יֶעֱשָׁ֑נוּ (at touch of ׳ י) Ps 104:32; Ps 144:5.
b. figurative, 3 masculine singular ׳ יֶעְשַׁן אַףיֿ, with ב against, Deut 20:19; Ps 74:1. Hence
2. subject person, fume, i.e.be wroth, Perfect 2 masculine singular ׳ עָשַׁנְתָּ בּ Ps 80:5.
