תְּמוּרָה noun feminine exchange, recompense; — ׳ ת Ruth 4:7 suffix תְּמוּרָתוֺ Lev 27:10 + 3 t.; תְּמוּרָתָהּ Job 28:17 — exchange = thing acquired by exchange Lev 27:10; Lev 27:33; Job 28:17 חֵיל תְּמוּרָתוֺ Job 20:18 wealth acquired by exchange (figurative; read possibly הֵילוֺ as his wealth (so) his exchange, Bu); recompense (for worldliness) Job 15:31 abstract exchanging Ruth 4:7 (|| גְּאוּלָּה).
