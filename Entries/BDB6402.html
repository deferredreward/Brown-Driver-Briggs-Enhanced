BDB6402 [H6203]

Biblical Hebrew

עֹ֫רֶף noun masculine Deut 31:27 back of neck, neck; — ׳ ע absolute Josh 7:12 +; construct Gen 49:8 +; suffix עָרְפִּי Job 16:12, עָרְפְּךָ Deut 31:27, etc.; —

1. back of neck of fleeing foe ׳ וגו ׳ יָָֽדְךָ בְּע Gen 49:8 (poem in J ), compare וְאָחַז בְּעָרְפִּי Job 16:12 (figurative); Exod 23:27 ( E ), Ps 18:41 = 2Sam 22:41 turn (הָפַח, פָּנָה) one's back before (לִפְנֵי) a foe Josh 7:8; Josh 7:12 ( J E ), absolute Jer 48:39 figurative of apostasy אֶל ׳ פָּנָה ע Jer 2:27; Jer 32:33 (opposed to פָּנָה פָנִים) absolute ׳ וַיִּתְּנּוּ ע 2Chr 29:6 of ׳ י's disfavour וְלֹא פָנִים אֶרְאֵם ׳ ע Jer 18:17 with the back and not the face will I look at them.
2. figurative in ׳ קְשֵׁהעֿ stiff of neck, i.e. obstinate, of Israel Exod 32:8; Exod 33:3; Exod 33:5; Exod 34:9 (all J E ), Deut 9:6; Deut 9:13 עָרְפְּךָ הַקָּשֶׁה Deut 31:27 with verb ׳ הִקְשָׁה ע i.e. be obstinate, intractable Deut 10:16; Jer 7:26; Jer 17:23; Jer 19:15; 2Kgs 17:14 (twice in verse); Neh 9:16; Neh 9:17; Neh 9:29; 2Chr 30:8 compare ׳ גִּיד בַּרְזֶל ע Isa 48:4 (|| קָשֶׁה אָ֑תָּה); ׳ הִקְשָׁה ע of individual 2Chr 36:13; Prov 29:1.
3. of bird: nip its head מִמּוּל עָרְפּוֺ Lev 5:8 ( P ).
