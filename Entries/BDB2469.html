2. feminine from יִזְרְעֶאל 1 a; always אֲחִינֹעַם ׳הַיִּז a wife of David; ending יִ֯ת 1Sam 30:5; 2Sam 2:2; 1Chr 3:1 ending תִ֯ — 1Sam 27:3; 2Sam 3:2 (= 1Chr 3:1).
