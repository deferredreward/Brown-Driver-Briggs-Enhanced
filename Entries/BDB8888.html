BDB8888 [H4932]

Biblical Hebrew

מִשְׁנֶה noun [masculine] double, copy, second; — absolute ׳ מ Gen 41:43 +; construct מִשְׁנֵה Jer 16:18 + 3 t., so read also Deut 15:18 (van d. H., > נֶה- Gi), Jer 17:18 (Gie Buhl and others); suffix מִשְׁנֵהוּ 1Sam 8:2 +; plural מִשְׁנִים 1Chr 15:18 ( 1Sam 15:9; Ezra 1:10 see below); —

1. (the) double: ׳ כֶּסֶף מ Gen 43:12 ( J ) money of double amount, compare כֶּסֶף ׳ מ Gen 43:15 ( J ), a double amount (in) money (Ges«GKC:131q»§ 131q); ׳ לֶחֶם מ Exod 16:22 ( P ); עַל אֲשֶׁר ׳ מ ׳ וגו Exod 16:5 ( P ) the double above what they gathered daily; מִשְׁנֵה שְׂכַר שָׂכִיר Deut 15:18 (see Dr); עֲוֺנָם ׳ מ Jer 16:18 the double of their guilt, Jer 17:18 (see above); a double portion Isa 61:7 (twice in verse); Zech 9:12 ; Job 42:10.
2. written copy of law Deut 17:18; Josh 8:32 ( D ).
3. second in order:
a. second rank, ׳ מִרְכֶּבֶת הַמּ Gen 41:43 ( J ), ׳ רֶכֶב הַמּ 2Chr 35:24 of person one second in rank, priest next to high-priest, ׳ (הַ)מ 2Kgs 23:4; 2Kgs 25:18 = Jer 52:24 ׳ אֶהְיֶהלְּֿךָ לְמ 1Sam 23:17, מִשְׁנֵה הַמֶּלֶךְ 2Chr 28:7, לַמֶּלֶךְ ׳ מ Esth 10:3; 1Chr 15:18 ; 1Chr 16:5 ; 2Chr 31:12 מֵאֶחָיו ׳ מ Neh 11:17. — 1Sam 15:9 read (compare ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ) הַמַּשְׁמַנִּים (Neh 8:10) Th, הַשְּׁמַנִּים or הַשְּׁמֵנִים We Dr Bu, etc. (Ezek 34:16); ׳ מִשׁ Ezra 1:10 is apparently corruption of a numeral.
b. second in age, 1Sam 8:2; 1Sam 17:13; 2Sam 3:3; 1Chr 5:12.
4. second quarter, district, of city, הַמִּשְׁנֶה 2Kgs 22:14 = 2Chr 34:22; Zeph 1:10, compare הָעִיר מִשְׁנֶה Neh 11:9 over the city, namely, the second quarter.
