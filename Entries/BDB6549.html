BDB6549 [H6320 H7163 H3583]

BDB6548 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6550

פּוּךְ noun [masculine] antimony, stibium (Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew; compare possibly Arabic open, separate; Syriac pulverize; but פּוּךְ perhaps foreign word); — black mineral powder, for increasing brilliance of eyes by darkening edges of lids; ׳וַתָּשֶׂם בֵּפּ עֵינֶוהָ 2Kgs 9:30, ׳תִּקְרְעִי וגו Jer 4:30 (on practice in Arabic see WeGGN, 1893, 443; in Egypt, LaneMod.Egypt.ed. 5, i. 45 f.); figurative Isa 54:11 of dark cement setting off precious stones (but WeDLZ, Aug. 2, 1890 CheHpt and others read נֹפֶךְ q. v.); ׳אַבְנֵי פ 1Chr 29:2 are perhaps stones of brilliant hue of antimony. — compare proper name קֶרֶן הַפּוּךְ, and כָּחַל.
