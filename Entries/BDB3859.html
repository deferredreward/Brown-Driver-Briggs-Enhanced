יוֺתֵר, יֹתֵר noun masculine superiority, advantage, excess (late) (properly

Qal participle of יתר) — absolute יוֺתֵר Eccl 7:16 + 2 t.; יֹתֵר Eccl 2:15 + 4 t.; — superiority, advantage מַהיּֿוֺתֵר לֶחָכָם מִןהַֿכְּסִיל Eccl 6:8 what advantage hath the wise over the fool? probably also יֹתֵר מֵהֵמָּה Eccl 12:12 (as to) what is more than these (see VB ); absolute מַהיֹּֿתֵר לָאָדָם Eccl 6:11 יֹתֵר לְרֹאֵי הַשָּׁ֑מֶשׁ Eccl 7:11 elsewhere adverb to excess, overmuch Eccl 2:15; Eccl 7:16 followed by מִן (as often in Late Hebrew), יוֺתֵר מִמֶּנִּי Esth 6:6 more than me; ׳ יֹתֵר שֶׁ = besides that Eccl 12:9.
