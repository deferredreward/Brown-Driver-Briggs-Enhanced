BDB7715 [H7246]

BDB7714 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7716

[רָבַךְ] verb mix, stir (Arabic mix, mingle; Late Hebrew רְבִיכָה, ᵑ7 רְבִיכָא [for Biblical Hebrew מֻרְבֶּכֶת], (dough) mixed, or stirred); —

Hoph`al Participle f. as technical term in sacrifice, always of סֹלֶת well mixed, מֻרְבֶּכֶת Lev 6:14; Lev 7:12, הִימּרְבָּ֑כֶת 1Chr 23:29.
