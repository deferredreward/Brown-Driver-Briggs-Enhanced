BDB4963 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4965

נָגִיד noun masculine leader (literally probably one in front), ruler, prince; — absolute ׳ נ 1Sam 9:16 + 31 t.; construct (נְגִד) נְגִיד 2Kgs 20:5 + 6 t.; plural נְגִידִים Job 29:10 + 3 t.; נְגִידֵי 2Chr 35:8 — ruler, prince Ps 76:13; Prov 28:16; Job 29:10; Job 31:37 (in simile). Especially

1. of king of Israel: of Saul עַל ׳ מָשַׁח לְנ (+ accusative of person), 1Sam 9:16 (subject prophet), compare of Solomon, subject people, 1Chr 29:22; 1Chr 10:1 (subject ׳ י); of David עַל ׳ צִוָּהוּ לְנ 1Sam 13:14; 1Sam 25:30, עַל צִוָּהוּ ׳ נ 2Sam 6:21 (in these ׳ י subject), על ׳ היה לנ 2Sam 5:2, על ׳ היה נ 2Sam 7:8; 1Chr 11:2; 1Chr 17:7 compare also 1Chr 5:2; 2Chr 6:5 of David also ׳ לְ ׳ נָתַן נ Isa 55:4 of Solomon צִוָּה אֹתוֺ על ׳ לִהְיוֺת נ 1Kgs 1:35 ( ׳ י subject); of Jeroboam על ׳ נתן נ ( + accusative of person) 1Kgs 14:7 (׳ י subject); so 1Kgs 16:2 of Baasha; of Hezekiah עַָמִּי ׳ נְ 2Kgs 20:5 הֶעֱמִיד בְּאֶחָיו ׳ לְנ ( + accusative of person) 2Chr 11:22 (of Abijah, subject Rehob.); of Judah 1Chr 28:4.
2. of a foreign ruler or prince Ezek 28:2; Dan 9:25; Dan 9:26.
3. the title of some high official connected with the temple Jer 20:1 (of Pashhur) וְהוּא פָקִיד נָגִיד ׳ בבית י and he was prince-overseer in, etc. (for ׳ פ see Jer 29:26); hence בית האלהים ׳ נ 1Chr 9:11; 2Chr 31:13; 2Chr 35:8 (three ׳ ה ׳ נגידי ב, including the high-priest), Neh 11:11 of high-priest Dan 11:22 ׳ בְּרִית נ.
4. ruler in other capacities (late): of tribe 1Chr 27:16; 2Chr 19:11 of Korahites 2Chr 9:20 of Aaronite warriors 2Chr 12:27 of division of army 1Chr 13:1; 1Chr 27:4; 2Chr 11:11 (commandant of fortress), 2Chr 32:21 (in Assyrian army: || שַׂר); of temple-treasuries 1Chr 26:24, compare 2Chr 31:12 הבית ׳ נ 2Chr 28:7 (i.e. of palace?).
5. princely thimgs, plural abstract Prov 8:6.
