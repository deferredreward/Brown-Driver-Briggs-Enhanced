BDB8890 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8892

I. שֵׁן noun feminine 1Sam 2:13 (yet masculine 1Sam 14:5 compare Dr) tooth, ivory; — ׳ שׁ absolute Exod 21:24 +, construct vExod 21:27 +, ׳ שֶׁן Deut 32:24 suffix שִׁנּם Exod 21:27 dual שִׁנַּיִם Prov 10:26, construct שִׁנֵּי Jer 31:29 +; suffix שִׁנֵּיהֶם Ps 124:6, שִׁנֵּימוֺ Ps 58:7, etc.; —

1. tooth:
a. of man, Exod 21:27 (3 t. in verse) ( E ), Num 11:33 ( J ), Gen 49:12 (poem in J ), Amos 4:6 ; Song 4:2 ; Song 6:6, רֹעָה ׳ שׁ Prov 25:19 , see II. רעע 2; of lex talionis, ׳ תַּחַת שׁ ׳ שׁ Exod 21:24 ( E ), Lev 24:20 ( H ), ׳ שֵׁן בְּשׁ Deut 19:21 irritated by acid Prov 10:26, dulled by it (in proverb of inherited guilt) Jer 31:29 ; Jer 31:30; Ezek 18:2 ׳ חָרַק שׁ Lam 2:16; Job 16:9; Ps 35:16 ; Ps 112:10 , see חָרַק ; אֶשָּׂא בְשָׂרִי בְשִׁנָּ֑י Job 13:14 = risk my life (strike out עַלמָֿה, dittograph; || אָשִׂים בְּכַּ פִּי נַפְשִׁי); בְּעוֺר שִׁנָּ֑י Job 19:20 , see מָלַט Hithpa`el (other conjectures see Di Bi Bu Du).
b. of beasts : Deut 32:24, crocodile Job 41:6, locusts Joel 1:6 (teeth of lions); especially figurative of false prophets Mic 3:5, of foes Zech 9:7, oppressors Job 29:17 , wicked Job 4:10; Ps 3:8; Ps 58:7; Ps 124:6 of their weapons Prov 30:14; Ps 57:5 of Israel shattered by ׳ י.
c. tooth of a fork, tine, 1Sam 2:13 (compare DrSm. 291); of cliff, שֵׁן הַסֶּלַע 1Sam 14:4 (twice in verse), compare Job 39:28, הַשֵּׁן 1Sam 14:5 .
2. ivory, of commerce, קַרְנוֺת שֵׁן Ezek 27:15 (i.e. tusks?); as material, ׳ מִטּוֺת שׁ Amos 6:4, ׳ כִּסֵּאשֿׁ 1Kgs 10:18 = 2Chr 9:17, ׳ בֵּית הַשּׁ 1Kgs 22:39, compare Amos 3:15, ׳ הֵיכְלֵי שׁ Ps 45:9 , also Ezek 27:6; Song 5:15; Song 7:5.
