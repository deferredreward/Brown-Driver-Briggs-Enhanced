BDB3308 [H2898]

BDB3307 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3309

טוּב noun masculine Job 20:21 good things, goods, goodness; — ׳ ט Gen 24:10 + 17 t.; suffix טוּבִי Exod 33:19; Jer 31:14 טוּבְךָ Ps 25:7 + 4 t.; טוּבוֺ Hos 3:5 + 2 t.; טוּבָהּ Neh 9:36; Jer 2:7 טוּבָם Job 21:15 — not in H P , Chronicles, Ezekiel or post-exilic prophets —

1. good things, collective, produce of the land to be eaten Gen 45:18; Gen 45:20; Gen 45:23 ( E ), Isa 1:19 ; Jer 2:7; Ezra 9:12; Neh 9:35; Neh 9:36, to be enjoyed; good things of ׳ י as given by him Hos 3:5; Jer 31:12; Jer 31:14; Ps 27:13 of house of ׳ י Ps 65:5, figurative of spiritual blessings.
2. goods, property Gen 24:10 ( J ), Deut 6:11 ; 2Kgs 8:9; Neh 9:25.
3. abstract:
a. fairness, beauty, of neck of heifer Hos 10:11 of people of ׳ י Zech 9:17 of ׳ י himself Exod 33:19 ( J E ).
b. טוּב לב(בׅ joy of heart Deut 28:47; Isa 65:14 (see I. טוֺב 2, II. ׳ ט 7).
c. prosperity Job 20:21 ; Job 21:16 of Jerusalem Ps 128:5 בְּטוּב צַדִּיקִים Prov 11:10 in the prosperity of the righteous the city rejoiceth.
d. goodness of taste, discernment Ps 119:66.
4. abstract, goodness of God:
a. in bestowing good things Neh 9:25.
b. in the salvation of his people Isa 63:7; Ps 25:7; Ps 145:7.
c. stored up for his saints Psalm Ps 31:20 .
