[קָרִיא] adjective called, summoned; — plural construct as substantive; קְרִאֵי מוֺעֵד Num 16:2 the summoned ones of assembly, term, techn. for representatives, councilors; so קְרִיאֵי הָעֵדָה Num 1:16 Kt (Qr קְרוּאֵי), Num 26:9 Qr (Kt קרואי ! compare √ Qal 5 d).
