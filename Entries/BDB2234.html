BDB2234 [H2039]

BDB2233 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2235

הָרָן proper name, masculine (mountaineer; i.e. mountain-people (or land)? compare Di Gen 11:27); —

1. son of Terah, brother of Abram and father of Lot Gen 11:27 (twice in verse); Gen 11:31 (all P ), Gen 11:28 ( J ); also father of Milcah (Nahor's wife) & Iscah Gen 11:29 ( J ).
2. a Levite (Gershonite) 1Chr 23:9. — On הָרָן in proper name [of a location] see below בית הרם, p. 111.
