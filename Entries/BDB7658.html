BDB7658 [H7203]

Biblical Hebrew

I. רֹאֶה noun [masculine] seer, with article ׳ הָר (except 2Sam 15:27); — old name for נָבִיא 1Sam 9:9b, used of Samuel 1Sam 9:9 a.; v 1Sam 9:11 1Sam 9:18 1Sam 9:19 and (as archaism) 1Chr 9:22 ; 1Chr 26:28 ; 1Chr 29:29 of חֲנָנִי 2Chr 16:7; 2Chr 16:10 הֲרוֺאֶה apparently of Zadok 2Sam 15:27, but corrupt (conjecture in We Dr); plural (with preposition) לָרֹאִים Isa 30:10 the seers, as a class.
