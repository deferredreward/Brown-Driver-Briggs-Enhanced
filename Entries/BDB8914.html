BDB8913 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8915

שַׁעַלְבִים proper name, of a location (perhaps haunt of foxes; Arabic , Assyrian šêlibu, fox); — in (original) territory of Dan, Judg 1:35 (compare GFM, on etymology and site), 1Kgs 4:9, Θαλαβεειν, Σαλαβειμ, Judges also αἱ ἀλώπεκες, as doublet, or (A ᵐ5L) alone; = שַׁעֲלַבִּין Josh 19:42, Σα(α)λαβειν.

שַׁעֲלַבִּין see foregoing.
