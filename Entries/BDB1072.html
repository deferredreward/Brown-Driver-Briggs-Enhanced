BDB1071 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1073

בֵּית מַעֲכָה proper name [of a location] so only ׳ מ ׳ אָבֵ֫לָה וּב 2Sam 20:14, where read as in v 2Sam 20:15 and in ׳ מ ׳ אָבֵל בּ 1Kgs 15:20; 2Kgs 15:29 Abel of Beth Maacah; with ָ  ה locative; הַמַּעֲכָה ׳ אָבֵ֫לָה בּ 2Sam 20:15 compare below II. אָבֵל.
