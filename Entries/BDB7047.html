BDB7046 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7048

צִידֹנִי adjective, of a people of foregoing; — ׳צ Judg 3:3; Ezek 32:30 elsewhere plural צִידֹנִים Deut 3:9 +, צִידוֺנִים Judg 10:12 צִדֹנִים Judg 18:7 +3 t.; feminine plural צֵדְנִיֹּת 1Kgs 11:1 — as substantive = Sidonians, coll with article Ezek 32:30; Judg 3:3 in earlier literature apparently = Phoenicians Deut 3:9; Josh 13:4; Josh 13:6; Judg 3:3; Judg 10:12; Judg 18:7 (twice in verse); 1Kgs 5:20 (= Tyre v.1Kgs 5:15), 1Kgs 16:31 (if, [JosAnt. «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 8:13»viii. 13, 1] Ethb. was king of Tyre also), Ezek 32:30 also ׳עַשְׁתֹּרֶת אְֶהֵֹי צ 1Kgs 11:5; 1Kgs 11:33, compare 2Kgs 23:13 named with Tyrians 1Chr 22:4; Ezra 3:7.
