Biblical Hebrew

I. [נָתַר] verb spring or start up; —

Qal Imperfect וְיִתַּר מִמְּקוֺמוֺ Job 37:1 yea, it (the heart) starts up from its place (|| יֶחֱרַד ).
Pi`el Infinitive לְנַתֵּר בָּהֵן עַלהָֿאָרֶץ Lev 11:21 ( P ), to leap with them (i.e. its legs) upon the earth (of locust).
Hiph`il Imperfect וַיַּתֵּר גּוֺיִם Hab 3:6 he (׳ י) looked, and made nations start up.
