BDB5191 [H5220]

Biblical Hebrew

נֶ֫כֶד noun [masculine] progeny, posterity, always with נִין in standing phrase ( Eccl 41:5 both with נין); — absolute בְּעַמּוֺ ׳ לֹא נִין וְלֹא נ Job 18:19, compare שֵׁם ׳ וּשְׁאָר וְנִין וְנ Isa 14:22 suffix לִי וּלְנִינִי וּלְנֶכְדִּי Gen 21:23 to me and to my offspring and to my posterity.
