BDB8139 [H7576]

Biblical Hebrew

[רָתַק] verb bind (Arabic close up and repair, sew up; Talmud רִיתְקָא fenced enclosure); —

Pu`al Perfect 3 plural רֻתְּקוּ בַזִּקִּים Nah 3:10 her great ones were bound with fetters
Niph`al Imperfect 3 masculine singular יֵרָתֵק Eccl 12:6 Qr (ירחק Kt ), from context = be snapped, broken; read probably יִנָּתֵק (Pfannkuche Thes and modern).
