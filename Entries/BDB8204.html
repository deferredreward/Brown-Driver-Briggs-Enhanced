BDB8204 [H7852]

Biblical Hebrew

[שָׂטַם] verb bear a grudge, cherish animosity, against (Late Hebrew שָׂטַם Niph`al, ᵑ7 Gen 27:41 שְׂטַם; perhaps akin to שׂטן q. v.); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּשְׁטֹם Gen 27:41, 3 masculine plural suffix יִשְׂטְמוּנִי Ps 55:4, etc.; — cherish animosity against, accusative of person Gen 27:41 ( J ), Gen 49:23 (poem in J ), Gen 50:15 ( E ), + בְּאַף Ps 55:4 of ׳ י's persistent assaults on Job, Job 16:9; Job 30:21.
