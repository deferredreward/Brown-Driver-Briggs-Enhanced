אִסָּר noun masculine Num 30:8 bond, binding obligation (compare Biblical Aramaic אֱסָר, Syriac ; so forms with suffix below see BaNB 62 compare Sta§ 208 a; but perhaps Aramaic loan-word see LagBN 175) — ׳ א absolute Num 30:3 +; construct v.Num 30:13 suffix אֶסְרָהּ v.Num 30:5 plural suffix אֱסָרֶיהָ v.Num 30:6 v.Num 30:15, אֱסָרֶהָ v.Num 30:8 — only Num 30 ( P ), binding obligation of oath or vow; mostly accusative of cognate meaning with verb אָסַר (q. v.); Num 30:3; Num 30:4 ; Num 30:5 (twice in verse); Num 30:6 ; Num 30:8; Num 30:11; Num 30:12 אִסָּר נַפְשָׁהּ v.Num 30:13 שְׁבֻעַת אִסָּר binding oath v.Num 30:14.
