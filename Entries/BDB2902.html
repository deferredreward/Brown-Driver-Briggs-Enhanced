II. [חמשׁ] verb denominative only

Pi`el וְחִמֵּשׁ אֶתֿ אֶרֶץ מִצְרַיִם Gen 41:34 ( E ) and he shall fifth (take the fifth part of) the land of Egypt , i.e. the fifth part of the produce (compare Arabic take a fifth part; Ethiopic 1. 2 quinque facere).
