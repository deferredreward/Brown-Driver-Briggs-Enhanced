BDB8359 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8361

שְׁאָגָה noun feminine roaring; —

1. like lion, absolute ׳שׁ Isa 5:29 (of invaders); of lion, construct שַׁאֲגַת Zech 11:3; Job 4:10 (figurative of wicked), suffix קוֺל שַׁאֲגָתוֺ Ezek 19:7 (figurative of conquering king).
2. human cry in distress, שַׁאֲגְתִי Ps 32:3, ׳דִּבְרֵי שׁ Ps 22:2, שַׁאֲגֹתָ֑י Job 3:24.

שֹׁאָה see שׁוֺאָה below שׁוא.
