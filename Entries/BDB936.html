בְּהֵמָה187 noun feminine beast, animal, cattle (Arabic ) — ׳ בּ Gen 1:24 + 137 t.; construct בֶּהֱמַת Num 3:41 (twice in verse) + 10 t.; suffix בְּהֶמְתְּךָ Lev 19:19 + 4 t.; בְּהֶמְתֶּ֑ךָ Exod 20:10 + 4 t.; בְּהֶמְתּוֺ Gen 36:6; Prov 12:10 plural absolute בְּהֵמוֺת Deut 32:24 + 6 t. + Ps 73:22 (see below); construct בַּהֲמוֺת Ps 8:8 + 5 t.; — beast, & collective beasts (Gen 8:1 + very often)

1. of living creatures other than man (אָדָם) Gen 8:1; Exod 8:13; Exod 8:14; Exod 9:9; Exod 9:10; Exod 9:22; Exod 22:18; Lev 18:23 (twice in verse) (where ׳ בּ is male, so Lev 20:16) Deut 27:21; Ps 36:7 etc.; ׳ מֵאָדָם וְעַד בּ (׳ בּ including all the larger animals) Exod 9:25; Exod 12:12; Ps 135:8; Jer 50:3 etc.; as inferior to man Job 18:3; Ps 49:13; Ps 49:21, so also Eccl 3:18; Eccl 3:19 (twice in verse); Eccl 3:21 opposed to also birds & reptiles Gen 6:7; Gen 7:23; Gen 8:17 compare Ezek 44:31 etc.; also to fishes 1Kgs 5:13; Job 12:7 compare vJob 12:8, Ps 8:9; Zeph 1:3.
2. opposed to also to wild beasts חַיַּת הָאָרֶץ, הַשָּׂדֶה ׳ ח Gen 1:24; Gen 1:25; Gen 1:26; Gen 2:20; Gen 3:14; Gen 7:14; Gen 7:21; Gen 9:10; Ps 50:10; Ps 148:10; Isa 46:1 etc.; especially therefore cattle, as owned and used by man, Gen 47:18 (׳ מִקְנֵה הַבּ) Exod 20:10 = Deut 5:14; Lev 19:19; Lev 26:22; Num 3:41 (twice in verse); Num 3:45 (twice in verse); Deut 2:35; Deut 11:15; Jonah 4:11; Zech 2:8; Zech 8:10; Zech 14:15; Neh 10:37 etc.; in one (late) passage of animal for riding (horse ? mule ?) Neh 2:12 (twice in verse); Neh 2:14.
3. rarely of wild beasts, especially carnivora, יַעַר ׳ בּ Mic 5:7 הָאָרֶץ ׳ בּ Deut 28:26; Isa 18:6 (twice in verse); Jer 7:33; Jer 15:3; Jer 16:4; Jer 19:7; Jer 34:20 הַשָּׂדֶה ׳ בּ 1Sam 17:44 alone, Prov 30:30 שֶׁןבְּֿהֵמוֺת Deut 32:24 ׳ שֹׁד בּ Hab 2:17. — On בְּהֵמוֺת Ps 73:22 compare below
