Biblical Hebrew

II. [מָאַס] verb

Niph`al flow, run (Aramaic id., but rare and dubious; — secondary form of מסס q. v.); — Imperfect 3 masculine singular וַיִּמָּאֵס Job 7:5 my skin hath hardened and run (i. e. again, afresh; > GHoffm Du read וַיִּמַּס, Bu וַיִּמָּאס, √ מסס); 3 masculine plural כְּמוֺ מַיִם יִמָּֽאֲסוּ Ps 58:8 (figurative of annihilation of wicked).

[מַאֲפֶה] see אפה .

מַאֲפֵל, מַאְפֵּלְיָה, see אפל .
