BDB2464 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2466

זֵרוּעַ noun [masculine] sowing, thing sown (intensive formative according to Ol§ 186 b Sta§ 228, yet on this and following compare BaNB 42, 178 Anm. 4) — ׳ זֶרַע ז Lev 11:37 ( P ), כְּגַנָּה זֵָֽרוּעֶיהָ תַצְמִיךְַ Isa 61:11 in simile of Yahweh's causing righteousness to spring up.
