BDB5634 [H5566]

Biblical Hebrew

סֶ֫מֶל noun masculine Ezek 8:5 perhaps originally proper name, of divinity, then generally image, statue (Phoenician סמל id., feminine סמלת, Lzb151, 329 , also פנסמלת Id329; BauRel. i. 88 understands ׳ ס to be designation of foreign god); — absolute ׳ הַסּ 2Chr 33:7; 2Chr 33:15, סָ֑מֶל Deut 4:16 construct סֵ֫מֶל Ezek 8:3; Ezek 8:5 — image, figure of anything, ׳ פֶּסֶל תְּמוּנַת כָּלסֿ Deut 4:16 idol-image ׳ פֶּסֶל הַסּ 2Chr 33:7 and ( פֶּסֶל omitted) 2Chr 33:15 סֵמֶל הַקִּנְאָה Ezek 8:3 the statue of jealousy, i.e. that rouses ׳ י's jealousy, = Ezek 8:5.
