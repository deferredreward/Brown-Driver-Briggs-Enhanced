[רָחַם] verb denominative love. Pi`el have compassion; —

Qal Imperfect 1 singular suffix אֶרְחָֽמְךָ Ps 18:2 I love thee (perhaps gloss; see not in || 2Sam 22:2 Hi Ch SS Gerber read אֲרֹמִמְךָ).
Pi`el Perfect 3 masculine singular רִחַם Ps 103:13 +; suffix וְרִחַמְךָ consecutive Deut 13:18, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְרַחֵם Isa 9:16 +; suffix יְרַחֲמֵהוּ Isa 55:7, etc.; Infinitive absolute רַחְֵ Jer 31:20; Hab 3:2 construct רַחֵם Isa 49:15; Ps 103:13 suffix רַחֶמְכֶם Isa 30:18 Participle מְרַחֵם Ps 116:5, etc.; — have compassion, compassionate; —
