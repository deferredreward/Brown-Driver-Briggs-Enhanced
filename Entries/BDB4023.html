BDB4022 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4024

כִּלָּיוֺן noun masculine

1. failing, pining;
2. annihilation (on formation compare LagBN 200); —
1. construct כִּלְיוֺן עֵינַיִם Deut 28:65 failing of eyes (in longing, compare foregoing, and see Dr).
2. כִּלָּיוֺן חָרוּץ Isa 10:22 annihilation, strictly decided.
