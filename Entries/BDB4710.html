מָנָה verb count, number, reckon, assign (Late Hebrew id.; Assyrian manû DlHWB 416; Arabic assign, apportion; Aramaic , מְנָא); —

1. count, number (compare סָפַר) dust (grains) Gen 13:16 ( J ), compare עֲפַר יַעֲקֹב Num 23:10 ( J E ); money 2Kgs 12:11 stars (מִסְפָּר לַכּוֺכָבִים ׳ מ) Ps 147:4 flock Jer 33:13 (symbol of peace); days Ps 90:12 people, army 2Sam 24:1; 1Chr 21:1; 1Chr 21:17 , compare 1Chr 27:24 (object omitted); 1Kgs 20:25.
