BDB8306 [H8272]

Biblical Hebrew

שַׂרְאֶ֫צֶר proper name, masculine in Assyrian (Babylonian) (van d. H. ׳ שׁ, but see Baer, and StrZMG xxxiii (1879), 302; in Assyrian perhaps [Ašur, Bêl, or Nergal] šar-uƒur , […] protect the king ! COT 2Kgs 19:37 ; Σαρασα(ρ)); —

1. son of Sennacherib, 2Kgs 19:37 = Isa 37:38 (SchrCOT l.c. WklKAT 3, 84 StevensonHast. DB. iv. 476).
2. a returned exile Zech 7:2 (Stevensonib. 477). — compare also ׳ נֵרְגַּל שׂ p. 669.
