[יוֺנֶ֫קֶת] noun feminine young shoot, twig (= foregoing) — יוֺנַקְתּוֺ Job 8:16 + 2 t.; יוֺנְקוֺתָיו Hos 14:7 יֹנְקוֺתָיו Ezek 17:22 יוֺנְקוֺתֶיהָ Ps 80:12 — shoot, twig, of tree Job 14:7 compare wicked under figure of tree Job 8:16; Job 15:30 Israel under figure of olive-tree Hos 14:7, of cedar Ezek 17:22, of vine Ps 80:12 .
