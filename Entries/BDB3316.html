BDB3316 [H2910]

Biblical Hebrew

טֻחוֺת noun feminine plural inward parts (as covered over, concealed) — טֻחוֺת of seat of faithfulness, ׳ אֱמֶת חָפַצְתָּ בַּטּ Ps 51:8 faithfulness thou desirest in the inward parts, i.e. in the heart (|| סָתֻם ); of seat of wisdom חָכְמָה ׳ מִי שָׁת בַּטּ Job 38:36 (|| שֶׂכְוִי q. v.); from context this can hardly = heart of man (kidneys, 'reins,' ᵑ7 , Jewish interpretations, see NHWBii, 144 and others), but is rather cloud-layers (as dark, hidden spaces, see Di VB ); their 'wisdom' appears in their obedience to natural law.
