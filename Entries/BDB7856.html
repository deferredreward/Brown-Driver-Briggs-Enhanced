BDB7856 [H7347]

Biblical Hebrew

[רֵחַה] noun [masculine], only dual רֵחַיִם (hand-)mill (probably = two mill-stones); — ׳ ר absolute, common household utensil, turned by maids, Exod 11:5 (הָרֵחָ֑יִלם; J ), female slaves Isa 47:1 (with טָחַן , q. v.); ׳ טָחַן בָּר Num 11:8 ( J ); וָרָ֑כֶב ׳ ר Deut 24:6 (see רכב ); ׳ קוֺל ר as sound of family life Jer 25:10.
