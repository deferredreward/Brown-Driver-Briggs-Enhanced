אַרְמוֺן noun masculine Isa 32:14 citadel — ׳ א absolute Jer 30:18 +; construct Isa 25:2 plural אַרְמְנוֺת absolute Amos 3:9 (twice in verse); construct Amos 1:4 +; אַרְמְנוֺתֵינוּ Mic 5:4 +, etc. — citadel, castle, palace, not used before royal period, mostly in prophets, especially common in Amos & Jeremiah; citadel as securely barred (in simile) Prov 18:19 בֵּית הַמֶּלֶךְ ׳ א i.e. the citadel, stronghold 1Kgs 16:18, compare 2Kgs 15:25 usually more general, of castle, palaces, prominent buildings; especially used in speaking of conquest, because the fine buildings would be especially object of attack & plunder; palaces of Israel Amos 6:8 of Jerusalem Isa 32:14 (singular collective) Lam 2:5; Lam 2:7; 2Chr 36:19; Ps 48:4; Ps 48:14; Ps 122:7 compare Hos 8:14; Mic 5:4; Amos 2:5; Jer 17:27 of Samaria Amos 3:10; Amos 3:11 also Jer 6:5; Jer 9:20 belonging to Benhadad (i.e. Aram) Amos 1:4; Jer 49:27 of Tyre Isa 23:13; Amos 1:10 of Babylon Isa 25:2 (singular collective), compare Isa 13:22 where read אַרְמְנוֺתָיו for אלמנותיו so ᵑ6 ᵑ7 ᵑ9 Che Di (|| היכלים; compare Ezek 19:7 according to ᵑ7 and others, but see rather מעון; DlBD xi defends ᵑ0 in Ezek 19:7 & compare Assyrian almattu , fortress); of Edom Isa 34:13, of Gaza Amos 1:7, of Rabbah Amos 1:14, of Bozrah Amos 1:12, of Kerioth Amos 2:2, of Ashdod Amos 3:9, of Egypt Amos 3:9.
