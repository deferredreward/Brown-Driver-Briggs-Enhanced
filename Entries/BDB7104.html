צָמֵא adjective thirsty; — absolute ׳ צ Isa 21:14 +; feminine singular צְמֵאָה Deut 29:18 plural צְמֵאִים Ps 107:5 — thirsty, usually + (or ||) רָעֵב: 2Sam 17:29; Prov 25:21; Ps 107:5, as substantive thirsty one Isa 29:8; Isa 32:6 so also Isa 21:14, and (figurative of thirst for ׳ י's favour) Isa 55:1 צָמֵא Isa 44:3 = thirsty land (Gunkl.c. proposes צְמֵאָה); הָרָוָה אֶתהַֿצְּמֵאָה Deut 29:18 the watered (plant, herbage) with the thirsty, figurative of entire people.
