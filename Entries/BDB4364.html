BDB4363 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4365

[לוּט] verb wrap closely, tightly, enwrap, envelope (Arabic cleave, stick to a thing; also transitive make to stick, or adhere); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular לָאט 2Sam 19:15 (so read, for ᵑ0 לָאַט, We Klo Dr Bu); Participle active לוֺט Isa 25:7 (compare Köi. 445); Passive participle feminine לוּטָה 1Sam 21:10 — לוּטָה בַשִּׂמְלָה 1Sam 21:10 it is wrapped up in a garment (of sword of Goliath); figurative of covering as sign of mourning, הַלּוֺט פְּנֵיהַֿלּוֺט עַלכָֿלהָֿעַמִּים Isa 25:7 the surface of covering which covereth over all the peoples ׅ || (מַסֵּכָה הַנְּסוּכָה.
