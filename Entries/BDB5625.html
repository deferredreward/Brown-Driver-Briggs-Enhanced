BDB5624 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB5626

סָלַף verb twist, pervert, overturn ( ᵑ7 סְלַף twist (rare); Arabic is pass, pass away, come to naught Lane1407 f.); —

Pi`el Imperfect 3 masculine singular יְסַלֵּף Job 12:19 + 2 t., וַיְסַלֵּף Prov 22:12 3 feminine singular תְּסַלֵּף Prov 13:6; Prov 19:3 Participle מְסַלֵּף Prov 21:12 —
1. pervert, Exod 23:8 ( E ) a bribe perverteth the case (cause, דִּבְרֵי) of righteous, = Deut 16:19.
2. subvert, turn upside down, ruin (only Wisdom Literature): with accusative of person Job 12:19; Prov 13:6 (opposed to תִּצֹּר), Prov 21:12 ׳ אִוֶּלֶת אָדָם תּ דַּרְכּוֺ Prov 19:3 a man's folly subverteth his way; וַיְסַלֵּף דִּבְרֵי בֹגֵד Prov 22:12 and he (׳ י) subverteth the affairs of a treacherous man.
