BDB2836 [H2529]

Biblical Hebrew

חֶמְאָה noun feminine curd; — ׳ ח Gen 18:8 + 6 t.; = חֵמָה Job 29:6 construct חֶמְאַת Deut 32:14 curd, curdled milk, modern labben ׳ מִיץ חָלָב יוֺצִיא ח Prov 30:33, elsewhere joined with חָלָב Gen 18:8( J ) Deut 32:14 (poem; חֶמְאַת בָּקָר וַחֲלֵב צאֹן), Judg 5:25 (where served בְּסֵפֶל אַדִּירִים, see ספל ); with דְּבַשׁ 2Sam 17:29; Isa 7:15; Isa 7:22 (twice in verse); compare ׳ נַחֲלֵי דבשׁ וח figurative of abundance Job 20:17 בִּרְחֹץ חֲלִיכַי בְּחֵמָ֑ה Job 29:6 (|| פַּלְגֵישָֽֿׁמֶן ׃ צוּר יָצוּק).

On mode of making lebben, by shaking milk in sour milk-skin, see DoughtyArab. Des. i. 221, 263; ii. 304, etc. ThomsonLand and Book ii. 418 See also ARSKEncy. Bib. MILK.
