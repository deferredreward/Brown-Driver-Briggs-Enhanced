I. [צָפַר] verb dubious; — Imperfect 3 masculine singular וְיִצְפֹּר מֵהַר הַגִּלְעָד יָשֹׁב Judg 7:3 let him return and ( Vrss ) let him depart, AV depart early (as Ki, from Aramaic צפרא , morning, improbable), wholly uncertain; Gr וְיַעֲבִר; on difficulty of ׳ מֵהַר הַגּ see גִּלְעָד 2 and GFM, who conjectures וְיִצְרְפֵם גִּדְעֹן and so Gideson put them to test (compare Judg 7:4), so Bu Now (compare DrHast. ii. 176 n).
