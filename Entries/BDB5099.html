נֶ֫זֶם noun masculine Job 42:11 ring, always of gold when material mentioned; — ׳ נ absolute Gen 24:30 +; construct Gen 24:22 +; suffix נִזְמָהּ Hos 2:15 plural נְזָמִים Gen 35:4 construct נִזְמֵי Exod 32:2 +; —

1. nose-ring (Syriac ) woman's ornament, עַלאַֿפָּהּ ׳ נ Gen 24:47 compare Gen 24:22 Gen 24:30 ( J ); נִזְמֵי הָאָף Isa 3:21 (|| טַבַּ֫עַת seal-ring); perhaps also Hos 2:15; Ezek 16:12 figurative of ׳ י's adorning Jerusalem (|| עֲגִילִים עַלאָֿזְנַיִךְ); compare בְּאַף חֲזִיר ׳ נ Prov 11:22 .
2. earring ornament of men and women, Gen 35:4, אֲשֶׁר בְּאָזְנֵיהֶם ׳ נ so Exod 32:2; Exod 32:3 (all E ); compare Prov 25:12 (in simile of wise reprover), and perhaps Exod 35:22 ( P ; || טַבַּ֫עַת); probably also Judg 8:24 (twice in verse); Judg 8:25; Judg 8:26 (of men, compare GFM), Job 42:11. — compare further GeiJüd.
