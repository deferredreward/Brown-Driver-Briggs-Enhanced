BDB1083 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1085

בֵּית פְּעוֺר proper name, of a location (= ׳בַּעַל פ ׳בּ, compare below בעל) east of Jordan Deut 3:29, in land of Amorites Deut 4:46 compare Josh 13:20 (where assigned to Reuben); in land of Moab Deut 34:6. On site compare Di Num 23:28 LagOnom. 232. 2nd ed. 246 CondHeth & Moab 142 f. PEF1882, 85 f. TristrMoab 305.
