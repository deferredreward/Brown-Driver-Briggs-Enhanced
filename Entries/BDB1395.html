BDB1394 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1396

גָּאָה verb rise up (Aramaic Pe`al ( ᵑ7 1Sam 2:5) & often Ethpe`el אֶתְגָּאֵי, be boastful, proud, compare also Late Hebrew) —

Qal Perfect ׳ ג Exod 15:1; Exod 15:21, גָּאוּ Ezek 47:5 Imperfect יִגְאֶה Job 8:11; Job 10:16 Infinitive absolute גָּאֹה Exod 15:1; Exod 15:21 —
1. rise up, of waters Ezek 47:5.
2. grow up, of plants Job 8:11.
3. be lifted up, exalted, of head Job 10:16, of ׳ י in triumph Exod 15:1; Exod 15:21.
