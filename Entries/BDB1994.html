דַּק adjective thin, small, fine — absolute masculine ׳דּ Exod 16:14 + 4 t.; דָּ֑ק Lev 13:30 feminine דַּקָּה Lev 16:12; 1Kgs 19:12 plural feminine absolute דַּקּוֺת Gen 41:6 + 2 t.; דַּקֹּת Gen 41:24 construct דַּקּוֺת Gen 41:3, דַּקֹּת vGen 41:4 —

1. thin, of kine בָּשָׂר ׳דּ Gen 41:3 compare vGen 41:4 (⅏ has in both רַקּוֺת compare ᵑ0 vGen 41:19 vGen 41:20 vGen 41:27); of ears of corn vGen 41:6 vGen 41:7 vGen 41:23 vGen 41:24 (all E); thin, shrunk, withered, of man Lev 21:20 (H).
2. small, fine, of the manna דַּק מְחֻסְפָּס דַּק כַּכְּפֹר Exod 16:14 of incense Lev 16:12, hair Lev 13:30 (all P); of dust אָבָק דַּק Isa 29:5 in simile of isles in hand of ׳י, אִיִּים כַּדַּק יִטּוֺל Isa 40:15 (דַּק = substantive fine thing, fine dust); once of a low whisper קוֺל דְּמָמָה דַקָּה 1Kgs 19:12.
