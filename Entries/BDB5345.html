II. נֵץ noun masculine a bird of prey, generic name, including hawk and falcon (TristrNHB; accipiter BoHieroz. ii. 266 ff.; ed. Rosenm. iii. 5 ff., compare NowArchaeology i.116); — unclean bird, Lev 11:16 (P), Deut 14:15 (varieties, לְמִינֵהוּ); bird of passage Job 39:26.
