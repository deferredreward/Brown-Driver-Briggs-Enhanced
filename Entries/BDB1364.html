BDB1363 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1365

בָּֽשְׂמִת proper name, feminine (perfume ?) —

1. Hittite woman, a wife of Esau Gen 26:34 (P); called daughter of Ishmael, and sister of Nebaioth Gen 36:3 (but due probably to R; this daughter of Ishmael is מַחֲלַת in Gen 28:9 P); see also Gen 36:4; Gen 36:10; Gen 36:13; Gen 36:17 (all P) (⅏ has מחלת throughout Gen 36).
2. daughter of Solomon, wife of Ahimaaz 1Kgs 4:15.
