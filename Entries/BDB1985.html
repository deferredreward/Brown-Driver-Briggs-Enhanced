דִּנְהָ֑בָה proper name, of a location capital city of king Bela in Edom Gen 36:32 = 1Chr 1:43 identification with Tennib (perhaps = Thenib, near the edge of the Belka, east-northeast from Heshbon, described by TristrMoab 222) by NeubauerAcad. 1891, 260 compare Tomkinsib. 284.

(רְעוּאֵל) דְּעוּאֵל see below ידע, רעה.
