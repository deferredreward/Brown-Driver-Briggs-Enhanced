BDB9338 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9340

אֲרוּ interjection lo (of uncertain derivation: only Biblical Aramaic (? Nabataean, see CISii. 186); אֲלוּ, and Late Hebrew הֲרֵי see! Here! Ab Dan 3:4 and elsewhere, are perhaps allied), וַאֲרוּ Dan 7:2 חָוֵי הֲוֵית ֗֗֗ וַאֲרוּ, Dan 7:5 Dan 7:6 Dan 7:7 Dan 7:13, always in description of a vision (compare אֲלוּ).
