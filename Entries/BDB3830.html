BDB3829 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3831

[מֵישָׁר] noun masculine evenness, uprightness, equity; — only plural: מֵשָׁרִים Prov 1:3 elsewhere מֵישָׁרִים Isa 26:7 + 17 t.; —

1. evenness, level Isa 26:7, of path of righteous (in the future), figurative for free from difficulties; smoothness, of the flow of wine, ׳ בְּמ Prov 23:31 ׳ למ Song 7:10.
2. in ethical sense, uprightness, equity, as taught in the school of wisdom Prov 8:6, || צֶדֶק Prov 1:3; Prov 2:9 of government ׳ (ב)מ Ps 9:9; Ps 58:2; Ps 75:3; Ps 96:10; Ps 99:4 of speech Isa 33:15; Prov 23:16 of Yahweh's promises Isa 45:19 ׳ רצה מ 1Chr 29:17 have pleasure in equity; ׳ עשׂה מ Dan 11:6 make an equitable arrangement.
3. adverb rightly, with חזה Ps 17:2 אהב Song 1:4.
