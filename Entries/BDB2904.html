חֲמִישִׁי masculine, חֲמִישִׁית feminine adjective ordinal number fifth; —

masculine חֲמִישִׁי Gen 1:23 + 22 t.; חֲמִשִׁי Zech 7:3 + 3 t.; feminine חֲמִישִׁית absolute 1Kgs 14:25 + 4 t.; חֲמִשִׁית Lev 19:25; Jer 36:9 חֲמִשּׁית 1Kgs 6:31 construct חֲמִישִׁית Lev 27:15; Lev 27:19 suffix חֲמִישִׁתוֺ Lev 5:16 + 2 t.; חֲמִשִׁיתוֺ Lev 22:14; Lev 27:31 חֲמִשִׁתוֺ Lev 27:27, compare חֲמִשִׁתָיו Lev 5:24, read תוֺ- Samaritan Di (q. v.); — fifth, usually in enumerated days, months, years, sons, etc.; definite, except masculine Gen 1:23; Gen 30:17, feminine Gen 47:24; 1Kgs 6:31; Neh 6:5 —
1. masculine Gen 1:23; Num 7:36; Num 29:26; Num 33:38; Josh 19:24 (all P ), Gen 30:17 ( E ), Judg 19:8 ; 2Kgs 25:8 ; Jer 1:3; Jer 28:1; Jer 52:12; Zech 7:3; 1Chr 27:8; Ezra 7:8; Ezra 7:9 noun omitted 2Sam 3:4; 1Chr 2:14; 1Chr 3:3; 1Chr 8:2; 1Chr 26:3; 1Chr 26:4 (all of בֵּן), 1Chr 24:9; 1Chr 25:12 (גּוֺרָל), Zech 8:19 (חֹדֶשׁ), 1Chr 27:8 ( יוֺם).
2. feminine Lev 19:25; 1Kgs 14:25; Jer 36:9; Ezek 1:2; 2Chr 12:2 ׳ פַּעַם ח Neh 6:5.
