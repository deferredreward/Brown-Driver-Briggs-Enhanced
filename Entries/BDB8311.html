BDB8310 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8312

I. שָׂרִיד noun masculine Jer 47:4 survivor (from a defeat, etc.); — absolute ׳ שׂ Isa 1:9 +; suffix שְׂרִידוֺ Job 27:15 Kt ; plural שְׂרִידִים Josh 10:20; Joel 3:5 construct שְׂרִידֵי Jer 31:2 suffix שְׂרִידָיו Obad 1:14 + Job 27:15 Qr (van d. H. also Kt ); —

1. survivor (often || פָּלִיט): especially ׳ עַד בִּלְתִּי הִשְׁאִיר לוֺ שׂ Num 21:35; Josh 8:22 (both J E ), Deut 3:3 ; Josh 10:33; Josh 11:8; 2Kgs 10:11 (all D ); more simply, ׳ שׂ ׳ לֹא הִשׁ Deut 2:34; Josh 10:28; Josh 10:30; Josh 10:37; Josh 10:39; Josh 10:40 (all D ); ׳ הוֺתִיר לָנוּ שׂ ׳ י Isa 1:9 לֹא יִהְיֶה לְ ׳ שׂ Jer 42:17; Jer 44:14; Obad 1:18 ׳ אֵין שׂ Job 18:19 וְהֶאֱבִיד מֵעִיר ׳ שׂ Num 24:19 ( J E ); עַם שְׂרִידֵי חָ֑רֶב Jer 31:2, plural also Josh 10:20 (subject of שָֽׂרְדוּ), Joel 3:5; Obad 1:14; Job 27:15 (see above); singular also Judg 5:13 (obscure, for לְ ׳ שׂ read perhaps יִשְׂרָאֵל GFM Now compare Bu), Jer 47:4; Lam 2:22.
2. of things: לְאָכְלוֺ ׳ אֵין שׂ Job 20:21, i.e. nothing has escaped his greed; בְּאָהֳלוֺ ׳ (אֵשׁ) יֵ֫רַע שׂ Job 20:26.
