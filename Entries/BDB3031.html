חֲצִי123 noun masculine Num 12:12 half (Late Hebrew id.; Phoenician חצי; on formative see LagBN 113) — absolute חֲצִי 1Kgs 3:25 (twice in verse) + 3 t.; הַחֵ֑צִי 1Kgs 10:7, compare 1Kgs 16:21 וָחֵ֔צִי Ezek 40:42 וָחֵצִי Dan 12:7 + 13 t. (usually with Pashta or Tiphcha), compare בַּחֵ֖צִי 2Sam 10:4; 1Chr 19:4 construct חֲצִי Exod 12:29 + 84 t.; suffix חֶצְיוֺ Exod 38:4 + 7 t. + Josh 8:33 וְהַחֶצְיוֺ (but article suspicious. see Ges «GKC:127»§ 127 R 3, 4); חֶצְיָהּ Neh 3:38 חֶצְיֵנוּ 2Sam 18:3 חֶצְיָם Zech 14:8 (twice in verse) + 2 t. —

1. half of anything: — blood of offering Exod 24:6 (twice in verse) ( J E ), beard 2Sam 10:4, a hin Num 15:9; Num 15:10; Num 28:14 ( P ); curtain Exod 26:12 ( P ); week השׁבוע ׳ ח Dan 9:27 etc.; אַמָּה ׳ ח half a cubit Exod 26:16; Exod 36:21 ( P ) +; especially שׁבט ׳ ח half a tribe Num 32:33; Num 34:13; Num 34:14; Num 34:15 ( P ) +; half of people 2Sam 18:3; 2Sam 19:41; 1Kgs 16:21 (3 t. in verse) +, etc. so הַמְּנֻחוֺת ׳ ח 1Chr 2:52 and הַמָּנַחְתִּי ׳ ח 1Chr 2:54 (see Be and מָנַחַת below נוח). — On כבחצי 1Sam 14:4 see We Dr.
2. middle הלילה ׳ ח Exod 12:29 ( J ) midnight, so Judg 16:3 (twice in verse); Ruth 3:8 of garments 2Sam 10:4; 1Chr 19:4 altar Exod 27:5 of the Mt. of Olives Zech 14:4 יָמַי ׳ ח Ps 102:25 the midst of my days (when they are but half done), Jer 17:11.
