BDB2502 [H2260]

BDB2501 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2503

חִבֵּל noun [masculine] probably mast (as corded or roped in place; denominative from חֶבֶל) — only in simile of drunken man ׳שֹׁכֵב בְּראֹשׁ ח Prov 23:24 (opposed to שֹׁכֵב בְּלֶביָֿם) i.e. in the lookout-basket at the mast-head, compare Thes in carchesio. — ᵐ5 ᵑ9 read חֹבֵל.
