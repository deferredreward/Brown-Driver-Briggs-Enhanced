BDB6192 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6194

[עָנַד] verb bind around, upon (compare Arabic turn aside from way; Syriac defecit, defuit; compare also עִמָּדִי (for עִמִּי)); —

Qal Imperfect 1 singular לִי אֶעֶנְרֶנּוּ Job 31:36 (object garland, in figure); Imperative suffix עָנְדֵם Prov 6:21 (figurative; || קָשַׁר).
