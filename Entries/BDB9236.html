תָּפְתֶּה proper name [of a location] (si vera lectio) id.; — as a place of burning, in figure of ׳ י's judgement on Assyria Isa 30:33 (Klo Che Buhl Marti and others תֹּפֶת, joining ה to following as interrogative).
