BDB3323 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3325

יְטוּר proper name, masculine and [of a people] (perhaps connected etymologically with טִירָה see Gen 25:16) — a 'son' of Ishmael Gen 25:15 = 1Chr 1:31 = tribe with which Reuben, Gad, and half Manasseh made war 1Chr 5:19 Ἰτουραῖοί τε καὶ Ἄραβες Straboxvi, 2, 18, Ἰτουραίους JosephAnt. «JOSEPHUS:Antiquities 13:11»xiii. 11, 3 ed. Niese; they gave name to their region, which was, substantially, Anti-Lebanon, compare τῆς Ἰτουραίας καὶ Τραχωνίτιδος χώρας Luke 3:1 on יְטוּר and (later) proper name, of a territory Ituraea, see especially GASmGeogr. 544 ff. and reff.
