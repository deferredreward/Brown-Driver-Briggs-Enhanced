BDB4257 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4259

[כָּתַשׁ] verb pound, pound fine, bray (Late Hebrew id.; Aramaic כְּתַשׁ ; Syriac is strive, contend; Zinjirli כתש break in pieces, shatter, DHMSendsch. 58 ); —

Qal Imperfect אִםתִּֿכְתּוֺשׁ אֶתהָֿאֱוִיל בַּמַּכְתֵּשׁ Prov 27:22 if thou shouldest bray the fool in the mortar.
