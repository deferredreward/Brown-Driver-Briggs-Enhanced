II. פָּרַח verb break out, of leprosy and like eruptions (in P ) (usually taken as = I. ׳ פ, but dubious; Late Hebrew id., also decay, ferment VogelstLandwirthschaft 22; Syriac spread, of leprosy, etc.; NS. leprous spots); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ פ Lev 13:39 (of בֹּהַקִ), ׳ וּפ consecutive Lev 14:43 (of נֶגַע); of צָרַעַת 3 feminine singular פָּרָ֑חָה Lev 13:20; Lev 13:25, also Imperfect 3 feminine singular + Infinitive absolute פָּרוֺחַ תִּפְרַח Lev 13:12, and Participle feminine פֹּרַחַת see 42; of שְׁחִין Participle masculine פֹּרֵחַ Exod 9:9; Exod 9:10.
