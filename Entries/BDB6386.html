BDB6385 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6387

מַּעֲרֶ֫נֶת noun feminine row, line; — only of the rows of 'shew'-bread; absolute ׳ מ Lev 24:7, מַעֲרָ֑כֶת Lev 24:6 ( P ); elsewhere Chronicles: ׳ לֶחֶם הַמּ 1Chr 9:32; 1Chr 23:29; Neh 10:34, ׳ שֻׁלְחַן הַמּ 2Chr 29:18, compare 1Chr 28:16 construct מַעֲרֶכֶת לֶחֶם 2Chr 13:11 the row of bread, so ׳ מ 2Chr 2:3 the row of continuity, bread continually present in rows; plural absolute שׁתַּיִם מַעֲרָכוֺת Lev 24:6.
