Biblical Hebrew

פָּסַג verb

Pi`el dubious; if correct, pass between (Late Hebrew Pi`el split, cut off , also=Biblical Hebrew; ᵑ7 פְּסַג cut in two); — Imperative masculine plural פִּסְּגוּ Ps 48:14 pass between her palaces; Gr Che פִּקְדוּ; Du conjecture 'possibly' פִּסְעוּ step, compare [Late Hebrew פָּסַע] Aramaic פְּסַע step, compare [פָּשַׂע].
