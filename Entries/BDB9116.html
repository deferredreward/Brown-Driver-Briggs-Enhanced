תּוּבַל proper name [of a territory and people] in east Asia Minor, perhaps nearly = Cappadocia ( = Assyrian Tabalu DlPa 250 f. COT Gen 10. 2, cf Di Gen 10:2 ); — ׳ ת Isa 66:19; Ezek 27:13, תֻּבַל Ezek 32:26, תֻּבָ֫ל Gen 10:2 = 1Chr 1:5; Ezek 38:2; Ezek 38:3; Ezek 39:1 Θοβελ.
