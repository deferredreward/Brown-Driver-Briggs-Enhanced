BDB4527 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4529

מְהֵרָה noun feminine haste, speed; — usually as adverb accusative hastily, quickly: Josh 8:19; Josh 10:6; Josh 23:16 (all J E ), Num 17:11 ( P ), Deut 11:17; Judg 9:54; 2Sam 17:16; 2Sam 17:18; 2Sam 17:21; Jer 27:16; Eccl 8:11 (in all these cases after verb); before verb (poetry) Isa 5:26 (|| קַל), Ps 31:3; Ps 37:2 ; Isa 58:8; Joel 4:4 (|| קַל); so also 1Sam 20:38 הוּשָׁה ׳ מ (compare Dr); 2Kgs 1:11 רֵ֑דָה ׳ מ; — but perhaps read מַהֲרָה, i.e. Pi`el Imperative of מהר (compare 1Sam 23:27); with preposition (late): ׳ בִּמ in haste = quickly Eccl 4:12 ׳ עַדמֿ id. Ps 147:15.
