Biblical Hebrew

[אָשַׁר] verb go straight, go on, advance (Assyrian ašâru ZimBP 11; Arabic, Aramaic in derivatives) —

Qal Imperative אִשְׁרוּ Prov 9:6 אִשְׁרוּ בְדֶרֶךְ בִּינָה go straight on in the way of understanding.
Pi`el Perfect אִשְּׁרוּ Mal 3:12; Gen 30:13 Imperfect תְּאַשֵּׁר Prov 4:14 + 4 t.; Imperative אַשֵּׁר Prov 23:19 Participle plural מְאַשְּׁרִים Mal 3:15 + 2 t.; —
1. intensive go straight on, advance Prov 4:14.
2. causative lead on Prov 23:19 ; Isa 3:12 ; Isa 9:15 .
3. set right, righten Isa 1:17.
4. pronounce happy, call blessed Gen 30:13 ( J ) Job 29:11; Ps 72:17; Prov 31:28; Song 6:9; Mal 3:12; Mal 3:15.
Pu`al Imperfect יְאֻשַּׁר Ps 41:3 Participle מְאֻשָּׁר Prov 3:18 ; Isa 9:15.
1. be led on Isa 9:15 .
2. be made happy, blessed Ps 41:3 ; Prov 3:18.
