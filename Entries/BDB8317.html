2. proper name [of a people] (usually masculine Judg 11:17 f. but feminine 1Sam 17:21; 2Sam 24:9 [not || 1Chr 21:5], compare Dron the passage AlbrZAW xvi (1896), 57 f.): name of Hebrew nation; usually derived from 1, but ׳ יִשׂ more common in early usage than ׳ בְּנֵי יִשׂ (see בֵּן p. 120:b):
a. (1) undivided kingdom; ׳ יִשׂ Gen 47:27 ( J ), Gen 49:7 (poem in J ) + 108 t. J E , Deut 1:1 ; Deut 18:6; Deut 33:10 (poem) + 76 t. D , Gen 34:7 ; Exod 12:15 + 42 t. P ; Judg 5:2 ; Judg 5:7 (twice in verse) + 104 t. Judges; often 1 and 2 Samuel; 1 Ki 1-12; ׳ כָּליִֿשׂ of whole people 2Sam 8:15 +, of whole army 2Sam 11:1 +, opposed to Judah 2Sam 2:9 + (so also later). (2) ׳ יִשׂ = Northern tribes, distinguished from Judah, even before disruption, 2Sam 2:9, compare 2Sam 2:10; 2Sam 3:10; 2Sam 4:1; 2Sam 5:5; 2Sam 12:7; 2Sam 20:1; 1Kgs 1:35; 1Kgs 4:20; 1Kgs 5:5 +; so at disruption 1Kgs 12:16 (3 t. in verse); 1Kgs 12:18; 1Kgs 12:19 then usually of Northern kingdom, till its fall, 1Kgs 12:28 ; 1Kgs 24:7; 1Kgs 24:10 + very often Kings, Amos 1:1 ; Amos 2:6; Amos 3:14 +, Hos 1:5; Hos 4:15 (twice in verse); Hos 5:3 (twice in verse); Hos 5:5 +, Mic 1:13, etc. (3) ׳ יִשׂ of Southern kingdom, Judah, rarely before fall of Samaria Isa 1:3; Isa 8:18; Mic 1:14; Mic 1:15, so ׳ בְּנֵי יִשׂ 1Kgs 12:17 after fall of Samaria, ׳ יִשׂ (less often ׳ בְּנֵי יִשׂ) occurs of entire people, in reference to past or future 2Kgs 21:8; 2Kgs 23:22 ; Isa 17:9; Ps 103:7; Jer 2:3; Jer 50:17; Jer 50:19 +; ׳ יִשׂ also = Judah Jer 2:14; Jer 2:31; Jer 4:1 +, Ezek 13:2; Ezek 13:4; Ezek 13:16; Ezek 14:1; Ezek 14:7; Ezek 14:9 +, Isa 40:27; Isa 41:8; Isa 42:24 +, Ezra 2:59 = Neh 7:61; Ezra 2:70; Ezra 3:11 +, Neh 10:34; Neh 10:3 +, Ps 14:7 (twice in verse) = Ps 53:7 (twice in verse), Ps 147:2; Ps 149:2 +, etc. (4) usage in Chronicles: ׳ יִשׂ of whole people 1Chr 2:7 + 110 t., of Northern kingdom 2Chr 11:1 + 16 t., of Judah 2Chr 12:6; 2Chr 19:8 + 9 t. (5) ׳ יִשׂ personified as ׳ עֶבֶד י, Isa 44:1; Isa 44:21; Isa 49:3.

Amos, Hosea; 2Chr 13:12 + 7 t. Chronicles. (3) of Judah (late) Ezek 2:3; Neh 1:6 (twice in verse); Ezra 3:1 ; Joel 4:16 +, 2Chr 31:5.
c. בֵּית ׳ יִשׂ Exod 16:31; Exod 40:38 + 142 t. (see בַּיִת 5.d. (δ); 81 t. Ezekiel, where = Judah; see especially Exod 37:16); ׳ שְׁנֵי בָֽתֵּי יִשׂ Isa 8:14.
