מוֺתָר noun masculineProv 14:23 abundance, pre-eminence; —

1. abundance, plenty מוֺתָ֑ר Prov 14:23; Prov 21:5 (in both opposed to מַחְסוֺר).
2. pre-eminence, superiority, מוֺתַר הָאָדָם מִןהַֿבְּהֵמָה אָ֔יִן Eccl 3:19 the superiority of man over beast is nothing.
