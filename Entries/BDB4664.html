BDB4664 [H4419]

Biblical Hebrew

[מַלָּח] noun masculine mariner (loan-word from Assyrian mala—u DlHWB 412 compare Id Prov 1.178 ; HalZA iv. 1880, 53; so also Aramaic (see Brock.), whence Arabic Frä221); — plural מַלָּחִים Jonah 1:5; Ezek 27:29 מַלָּחַיִךְ Ezek 27:27 מַלָּחֵיהֶם Ezek 27:9 (BaNB 49 Anm. 3); — mariners, sailors of ship going to Tarshish Jonah 1:5 (compare רַב הַחֹבֵל Jonah 1:6); to Tyre Ezek 27:9, compare Ezek 27:27 Ezek 27:29 (|| חֹבְלִים).

מִלְחָמָה see I. לחם .
