BDB4332 [H7957]

Biblical Hebrew

שַׁלְהֶ֫בֶת noun feminine flame (Aramaic שַׁלְהוֺבִיתָא ) — in ׳ לַהֶבֶת שׁ Ezek 21:3, of judgment; so שַׁלְהָ֑בֶת Job 15:30 רִשְׁפֵּי אֵשׁ שַׁלְהֶבֶתְיָה Song 8:6 i.e. Yahweh-flame = powerful flame (> JägerBAS i.471 JastrJBL xiii (1894), 111 explains יָֿה here as encl. particle)
