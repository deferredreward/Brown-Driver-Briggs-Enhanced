BDB6768 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6770

[פְּרָזָה] noun feminine open region, hamlet; — only plural ׃פְּרָזוֺת ׳ אֶרֶץ פ Ezek 38:11 a land of hamlets (opposed to חוֺמָה etc.); תֵּשֵׁב יְרוּשָׁלַם ׳ פ Zech 2:8 as open regions shall Jerusalem sit (יָשַׁב 4); ׳ עָרֵי הַפּ Esth 9:19 (opposed to שׁוּשָׁן Esth 9:18); perhaps Judg 5:7 (׳ פ for פְּרָזוֺן, compare ᵑ6 ᵑ7 GFM).
