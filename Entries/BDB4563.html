BDB4563 [H4463]

Biblical Hebrew

[מָמוֺת] noun [masculine] only plural death (compare Arabic ); — construct מְמוֺתֵי תַחֲלֻאִים Jer 16:4 of painful death by diseases; חָלָל ׳ מ Ezek 28:3. — For הַמְּמוֺתִים ( Kt ) 2Kgs 11:2 , the slain, Qr . הַמּוּמָתִים is certainly correct (see מוּת Hoph`al above)
