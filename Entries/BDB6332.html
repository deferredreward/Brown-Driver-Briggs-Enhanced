עִקֵּר verb

Pi`el denominative (from עֵקַר or other like word = (root-sinew), hamstring; — hamstring horses (on Arabic funeral usage, with camel and horse see WeHeid. 2, 181): Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ ע Josh 11:9, Imperfect 3 masculine singularוַיְעַקֵּר 2Sam 8:4 = 1Chr 18:4, 2 masculine singularתְּעַקֵּר Josh 11:6 all with accusative; Perfect 3 masculine plural עִקְּרוּ שׁוֺר Gen 49:6 they hamstrung an ox.
