אוֺצָר noun masculine Prov 15:16 treasure, store, treasury, storehouse (so Aramaic, also ) — אוֺצָר Prov 15:16 +; construct אוֺצַר Josh 6:19 +; suffix אוֺצָרוֺ Deut 28:12 + 2 t.; plural אוֺצָרוֺת Joel 1:17 +; אֹצָרוֺת Prov 21:6 +; construct אוֺצְרוֺת Mic 6:10 +; אֹצְרוֺת Job 38:22 +; plural suffix אוֺצְרֹתָֽי׃ Deut 32:34; Isa 39:4 אֹצְרֹתָֽי׃ Deut 32:34 אוֺצְרוֺתֶיךָ Jer 17:3 אוֺצְרֹתָם Isa 30:6 אֹצְרֹתֵיהֶם Prov 8:21, etc. —

1. treasure(s), generally plural (gold, silver, costly utensils, etc.) Josh 6:19; Josh 6:24; Isa 2:7; Isa 30:6; Isa 45:3 (חשֶׁח ׳ א, i.e. concealed, hoarded) Hos 13:15; Jer 15:13; Jer 17:3; Jer 20:5; Jer 48:7; Jer 49:4; Jer 51:13; 1Kgs 14:26 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 24:13 (twice in verse), perhaps also Isa 39:2; Isa 39:4 = 2Kgs 20:13; 2Kgs 20:15 wealth Prov 15:16; Prov 21:6; Prov 21:20 gathered for temple-building (singular) ׳ בֵּית י ׳ א 1Chr 29:8, הַמְּלָאכָה ׳ א Ezra 2:69 = Neh 7:70, also vNeh 7:69 (compare 1Chr 26:27).
2. store, supply of food, drink, etc., 2Chr 11:11; 1Chr 27:27; 1Chr 27:28 figurative רֶשַׁע ׳ א Mic 6:10; Prov 10:2 הִיא אוֺצָרוֺ ׳ יִרְאַת י Isa 33:6.
3. בֵּית אוֺצָר
a. treasure-house Neh 10:39 compare Dan 1:2
b. storehouse, magazine Mal 3:10 & without בית
a. treasure-house or chamber, treasury 1Kgs 7:51 ; 1Kgs 15:18 (twice in verse); 2Kgs 12:19; 2Kgs 14:14; 2Kgs 16:8 ; 2Kgs 18:15; 2Kgs 50:37 (?) 1Chr 9:26; 1Chr 26:20 (twice in verse); 1Chr 26:22; 1Chr 26:24; 1Chr 26:26; 1Chr 28:12 (twice in verse); 2Chr 5:1; 2Chr 16:2 (?) 2Chr 32:27 compare Ezek 28:4
b. storehouse Joel 1:17; Prov 8:21; 1Chr 27:25 (twice in verse); Neh 13:12; Neh 13:13
c. magazine of weapons, figurative of Yahweh's armoury Jer 50:25
d. storehouses of God for rain, snow, hail, wind, sea Deut 28:12 ; Job 38:22 (twice in verse); Jer 10:13; Jer 51:16; Ps 135:7; Ps 33:7.
