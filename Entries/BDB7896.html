BDB7896 [H7376]

Biblical Hebrew

[רָטַשׁ] verb Pi`el dash in pieces (compare ᵑ7 רְטַשׁ cast away, reject; but possibly akin to לָטַשׁ); —

Pi`el Imperfect 2 masculine singular תְּרַטֵּשׁ 2Kgs 8:12 their children thou will dash in pieces; 3 feminine plural קְשָׁתוֺת נְעָרִים תְּרַטַּשְׁנָה Isa 13:18, but probably corrupt, Du conjecture נְעָרוֺת תְּרֻטַּשְׁנָה compare Che.
Pu`al Perfect 3 feminine singular אֵם עַלֿ בָּנִים רֻטָּ֑שָׁה Hos 14:14 Imperfect 3 masculine plural יְרֻטְּשׁוּ Isa 13:16; Nah 3:10, יְרֻטָּ֑שׁוּ Hos 14:1, all of children.

רִי see רוה .
