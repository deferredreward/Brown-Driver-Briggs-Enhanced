Biblical Hebrew

צֶ֫מֶד noun masculine 1Kgs 19:19 couple, pair; — ׳ צ construct Judg 19:3 +; suffix צִמְדּוֺ Jer 51:23 plural צְמָדִים 1Kgs 19:19; 2Kgs 9:25 (but see below); construct צִמְדֵּי Isa 5:10 (Ges«GKC:93m»§ 93m); —

1. couple, pair, usually of animals, חֲמֹרִים ׳ צ Judg 19:3; Judg 19:10; 2Sam 16:1 בָּקָר (הַ) ׳ צ span of oxen 1Sam 11:7; 1Kgs 19:21 , plural of more than one span 1Kgs 19:19 (ploughing), Job 1:3; Job 42:12 צֶמֶד פְּרָדִים 2Kgs 5:17 פָּרָשִׁים ׳ צ Isa 21:7; Isa 21:9 a pair of horsemen; plural also of one pair of riders 2Kgs 9:25 (but read probably צֶמֶד, so Kit); ׳ צ alone = span (of oxen) Jer 51:23.
2. a measure of land (only square measure in OT) like acre; originally apparently what a span can plough (in a day; see NowArchaeology i. 202 BenzArchaeology 208; compare modern faddan , = what, can be ploughed in a season SchumacherZPV xii (1889), 163 f., Across Jordan 22, or in a day BergheimPEQ, 1894, 192, Ency. Bib.ACRE), שָׂדֶה ׳ צ 1Sam 14:14 (on text see Dr HPS); צִמְדֵּיכֶֿרֶם Isa 5:10.
