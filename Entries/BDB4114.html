BDB4113 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB4115

[כֶּ֫סֶת] noun feminine band, fillet (= charm or amulet according to Ehpraimitic source Syriac, φυλακτήρια according to ὁ Ἑβραῖος of Hexapl., compare RSJPh xiii, 286; in Late Hebrew כסת usually = cushion, bolster, pillow), only plural מְתַפְּרוֺת כְּסָתוֺת עַלֿ Ezek 13:18 women sewing bands upon elbows; with suffix 2 feminine plural כִּסְתוֺתֵיכֶ֫נָה vEzek 13:20.

כֶּסֶה see כֶּסֶא.

כִּסֵּה see כִּסֵּא.
