II. [כֶּ֫לַח], כָּ֑לַח proper name, of a location city in Assyria Gen 10:11; Gen 10:12 = Assyrian kal—u, SchrCOT on the passage DlPar 261, modern Nimrûd, at northeast angle of Upper Zab and Tigris (see Billerbeck u. Jeremias BASiii. 1895, 130 ff.)
