אָמֵן adverb verily, truly Deut 27:15; Deut 27:26 (12 t.) 1Kgs 1:36; Neh 5:13; Jer 11:5; Jer 28:6 & doxologies 1Chr 16:36 ( = Ps 106:48); אָמֵן (ו) אָמֵן Num 5:22 ( P ) Neh 8:6, & in the doxologies Ps 41:14; Ps 72:19; Ps 8:9; Ps 48. ἀμήν = Amen: אֱלֹהֵי אָמֵן Isa 65:16 (twice in verse) God of Amen De Che RVm ; compare Rev 3:14, or God of faithfulness, God of truth ( RV ) (perhaps read אֹמֶן Che Di).
