BDB8306 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB8308

[שָׂרַג] verb Pu`al be intertwined (Talmud סָרַג Pi`el enmesh, weave around; Aramaic intertwine, involve, סְרַג entwine; Arabic set in order, join, weave, mix); — Imperfect 3 masculine plural יְשׂרָ֑גוּ Job 40:17 are intertwined (of sinews of hippopotamus). Hithpa`el Imperfect 3 masculine plural יִשְׂתָּֽרְגוּ Lam 1:14 they intertwine themselves (figurative of פְּשְׁעַי).
