BDB10010 [H8648]

Biblical Aramaic

[תְּרֵין] noun masculine, תַּרְתֵּין ( K§ 65, Anm. 1) feminine, two (so in Aramaic dialects generally (note Nabataean feminine תרתין over against Palmyrene תרתן Lzb388); see PhilippiZMG xxxii (1878), 21 ff.; adjective according to M§ 89 b); — masculine only construct in תִּרֵי עֲשַׂר twelve, after noun masculine plural Dan 4:26; Ezra 6:17 feminine after noun feminine plural ׳ שְׁנִין שִׁתִּין וְת Dan 6:1 years sixty and two; = ordinal, שְׁנַת תַּרְתֵּין Dan 4:24 second year.
