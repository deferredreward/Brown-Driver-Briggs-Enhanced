רָגַן verb murmur, whisper (Late Hebrew id.; ᵑ7 Ithpe`el bickbite, slander); —

1. murmur (rebelliously): —
Qal Participle plural רוֺגְנִים Isa 29:24 murmurers;
Niph`al Imperfect 3 masculine plural וַיֵּרָֽגְנוּ Ps 106:25 and they murmured in their tents, so 2 masculine plural וַתֵּרָֽגְנוּ Deut 1:27.
2. whisper (maliciously), backlite, slander:,
Niph`al Participle נִרְגָּן backbiter Prov 16:28; Prov 18:8; Prov 26:20; Prov 26:22.
