BDB7746 [H7273]

BDB7745 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7747

רַגְלִי adjective on foot; &; ׳אִישׁ ר (after number) footmen, especially foot-soldiers, Judg 20:2; 2Sam 8:4 = 1Chr 18:4; 1Chr 19:18 (פָּרָשִׁים in || 2Sam 10:18); elsewhere ׳ר as substantive, usually collective, of Israel at Exodus. Exod 12:37; Num 11:21 (both J E); = foot-soldiery 1Sam 4:10; 1Sam 15:4; 2Sam 10:6; 1Kgs 20:29; 2Kgs 13:7 plural noun רַגְלִים footmen, men on foot Jer 12:5 (opposed to הַסּוּסִים).
