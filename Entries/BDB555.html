אֲמַצְיָ֫הוּ, אֲמַצְיָה proper name, masculine (׳ י has been)

1. king of Judah, son of Joash, father of Azariah; אֲמַצְיָהוּ 2Kgs 14:1; 2Kgs 14:9 + 8t. 2Kgs 14; 2Kgs 15:3; 1Chr 3:12; 2Chr 24:27; 2Chr 25:1 + 15 t. 2Chr 24; 2Chr 26:1; 2Chr 26:4 אֲמַצְיָה 2Kgs 12:22; 2Kgs 13:12; 2Kgs 14:8; 2Kgs 15:1.
2. אֲמַצְיָה, a Simeonite 1Chr 4:34.
3. id., a Levite 1Chr 6:30.
4. priest of Bethel, under Jeroboam II, Amos 7:10; Amos 7:12; Amos 7:14.
