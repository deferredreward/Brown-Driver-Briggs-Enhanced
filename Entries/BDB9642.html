BDB9641 | BIBLICAL ARAMAIC | BDB9643

לְוָת preposition to, at, beside (derivation uncertain; see K128 n .; perhaps akin to לָוָה, לְוִי, join; ᵑ7 לְוָת, Syriac to), with מִן, Ezra 4:12 the Jews which came up מִןלְֿוָתָח from thee (de chez toi; compare , מִלְּוַת, e.g. Exod 8:8; Exod 9:33 ᵑ6 ᵑ7 = Hebrew מֵעִם).
