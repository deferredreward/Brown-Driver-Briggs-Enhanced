BDB632 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB634

[אֲסֻפָּה] noun feminine collection (compare Phoenician אספת assembly) only בַּעֲלֵי אֲסֻפּוֺת Eccl 12:11 (דִּבְרֵי חֲכָמִים) compared to driven nails; members of learned assemblies Thes MV and others; so Post-Biblical Hebrew NHWBi. 127; but Hi-Now and others refer to the wise utterances, called lords (possessors) of collection because of their well-connected grouping.
