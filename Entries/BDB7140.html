BDB7139 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7141

[צָנַף] verb wrap, or wind up, together (compare Arabic hem of garment, ᵑ7 j צְנָפָא skirt ( = כָּנָף); מַצְנֵפָא turban, Christian-Palestinian Aramaic tassel); —

Qal Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִצְנֹף Lev 16:4 he shall wind (his head) with (ב) the turban ( מִצְנֶפֶת q. v.); suffix, + Infinitive absolute and accusative of cognate meaning with verb צָנוֺף יִצְנָפְךָ צְנֵפָה Isa 22:18 he will wind thee entirely up (with) a winding (under figure of ball, to be driven far off, in exile). — צנוף Isa 62:3 see following.
