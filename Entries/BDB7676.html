BDB7675 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7677

I. רָאמוֺת noun [feminine plural] usually corals (so Ki; black corals Thes (פְּנִינִים = red corals); compare Arabic , sea-shell, WeHeid. 2. 163); — as costly Job 28:18, as merchandise, רָאמֹת Ezek 27:16 — רָאמוֺת לֶאֱוִיל חָכְמוֺת Prov 24:7, reading and sense dubious, see Toy.

רָֽאֲמָה, II. רָאמוֺת, רָאמַת see רום, II. רמה. above
