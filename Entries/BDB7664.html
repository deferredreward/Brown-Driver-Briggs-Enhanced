BDB7663 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7665

רֳאִי noun [masculine] looking, seeing, sight; —

1. seeing, ׳אֵל ר Gen 16:13 a a God of seeing ( =who sees).
2. appearance ( =מַרְאֶה), טוֺב רֹ֑אִי 1Sam 16:12 מֵרֹ֑אִי Job 33:21 without (healthy, fair) appearance.
3. sight, (warning-) spectacle, כְּרֹ֑אִי Nah 3:6. — ׳ר Gen 16:13b Job 7:8 is Qal Participle suffix
