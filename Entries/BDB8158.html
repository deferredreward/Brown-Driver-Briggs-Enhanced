II. [שָׂבַר]

Pi`el wait, hope (Aramaism; compare Aramaic סְבַר think, Pa`el hope ; believe, hope, Pa`el think, Aph`el hope); — Perfect 1 singular שִׂבַּרְתִּי Ps 119:166 , 3 plural שִׂבְּרוּ Esth 9:1 Imperfect 3 masculine plural יְשַׂבְּרוּ Isa 38:18, יְשַׂבֵּ֑רוּ Ps 145:15, יְשַׂבֵּר֑וּן Ps 104:27 2 feminine plural תְּשַׂבֵּרְנָה Ruth 1:13 —
1. wait for, ל person, Ruth 1:13.
2. hope for, ל of thing Ps 119:166, אֶל of thing Isa 38:18 , אֶל person Ps 104:27; Ps 145:15 ל infinitive Esth 9:1 hope to rule.
