יִשְׁמְעֵאלִי adjective [of a people] of ׳ יִשׁ

1.; — singular with article ׳ הַיִשׁ 1Chr 2:17 + || 2Sam 17:25 (so read for. ׳ הַיִּשְׂרָאֵלִ, see Commentaries); הַיִּשְׁמְעֵלִי 1Chr 27:30 plural (הַ)יִשְׁמְעֵאלִים Ishmaelites Gen 37:25; Gen 37:27; Gen 35:28; Gen 39:1; Judg 8:24; Ps 83:7.
