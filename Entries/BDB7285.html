BDB7285 [H6907]

BDB7284 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7286

קֻבַּ֫עַת noun feminine cup (perhaps loan-word from Assyrian [ ‡abu°tu ], plural ‡abûâtê , probably cups, goblets; compare Arabic flower-cup, calyx); — construct כּוֺס ׳ הַתַּרְעֵלָה ק Isa 51:17 compare Isa 51:22 (both figurative, and כּוֺס in both probably explanatory gloss, see Commentaries).
