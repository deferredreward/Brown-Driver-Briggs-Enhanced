BDB2061 [H1959]

Biblical Hebrew

הֵידָד noun masculine Isa 16:9 shout, shouting, cheer (of a multitude; on formation compare Ol§181 a Sta§ 218 b); — ׳ ה only absolute, Isa 16:9 + 6 t. Isaiah & Jeremiah; —

1. shouting in harvest Isa 16:10; Jer 48:33 (3 t. in verse) where לֹא יִדְרֹךְ חֵידָד הֵידָד לֹא הֵידָד, i.e. the shouting is no vineyard-shouting, but that of foe, compare below; compare Jer 25:30 ׳ י shall utter (עָנָה) a shout like the (grape-) treaders (followed by אֶל כָּליֿשְׁבֵי הָאָרֶץ). Hence also
2. shout of the foe Isa 16:9 (נָפַל עַל), Jer 51:14 (וְעָנוּ עָלַיִךְ הֵידָד ); compare Jer 25:30; Jer 48:33 above
