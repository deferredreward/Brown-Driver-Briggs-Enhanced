BDB1515 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB1517

[גָּדַף] verb only Pi`el revile, blaspheme (Late Hebrew גָּדַף cut, wound, then (especially Pi) revile; Arabic cut, cut off, II. deny a favour, be ungrateful, etc.; Aramaic Pa`el גַּדֵּף, , revile) — Perfect 2 masculine singular וְגִדַּפְתָּ 2Kgs 19:22 = Isa 37:23 3 plural גִּדְּפוּ 2Kgs 19:6 = Isa 37:6 Participle מְגַדֵּף Num 15:30; Ps 44:17 —

1. revile, between man and man, (absolute) קוֺל מְחָרֵף וּמְגַדֵּף Ps 44:17 the voice of (him that) reproacheth and revileth.
2. blaspheme, followed by accusative ׳י Num 15:30 (P); 2Kgs 19:22 = Isa 37:23 (|| חֵרֵף; object אֶתמִֿי, reference to ׳י as above); Ezek 20:27 2 accusative הַדְּבָרִים נַעֲרֵי מֶלֶךְ אַשּׁוּר אֹתִי ׳אֲשֶׁר ג ... 2Kgs 19:6 = Isa 37:6 the words with which the servants of the king of Assyria blaspheme me.
