II. עֶלְיוֺן noun masculine Highest, Most High (probably = foregoing); —

1. name of God Num 24:16; Deut 32:8; Ps 18:14 = 2Sam 22:14; Ps 9:3; Ps 21:8; Ps 46:5; Ps 50:14; Ps 73:11; Ps 77:11; Ps 78:17; Ps 83:19; Ps 87:5; Ps 91:1; Ps 91:9; Ps 92:2; Ps 107:11; Isa 14:14; Lam 3:35; Lam 3:38 with other divine names: אל עליון Gen 14:18; Gen 14:19; Gen 14:20; Gen 14:22 (see Di) Ps 78:35 יהוה עליון Ps 7:18; Ps 47:3 אלהים עליון Ps 57:3; Ps 78:56.
5. of rulers, either monarchs or angel-princes, בְּנֵי עֶלְיוֺן = אלהים Ps 82:6.
