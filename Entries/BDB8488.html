שֵׁשׁ, שִׁשָּׁה216 noun masculine and feminine six; — masculine שֵׁשׁ (c. noun feminine) Gen 31:41 + 120 t.; feminine שִׁשָּׁה (with noun masculine) Gen 30:20 + 39 t.; construct שֵׁ֫שֶׁת Exod 23:12 + 30 t. (6 + 10 = 16 are additional); — six (Hexateuch chiefly P ):

1. no other number:
a. before plural noun 75 t.: e.g. שֵׁשׁ שָׁנִים Exod 21:2 ( E ) + 11 t., שֵׁשׁ אַמּוֺת 1Sam 17:4 + 8 t.; שֵׁשׁ בָּאַמָּה 1Kgs 6:6 שִׁשָּׁה בָּנִים Gen 30:20 ( E ) + 2 t.; שֵׁשֶׁת יָמִים Exod 23:12 ( E ) + 14 t.; after noun (late) Exod 28:2; Josh 15:59; Josh 15:62 (all P ), 1Chr 4:27 ; 1Chr 26:17 ; Neh 5:18 rarely noun omitted Lev 24:6 ( P ) + (distributive 2Sam 21:20 = 1Chr 20:6).
b. = ordinal ׳ שְׁנַת שֵׁשׁ לְחִזְק 2Kgs 18:10 ( Ges«GKC:134o»§ 134o).
2. 16, with noun feminine: שֵׁשׁ עֶשְׂרֵה, before נָ֑פֶשׁ Gen 46:18 ( P ), שָׁנָה 2Kgs 13:10 + 9 t., בָּנוֺת 2Chr 13:21 after עָרִים Josh 15:41; Josh 19:22 ( P ); with noun masculine שִׁשָּׁה עָשָׂר, before אֲדָנִים Exod 26:25; Exod 36:30 ( P ), also אֶלֶף, 1000, Num 31:40 + ( P ); after בָּנִים 1Chr 4:27, etc.; = ordinal 16th 1Chr 24:14; 1Chr 25:23 +, ׳ ע ׳ יוֺם שׁ 2Chr 29:27.
3. 600 = שֵׁשׁ מֵאוֺת, Gen 7:6 ( P ) + 63 t.; 6000 = שֵׁשֶׁת אֲלָפִים Num 3:34 ( P ) + 8 t.; אֶלֶף ׳ שׁ Judg 20:15 + 2 t.
4. with tens, ׳ שׁ following Gen 46:26 + 13 t., precedes Num 31:44 ( P ).
5. ׳ שׁ as round number, ׳ חָמֵשׁ אוֺשֿׁ 2Kgs 13:19, compare Job 5:19; Prov 6:16.
