I. צַלְמוֺן

proper name, of a mountain 1. ׳הַרצֿ Judg 9:48 near Shechem, not identified, compare GFM, BuhlGeogr. 100; Ερμων (erroneous).
2. snow-capped mountain Ps 68:14, probably east of Jordan (in Hauran according to Wetzst, compare BuhlGeogr. 118), Σελμων.
