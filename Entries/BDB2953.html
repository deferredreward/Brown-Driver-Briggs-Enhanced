Biblical Hebrew

תְּחִנּה noun feminine favour, supplication for favour; — ׳ ת Josh 11:20 + 4 t.; construct תְּחִנַּת 1Kgs 8:30; 1Kgs 8:52 (twice in verse); תְּחִנָּתִי Jer 37:20 + 5 t.; תְּחִנֹּתֵיהֶם 2Chr 6:39 etc. + 10 t. suffix; —

1. favour, shewn by Israel Josh 11:29 ( D ); מאת יהוה Ezra 9:8.
2. supplication for favour, from God 1Kgs 8:52 (twice in verse); 2Chr 33:13; Ps 119:170 || תְּפִלָּה 1Kgs 8:28 = 2Chr 6:19; Ps 6:10; Ps 55:2 תפלה (ו)תחנה 1Kgs 8:38; 1Kgs 8:45; 1Kgs 8:49; 1Kgs 8:54 = 2Chr 6:29; 2Chr 6:35; 2Chr 6:39; 1Kgs 9:3 אשׁר התפלל ׳ ת 1Kgs 8:30 (= תַּחֲנוּן 2Chr 6:21); לִפְנֵי ׳ תִּפֹּלּ ת let the supplication fall before, Yahweh Jer 36:7, the king Jer 37:20, Jeremiah Jer 42:2 לפני ׳ הִפִּיל ת present supplication before, Yahweh Jer 42:9; Dan 9:20, the king Jer 38:26.
