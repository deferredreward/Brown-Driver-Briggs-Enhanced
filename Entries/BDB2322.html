Qal (late) Perfect 3 masculine singular זָע Esth 5:9 Imperfect 3 masculine plural with rel. שֶׁיָּזֻעזּ Eccl 12:3 — tremble, quake, in presence of powerful superior Esth 5:9 (followed by מִמֶּנּוּ); said of שֹׁמְרֵי הַבַּיִת in description of infirm old man Eccl 12:3
Pilpel Participle suffix מְזַעְזְעֶיךָ Hab 2:7 causative and intensive they that shall violently shake thee, figurative of foes of Babylon, instruments of Yahweh's judgment.
