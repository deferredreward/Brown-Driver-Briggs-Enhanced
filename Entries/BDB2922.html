BDB2921 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB2923

[תַּחֲנָה] noun feminine encamping, or encampment; — plural suffix תַּחֲנֹתִי (si vera 1.) אֶלמְֿקוֺם פְּנִֹי אַלְמֹנִי 2Kgs 6:8, unto such and such a place is my encamping; but form very strange. Read probably תֵּחָֽבְאוּ ye shall hide yourselves, so ᵑ6 Th Klo.
