BDB915 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB917

III. [בַּד] noun masculine only Plural בַּדִּים.
\ \ a. empty, idle talk (Phoenician CIS3, 6 listen not to בדנם = Hebrew בַּדֵּיהֶם; compare vain talk), especially with collateral idea of imaginary pretensions or claims: Job 11:3 בַּדֶּיךָ מְתִיםיַחֲרִישׁוּ thy idle talk brings men to silence (|| וַתִּלְעַג), Isa 16:6 (of Moab) לֹא כֵן בַּדָּיו his boastings are not right (unfounded), hence Jer 48:30.
\ \ b. concrete empty talkers, praters (compare Late Hebrew בַּדָּאָה, Syriac , liar), of false prophets, Isa 44:25; Jer 50:36.
