BDB3723 [H3138]

BDB3722 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3724

יוֺרֶה noun [masculine] early rain, which falls in Palestine from the last of October until the first of December, opposed to מַלְקוֺשׁ: Deut 11:14; Jer 5:24; Hos 6:3 (where ᵑ0 makes ׳ יו Participle, or Hiph`il Imperfect, but see We); compare also I. מוֺרֶה. See further RobBr i, 429 f. ChaplinPEF 1883, 8 ff. KleinZPV iv, 72 f.
