[מַעֲלָל]41 noun masculineNeh 9:35 deed, practice; — only plural מַעֲלָלִים 1Sam 25:3 construct מַעַלְלֵי Ps 77:12; Ps 78:1 suffix מַעֲלָלֶיךָ Deut 28:20, מַעֲלָלָיו Hos 4:9 +, מַעַלְלֵיכֶם Isa 1:16 +, מַעַלְלֵיהֶם Isa 3:10 +, etc.; —

1. usually (especially Jer 17 t.) bad practices of men, רַע מַעֲלָלִים 1Sam 25:3 he was evil in his practices; Judg 2:19 (|| דֶּרֶךְ), Hos 5:5; Hos 7:2; Isa 3:8; Jer 11:18; Ps 106:29; Ps 106:39 (|| מַעֲשֵׂיהֶם); הָרָעִים ׳מ Neh 9:35 ׳רֹעַ מ the evil of your (their, etc.) practices Hos 9:15; Isa 1:16; Jer 4:4; Jer 21:12 (suffix 3 masculine plural Kt, 2 masculine plural Qr), +; הָרָעִים ׳מ Zech 1:4 Qr (+ הָרָעִים ׳ד; Kt מעליליהם), אֲשֶׁר לֹא טוֺבִים ׳מ Ezek 36:31 (+ id.); ׳הֵרֵעוּ מ Mic 3:4 ׳פְּרִי מ, i.e. their consequences, Isa 3:10; Jer 17:10; Jer 21:14; Jer 32:19 (+ דֶּרֶךְ).
2. deeds of ׳י Ps 77:12, of אֵל Ps 78:7 compare Mic 2:7.
3. acts, in general, of youth Prov 20:11 (|| פָּעֳלוֺ).
