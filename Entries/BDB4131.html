[כִּרְסֵם] verb quadriliteral = Pi`el, tear off (compare קִרְסֵם Late Hebrew cut or eat away, Deut 28:38 ᵑ7J for חָסַל; on form see Ges«GKC:56»§ 56); — only Imperfect 3 masculine singular suffix: — יְכַרְסְמֶנָּה חֲזִיר מִיָּ֫עַר Ps 80:14 teareth it off (that is, the vine, figurative of Israel).
