BDB6368 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB6370

[עָרַג] verb long for (compare Arabic ascend, II. bend, incline toward ( ); high, elevated; Ethiopic ascend; Late Hebrew עֲרוּגָה = Biblical Hebrew); —

Qal Imperfect 3 feminine singular תַּעֲרֹג Ps 42:2 (of stag, with עַל of thing in simile); with אֶל, of longing for God Ps 42:2 (subject נַפְשִׁי ), so תַּעֲרוֺג Joel 1:20 (of beasts, craving water).
