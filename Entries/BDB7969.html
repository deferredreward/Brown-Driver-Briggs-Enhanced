BDB7968 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB7970

מִרְמָס noun [masculine] trampling-place, trampling; — absolute ׳מ Mic 7:10 +, מִרְמַס (Köii.1, 96) Isa 10:6 construct מִרְמַס Isa 7:25; Ezek 34:19 —

1. trampling-place, שֶׂה ׳מ Isa 7:25 (|| מִשְׁלַח), רַגְלֵיכֶם ׳מ Ezek 34:19 (figurative).
2. trampling, ׳וְהָיָה לְמ Isa 5:5 it shall become a trampling, be trampled down, so Mic 7:10; Isa 28:18 והייתם לו למרמס, compare Isa 10:6; Dan 8:13.
