BDB5729 [H4565]

Biblical Hebrew

מִסְתָּר noun [masculine] secret place, hiding place; — absolute ׳ מ Ps 10:9; Hab 3:14 usually plural מִסְתָּרִים Jer 13:17 + 6 t.; suffix מִסְתָּרָיו Jer 49:10 —

1. secret place(s), concealed from view, Jer 13:17 where treasures are stored Isa 45:3.
2. hiding-place(s):
a. for protection Jer 23:24; Jer 49:10.
b. for perpetration of crime, especially murder: Ps 10:8 (|| מַאֲרָב), simile of lion Ps 10:9, Ps 17:12, compare Hab 3:14; Ps 64:5 of ׳ י lying in wait Lam 3:10 (as a lion).
