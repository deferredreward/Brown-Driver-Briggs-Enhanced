BDB3685 | BIBLICAL HEBREW | BDB3687

מוֺקְדָה noun feminine hearth ( = place of burning), only of altar-hearth, the plate or top of altar, on which burnt-offering was laid and consumed, עַל הַמִּזְבֵּחַ ׳הִוא הָעוֺלָה עַל מ Lev 6:2 (followed by וְאֵשׁ הַמִּזְבֵּחַ תּוּקַד בּוֺ).
