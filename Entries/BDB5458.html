נָתַץ verb pull down, break down (Late Hebrew Hoph`al, and derivatives; compare perhaps Ethiopic (with transposition and weakening of sibilant), destruere, demoliri, excidere Di634); —

Qal Perfect 3 masculine singular ׳ נ Judg 6:30 +, 3 plural נָָֽתְצוּ 2Kgs 25:10; Jer 52:14, etc.; Imperfect 3 masculine singular יִתֹּץ Ezek 26:9 + Ezek 26:12 ( Co, for ᵑ0 יִתְּצוּ); וַיִּתֹּץ Judg 9:45; 2Kgs 23:7 suffix יִתְּצֵנִי Job 19:10, יִתָּצְךָ Ps 52:7 1 singular אֶתֹּץ Judg 8:9 3 masculine plural וַיִּתְּצוּ 2Kgs 10:27 (twice in verse); 2 masculine plural תִּתֹּ֑צוּ Deut 7:5, תִּתֹּצ֑וּן Exod 34:13; Judg 2:2 וַתִּתְֿצוּ Isa 22:2, etc.; Imperative נְתֹץ Ps 58:7 Infinitive construct לִנְתֿוֺץ Jer 1:10; Jer 18:7, לִנְתֹֿץ Jer 31:28 (on תֿ see Ges«GKC:45g»§ 45g); Passive participle plural הַנְּתֻצִים Jer 33:4 —
1. literally pull down a structure (accusative): altar Judg 2:2; Judg 6:30; Judg 6:31; Judg 6:32; Exod 34:13 ( J ), Deut 7:5 ; 2Kgs 23:12 high place (במה) 2Kgs 23:8, בָּמָה + מִזְבֵּחַ 2Kgs 23:15 מַצֵּבָה 2Kgs 10:27 בֵּית הַבַּעַל 2Kgs 10:27; 2Kgs 11:18 = 2Chr 23:17, compare 2Kgs 23:7 a tower Judg 8:9; Judg 8:17, compare Ezek 26:9 ( בְּחַרְבוֺתָיו ); (any) house Isa 22:10; Ezek 26:12; Lev 14:45, so הַנְּחֻצִים Jer 33:4 (strangely + אֶל, compare Gf Gie); city-wall Jer 39:8; 2Kgs 25:10 = Jer 52:14 city Judg 9:45.
2. figurative:
a. pull down a nation, break its power, וּלְהַאֲבִיד ׳ וְלִנְתוֺשׁ וְלִנ (וְלַהֲרוֺס) (object not expressed) Jer 1:10 (so ), = Jer 18:7, compare Jer 31:18.
b. an individual, subject God, יִתְּצֵנִי סָבִיב Job 19:10 , יִתָּצְךָ Ps 52:7.
c. jaw-teeth of lions (metaphor) Ps 58:7 (break down, break off, so only here, but see נָתַע ).
