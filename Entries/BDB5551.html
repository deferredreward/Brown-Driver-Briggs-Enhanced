[סָחָה] verb scrape (Arabic ( , and Lane1322) scrape off, clear away; compare Talmud סְחוּתָא, סְחִיתָא refuse, ᵑ7 סְחִיתָא dirt, dung); — only

Pi`el scrape clean, scour, Perfect 1 singular consecutive וְסִחֵתִ֫י עֲפָרָהּ מֶּנָּהָ Ezek 26:4 and I will scrape clean her dust from her.
