BDB5102 [H5145]

Biblical Hebrew

נֵ֫זֶר noun masculine Ps 132:18 consecration, crown, Naziriteship; — ׳ נ absolute 2Sam 1:10 +; construct Exod 29:6 +; suffix נִזֵרוֺ Ps 89:40 +, נִזְרֵךְ Jer 7:29 —

1. crown (sign of consecration; WeSkizzeu iii. 118 compare Syriac קדשא [ ], earring; see also GeiJüd. Zeitschr. x. 45 ff. on נֶזֶם):
a. of king 2Sam 1:10; 2Kgs 11:12 = 2Chr 23:11 symbol of royal power Ps 89:40; Ps 132:18 compare Prov 27:24 ׳ אַבְּנֵי נ Zech 9:16 stones of a crown, diadem (probably; > We Now — who strike out, compare GASm — stones of charming, from use of precious stones as charms.
b. of high priest, הַקֹּדֶשׁ ׳ נ Exod 29:6, made of gold Exod 39:30; Lev 8:9 (all P ).
2. woman's hair (originally probably of long hair as sign of consecration, as in Nazirite vow, compare Num 6:19 below, and נָזַיר also WeSklzzen iii. 117, 107; Arab. Heidenthum 2, p. 143 RSSemitic i. 464. 2d ed. 483), Jer 7:29 (of personified Jerusalem).
3. consecration
a. of high priest עליו ׳ שֶׁמֶן מִשְׁחַת א ׳ נ Lev 21:12 ( P ) the consecration of the oil of anointing of his God is upon him.
b. in Num 6 ( P ), specifically of Nazirite consecration (compare נָזִיר) : אֱלֹהָיו עָלָיו ׳ נ Num 6:7, נֶדֶר נִזְרוֺ Num 6:5, ׳ טָמֵא נ Num 6:12, ׳ ראֹשׁ נ (because of unshorn hair) Num 6:9 Num 6:18 Num 6:18, ׳ (כָּלֿ) יְמֵי נ Num 6:4 Num 6:8 Num 6:12 Num 6:13 ׳ תּוֺרַת נ Num 6:21 b compare ׳ עַלנֿ Num 6:21a; in Num 6:19 ׳ נ = the hair of his consecration (compare Jer 7:29 above).
